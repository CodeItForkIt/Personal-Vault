## Mercy

**Mercy** is a [Shard](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shard "Shard") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium") that has not been [Splintered](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Splinter#Formation "Splinter") and whose Vessel is unknown.[^1]

## History

Mercy was created at the [Shattering of Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shattering_of_Adonalsium "Shattering of Adonalsium") and picked up by an unknown Vessel.[^2] It is not known what Mercy did directly after their Ascension, or where they settled, if they settled at all.

Mercy was involved in a battle between [Odium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Odium "Odium") and [Ambition](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ambition "Ambition"), which resulted in a wound upon the [Spiritual Realm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spiritual_Realm "Spiritual Realm") and ultimately in Ambition's Splintering. This clash had profound effects on [Threnody](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Threnody "Threnody").[^3]

Some time around the [True Desolation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/True_Desolation "True Desolation"), [Harmony](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Harmony "Harmony") reached out to Mercy about the threat posed by Odium, at [Hoid's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoid "Hoid") request, but became troubled by Mercy as a result of their exchange.[^1] [^4]

## Trivia

- Before [Aona's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aona "Aona") Shard was known (and well before it was known that Mercy is a Shard), fans speculated that its Intent might be Mercy.[^5] It was later revealed Aona's Shard is Devotion.[^6] [^7]
