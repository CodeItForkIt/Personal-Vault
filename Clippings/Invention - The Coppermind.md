## Invention

**Invention** is a [Shard](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shard "Shard") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium") that has not been [Splintered](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Splinter#Formation "Splinter") and whose original Vessel is [Chan Ko Sar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chan_Ko_Sar "Chan Ko Sar").[^1] The Vessel may be one of the scholars [Hoid](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoid "Hoid") questioned about talent, who answered that the highest talent is the invention of marvelous devices.[^3]

Invention created a number of planets, and [Starling](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starling "Starling") believes that they are more technologically advanced than [Scadrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Scadrial "Scadrial").[^4] The lawnarks native to the [Grand Apparatus](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Grand_Apparatus "Grand Apparatus") are mentioned to be 'of Invention'.[^5]

## History

Like all Shards, Invention was created at the [Shattering](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shattering "Shattering") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium"), and picked up by the Vessel [Chan Ko Sar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chan_Ko_Sar "Chan Ko Sar"). After Ascending, Chan Ko Sar traveled the cosmere creating "great marvels." [^1]

Some time around the [True Desolation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/True_Desolation "True Desolation") [Harmony](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Harmony "Harmony"), on the advice of [Hoid](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoid "Hoid"), reached out to Invention about the threat posed by [Odium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Odium "Odium"). After that initial contact, Harmony was unable to locate Invention again.[^6] [^2]

## Trivia

- If Brandon were to become a Vessel, Invention is one of the Shards he has said he might choose.[^7]
