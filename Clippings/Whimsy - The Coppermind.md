## Whimsy

**Whimsy** is a [Shard](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shard "Shard") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium") that has not been [Splintered](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Splinter#Formation "Splinter") and whose Vessel is unknown.[^1]

## Intent

Very little is known about Whimsy's Intent, outside it not being congruous with intense planning and ambitions.[^2] It is also not calculating nor deliberate.[^3]

## History

Like all Shards, Whimsy was created at the [Shattering](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shattering "Shattering") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium"), and picked up by an unknown Vessel.[^4] It is not known what Whimsy did directly after their Ascension, or where they settled, if they settled at all.

Some time around the [True Desolation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/True_Desolation "True Desolation"), [Harmony](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Harmony "Harmony"), by [Hoid's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoid "Hoid") request, reached out to Whimsy about the threat posed by [Odium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Odium "Odium"), but their exchange left him with the impression that Whimsy would not be particularly useful.[^5] [^1] Members of the [Set](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Set "Set") were aware of the existence of Whimsy.[^2]

## Trivia

- [Hoid](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoid "Hoid") would not be happy to take up the Whimsy Shard.[^3]
- Whimsy is the most dangerous Shard to a person's sense of decorum and self-worth.[^6]
- In *[Magic: The Gathering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic:_The_Gathering "wikipedia:Magic: The Gathering")* colors, Whimsy would be mono-red.[^7]
