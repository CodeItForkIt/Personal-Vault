## Reason

**Reason** is one of the sixteen [Shards of Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shard "Shard"). The original Vessel of the Shard is [Euridrius](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Euridrius "Euridrius").[^1]

They originally wanted only to hide from and survive the conflicts of other Shards;[^2] this desire is only tangentially related to their Intent and comes from their knowledge of events in the cosmere.[^3] They managed to hide well enough that other Shards, like [Honor](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honor "Honor"), were unable to find them.[^1] However, they have realized over time that this desire may not be the most important thing.[^4] [^5]

## Trivia

- Reason's existence was first hinted at in 2011, when Brandon responded to the question "Give us the name of a Shard's Intent we have not seen before" with "there is one who just wants to hide and survive." [^2] Their Intent was eventually revealed thirteen years later, in *[Wind and Truth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wind_and_Truth "Wind and Truth")*, and was the last Shardic Intent to be revealed. Before their Intent was revealed, fans often referred to them as "the Survival Shard."
