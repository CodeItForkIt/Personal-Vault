## Polestone

The **Polestones** are the ten varieties of gemstone that naturally hold [[Lights|Light]]. They also serve as the basis of [[Spheres - The Coppermind|spheres]], the basic Rosharan money system.[^2]

## Attributes

### Composition

Some Polestones are the same type of crystal but with different color and chemical signatures. Sapphire and ruby are both types of corundum. Smokestone and amethyst are both types of quartz. Emerald and heliodor are both types of beryl. Much like how [[Iron - The Coppermind|iron]] and [[Steel - The Coppermind|steel]], despite being very similar chemically, are different metals in the [[Metallic Arts]], the exact color and chemical signatures are enough to differentiate the Polestones. As a result, an off-color Polestone acts much like an impure alloy in [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]].[^3] If a Polestone was drained of its color by an [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakener]], it would still hold Stormlight but would have the properties of a dusty or cloudy quartz.[^4] [^5] If a spren was trapped inside, they would be released.[^6]

If sung to, while holding Light, Polestones will break.[^7]

### Light and Surgebinding

All Lights can be stored in gems. How long a given quantity of Light will remain in a specific gem depends on the type of Light and the quality of the gem.[^8] The rate at which a Light leaks from gemstones is not constant either - it appears slow at first, but accelerates rapidly near the end, making gems that begin to dim, dim *fast.*[^9] Regardless of the type of Light, perfect gems are said to be able to hold it indefinitely, but they are extremely rare.[^10]

Although the type of Polestone Stormlight is stored in is irrelevant for most [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]], Polestones do have an important impact on Soulcasting. Namely, they determine what kind of substances a Soulcaster can transmute objects into.[^11] It is believed to be impossible to create Polestones via Soulcasting.[^12]

### Spren

In addition to the ability to hold Stormlight, Polestones are able to trap [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]]. The type of spren that can be trapped depends on the Polestone used and its cut. This quality serves as the basis for [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]].[^13] Each Polestone is associated with one of the [Ten Essences](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ten_Essences "Ten Essences") and with an Order of [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]].[^14] However, there appears to be no functional difference between Polestones apart from [[Soulcasting - The Coppermind|Soulcasting]] and fabrial creation.

If a Polestone is set in the pommel of a deceased [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") it allows the blade to be bonded, whereby it can be summoned and dismissed.[^15] Synthetic versions of the Polestones could also be used in fabrials.[^16]

## The Ten Polestones

### Sapphire

**Sapphire** is associated with the Essence of Zephyr and the [[Order of Windrunners - The Coppermind|Order of Windrunners]]. They are used to Soulcast translucent gases, like air.[^14] The gemhearts of [[Mawli - The Coppermind|mawli]] are sapphire, a major export of [Azir](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Azir "Azir").[^17]

Sapphire spheres are given the "sky-" prefix.[^18]

### Smokestone

**Smokestone** [^1] is associated with the Essence of Vapor and the [[Order of Skybreakers - The Coppermind|Order of Skybreakers]]. They are used to Soulcast opaque gases, like smoke and fog.[^14]

Smokestones are used in a type of fabrial clock.[^20]

### Ruby

**Ruby** is associated with the Essence of Spark and the [[Order of Dustbringers - The Coppermind|Order of Dustbringers]]. They are used to Soulcast fire.[^14]

Rubies are used in the production of [[Fabrial - The Coppermind#Pairing fabrials|conjoiner fabrials]], where a single infused ruby is split in two. Once activated the two halves will mirror each others movements, lift one and the other will also rise.[^14] [Spanreeds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spanreed "Spanreed") are a common application of this phenomenon.[^21]

Rubies are also used in a kind of heat producing fabrial.[^22] They are also used in the construction of a [painrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Painrial "Painrial"), a pain reducing fabrial, designed by [Longshadow](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Longshadow "Longshadow") and adapted by [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani Kholin]].[^23] [^24] A ruby was also part of the [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] at the center of the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]].[^25]

Ruby spheres are given the "fire-" prefix.[^2]

The [King's Drop](https://coppermind.net/wiki/King%27s_Drop "King's Drop"), a large perfect gemstone that was possibly part of the [Stone of Ten Dawns](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stone_of_Ten_Dawns "Stone of Ten Dawns") and used to imprison the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] [Nergaoul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nergaoul "Nergaoul"), is a ruby.[^26]

### Diamond

**Diamond** is associated with the Essence of Lucentia and the [[Order of Edgedancers - The Coppermind|Order of Edgedancers]]. They are used to Soulcast crystals, like quartz and glass.[^14]

Diamonds are used in a kind of light producing fabrial.[^25]

Diamond spheres are given the "clear-" prefix.[^27]

### Emerald

**Emerald** is associated with the Essence of Pulp and the [[Order of Truthwatchers - The Coppermind|Order of Truthwatchers]]. They are used to Soulcast plant material, like wood, moss, and grain.[^14] The gemhearts of [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]] are emerald.

Emeralds can be used to capture flamespren.[^13]

### Garnet

**Garnet** is associated with the Essence of Blood and the [[Order of Lightweavers - The Coppermind|Order of Lightweavers]]. They are used to Soulcast the eight types of blood and all other non-oil liquids.[^14]

Garnets are used in a type of water attracting fabrial.[^28] They are also used in ancient [suppression fabrials](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Suppression_fabrial "Suppression fabrial").[^29]

Garnet spheres are given the "blood-" prefix.[^30]

### Zircon

**Zircon** is associated with the Essence of Tallow and the [[Order of Elsecallers - The Coppermind|Order of Elsecallers]]. They are used to Soulcast oils.[^14] The gemhearts of [yu-nerigs](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Yu-nerig "Yu-nerig") are zircon.[^31]

### Amethyst

**Amethyst** is associated with the Essence of Foil and the [[Order of Willshapers - The Coppermind|Order of Willshapers]]. They are used to Soulcast metals.[^14]

Amethysts are used in the production of [[Fabrial - The Coppermind#Pairing fabrials|reverser fabrials]], where a single infused amethyst is split in two. Once activated the two halves will inversely mirror each other's movements, lift one and the other will be lowered.[^14]

While in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]], the Unmade [[Yelig-nar - The Coppermind|Yelig-nar]] inhabits a amethyst gem.[^32]

### Topaz

**Topaz** is associated with the Essence of Talus and the [[Order of Stonewards - The Coppermind|Order of Stonewards]]. They are used to Soulcast rock and stone.[^14]

A topaz was observed as part of a fabrial replicating Regrowth, an application of the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind#Progression|Surge of Progression]].[^33]

### Heliodor

**Heliodor** is associated with the Essence of Sinew and the [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Order of Bondsmiths]]. They are used to Soulcast animal material, like meat and flesh.[^14] The gemheart of [[Gumfrem - The Coppermind|gumfrems]] are heliodor.[^34]

Heliodors are used in the production of [[Fabrial - The Coppermind#Warning fabrials|alerter fabrials]]. These fabrials can be used to detect a variety of stimuli, such as people and emotions, and give notice to its user.[^35] The larger the heliodor, and as a result the more Stormlight that can be infused in it, the wider the fabrial's range is.[^14]

A heliodor was observed as part of a fabrial replicating Regrowth, an application of the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind#Progression|Surge of Progression]].[^33]

A perfect heliodor was used to imprison the Unmade [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]].[^36]

## Obtaining

While Polestones are produced via mining, a significant source of them on Roshar is the harvesting of the [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]] that grow inside [[Greatshell - The Coppermind|greatshells]]. Larger species of greatshell, like [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]], produce gemhearts of great size, which are incredibly useful in Soulcasting as a larger stone is less likely to break from the strain.[^37] This trait however has led to many species of greatshells, such as the [[Lancer - The Coppermind|lanceryn]], to go extinct.[^38] The exact method by which gemhearts are grown is not understood, though it is known to be caused by a leak from the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]] into the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical]], and is analogous, though not identical, to the production of [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]] in the [Pits of Hathsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pits_of_Hathsin "Pits of Hathsin") on [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^39] Although they may have flaws, gemheart Polestones have virtually no impurities, unlike their conventionally mined counterparts.[^40]

## Perfect Gemstones

**Perfect gemstones** occur when one of the Polestones forms without any flaws or imperfections which allows them to hold light indefinitely.[^41] The money exchangers living in major cities of [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar") use perfect stones to store the Stormlight they use for currency.[^42] The [King's Drop](https://coppermind.net/wiki/King%27s_Drop "King's Drop") is a perfect gemstone that has been held in the [Thaylen Gemstone Reserve](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_Gemstone_Reserve "Thaylen Gemstone Reserve") for over two hundred years without being renewed or losing Stormlight.[^41] In addition to not leaking, a perfect gemstone can hold far more Stormlight than a normal gemstone of the same size could.[^43] The [[Order of Elsecallers - The Coppermind|Elsecallers]] were tasked with protecting perfect gems, including a ruby named [Honor's Drop](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honor%27s_Drop "Honor's Drop").[^44] The [honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren") have gathered many perfect gemstones in their stronghold [Lasting Integrity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lasting_Integrity "Lasting Integrity").[^43] [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin]] believes that there are far fewer perfect gemstones around in the Physical Realm than there should be, suggesting that someone may have been gathering them up before their value was more widely known;[^45] possibly the [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]] as they are experimenting with transporting Stormlight off-world.[^10]

Perfect gemstones have the capacity to hold [[Cognitive Shadow - The Coppermind|Cognitive Shadows]] in addition to spren, the latter of which they have a far larger capacity than normal gemstones--they can hold [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald"), the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]], and even [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]].[^26] [^46] [^47] They could also capture some of the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] or the sunlight from [[Taldain - The Coppermind|Taldain]].[^48]

## Transfer

It is possible to transfer Light from a filled, small gemstone to a large and empty gemstone of its type by holding the filled gemstone to the empty one. This method of transfer is slow and inefficient; however, the [[Thaylenah - The Coppermind|Thaylens]] have discovered a more efficient way of transferring Stormlight from one gemstone to another. By using a tuning fork of a specific frequency, a person can touch an infused gemstone and then touch the fork to the empty gemstone for a rapid mode of transfer. With the Thaylen method, the type of Polestone does not matter, and light can be transferred from any type to any type. The decay rate is unknown, as is the distance the tuning fork can be brought before transfer is impossible.[^49]

Surgebinders are also able to infuse Polestones regardless of the type, and can do so very quickly.[^50] [^51]

## Roshar Economy

Gemstones form the basis for the Roshar economy. Gemstone weight is measured in kivs.[^49] When used as currency, the gems are encased in glass [[Spheres - The Coppermind|spheres]]. Although the spheres are of uniform size, the gemstones inside are of three sizes.[^2] In descending order of size (and value), these gem sizes are broam, mark, and chip.[^2] In addition, the value of the “sphere” is determined by the type of gemstone, which have different values as determined by their utility rather than their rarity.[^52]. Their relative value in descending order is as follows: emerald; amethyst and sapphire; ruby, smokestone, and zircon; garnet, heliodor, and topaz; and diamond.[^53] [^54]
