## Vax

> “ *Ati ran his hand through his red hair, then looked about. “Vax?” he said, sounding confused.* ”

**Vax** is a planet in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]].[^2]

Little is known of Vax, its peoples, or any Shards that may or may not be located there. All that is certain is that it has a manifestation of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] in which people are Initiated differently than on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]], [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]], [[Nalthis - The Coppermind|Nalthis]], or [[Taldain - The Coppermind|Taldain]].[^3]

On Scadrial, when [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] is killed by [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] while she is holding [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]], [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati's]] soul is finally freed from [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] and appears in the Cognitive Realm. Right before moving on to the Beyond, Ati looks around Scadrial's subastral and seems to wonder aloud if it is Vax.[^1] [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] would recognize Vax.[^4] [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss]] is aware of Vax and how people are Initiated on or within it.[^3]

For a time, it was not clear whether Vax was a planet, or simply a place.[^5] [^6] [^3]

"True Vaxilians," presumably people from Vax, are a mortal race distinct from humans and [[Sho Del - The Coppermind|Sho Del]]. They, like humans and Sho Del, are said to fear the [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]].[^7] [Eddlin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Eddlin "Eddlin") is partially a Vaxilian, but mostly human.

## Trivia

- Brandon refuses to say anything about Vax.[^8] [^9] [^10] [^11] [^12]
- If Vax is inhabited, they have not yet developed vaccines.[^6]
