## Lancer

The **lanceryn** (singular: lancer) are a type of [[Greatshell - The Coppermind|greatshell]] native to [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]].[^1]

The lanceryn were originally the **Ancient Guardians** or **Guardians of Ancient Sins**,[^2] tasked with the keeping of the [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshard]] underneath [Akinah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Akinah "Akinah"). Like all greatshells, the lanceryn were enormous. It is likely they could fly and bonded [mandras](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mandra "Mandra"). They, like the modern larkin, had the ability to feed on [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. At the fall of their species, the [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] took over their role protecting the Dawnshard. Some hordelings had bred with lanceryn prior to their fall, thus passing on the ability to feed on Investiture, albeit in a limited manner.[^3]

It is commonly believed that the lanceryn died off during the [[Scouring of Aimia - The Coppermind|scouring of Aimia]], to the dismay of those who had harvested their [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]]. They were the last creature known to produce gemhearts of large size prior to the discovery of the [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]] on the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]].[^1] However, it appears that the lanceryn live on in some way in [[Larkin - The Coppermind|larkin]].[^4] [^5] The [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] [Nikliasorm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nikliasorm "Nikliasorm") believes that [Chiri-Chiri](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chiri-Chiri "Chiri-Chiri"), a larkin, is an Ancient Guardian in her own right. It also says that lanceryn are now called larkin.[^3] They are "kind of the same species." [^5] Although there is some relationship between the two, it is unclear as of yet exactly what that relationship is.

In the undersea caverns of Aimia, there is a room containing six greatshell skulls made of carapace, presumably belonging to lanceryn. They appear to turn to face [[Rysn Ftori - The Coppermind|Rysn]] while she is in a nearby cavern.[^3]
