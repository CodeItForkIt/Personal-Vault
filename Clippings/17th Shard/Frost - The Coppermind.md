## Frost

> “ *Our interference so far has brought nothing but pain. My path has been chosen very deliberately.* ”

**Frost** is a [dragon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\) "Dragon (cosmere)") from [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]].[^5] He was present at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]],[^6] and has been corresponding with [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] through [letters](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Letters "Letters").[^7]

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *Please, hearken to my plea. Leave that place and join me in my oath of nonintervention. The cosmere itself may depend upon our restraint.* ”

When in his human form, Frost is pale-skinned with a long silver beard. He appears to be around sixty years old, and dresses in white and silver robes with a conical headdress. His draconic appearance is currently unknown.

Frost is a caring, empathetic dragon, who teaches his [niece](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illistandrista "Illistandrista") to always see the best in others. Like most dragons, he dedicates a significant portion of his time to listening to the prayers of his followers. He is also quite playful and teases Starling.[^2]

Frost has lived through significant events in the cosmere and has adopted a non-interference policy.[^10] He's sarcastic and insulting towards Hoid,[^11] [^8] although it is clear that their personal relationship is complicated.[^12]

## Attributes and Abilities

Like all dragons, Frost is functionally immortal, meaning that he does not age, but he can be killed.[^13] [^14] He is the oldest known character in the cosmere, even older than Hoid.[^15] He has gained a second form of immortality on top of his natural dragon immortality through unknown means.[^16]

Dragons are also known to have the abilities of flight [^17] and shapeshifting, including the ability to appear human.[^18]

While not a [[17th Shard|worldhopper]], he is still highly knowledgeable about the cosmere.[^19] He is associated with the [[Seventeenth Shard - The Coppermind|Seventeenth Shard]],[^3] a group that shares his nonintervention policy.[^20] He is able to keep track of current events in the cosmere through the Seventeenth Shard, correspondence, or other means. Among other facts, he knows that Hoid possesses a bead of [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]] from [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]],[^21] [^22] that [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] is trapped in the [Rosharan system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rosharan_system "Rosharan system"),[^23] and that a gemstone of Hoid's (presumably the [First Gem](https://coppermind.net/wiki/First_Gem "First Gem")) is "dead".[^24] Despite the existence of the [Silverlight Mercantile](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Silverlight_Mercantile "Silverlight Mercantile") interplanetary postal service, this was not the method Hoid and Frost used to exchange letters, meaning that they had some other means of contacting one another.[^25]

Hoid considers Frost to be one of the best contract negotiators he knows, and a person of good judgement.[^1]

### Immortality

Frost's current mortality status is ambiguous. As a dragon, he is naturally ageless; immortal in the weak sense that he could live forever absent outside intervention. However, Hoid says "as you are **now** essentially immortal",[^26] he must be immortal in *some* sense stronger than that. But contravening that, he still can be killed.[^13] Frost is both sufficiently difficult to kill that Hoid would consider him "essentially immortal", but still possible to kill. What this means is not clear.

## History

Little is known of Frost's history even though he is truly ancient. He is slightly older than Hoid,[^15] and both of them are known to have lived on [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]]. In their correspondence, Frost and Hoid seemed to know each other well, and Frost hinted that they had worked together in the past.[^4] They also exhibited a shared personal knowledge of a number of the original sixteen [[Shards|Vessels]] who perpetrated the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering of Adonalsium]] on Yolen and took up the power of the [[Shards]].[^27] [^28] Specifically, the letters mentioned [[Rayse - The Coppermind|Rayse]], [[Bavadin - The Coppermind|Bavadin]], [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona]], [[Skai - The Coppermind|Skai]], [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati]], and [[Tanavast - The Coppermind|Tanavast]] by name, and Frost referenced "the sixteen".[^29] He was present at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]].[^6]

[Cakoban](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cakoban "Cakoban") knew Frost in some way, and Frost was there when Cakoban challenged the [Dakwara](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dakwara "Dakwara").[^30] Frost left a message at Cakoban's grave on the [Dakwara's island](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dakwara%27s_island "Dakwara's island"), stating that he was too late to save Cakoban, but that the man died with honor.[^31]

In Hoid's initial letter to Frost, it seemed that the two had not spoken in some time; Hoid was not sure if Frost was fully aware of the [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintering]] of [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] [^26] even though several millenia had passed since that event.[^29] Hoid begged Frost for his support, implying that Frost still had clout in the cosmere.[^32] Frost rebuffed Hoid, emphasizing his nonintervention policy, and referred to their past actions as having caused more harm than good.[^4] Although Frost seemed to agree with Hoid's assessment that Odium was dangerous,[^33] he did not understand Hoid's level of concern [^34] since Odium was trapped in the [Rosharan system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rosharan_system "Rosharan system").[^23] The two were diametrically opposed on the best way to save the cosmere, and Frost entreated Hoid to abandon his quest; he felt that this was the only way to ensure that Hoid would not make things worse with his meddling.[^24]

Frost still lived on Yolen during Roshar's [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]].[^5] While he would not assist Hoid with analyzing a Rosharan contract when contacted about it by [[Tamukek - The Coppermind|tamukek]], his sister did agree.[^1]

Frost raised his niece, [Starling](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starling "Starling"), though it is unclear what exactly happened to her parents.[^2] His current location is unknown, and many assume him to be dead.[^35] Starling and Hoid believe that he is still alive.[^36] [^31]

## Relationships

### Starling

> “ *You can never be ruined, child. Never, ever.* ”
> 
> —Frost to Starling on the day of her transformation [^2]

Illistandrista, or Starling, is Frost's niece, most likely by his sister. Starling's parents died when she was young, and she was raised by her uncle. Frost teaches her how to empathise with mortals, in preparation for when she will have followers of her own. The two are very close, and Starling looks up to her uncle a lot. He encouraged her apprenticeship with Hoid. Though Frost does not intervene in cosmere events, he appreciates that Hoid acts with care for those weaker than him.[^37] Frost worried that Hoid would get Starling into trouble, but allowed the apprenticeship regardless.[^36]

### Hoid

Frost and Hoid have known each other for a very long time, and Starling describes the two as friends. Frost and Hoid have very opposing views, but remain in contact. Hoid speaks fondly of Frost, and seeks his assistance when trapped in a vault.[^36]

## Trivia

- It is likely that there are characters older than Frost in the cosmere, but they have not been canonized.[^38]
- Frost first appeared in the unpublished novel *[Dragonsteel Prime](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragonsteel_Prime "Dragonsteel Prime")*, some of which can be read on Brandon's website.[^39] A series of *[Dragonsteel](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragonsteel_\(series\) "Dragonsteel (series)")* novels are still in Brandon's long-term plans for the cosmere, and would likely include Frost.[^40]
- In the original Dragonsteel Prime, Frost was posing as a human, and Hoid was able to prove he was a dragon by pranking him using a [[Tamukek - The Coppermind|Tamu Kek]].[^41]
