## Core Archipelago

The **Core Archipelago** is an island chain in the [[Emerald Sea - The Coppermind|Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]].[^1] It is the home of the [Verdant King](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Verdant_King "Verdant King"), who lives in a palace on one of the islands,[^2] presumably in the city of [[Kingsport - The Coppermind|Kingsport]].

## Geography

The archipelago is a good distance from [Shimmerbay](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shimmerbay "Shimmerbay") [^3] and far from [Diggen's Point](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diggen%27s_Point "Diggen's Point").[^2] [^4] The number of islands and their size are both unknown, but [Huck](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Huck "Huck") implies that some islands are as large as forty miles wide.[^5]

## History

The [[Charlie's father - The Coppermind|duke]] of Diggen's Point would periodically visit the king to report on his territory.[^6] A "cursed" [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] claimed to have visited the archipelago during his time on Lumar.[^7]

The archipelago was the claimed destination of the *[Oot's Dream](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Oot%27s_Dream "Oot's Dream")* after it forged a king's writ to buy six kegs of [Brick's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brick "Brick") ale on Diggen's Point.[^1] [^8] [[Tress - The Coppermind|Tress]] posed as a royal inspector hoping to gain passage to the king's island on the ship as part of her plan to free [[Charlie - The Coppermind|Charlie]].[^2]
