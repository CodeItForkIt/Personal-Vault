## Midpeace

**Midpeace** is a period that marks the middle of the [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Rosharan]] year.[^1]

The Rosharan year is 500 days long [^2] and ends with a four week period of rain known as the [[Weeping - The Coppermind|Weeping]].[^3] Midpeace occurs at the midpoint between Weepings.[^1] [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|Highstorms]] occur more frequently as Midpeace approaches and after it ends,[^4] but they do not occur at all during Midpeace.[^1] Although the exact duration of Midpeace is not mentioned, it is the longest sustained period on Roshar without rain; since "natural" storms that are not highstorms or the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] are rare,[^5] many rivers typically dry up by the end of Midpeace.[^6]

Residents of Roshar use the fair weather of Midpeace to celebrate and travel.[^7] [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar"), [Jah Keved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jah_Keved "Jah Keved"), and possibly other nations hold outdoor festivals known as [[Middlefest Fair - The Coppermind|Middlefest Fairs]].[^1] [[Yokska - The Coppermind|Yokska]] mentions traveling to [[Liafor - The Coppermind|Liafor]] during Midpeace to see the latest fashion trends.[^8]
