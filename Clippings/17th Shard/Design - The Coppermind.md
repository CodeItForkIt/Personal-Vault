## Design

> “ *Design!... What did I tell you about spoiling the ending of stories!”*
> 
> *“Something stupid, so I forgot it!*”
> 
> —Hoid and Design [^1]

**Design** is a [Cryptic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cryptic "Cryptic") from [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] bonded to [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]].[^2]

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *I get annoyed easily! It's endearing... to everyone but him!* ”

### Appearance

Like other Cryptics, Design is a creature with stiff robes and a shifting pattern for a head. Her head pattern is lacy and intricate, and seems to be more fine and graceful than [[Pattern - The Coppermind|Pattern's]]. She has a musical, feminine voice. She can flare out her pattern, which is her way of giving a bow.[^1]

When living on [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]], she took the form of an attractive Nagadan woman who appeared to be in her early twenties, with a full form and long white hair. This was achieved via a Lightweaving illusion.[^3] She specifically asked for a striking and voluptuous figure, both as a way to be particularly disturbing to people if she ever reveals her true appearance, and because she likes the appearance of human curves.[^3]

### Personality

Design is cheerful and energetic. She gets annoyed easily and considers that to be endearing. She does not mind spoiling the end of stories and considers surprises to be dumb,[^1] unless, it appears, the surprise is for the purposes of playing a prank on someone.[^3]

Design enjoys annoying Hoid, poking holes in his stories and tricks in front of others. She happily points out when Hoid makes a mistake. She likes getting into arguments with Hoid.[^1] [^4] However, she is not a great match for Hoid and both bring out negative aspects of the others personality. She and Elhokar would have gotten along excellently, though.[^5]

She dislikes people being overly formal and reverent with her, and she actively tells off those that try to worship her.[^6] Design sometimes spins her pattern a little more vibrantly as a way of acknowledgement.[^7]

She does not see herself as female, though she does enjoy presenting herself in a female form.[^6]

## Abilities and Attributes

Being a Cryptic, Design is able to form [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bonds]] with humans. This gives the bonded human access to the [Surges](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Surges "Surges") of the [[Order of Lightweavers - The Coppermind|Order of Lightweavers]], namely [Illumination](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illumination "Illumination") and [Transformation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Transformation "Transformation"). Once the bonded has stated enough truths to be a full Radiant, she is able to become a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade"). By the time she took up residence on [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]], Design felt she could serve as "an encouragingly mediocre sword".[^8]

Design also has the ability to manipulate [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]] to a certain degree. She uses this to lengthen the connection between [[Nikaro - The Coppermind|Nikaro's]] spirit form and his body, allowing his spirit to move further away.[^9]

## History

> “ *I will give you truths. And I know some juicy ones.* ”
> 
> —Hoid to Design [^2]

### Roshar

In the months or years leading up to his death, King [[Elhokar Kholin - The Coppermind|Elhokar Kholin]] had been seeing [Cryptics](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cryptic "Cryptic"), in their [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar") form, in mirrors.[^10] He seems to have also spotted a [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical]] form of a Cryptic at some point, possibly Design.[^11] While Elhokar was seeing Cryptics in mirrors for a while, he only began saying the [First Ideal](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Immortal_Words#The_First_Ideal "Immortal Words") just before he died.[^12]

Design was later rescued from the [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] in [Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar "Kholinar") by [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], who found her near the location Elhokar died. Hoid then bonded her.[^13] [^14]

Design was with Hoid when [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] took [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin's]] consciousness to [[Braize - The Coppermind|Braize]]. Design played Hoid's flute while he told the story "The Dog and the Dragon". Design was able to play the flute despite not having lips, moving her fingers randomly and simultaneously holding a conversation with Hoid. She spoiled the ending of the story to Kaladin, which was appalling to Hoid. She argued with Hoid and provided a counterpoint to many things he said throughout the story.[^1]

Design had a conversation with Hoid in Elhokar's old palace before Hoid confronted Odium. She argued with Hoid about [Lightweaving](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illumination "Illumination"). She went away with some corrupted [windspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Windspren "Windspren") when Hoid told her to bother someone else. She was not present when Hoid confronted the new Odium. When Hoid repeated his performance after confronting Odium, he had forgotten that Design already left.[^4]

Following the [[Contest of champions - The Coppermind|contest of champions]], [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]] came for Hoid and vaporized him. Anticipating that something like that could happen, he had set up unspecified protections on Design; upon his revival on [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] soon after, he confirmed he could still Lightweave, but would be unable to summon Design as a shardblade. Design was thus left alone on Roshar for some time.[^15]

### Worldhopping

> “ *Besides, I’m on vacation. No worshipping the bits of God when they’re on vacation. It’s a rule I just made up.* ”
> 
> —Design [^6]

Eventually, she and Hoid reunited and managed to escape [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] to travel the cosmere, with some difficulty.[^6] [^16] At some point they found a way to have Design appear and interact as if she were human, using "a complicated wireframe Lightweaving with force projections attached directly to her cognitive element as it manifested in the physical realm." [^3] They went to [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnody]],[^6] and later went to the planet [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]], where Hoid found himself frozen in stasis. Design took advantage of this 'vacation' to set up a successful noodle shop called the [[Noodle Pupil - The Coppermind|Noodle Pupil]], using Hoid's immobilized body as a coat rack.[^3] [^9]

She developed a cordial relationship with [[Nikaro - The Coppermind|Painter]], who frequented her restaurant,[^3] along with his co-worker [[Masaka - The Coppermind|Masaka]], whom Design recognized as a secret [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|sleepless]] and thus felt protective of.[^17] Later, she came to help Painter and [[Yumi - The Coppermind|Yumi]] with their predicament, and educated them on basic [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realmatic Theory]] to help explain to them what was happening [^6] [^9] along with using the [aura fabrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aura_fabrial "Aura fabrial") to analyze Yumi's spiritweb and Investiture.[^8] After the pair freed the [[Hijo - The Coppermind|hijo]] from the [[Father machine - The Coppermind|father machine]], Hoid found himself restored, and he and Design plotted to escape Komashi aboard one of its spacecraft.[^18]

Design accompanied Hoid to the [Spirit Shores](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spirit_Shores "Spirit Shores") when he met there with [Illistandrista](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illistandrista "Illistandrista"), though she did not speak during this encounter.[^7]
