## Shattering

> “ *Sixteen people, together, killed Adonalsium, ripping it apart and dividing its essence between them, becoming the first who Ascended.* ”
> 
> — [Khriss](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khriss "Khriss") on the Shattering [^1]

The **Shattering of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]]** (or simply the **Shattering**) was a pivotal moment in the history of the [[17th Shard|cosmere]]. Adonalsium, an entity of immense power considered to be the creator of the cosmere,[^2] was killed using the [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshards]].[^1] [^3] [^4] It was shattered into what became known as [[Shards]] of Adonalsium, sixteen godlike powers that have been the dominant influence in the cosmere since they were taken up by [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolish]] co-conspirators in the plot.[^5] The Shattering took place over ten thousand years before the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]].[^6] [^7] [^8]

## Prior to the Shattering

It is not clear how different the cosmere was prior to the Shattering, but the Shattering did have an effect on the magic.[^9] An example is the [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorms]],[^10] natural phenomena that are central to the life cycle of many of the native inhabitants of Roshar; the highstorms were later infused with the power of Honor.

> “ *The words that proclaim, in bald-faced arrogance, "I don't trust you to make your own decisions." (..) I said them with sixteen other people, in fact.* ”
> 
> — [Midius](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Midius "Midius") [^13]

For a list of all Shards, including current and past Vessels, see [[Shards|Known Shards]].

The people who killed Adonalsium on Yolen [^14] all had different motivations, including a desire for power and a belief in the necessity of the action.[^1] [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] was there at the Shattering and was offered a Shard, but he refused.[^15] [^16] Hoid participated in the plan to kill Adonalsium,[^11] although at one point [[Tanavast - The Coppermind|Tanavast]] expressed that only he and fifteen other participants did "something terrible" on Yolen.[^17] [[Frost - The Coppermind|Frost]] was also present;[^12] his role (if any) is unclear, but he later seems to express regret about causing destruction in a letter to Hoid.[^18]

According to Tanavast, Adonalsium did not fight back, and let itself be Shattered. He assumed it was done in order to prevent the destruction that could come to be if it did fight back.[^19]

Almost all of the Investiture in the cosmere was divided between and became associated with a Shard,[^20] with some exceptions like the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 17|Aethers]] and the Dawnshards.[^21] [^3] [^22] All of the sixteen Shards were created at once, and they all started with the same amount of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]].[^23] [^24] [^25]

It was not random which Vessel got which Shard;[^26] some chose Shards that they were particularly Connected with, while others chose Shards that they believed they could control well.[^27] It is possible for Adonalsium to have Shattered into different Shards than the Shards currently in existence.[^28]

Following the Shattering, the Vessels spread across the cosmere, Investing into various planets, with Ruin and Preservation themselves creating the planet of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^29]

Various Shards fought amongst themselves for various reasons, leading to the death of Vessels and Splintering of the Shards. In particular, this applies to Devotion, Dominion, and Ambition.[^23] [^30] [^31] All of these Splinterings had strong and lasting effects upon the planets they were Invested within, and are also largely the result of Odium having gone to kill every Shard he could.[^23] [^30] [^32] [^33] [^34] Ruin and Preservation were also later fused into one Vessel after the death of their previous holders, creating the hybrid Shard, [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]].[^35] Honor and Odium were also later fused into one Vessel creating the hybrid Shard, [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]].

### Reasoning

The reasoning for the Shattering differs between each individual involved. Some wanted Adonalsium's power, while others felt it was necessary.[^36]

- **Koravellium Avast** participated in the Shattering due to the belief that one god could not supply the needs of everyone, thus necessitating the existence of a plethora of deities to truly meet everyone's needs.[^37]
- **Tanavast** believed that the Shattering was for Adonalsium's own good.[^8]
