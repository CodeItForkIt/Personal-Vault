## Expulsion

The **Expulsion** is an ancient event in pre- [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Rosharan]] history.

## Vorin Doctrine

In [[Vorinism - The Coppermind|Vorin]] mythology, it marks the defeat of the population of the [[Tranquiline Halls - The Coppermind|Tranquiline Halls]] and their exile to [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] by the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]].[^1] Following the Expulsion, the Voidbringers followed mankind to Roshar and the [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]] began. It is unclear why the humans lost the war in the Tranquiline Halls or how they traveled from there to Roshar.

Arrival in Roshar

The humans who first settled on Roshar arrived from [[Ashyn - The Coppermind|Ashyn]] --another inner planet in the [Rosharan system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rosharan_system "Rosharan system").[^3] While the exact reason for their migration is unknown, [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] implied that they needed a new home after destroying their previous planet with the [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshards]].[^4] Firestorms had raged across the surface of Ashyn and the very stones melted in pools of lava, due to altercation between an empire made by Odium's favoured people with access to Surges and an opposing Kingdom set up by people who had been granted the power to resist the imperialist nation. According to Honor, the sky burned. The [Eila Stele](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Eila_Stele "Eila Stele"), an ancient [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] text, claimed that the humans were welcomed to Roshar by their gods despite access to "dangerous powers, of [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] and [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]]." The stele further claimed that the humans had betrayed them, as subjects of their own god-- [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], and ultimately implied that the humans themselves were the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]], come to conquer Roshar.[^5] The myth of the Expulsion seems to be based on these events.

Ishar, who had some access to Elsecaller-like abilities opened a portal to Roshar following a tone.

Rats could make the transition, but dogs, due to their sparse numbers, died out after a few generations.
