## Obrodai

> “ *You should not return to Obrodai. We have claimed that world, and a new avatar of our being is beginning to manifest there.* ”

**Obrodai** is a [[17th Shard#Planets|planet]] in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]].[^3]

The [[Shards|Shard]] [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]] has claimed Obrodai and is beginning to manifest an "avatar of \[their\] being," an unknown female entity, on the planet.[^2] [^1] It is unknown whether there are any other Shards active on Obrodai.

In a [letter](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Letters#Second_Oathbringer_Letter "Letters") to [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], [Patji](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Patji_\(being\) "Patji (being)"), an avatar of Autonomy, warned him not to return to Obrodai and that, as a precaution to discourage his return, the avatar there has been instilled with a great dislike of him.[^1] [^4]

Due to Hoid being able to go to the planet, it likely has some form of [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]].

## Trivia

- When asked if Obrodai was a name one of the planets of the [Drominad System](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Drominad_System "Drominad System") used for themselves, Brandon responded with a [RAFO](https://coppermind.net/wiki/RAFO "RAFO").[^5] He has also avoided saying if it is a planet that we have already seen.[^6]
