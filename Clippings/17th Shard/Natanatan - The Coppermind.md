## Natanatan

**Natanatan** was one of the [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver Kingdoms]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs - The Coppermind|Heraldic Epochs]].[^2]

## Geography

### Silver Kingdom

Natanatan encompassed the southeast corner of the Rosharan continent. It bordered [[Alethela - The Coppermind|Alethela]] to the north, with the border running through a prominent mountain range to the [Sea of Spears](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sea_of_Spears "Sea of Spears"). It bordered [[Thalath - The Coppermind|Thalath]] to the west, and had a long coastline to the south and east.[^2] The capital was the city of [[Stormseat - The Coppermind|Stormseat]],[^1] which contained an [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]].[^3]

### Modern Roshar

In current-day Roshar, the land that formerly comprised Natanatan now includes the [[Unclaimed Hills - The Coppermind|Unclaimed Hills]], the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]], the [Eastern Crownlands](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Eastern_Crownlands "Eastern Crownlands") of [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar"), and the eastern [Frostlands](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Frostlands "Frostlands").[^2] [^4] The name "Natanatan" is still commonly used to describe the former nation's lands, which remain largely unexplored by both humans and [[Listener - The Coppermind|listeners]].[^5] A few settlements remain; the ruins of Stormseat were repurposed as Narak by listeners, the city-state of [[New Natanan - The Coppermind|New Natanan]] is the last remnant of the former kingdom,[^6] and [Dawn's Shadow](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dawn%27s_Shadow "Dawn's Shadow") and The [Shallow Crypts](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shallow_Crypts "Shallow Crypts") lie on the coast.[^4] Native Natans also live scattered throughout their former nation.[^7]

Natanatan's former territory is generally thought of as an "empty wasteland" [^8] with many forests and jungles.[^9] [[Gavilar Kholin - The Coppermind|Gavilar]] and [[Eshonai - The Coppermind|Eshonai]] each recall expeditions into the Unclaimed Hills in their youth.[^7] [^9] The strength of the highstorms makes sailing the east coast of Natanatan in the [Ocean of Origins](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ocean_of_Origins "Ocean of Origins") extremely dangerous,[^10] although not unheard of.[^11]

## History

As one of the Silver Kingdoms, Natanatan played a prominent role in the history of Roshar, but many of the details about it have been lost.[^12] Natanatan was the last of the Silver Kingdoms to be established, and its Oathgate the tenth to be built.[^13] One of [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar's]] first visions, in which he fights off [Re-Shephir's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Re-Shephir "Re-Shephir") [[Midnight Essence - The Coppermind|Midnight Essence]] alongside the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]], takes place in Natanatan during the Eighth Epoch.[^14]

It is thought that Stormseat was destroyed during the [[Last Desolation - The Coppermind|Last Desolation]] (Aharietiam), thousands of years ago.[^1] Vorin tradition holds that the nation of Natanatan persisted despite the loss of its capital and later allied with [[Thaylenah - The Coppermind|Thaylenah]], [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar"), [Jah Keved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jah_Keved "Jah Keved"), and [[Kharbranth - The Coppermind|Kharbranth]] as the "five Vorin kingdoms" after the [[Day of Recreance - The Coppermind|Day of Recreance]].[^15] Natanatan was presumably still part of this coalition during the [[Hierocracy - The Coppermind|Hierocracy]] and the [War of Loss](https://coppermind.net/wiki/War_of_Loss "War of Loss").[^16] Despite this alliance, the kingdom fell several hundred years ago [^14] under unknown circumstances.

In the present day, a Natan lighthouse keeper named [Puuli](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Puuli "Puuli") ponders whether the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] heralds the time that his grandfather had warned of, "the time of changes, when the men from the hidden island of the Origin at last come to reclaim Natanatan." [^17]

## Culture

Natan people have pale blue skin, wide noses, and wool-like white hair.[^7] Natans view the moons of Roshar as deities, and revere the blue moon [Nomon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nomon "Nomon") in particular. [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] tells a folk tale about Nomon conceiving a child with the Natan Queen [Tsa](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tsa "Tsa"), resulting in blue-skinned descendants who were blessed with poise and beauty.[^8] However, it is heavily implied that Natans are actually human- [[Siah Aimian - The Coppermind|Siah Aimian]] hybrids,[^18] [^19] giving them their blue skin and an increased lifespan.[^20] How or why these cultures came together is unknown.

Natanatan was nicknamed the "Granite Kingdom".[^21] Hoid's tale describes the kingdom as having a rich culture and fine architecture.[^8] The historicity of his story is unclear, but Hoid was alive during the Heraldic Epochs, and stories of a civilized Natanatan persist on modern-day Roshar.[^21]

[[Highstorm - The Coppermind|Highstorms]] are particularly strong in eastern Roshar,[^21] and many people believe that this is due to its proximity to the [Origin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Origin "Origin").[^22] [^10] Natans adapted to life with the storms, devising ways to farm the area by using low-profile crops.[^23] The Origin features prominently in at least one folk tale told by modern Natans.[^17]

## New Natanan

The city-state of New Natanan, located on the easternmost coast of Roshar, is all that remains of the former Natan government. It functions as a sovereign nation, with a few smaller settlements as protectorates.[^6] Despite its geographical isolation, New Natanan appears to retain influence in the Vorin church.[^24] New Natanan has diplomatic relationships with other nations, and often communicates with them by [spanreed](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spanreed "Spanreed").[^24] [^25] Dalinar includes New Natanan in his meetings in [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] when he attempts to form a coalition to fight the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]].[^6] The Natans are appreciative of Dalinar's efforts, but are primarily concerned with reclaiming their homeland and its Oathgate.[^26]
