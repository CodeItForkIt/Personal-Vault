## Intent

> “ *You should know, perhaps, that each of these powers—these Shards—has what we call an Intent. A driving motivation.* ”
> 
> — [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] explaining the nature of Intent [^1]

**Intent** is a Spiritual concept in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] referring to a being's express will or purpose. Intent plays a vitally important role in the workings of most manifestations of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] in the cosmere.[^2] [^3] [[Shards]] have an innate Intent to them, a driving motivation that is encompassed by the name of the Shard itself.[^1] Individuals can also focus their will into a deliberate Intent, something necessary when working with many types of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]].[^4]

## Nature

The precise nature of Intention, and how it functions and is used, is currently unclear. When Intent is exercised, one's mind reaches out into and interacts with the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]], allowing for one's intentions to have influence in the Spiritual Realm.[^5] Intent also relates to the conscious awareness of objects and effects, indicating a connection to the Cognitive Realm. This influence can range from the power involved in chaining a [[Shards|Shard]] to a simple [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aon's]] effectiveness.

Intention plays an important, esoteric role in the underlying framework of the cosmere's magic. Within the context of specific magic systems it is often involved in specific ways, though the extent and importance of these functions varies.

## Shards

While a Vessel holds a Shard, the Intent of that Shard can inhibit what they can do with its power or drive them toward a particular goal. Over long spans of time, the Shard's Intent can influence or override their personality. As the Shards were once unified in Adonalsium's power, they are incomplete in their current state and lack much of the context that made each individual Intent valuable. Instead, each Shard and its corresponding Vessel tends to push toward the single ideal described in its Intent, without nuance or temperance.

Ruin

[[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] desires to facilitate change, especially to break things down and collapse existence into entropy. [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati]] was described as kinder than any person [[Tanavast - The Coppermind|Tanavast]] or [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] had previously met. He volunteered to hold the volatile Shard of Ruin, but after thousands of years, he had become nihilistic, braggadocios, and reveled in destruction.

> “ *This process we are engaged in, the end of all things--it's not a fight, but the culmination of inevitability. Can any man make a pocket watch that won't eventually wind down? Can you imagine a lantern that won't eventually burn out? All things end. Think of me as a caretaker--the one who watches the shop and makes certain that the lights are turned out, that everything is cleaned up, once closing time arrives.* ”
> 
> —Ruin speaking to [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] [^6]

Preservation

[[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]] seeks to protect those around it and to prevent change. If given the opportunity, it would drive civilizations and worlds into stagnancy. The [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Lord Ruler]] attempted to align himself with this Intent by inhibiting technological progress during the [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|Final Empire]].

Harmony

When [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] wished to create a world, he found he was unable to until he collaborated with Ati to do so. [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] found that the combination of Preservation and Ruin could be used to benefit Scadrial, but they later grew into conflict and forced him into inactivity.

Devotion

[[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] represents divine love, bonds of attachment, and selfless service. The [[Seon - The Coppermind|seons]] are a manifestation of this Intent.

> “ *You look at things as a human, young master. You see rank and distinction; you try to order the world so that everything has a place either above you or beneath you. To a seon, there is no above or beneath. There are only those we love. And we serve those we love.* ”

Dominion

[[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] seeks to incite conquest and to place some beings in power over others. This Intent may be compared to Autonomy, applied to nations or factions rather than individuals.

Endowment

The exact Intent of [[Endowment - The Coppermind|Endowment]] is unclear, but it relates to gift-giving, and is manifest in [[BioChromatic Breath - The Coppermind|BioChromatic Breath]] and the [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]].

Autonomy

[[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]] desires to single out the best of the best from a population and empower them. Their followers continually compete against each other, striving to prove their worthiness before [Trell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Trell_\(Trellism\) "Trell (Trellism)") or [[Bavadin - The Coppermind|Bavadin]]. [Patji](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Patji "Patji") expresses an exclusive interest in blessing followers who have passed his trials and overcome great danger.

> “ *Trell demands that her servants prove themselves. Against adversity. Against society. Against the roles we take. And when there are several who fit the same slot in life … well, only the strongest can survive and be rewarded.* ”
> 
> — [Dumad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dumad "Dumad") [^8]

Honor

The power of [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] demands that oaths should be kept. It does not understand a nuanced distinction between morality and integrity. This power can intentionally abandon Vessels that fail to live up to its standards. Honor is also implied to represent the natural laws of physics and math.

Cultivation

[[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]] seeks to inspire change, especially growth, development, and self-improvement.

Odium

Other powers describe [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] as the embodiment of divine wrath, with none of the mercy or virtues that gave it context. However, Odium describes himself as the god of emotions, and that all passions are a part of his domain. His Intent informed the faith of the [Passions](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Passions "Passions").

Virtuosity

Not much is known about [[Virtuosity - The Coppermind|Virtuosity]], but it relates to artistic mastery. Its influence on [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]] led to the creation of the [[Hijo - The Coppermind|hijo]] and [[Nightmare - The Coppermind|nightmares]], spirits that react especially to artistic demonstrations.

## Role

In general, while use of magic can often be learned without guidance, and even be performed subconsciously, many applications can be learned significantly faster and with greater ease when the magic user receives instruction and is able to perform the magic intentionally within the bounds of its nature.[^3]

Intent is involved to some degree in the following contexts:

Selish Magic

Intent plays a particularly notable role on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]], where the copious manifestations of Investiture are accessed in ways that are distinctly tied to human culture.[^9] The precise shapes and movements associated with these magic systems must be composed with the intention of accessing the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] for a specific purpose.[^5] For example, experiments performed by [Khriss](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khriss "Khriss") suggest that an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") must know the required shape of an [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aon]] and draw it willingly in order for the magic to manifest.[^2] For the creation of [soulstamps](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulstamp "Soulstamp"), a [Forger](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Forger "Forger") must not only have proper Intent themselves, but also understand the intent behind the thing being stamped.[^10]

BioChromatic Breath

While the exact wording of a [Command](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Command "Command") in [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakening]] is important, one must also exercise Intent through careful visualization of what the Awakener intends to happen.[^11] [^3] Without explicitly willing the expressed ideas to take place, merely pronouncing the words of the Command will not allow for a successful Awakening.[^12] Notably, the Command used for [Breath](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Breath "Breath") transfer, *"My life to yours, my Breath become yours,"* cannot be forced; in order for one person to give their Breath to another they must willingly speak the Command phrase.[^13]

Allomancy and Feruchemy

With [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]] and [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]], the role of Intent is less significant than in many other systems. The specific application of Investiture is shaped by the metal used rather than by the user's will. However, Intention does allow for faster learning and greater skill with the magic.[^3] This also affects the usability of metalminds: if the user doesn't realize they are metalminds, they will be unable to use them.[^14] [^15] More complex uses, such as [[Compounding - The Coppermind|Compounding]] or tapping specific Investiture from a metalmind, also require some measure of intentionality.[^3] [^16]

For example, when [Waxillium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Waxillium_Ladrian "Waxillium Ladrian") receives the [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind#Unsealed Metalminds|unsealed metalmind]] (disguised as a coin) from [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] in [New Seran](https://coppermind.net/wiki/New_Seran "New Seran"), he is unable to tap it, or even feel a Feruchemical store within.[^14] It is only once he understands the true nature of the metalmind that he his able to tap it and reveal the secrets within.[^17] Or when [Wayne](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wayne "Wayne") first picks up the [Bands of Mourning](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bands_of_Mourning "Bands of Mourning"): since he thinks the spearhead is simply [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]], he is unable to sense the power within the Bands -- and Wayne is an experienced Feruchemist.[^18] [^19]

Hemalurgy

While [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]] can be performed anywhere in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] and by anyone, Intent is required to some degree during the placement of the Hemalurgic spike.[^20] It is unclear whether this means the person must only know generally what they are doing, or if they must have specific knowledge as to what the spike will do, where it must be placed, or why.

On Roshar

[[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor's]] power could be used to release and expel [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] from [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], if the wielder of the power did so with Intent.[^21] Prior to the [Recreance](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Recreance "Recreance") some believed that the [[Sibling - The Coppermind|Sibling]] had withdrawn from humans by intent, though there is some evidence that this was not the case.[^22] In order to access the powers granted by an [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblade]], one must do so intentionally. It is possible to hold an Honorblade and not have access to its powers if they are unknown.[^23]

The [Rhythms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm") of the singers are attuned to particular Intents, and [Nergaoul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nergaoul "Nergaoul") seems to alter and amplify the emotions of people who act in accordance with its Intent for violence.

Aethers

Each variety of [[Aether - The Coppermind|Aether]] manifests as its own type of spore. When spores are exposed to water they react uniquely depending on the related aether, and on the intention of the one who initiated the reaction. For example, [[Midnight Essence - The Coppermind|Midnight Essence]] will act and communicate according to the intention of the host [^24], and similarly the one who initiates a verdant spore reaction forms a sort of [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]] to the verdant vines and can control their growth.[^25] The more practised the sprouter, the stronger their connection to the manifestation.[^26]

Technology

Some types of locks that are [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakened]] can read your intent to tell if you have obtained the passcode with the owner's permission in order to increase security.[^27]
