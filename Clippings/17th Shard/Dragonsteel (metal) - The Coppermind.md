## Dragonsteel (metal)

**Dragonsteel** is a metal that forms part of the body of [dragons](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\) "Dragon (cosmere)") in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]]. It looks silvery and can appear liquid in the right light. It is extremely strong and ultralight, and can be used for construction.[^1]

At least in the case of [[Xisisrefliel - The Coppermind|Xisisrefliel]], dragonsteel makes up a dragon's horns and claws, as well as spikes on its back and ridges along its neck and limbs. [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] has speculated that the dragonsteel left on dragon corpses is the origin of the myth that dragons hoard material wealth.[^2] [^3]

## Trivia

- [Brandon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brandon "Brandon") has [RAFO'd](https://coppermind.net/wiki/RAFO "RAFO") whether dragonsteel is the [God Metal](https://coppermind.net/wiki/God_Metal "God Metal") of [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium") and whether the silvery chain seen in [Celebrant](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Celebrant "Celebrant") is made of dragonsteel.[^4] [^5]
- According to [Dragonsteel Prime](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragonsteel_Prime "Dragonsteel Prime"), dragonsteel is a liquid metal that comes out of Dragonsteel Wells on the Shattered Plains of Yolen. It can be shaped into anything, but only by the person who first touched it. It can also grow from dragons, appearing on their horns, fangs, and claws.
