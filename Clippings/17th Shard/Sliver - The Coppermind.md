## Sliver

In the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], **Sliver** is a term generally used by scholars [^1] [^2] for someone who previously held at least a significant portion of a [[Shards|Shard's]] [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] and has since released it,[^3] [^2] [^4] [^5] though the term can have a variety of meanings and its exact usage is something of a moving target.[^2] [^6] [^7]

## Nature

The effects of being a Sliver can vary, based on factors like the [[Intent - The Coppermind|Intent]] associated with the Investiture they held and how long they held that power for.[^8] In general, holding the amount of Investiture required to become a Sliver greatly expands a person's mind, allowing them to better use the vast power they hold.[^9] When a person later releases that power and becomes a Sliver, their mind and soul do not simply return to a baseline, as holding that much Investiture has a lasting effect.[^8] [^10] [^3] Some changes, such as how the Intent of a Shard will warp the mindset of its Vessel, will fade over time, while other changes are permanent.[^11]

Despite having released the power they once held, a Sliver is still noticeably more Invested than the average person,[^12] which likely contributes to some of their other traits. Most notably, Slivers can resist the pull of the [[Beyond - The Coppermind|Beyond]] indefinitely and choose to instead remain in the cosmere as a [[Cognitive Shadow - The Coppermind|Cognitive Shadow]] if they wish.[^13] [^14] Beyond that, they are also more capable of influencing the Physical Realm from the Cognitive and are more resistant to the influence and control of a Shard.[^15] [^16] Immediately after holding a Shard, a Sliver may be able to sense their [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connections]] with others, and even send feelings and ideas to them.[^17] [^18]

## Known and Potential Slivers

[[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek]] and [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] are both Slivers as a result of holding [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation's]] power at the [Well of Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Ascension "Well of Ascension").[^2] [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] is also a Sliver of Preservation, as he served as the Shard's Vessel between the death of [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] and the Ascension of Vin.[^2] [^13]

When [[Tanavast - The Coppermind|Tanavast]], the Vessel of [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]], died, the Shard gave his memories and a portion of his soul to the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]].[^19] As a result, the Stormfather is also a Sliver,[^20] though he may not fit into standard categories as well as most entities do.[^21] [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin]] may have become something like a Sliver as a result of his [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] to the Stormfather; [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] refers to Dalinar as having Ascended after he opened [[Honor's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Honor's Perpendicularity]] at the [[Battle of Thaylen Field - The Coppermind|Battle of Thaylen Field]],[^22] but Dalinar's exact status after this event remains unclear.[^23] If he wasn't already a Sliver, Dalinar would have become one after Ascending to and then releasing Honor during the [[Contest of champions - The Coppermind|contest of champions]].[^18]

[Telsin Ladrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Telsin_Ladrian "Telsin Ladrian"), who was on the path to becoming an [[Shards|avatar]] of [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]], claims that she would be a Sliver of Autonomy once she is fully Invested.[^24]

While most scholars would not consider [[Nightblood - The Coppermind|Nightblood]] or [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] to be Slivers, some might argue that they are because of the amount of Investiture they have accumulated.[^6] [^7]
