## MaiPon

**MaiPon** is a sub-state of the [[Rose Empire - The Coppermind|Rose Empire]] on the planet of [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]]. It was absorbed into the Rose Empire long prior to the events of [The Emperor's Soul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Emperor%27s_Soul "The Emperor's Soul").

MaiPon is the homeland of [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]].[^1] [^2] Because [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] on Sel is concentrated in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], and thus dependent on geographic location, the power of a Forgery diminishes the further a forgery is from MaiPon, similar to how the Elantrian Aons function.[^3] All Forgeries include a lithograph resembling MaiPon on the reverse side of the stamp.

The MaiPon are descended from the same people group as the [[JinDo - The Coppermind|JinDo]]; they diverged long ago. They are commonly mistaken by other cultures as being identical.[^4] The MaiPon use chopsticks, known as [MaiPon sticks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/MaiPon_sticks "MaiPon sticks"), as eating utensils. The sticks were later adopted in JinDo culture.[^5]

## Physical Traits

The MaiPon are short of stature,[^6] have dark brown skin and fine features.[^7]

## Religion

The early MaiPons worshiped meteors, which they call the souls of broken gods. These meteors would be carved to bring out the shape of the gods.[^2] It is believed that the history of carving was responsible for the invention and advancement of [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]]. [[Wan ShaiLu - The Coppermind|Wan ShaiLu]] is from MaiPon, and is a follower of the [God Beyond](https://coppermind.net/wiki/God_Beyond "God Beyond"). It is unclear if the religion is common in MaiPon or unique to Shai.[^6] [^2]
