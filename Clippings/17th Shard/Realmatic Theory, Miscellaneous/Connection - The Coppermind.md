## Connection

**Connection** is a [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual]] attribute that represents spiritual connections and relationships between different entities -- living being, locations, items and so on. Like other Spiritual attributes in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], Connection is quantifiable and can affect or be affected by different [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested Arts]].

## The Nature of Connection

> “ *You see the Connections all people have: to others, to spren, to time and reality itself. Everything is Connected, Dalinar, by a vast web of interactions, passions, thoughts, fates.*”
> 
> —The Stormfather [^1]

Connection uses [[Spiritweb - The Coppermind|spiritwebs]], and connects them in the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]]. Connection is thought to be part of the Spiritual aspect of a being. People are generally Connected to other people or certain locations, and can be Connected to more than one place at a time.[^2] [^3] The Spiritual Realm itself is said to be made up of Connections, and that within it, all things are Connected.[^4] [^5] [^6]

As a Spiritual attribute, Connection is not bound by ordinary laws of time, and is thus capable of Connecting one to the past and future.[^7] [^8] [^1] [^9] [^10] [^11] One's Connection to the past allows the soul to know when the body it was associated with was born, and thus be aware of its "age." [^11] This network of Connections does not appear to be limited to only future possibilities, but all possible worlds, including ones that simply could have been. These can be traced by various forms of Invested Art in order to see visions of possibilities.[^12] [^13]

Connection is not directly composed of anything, existing as something more akin to a spiritual force that binds things together.[^14]

When made visible in any Realm, Connection appears as glowing cords of light connecting things together; be it in a complex web, or a single string with the ends vanishing into nothing.[^15] [^16] [^5] [^17] The cords are considered difficult to read or understand.[^7]

### Connection between Aspects

The Spiritual aspect is Connected to the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical]] and [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive aspects]] of an entity or object. Furthermore, a Connection exists directly between the Physical and Cognitive aspects.[^15] [^18] Due to this, Cognitive and Physical Aspects of objects do not need to overlap due to how their interconnection operates, and can be separated by any distance.[^18] [^19]

The Spiritweb is capable of being "confused" on what constitutes the Physical aspect of an individual, via the manipulation of Connection.[^17] This can, for instance, make the soul and power believe that the ground is part of the body, and therefore have the Investiture attempt to flow towards and fill the ground.[^17]

### Distance and Connection

Despite the Spiritual properties of Connection, [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 1|Cognitive Shadows]], [[Splinter - The Coppermind|splinters]], and in certain instances [[Shards]], can get tied to an area, or [[17th Shard|planet]] in the cosmere due to their Connection to it.[^15] [^20] [^21] This primarily occurs to beings that are heavily Invested or exist through Investiture.[^20] [^21] In the case of [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald"), [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] and [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]] being unable to leave Roshar, it is not only tied to Connection alone, but also to the Oaths sworn by the involved Shards, binding them, and beings Invested by them, to the system.[^22] [^23]

Forcibly separating such beings or objects can, in some cases, separate their spiritual component from their body.[^24] As one gets further from that which they have such a Connection to, they begin to feel a pulling sensation that gets stronger the further they travel.[^21] [^25]

There are methods that can bypass the movement restrictions on Investiture and Invested beings, without needing to sever the Connection, but the details are not fully understood.[^21] [^26] It is known that it is possible to "stretch" Connection, in order to allow two strongly Connected beings to travel a further distance from one another, stretching it too far, however, risks degradation of the Connection.[^15]

This does not appear to universally apply, as the Connection between Physical and Cognitive Aspects does not bind an object by distance.[^18] However, they will naturally move towards their Physical Aspect when not being moved by another.[^18]

### Forming Connection

Connection is primarily formed through social or other such relations between various social actors. It can also be achieved through various other forms of relation between actors. With actors including not just sapient beings, but all things with a Spiritual Aspect to be Connected to.[^7] [^15] Connection can be affected by various forms of Invested Arts, including [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]] and [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]].[^27] [^16] [^28] Additionally, it is possible to alter Connection through artificial means,[^29] though the particulars of how this is accomplished are unknown.

Connection is capable of being inherited.[^30]

## Uses of Connection

### Accessing Investiture

Many methods of accessing [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] have been observed throughout the cosmere, but all share certain cardinal similarities: an established Connection or ability to create a Connection to a source of power and a way of directing that power.[^31]

### Linguistic Connection

Connection can be used in many ways to allow the user to speak the language of another person they are Connecting with or the native language of a specific location.[^32] [^28] [^33] This can be done without the user learning any of the language beforehand. Some methods replace their native language, tricking a person's soul into thinking they were born where they are currently located.[^34] [^35]

This ability has flaws however, as it will fill in local idioms and phrases on its own, but if the user is careless and focuses on a specific word or phrase the ability may fail and they will say the word verbatim in their own tongue [^36] or it may be said in an odd way.[^37] This includes slip-ups that confuse others,[^38] such as saying coin [^39] or bunny [^40] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. They may also interpret local words as something more familiar, even if it is less accurate.[^41]

Blanking [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 4|Identity]] and tapping Connection can cause someone to gain an accent for a particular area.[^42]

- **Feruchemical Duralumin**: In [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]], Connection is stored in [[Duralumin - The Coppermind|duralumin]].[^13] Using this, a Feruchemist can communicate with others on foreign lands.[^32] Connection can also be used by anyone by wearing [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|unsealed metalminds]].[^43] These medallions are worn by a person who is in a land that is not their own, and they are then able to speak the language of the place they are in. A person wearing their medallion in their own land will still speak their own language, not the language of the person they are communicating with. Occasionally, words do not translate fully.
- **Spiritual Adhesion**: Using the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surge]] of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Adhesion]] to manipulate spiritual, rather than purely physical forces, a [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmith]] can touch another person and forge a spiritual Connection between them. This allows the Bondsmith to temporarily speak whatever languages this other person can speak.[^28]
- **Regal Forms of Power**: By staying out in the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]], [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] are capable of bonding with a [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]], which has the effect of changing their form, which alters their physical appearance and mental capabilities.[^44] One [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regal]] form of power is envoyform, which allows the user to have Connection to other cultures and allows the user to speak and read their languages.[^44] [^45]
- **Ones Above**: The [Malwish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Malwish_Empire "Malwish Empire"), also known as the Ones Above, have a handheld device that is a language translator and can even translate written words.[^46]
- **Nalthian Technology**: [Fort](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fort "Fort") uses a computer tablet from [[Nalthis - The Coppermind|Nalthis]] that is said to use [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakened]] predictive Connection circuits. While Fort uses the tablet to communicate with people who read and write the same language as him, the individual he received the tablet from used it to translate between languages.[^47]
- **Dawnshard**: At least one [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshard]] can Connect a user to their current location, allowing them to speak and read the local language.[^48] For [[Sigzil - The Coppermind|Sigzil]], this requires at least two-thousand [[Investiture - The Coppermind|BEUs]].[^49] [^50]
- **Hoid**: [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] has access to some manifestation of Investiture that allows him to manipulate the Spiritual attribute of Connection. The full extent of this ability is unknown, but its primary known use is to help Hoid speak the various languages of the worlds of the cosmere that he visits. The ability allows him to Connect to whatever land he is in, temporarily altering his Spiritual aspect to believe that he was raised in that place, allowing him to speak as a native.[^36]

### Possibility Sight

There are various methods in which one can trace Connection through time in order to display images from or visions of other possible worlds.[^12] This can range from the manifestation of ephemeral "shadows" representing an object or entity's alternate possibilities, to it manifesting as a complete vision.[^51]

- **Allomantic Atium**: Allomantic Atium is capable of tracing Connections to future possibilities in order to display them as an "atium shadow." [^12] As with all future-sight, it can be influenced by another using future-sight, as it multiplies the amount of possible world-states that could plausibly occur.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>
- **Allomantic Malatium**: Flaring [[Malatium - The Coppermind|malatium]] allows an [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancer]] to see not just a malatium shadow of a person, but seems to give them a glimpse into the Spiritual Realm, allowing them to see a vision of a past event in another person's life by seeing their Connection and their past. Given the only circumstance in which this effect is seen, it may be a result of flaring malatium at the moment of death and transition to the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], rather than a result of simply flaring the metal in and of itself.[^12] [^51]
- **Allomantic Gold**: Allomantic Gold is capable of tracing Connections to alternative possibilities in order to display a vision of how one's life could have been, if it went down a different path.[^12] [^13]
- **Allomantic Electrum**: Allomantic Electrum is capable of showing your own future possibilities through the tracing of Connections.[^12] [^13]
- **Sak**: [Sak](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sak "Sak") is capable of displaying visions of near future possibilities via tracing future Connections.[^52] [^53] [^12] [^54] However, she is only capable of sharing these visions with those she is strongly Connected with.[^55]

### Surgebinding

Surgebinding makes use of Connection in a variety of ways.<sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

- **Lightweaving**: In order to [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Lightweave]] properly, a strong spiritual component requiring a certain measure of Connection and a full mental picture of the desired illusion is necessary.[^59]
- **Stoneshaping**: Stoneshapers are able to trace Connection within stone through the generations, allowing them to create images of the past with the stone; displaying events, feelings, emotions, and ideas. Furthermore, this creates a strong Connection between the user and the stone.[^9]
- **Bondsmith powers**: After the death of Honor, [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmiths]] can use their powers to affect Connection more directly. These expanded powers allow a skilled Bondsmith to see the Connections a person has when touching them,[^16] to actively drain Investiture from people, and steal another person's Nahel bonds.[^17] The Stormfather's Bondsmith is capable of using their Connection to the land in tandem with the Lightweaving of another Knight, in order to create an updating map of Roshar itself out of the light.[^60] <sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>
- **Swearing Ideals:** Swearing an Ideal of the Knights Radiant strengthens ones Connection to their bonded spren, and creates a Connection with the Spiritual Realm. This Connection to the Spiritual Realm is used to open a temporary miniature perpendicularity.[^61]

### Other Uses

- **Allomantic Lerasium**: For normal people, burning [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]] forges a permanent Connection between them and [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]], turning them into a very powerful [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Mistborn]] by rewriting their spiritual DNA.[^62] For people who are already Mistborn, the same effect increases their power.[^63]
- **Selish Magic**: All magic on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] (including [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|AonDor]], [[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]], [[ChayShan - The Coppermind|ChayShan]], [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]], and [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealing]]) requires a Connection to certain lands to function properly. The magics will decrease in power the farther from the land the user is,[^64] and will not normally work outside of Sel.[^65] [^66]
- **Ba-Ado-Mishram**: [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]], after absorbing the [Well of Control](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Control "Well of Control") is able to Connect with the vast majority of singers on Roshar and is able to grant them forms of power. This made them into [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regals]]. She also provided them with [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]].[^67] However, forcibly breaking these Connections by sealing Ba-Ado-Mishram away permanently damages the souls of those she Connected to, removing their Identity and Connection, and leaving them in [[Singer - The Coppermind|Slaveform]].[^68] It also fundamentally wounded Roshar itself.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>
- **Singers**: Singers are able to speak to rhythms.[^69] Rhythms represent a certain state of mind, the feelings of the singer using it. The rhythms Connect the singers,[^70] and there is Connection between them and the songs of Roshar.[^71]
- **Realms**: Connection can be used to exist in different realms.[^72]
- **Tracking**: Certain entities, such as the [messengers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Messengers "Messengers") used by [Silverlight Mercantile](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Silverlight_Mercantile "Silverlight Mercantile"), are able to follow Connection in order to find people.[^73] [^74]
- **Body Swap**: Manipulation of Connection in certain ways can allow one to swap souls between bodies. How this would be done intentionally is unknown, but it can be done on accident when creating a Connection between two people.[^75]
- **Navigating the Spiritual Realm:** When within the Spiritual Realm itself, one can use Connections in order to shape which pasts or futures they are drawn into.[^76] [^77] [^78] In a sense, the network of Connections that make up the Spiritual Realm are capable of granting a sense of direction within its chaos.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>
- **Vision Role Determination:** Connections can also be used to change which "role" one plays in the visions they experience. This can be done by intentionally strengthening the Connection between the individual, and the individual whose role they want to take.[^79]
- **Dragons:** [Dragons](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\) "Dragon (cosmere)") have a direct Connection to the Spiritual Realm, which allows them to feed themselves with energy and never run out of food or water. Severing this Connection is the easiest way to kill a dragon.[^80]

## Bonds

Bonds are Connections between entities in the cosmere that exist outside the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]].[^81] All bonds are basically the same, with the powers and functions depending on the holders of the bond.[^82] Bonds are typically formed between entities with greater-than-normal Investiture and other entities, allowing them to gain enhancements. Forming a bond utilizes Connection and Identity in some manner. Lessening those attributes, such as by storing them in a metalmind, would affect the bond.[^83] Using [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]], bonds may be stolen and given to a new person.[^84] [^85] [^86] There is an upper limit to how many bonds a person can have.[^87]

Bonds on some planets provide the holders of the bonds with additional powers.[^82] Bonds other than the [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] will provide powers to the constituent entities of that bond while on Roshar. What these powers are is unknown, and will not be of the ten [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]]. The seon bond is confirmed to do this, as the Nahel bond and the seon bond are very similar.[^88]

### Types of known Bonding Entities

- **The Nahel Bond**: The [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] is formed between sapient beings and certain types of intelligent [[Spren - The Coppermind|Spren]].[^89] It gives access to [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]], while granting the spren the ability to maintain their cognition in the Physical Realm. There are ten known forms the Nahel Bond can take, each giving access to two different Surges among the ten.[^90] The more Ideals a Knight swears, the stronger their Connection to their bonded spren becomes.[^61]
	- **Lesser Spren Bonds**: Non-intelligent spren can theoretically form symbiotic bonds with people.[^91]
		- **Other Spren Bonds**: Certain spren bond with animals native to Roshar, enhancing those animals and granting them abilities. Animals that are confirmed to have these bonds include [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]], [[Chull - The Coppermind|chulls]], [[Ryshadium - The Coppermind|Ryshadium]], [[Larkin - The Coppermind|Larkin]] and [skyeels](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Skyeel "Skyeel").[^92] [^93] [^94]
- **Shardblades**: Dead [Shardblades](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") are able to bond with people through the use of charged gemstones.[^95] The gem is placed in the hilt of the Blade and after a week of constant contact, the Blade is bonded and can be dismissed from and recalled to the Physical Realm.[^96] Once bonded the blade can be dismissed and recalled by syncing with the user's heart to the blade. Ten heartbeats are required.[^97] A person's irises will lighten during the bonding process.[^98]
- **Honorblades**: [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblades]] grant Surgebinding abilities to humans who touch them, but require dangerous levels of [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]].[^99]
- **Shardplate**: There is some manner of bond between Dead [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]] and its wearer that will disappear when the wearer dies, causing the Plate to fall off of their body.[^100] [^101] Specific lesser spren are able to bond with Surgebinders to form their Radiant [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]].[^102]
- **Singer Bond**: The singers can bond with a spren. Doing so grants the singer a form with a specific physical and mental change.[^103] [^104] Not all spren grant a unique form; most spren give the singers dullform.[^69] Without a spren, singers exist in what is known as slaveform ([[Singer - The Coppermind|Parshmen]]).[^105]
	- **Regal**: [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regals]] are singers who bonded with [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]], granting them a transformation called a form of power. These forms grant them supernatural abilities.[^106] [^107] This bond can drastically alter a singer's ways of thinking.[^108] [^109] It also allow singers to hear and use new rhythms, but makes hearing the old ones extremely difficult.[^108]
- **Unmade**: Several [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] hold the ability to manipulate Connection and bond with sapient beings, granting various powers or psychological effects.
	- **Yelig-nar**: [[Yelig-nar - The Coppermind|Yelig-nar]] is able to bond with a human or [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] and gives his host command of the Surges.[^110] [^111] [Hessi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hessi "Hessi") believes this bond grants the host the use of all ten Surges,[^111] though given the Voidbringers' view of the Surge of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Adhesion]],[^112] Yelig-Nar may only grant the other nine. The people he bonds with appear to remain fully in control of themselves,[^113] but they must control Yelig-nar if they do not wish to be consumed by him.[^110]
		- **Nergaoul**: [Nergaoul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nergaoul "Nergaoul") is able to bond with humans who embrace the Thrill.[^114] This imbues them with bloodlust and the urge to fight above all else.[^115] With the help of [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], he is able to fully control large groups of people through the bond.[^116]
- **Seons**: A [[Seon - The Coppermind|seon]] can create bonds with humans, including [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian").[^117] Little is known of this bond other than that the [[Seon - The Coppermind|seon]] follows and helps the person it is bonded to, functioning as a servant to the owner of the bond.[^118] Seons will automatically seek out a new person to bond with following the death of its master; often the new master has been prearranged.[^119] [^120] This bond is similar to the Nahel bond and would grant some sort of ability if manifested on Roshar.[^121] [^88]
- **Aviar**: [Aviar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aviar "Aviar") create a bond with a person and grant a specific power based on the type of Aviar bonded. Aviar are granted their ability if raised on one of the pantheon islands and consume a worm native to the island.[^122]
	- **Other Worm-Infected Animals**: Other birds besides native island Aviar are able to eat the worms and create a bond with a new effect.[^46]
		- **Navigators**: [Navigators](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Navigator "Navigator") who have bonded an Aviar are able to give their gift to their Aviar as well.[^123]
- **Sentient Awakened Entities in an Inorganic Host**: Also known as Type IV BioChromatic Entities.[^124] The only known entities of this kind are [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 19|Nightblood]] and [[Vivenna's Blade - The Coppermind|Vivenna's Blade]], though much less is known about the latter. Nightblood is a sentient sword made using one thousand [[BioChromatic Breath - The Coppermind|Breaths]] and the command *destroy evil*. Nightblood will bond with anyone they wish to communicate with, and is able to read and speak with minds directly.[^124] Distance appears to be a factor, but this may be a limitation of the mind of Nightblood and not their ability. While idle, Nightblood reaches out to other people, tempting them to draw the blade. "Pure" people who cannot be tempted would feel great nausea, even induce vomiting. Anyone who is able to wield Nightblood and survive bonds with the blade to a greater degree and is no longer repelled by or abnormally drawn to use it.[^125] Those unable to control Nightblood become controlled themselves, leading to them using Nightblood to kill everyone around them and allowing Nightblood to absorb all Investiture from the victims including the spark of life (i.e. the soul).[^126] [^127]
- **The Luhel Bond**: The [[Luhel bond - The Coppermind|Luhel bond]] is typically formed between sapient beings and [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 17|aethers]].[^128] It gives access to aether manipulation abilities in exchange for the sophont giving up water.[^33] This bond can be either symbiotic or parasitic depending on the context, and varies in duration and nature.[^128] [^129]
- **Cognitive Shadows**: It is theoretically possible for a [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 1|Cognitive Shadow]] to form a bond with a human.[^130]
	- **Nightmares**: It is theoretically possible to form a bond with a [[Nightmare - The Coppermind|nightmare]].[^131] How exactly one would do this, or what effects such a bond would have on either party, is unknown.

## Manipulating Connection

- **Feruchemical Duralumin**: In Feruchemy, Connection is stored in duralumin.[^13] Storing Connection lessens others' trust in and awareness of the Feruchemist, while tapping Connection allows the Feruchemist to form trusting relationships quickly.[^13] Tapping the metalmind will allow the Feruchemist to form stronger Connections, possibly depending on what Connections were stored. It can allow Feruchemists to form trust relationships with others much faster, for example. Duralumin Feruchemy can also change one's Spiritual Connection to a planet, which can help [[17th Shard|worldhoppers]] with magic systems. If a Feruchemist were able to [[Compounding - The Coppermind|compound]] duralumin, they would be able to use duralumin to have a nearly infinite supply of Spiritual Connection.
- **Hemalurgic Duralumin**: In [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]], Connection can be stolen by a duralumin spike.[^132]
- **Singers**: If a singers Connection and Identity is stolen, they will change into slaveform.[^133] [^68]
- **The Ire's orb**: The [[Ire - The Coppermind|Ire's]] [orb](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ire%27s_orb "Ire's orb") is an arcane device that can magically grant Connection. The members of the Ire intended to use it to allow one of their members to ascend as a Vessel of a Shard of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]].[^29] [^134]
- **Aons**: There is an [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aon]] for Connection, but its use is unknown.[^135]
- **Fabrials**: [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|Fabrials]] can be used to alter Connection, though how is currently unknown.[^136]
- **Bondsmith powers**: After the death of Honor [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmiths]] can use their powers to affect Connection more directly. These expanded powers allow a skilled Bondsmith to bind Surgebinders to the ground causing their Stormlight to be drained, or steal a Nahel bond.[^17] They are also capable of manipulating the strength of a Connection.[^79]
- **Dawnshards**: At least one Dawnshard is able to shortly reinforce Connections between former holders, allowing one to appear to another as an illusion.[^137] This requires at least ten percent Skip capacity of Breath Equivalent Units.

Through an unknown manner, one can directly interact with and manipulate Connection. They can directly stretch it and check for issues in the Connection. Stretching it too far can risk degradation to the Connection.[^138] How exactly distance functions in relation to Connection, given the lack of such concepts in the Spiritual Realm, is unknown.

## Connection with Shards

The closer the Connection between an entity and a Shard, the more powerful that entity becomes. How a person becomes Connected to a Shard varies between planets. It is assumed that the Intent of the respective Shard influences these conditions. On [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] Connection to a Shard is determined by genetics. On Sel it will depend on a person's location and their Connection to the land, and on Roshar it has to do with a person's actions and how they live their life.[^139]

Since Shards are Connected to their magic, a Shard is able to directly interact with its users and provide the needed Investiture directly when needed.[^31] [^140]

When a Shard Invests a planet, they Connect with every [[Axon - The Coppermind|axon]] of the planet.[^10]

### Ascension

Connection has an influence on who can or cannot Ascend as the holder of a certain Shard,[^5] [^10] and Connection to a Shard is required to Ascend. Improper Connection decreases the vessel's ability to control the powers and weakens the vessel's power.[^5] Connection to an opposing Shard makes it more difficult to maintain Ascension. Proper Ascension can happen either forged artificially or driven by a natural inclination towards the Shard's Intent.[^29]

## Trivia

- Connection is vaguely inspired by the Jungian idea of the [Collective unconscious](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Collective_unconscious "wikipedia:Collective unconscious"), in which all minds are ultimately connected together in a great Collective unconscious, which underpins and surrounds the unconscious mind. Ideas and archetypes exist within this unconscious mind, and can filter into the conscious mind. Brandon Sanderson used this as inspiration, with beings being Connected by an unconscious "force" manifested through social relations and other such relations.[^141] [^70]
