## DaiKeen

A **DaiKeen** is a [[Kerzta - The Coppermind|Kerztian]] economic and social grouping similar to the Professions in [[Lossand - The Coppermind|Lossand]]. Members of a DaiKeen indicate which one they belong to with symbols or markings at their foreheads.

## The DaiKeenin

Warrior DaiKeen

The warrior DaiKeen consists of soldiers. Warriors usually wear armor in battle and fight with swords and [zinkallin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Zinkall "Zinkall"). Warriors tattoo an 'X' on their foreheads.[^1]

Priest DaiKeen

The priest DaiKeen teach and administer the [[Ker'reen - The Coppermind|Ker'reen]] religion among Kerztians. They are led by the A'kar, or high priest.[^2] Priests wear a square-shaped scar on their foreheads.[^1]

Merchant DaiKeen

The merchant DaiKeen consists of merchants. The king of Kerzta has been chosen from the merchant DaiKeen in all four [[Kerzta - The Coppermind|Choosings]] over the past 200 years.[^2]

Warrior priest DaiKeen

Recent evidence suggests that a new "warrior priest" DaiKeen may be forming, apparently backed by the A'kar.[^2] These seem to cut X-shaped scars on their foreheads -- often on top of their prior markings.[^1] They have also been seen to shave their heads and wear no armor in battle.
