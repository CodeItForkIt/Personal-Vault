## Yolish Lightweaving

**Yolish Lightweaving** is a manifestation of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] on [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]] that originates prior to the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite|Shattering]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]].[^1] All pure illusion magic is considered [Lightweaving](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lightweaving "Lightweaving").[^2]

## Abilities and Weaknesses

Yolish Lightweaving allows for the creation of optical and auditory illusions.[^3] Lightweavers can use this to make disguises, and to produce light.[^4] [^5] It could also make illusions in smoke such as making images of [people](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Derethil "Derethil") and [ships](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wandersail "Wandersail").[^6] [^7] All illusion magic will work in a similar manner.[^2] Illusion magic is capable of creating auditory and visual manifestations either independent of a Lightweaver (i.e. fixed in place) or linked to a host.[^3] Both require a steady source of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. These manifestations are linked to a person's mind and are largely a mental creation having no physical mass. They are capable of movement. However this is more complicated than a static image and requires greater mental control to function. The range of an image is limited to its proximity to a source of Investiture. As the Lightweaver moves away from an image it will begin to blur and then dissolve when its connection is broken. Increased focus on the part of a Lightweaver can increase the range but not greatly. Lightweaving would be affected by aphantasia.[^8]

Yolish Lightweaving is limited in some ways and may not always function perfectly,[^9] though [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Rosharan Lightweavers]] may be able to help them fully craft an illusion.[^10] Conversely, under certain circumstances, a Yolish Lightweaver may be able to guide a Rosharan Lightweaver's power.[^9]

Yolish Lightweaving does not require one to be bonded to a [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] to use it,[^1] though beyond that its method of [[Initiation - The Coppermind|Initiation]] is unknown. Gaining Rosharan Lightweaving can make Yolish Lightweaving more powerful and removes some of its limitations.[^11] [^9]
