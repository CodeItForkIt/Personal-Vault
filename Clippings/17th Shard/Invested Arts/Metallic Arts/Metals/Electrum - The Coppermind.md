## Electrum

**Electrum** is the internal temporal pushing metal alloy made from [[Gold - The Coppermind|gold]] and [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|silver]]; it is the alloy paired with gold in the Metallic Arts. An Allomancer burning electrum is able to see possible futures of oneself, up to several seconds. Feruchemists can use electrum to store determination, and when used as a Hemalurgic spike, electrum steals Enhancement Allomantic powers.

## Alloy Composition

The electrum used in the Metallic Arts is forty-five percent gold, fifty-five percent silver.[^1]

## Allomantic Use

An electrum [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Misting]] is known as an **Oracle**. Burning electrum will reveal possible futures of the Oracle up to several seconds, by casting shadows of oneself like [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]] shadows.[^2] One use of electrum is its ability to counter [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]], in this regard and the fact that gold and silver are relatively cheap compared to atium. [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") called it "poor man's atium".

While burning electrum the Oracle's future will not be revealed to enemy Allomancers burning atium. Instead, it will cause the Oracle's observed atium shadow to split, preventing the enemy Allomancer from predicting one's actions.[^3]

If burned with [[Duralumin - The Coppermind|duralumin]], it would likely have a similar effect to atium, in that the user would be able to see into the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]].[^4]

## Feruchemical Use

An electrum [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Ferring]] is known as a **Pinnacle**. Electrum is used to store determination. A Pinnacle filling an electrummind will become depressed, and can tap it later to enter a manic state.[^2]

## Hemalurgic Use

When used as a Hemalurgic spike, electrum steals Enhancement Allomantic powers ([[Chromium - The Coppermind|chromium]], [[Nicrosil - The Coppermind|nicrosil]], [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]], and [[Duralumin - The Coppermind|duralumin]]).[^5]

### Bind Points

- Allomantic Duralumin: Allomantic duralumin can be stolen by placing an electrum spike between two ribs.[^6]

## History

The [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Lord Ruler]] hid electrum from public knowledge since it would completely destroy the value of atium and the system of atium economy. Vin later found descriptions of this metal on a metal plate in the [Urteau](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Urteau "Urteau") [storage cavern](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Storage_cavern "Storage cavern").[^7]

## Trivia

- Brandon made a mistake while writing [The Well of Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Well_of_Ascension "The Well of Ascension"). The Feruchemical effects of [brass](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brass "Brass") and electrum were to be swapped. By the time he realized this was forgotten, however, the book was already in print. Brandon didn't want to retcon this, so the effect of electrum was canonized.[^8]
