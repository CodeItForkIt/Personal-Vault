## Duralumin

**Duralumin** is the internal enhancement pushing metal. An Allomancer burning duralumin is able to greatly enhance other metals they are burning by releasing all of the power of the metals at once instead of over time. Feruchemists can use duralumin to store [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]], and when used as a Hemalurgic spike, duralumin steals Connection and [[Identity - The Coppermind|Identity]].

Duralumin is the alloy paired with [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]] in the Metallic Arts.

## Alloy Composition

The duralumin used in the Metallic Arts is ninety-six percent aluminum, four percent [[Copper - The Coppermind|copper]].[^1]

## Allomantic Use

A duralumin [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Misting]] is known as a **Duralumin Gnat**. An [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancer]] burning duralumin instantly uses up any metals being burned simultaneously, releasing an enormous burst of power from those metals.[^2] [^3] Due to this, the more metal they have on them upon burning duralumin, the greater the burst of power.[^3] Duralumin itself is not completely consumed like metals it is burned in concert with, but it still continues to burn away at a steady rate.[^4] [^5] A Duralumin Gnat gains nothing from their powers.[^2] The use of duralumin could seriously hurt a Allomancer due to the explosion of power.[^5] [^6]

Burning duralumin while also [[Compounding - The Coppermind|Compounding]] any other metal does not gain anything other than time, and is quite dangerous due to the swift effects of the duralumin.[^7]

- [[Iron - The Coppermind|Iron]] / [[Steel - The Coppermind|Steel]]: While Pulling or Pushing with duralumin, an Allomancer gains an explosion of power, but grants them a far greater base strength, allowing them to Pull or Push on objects far heavier or [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested]] than normal.[^8] [^6] [^9] [^10] These Pulls/Pushes could become so strong it can feel like the Allomancer is being torn apart.[^6] When using duralumin, Allomancers usually burn pewter along with iron/steel to avoid injuries.[^11] A Coinshot could also launch incredibly far with a single Push.[^12]
- [[Tin - The Coppermind|Tin]]: While burning tin, a Tineye risks being shocked by the sensory overload and becoming temporarily numb, blind, and deaf.[^5]
- [[Pewter - The Coppermind|Pewter]]: While burning pewter, a Thug can withstand or deal damage far greater than normal.[^13] A Thug could also briefly resist the effects of drugs, but this does not remove the effects of the drugs long term.[^14]
- [[Zinc - The Coppermind|Zinc]] / [Brass](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brass "Brass"): Pulling or Pushing on emotions while burning duralumin causes the targets to feel an intense Rioting or Soothing of their emotions.[^15] Those Soothed feel a flattening of emotions, making them feel empty and devoid of all feelings.[^16] [^17] Those Rioted feel an intense flare of emotions, making them to be more likely to act.[^15] Duralumin also allows an Allomancer to Riot/Soothe the emotions of a Smoker through a [[Copper - The Coppermind|coppercloud]] momentarily.[^17] When combined with emotional Allomancy, duralumin also allows Allomancers to "break into" and gain control of [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgic]] constructs such as [[Kandra - The Coppermind|kandra]] and [[Koloss - The Coppermind|koloss]], even if they would not normally have enough raw power to do so.[^18] [^19]
- [[Bronze - The Coppermind|Bronze]]: While burning bronze, a Seeker feels Allomantic pulses much louder and more acutely than normal.[^20] With duralumin, Seekers can also feel Allomantic pulses through a coppercloud momentarily, although not as effective as being able to pierce the coppercloud naturally.[^21] This combination also allows emotional Allomancy to work on Smokers.[^22]
- [[Cadmium - The Coppermind|Cadmium]] / [[Bendalloy - The Coppermind|Bendalloy]]: While creating a speed bubble with duralumin, an Allomancer can condense or expand the time that the bubble is affecting. For bendalloy, this makes the time inside the bubble compressed, and from inside, time outside the bubble slows to a complete halt.[^3] [^23] For cadmium, it is likely the opposite effect, expanding the time outside the bubble and being able to skip time at a much faster pace. Cadmium's effect specifically is important for FTL space travel.[^24]
- [[Atium - The Coppermind|Atium]]: While burning atium, an Allomancer is able to see farther into the future, by allowing them to peer into the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]].[^25] [^26] [^27]

Burning duralumin could potentially be used to gain a burst of power from other forms of Investiture, such as [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]] or [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]].[^28] [^29]

## Feruchemical Use

A duralumin [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Ferring]] is known as a **Connector**. Duralumin is used to store Spiritual [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]]. Filling a duraluminmind can be used to reduce other people's awareness of and friendship with the Connector, as these Spiritual Connections become stored away. Tapping it would strengthen Connections or allow the Connector to form relationships faster.[^2]

[[Southern Scadrian - The Coppermind|Southern Scadrians]] use [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|unsealed]] duraluminminds to enable them to communicate with others on foreign lands.[^30] They still retain their native accent when doing so,[^30] though someone could develop a local accent by blanking their [[Identity - The Coppermind|Identity]].[^31] This ability can be used on other planets, though it does not grant the ability to travel between worlds.[^32] Tapping a duraluminmind would also allow someone to overcome the location restriction on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Selish]] Invested Arts; for example, an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") could use [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]] at full power regardless of location, though there are also easier ways to achieve this.[^33] It could also allow a person to become a squire to multiple Orders of [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]], though there are, again, easier ways.[^34]

A duraluminmind would be able to interpret [rhythms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm"), albeit not very accurately.[^35]

### Compounding

A duralumin [[Compounding - The Coppermind|Compounder]] would be able to use duralumin to have a nearly infinite supply of Spiritual Connection. The Compounder would be able to put a [[Hemalurgic chimera - The Coppermind|Hemalurgic]] creature under complete control, similar to what [Soothers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brass "Brass") and [[Zinc - The Coppermind|Rioters]] can do.[^36]

## Hemalurgic Use

When used as a Hemalurgic spike, duralumin steals [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]] and [[Identity - The Coppermind|Identity]].[^37]

## History

During the [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|Final Empire]], the [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Lord Ruler]] kept [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]] and its alloy duralumin from public knowledge to give the [[Steel Inquisitor - The Coppermind|Inquisitors]] an advantage.[^38] Aluminum and duralumin were also unknown to people due to the advanced technology (electrolysis) required to refine it. Without that technology, the Lord Ruler acquired small amounts of aluminum from ashmounts.

[[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]], after being fed aluminum by the Inquisitors, figured there should be an alloy for it, too. Despite the risk to herself, she tested out various alloys of aluminum with [Terion's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Terion "Terion") help, and eventually discovered an alloy that didn't cause sickness when burned -- duralumin. However, since she wasn't burning any other metal at the same time, the duralumin seemed to generate no effect.[^4] Later, while sparring with [Zane](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Zane "Zane"), Vin was stunned to find out the effect of duralumin.[^5]

She carefully kept the secret of duralumin and used it to great advantage on several occasions later in combination with both physical [^6] [^13] [^9] [^11] [^8] and mental [^20] [^16] Allomancy. [TenSoon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/TenSoon "TenSoon") and Zane eventually found out about duralumin, however, allowing Zane to use it against her.[^39]

Duralumin became Allomantically useless in practice over the centuries after the [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|Catacendre]] due to the fading away of Mistborn, though the Southern Scadrians made Feruchemical use of it in their unsealed metalminds. Duralumin's great power has essentially faded into myth. It is primarily associated with the Ascendent Warrior, and only recognized as a thing of scripture.[^40] [^12]

However, knowledge of it was not lost even in the North; it eventually became commonplace with the development of the right metallurgical processes,[^41] [^30] [^40] and once the [Set](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Set "Set") began working with Hemalurgy, it became relevant again to specific people. The Set gave [Dumad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dumad "Dumad") a spike for duralumin Allomancy, which he used effectively in combination with Steelpushing.[^40] [^12]

After Dumad's death, [Wax](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wax "Wax") used the spike on himself and used his newly duralumin-enhanced Steelpushing to reach the [Pewternaut](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pewternaut "Pewternaut") A16.[^42] [^43] [Wayne](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wayne "Wayne") then became Mistborn by burning [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]] and used duralumin with [[Bendalloy - The Coppermind|bendalloy]] in order to prematurely detonate the bomb on board the ship, which required precise timing that would have been impossible to achieve otherwise.[^3]

## Trivia

- [Duralumin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duralumin "wikipedia:Duralumin") is a real alloy of aluminum, and is fairly common.[^44]
- If Vin hadn't had duralumin in the fight at the [Luthadel Assembly](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Luthadel_Assembly "Luthadel Assembly") meeting, both she and [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") would have died.[^13] [^45]
- An alternative ending to *[The Well of Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Well_of_Ascension "The Well of Ascension")* has a conversation about duralumin between Vin and [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend").[^46]
- Brandon had [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") burn both duralumin and [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]] at the [Battle of Hathsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Battle_of_Hathsin "Battle of Hathsin") because a lot of people had asked about the combination.[^47]
