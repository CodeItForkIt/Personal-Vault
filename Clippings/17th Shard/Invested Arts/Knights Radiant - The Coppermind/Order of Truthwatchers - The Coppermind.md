## Order of Truthwatchers

The **Order of Truthwatchers** is an order of the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^2]

The Truthwatchers are [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinders]] who used the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of Progression and Illumination, and they are associated with the emerald [[Polestone - The Coppermind|polestone]]. They form a [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] with [mistspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mistspren "Mistspren").

## Philosophy

> “ *I will seek truth.*”
> 
> —The core philosophy of the Truthwatchers [^3]

Those who join the Truthwatchers generally subscribe to a philosophy of finding the ultimate truth and sharing it.[^4] [^3] The order is concerned with the fundamental truths of the universe, and whether or not those in power are being truthful with the people they lead.[^5] [^6] A small percentage of Truthwatchers are greatly concerned with the actions of the powerful and might be likened to investigative reporters.[^4] The order will make their opinions known loudly and forcefully, particularly if they think someone in power is abusing that power or lying about fundamental truths.[^3] Truthwatchers are open to changing their beliefs if new information is presented.[^3] They enjoy differences in opinion and engaging in debates. They tend to attract scientists, scholars, or thinkers of all types.[^7]

Divine attributes of the Truthwatchers are Learned and Giving.

## Ideals of the Truthwatchers

The Ideals of the Knights Radiant, also known as the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Immortal Words]], are a set of rules by which the Radiants live. The First Ideal is shared by all of the orders, and is used as a motto for the Knights Radiant as a whole. Each of the orders then have an additional four Ideals that are unique to that order.[^8]

The Ideals of the Order of Truthwatchers all focus on seeking and sharing truth, as well as making sure it is not improperly exploited. However, unlike the [[Order of Lightweavers - The Coppermind|Lightweavers]], who pursue personal truths, the Truthwatchers are concerned more with uncovering the greater secrets of the universe.[^4] [^3] An Enlightened Truthwatcher will have different oaths from a standard one.[^9]

### The First Ideal

> “ *Life before death, strength before weakness, journey before destination.* ”
> 
> —The First Ideal of the Knights Radiant [^8]

The Truthwatchers share the first ideal with all other orders of Radiants, which serves as the motto of the organization as a whole.[^8] The words are broadly interpreted to state the value of life, the importance of strength and service and the importance of the methods by which one accomplishes a task.

### The Second Ideal

> “ *I will seek truth wherever it is hidden.* ”
> 
> —The Second Ideal of the Truthwatcher order [^3]

This oath is centered around searching for new knowledge.[^3] Truthwatchers may choose which truths to seek, and success or failure is not important to the search, only that they seek to learn.

### The Third Ideal

> “ *I will reveal truth to all who seek it.* ”
> 
> —The Third Ideal of the Truthwatcher order [^3]

This oath focuses on knowledge being available for everyone.[^3] Truthwatchers believe that hoarding secrets harms the greater world, and this Ideal pushes Truthwatchers to share truth with anyone it would benefit. This oath does not require a Truthwatcher to share everything they have learned with everyone, but instead focuses on truths that were deliberately hidden from those who would benefit from it. It must be reached before a Truthwatcher can manifest their mistspren as a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade").[^10] [^11]

### The Fourth Ideal

The specific words of this Ideal are individualized, but the overarching theme is acknowledging the consequences of sharing hidden secrets and forbidden truths, and then sharing them despite the risks to themselves.[^12] Upon achieving this Ideal, a Truthwatcher gains their [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]], which is formed of [[Spren - The Coppermind|concentrationspren]].[^10] [^13]

## Abilities

The Truthwatchers use the Surges of **Progression** and **Illumination**.

### Progression

[[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|**Progression**]] can be manipulated to alter the growth and healing of organisms.[^14] It is described as "The Surge of Growth and Healing, or Regrowth". This ability heals the damage done to the body and the soul of a person instantly and without any scarring. There is a degree of skill involved for more advance healings.[^15]

### Illumination

[[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|**Illumination**]] is used to craft illusions by weaving light, sound and various waveforms.[^14] This can be used to change their appearance for disguises or other purposes, create illusions of walls and rocks to hide behind and in, and to create illusions to confuse or distract.[^16] The Truthwatcher [Honorbearer](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorbearer "Honorbearer") also summons a globe of light in her hands, which makes the shadows come alive and amplifies the voices from the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]] [^17] [^18] of people that [[Szeth - The Coppermind|Szeth]] killed that he hears,[^19] though it is unclear whether this ability relates to Illumination alone.

### Enlightened

Truthwatchers who have had their mistspren Enlightened by [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat]] have different abilities from standard Truthwatchers. Progression remains normal, still allowing the healing of themselves and others.[^20] [^21] Where standard Truthwatchers can use Illumination to create illusions, Enlightened ones can only summon lights, which can sometimes act in strange and unnatural ways.[^16] During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field - The Coppermind|Battle of Thaylen Field]], Renarin makes the spirit of a wounded [[Thunderclast - The Coppermind|thunderclast]] flee her body by drawing a large amount of Stormlight,[^22] though how he did so and whether an ordinary Truthwatcher would also have this ability remains unclear. Enlightened Truthwatchers are able to influence some control over the Spiritual Realm while inside of it.[^23] They are also capable of hearing Odium's [Rhythms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm").[^24]

Future Sight

This bond also allows the Radiant to see the visions of past and future events.[^25] [^26] Renarin stated that this is because Truthwatchers are unique because "they see".[^27] Visions are sometimes shown through dark glass and show possible outcomes, but are not always accurate.[^28] [^29] Those without a strong Connection to the realms are not able to see this glass normally, however, it has been shown that Enlightened Truthwatchers can bring other Enlightened Truthwatchers into visions.[^24] They do not come specifically from Odium, but since their powers come from him it is possible that his desires influence what the visions will show.[^28] Ba-Ado-Mishram has also been shown to be able to affect visions in small ways, although this might be because she was trapped in the Spiritual Realm.[^23] Vision are more likely to happen when the rhythms of Roshar swell.[^24] Seeing visions also have the added benefit of causing others who see the future to see the Truthwatcher and those close to them as a black spot, as those who see the future cause a conflict in [[Fortune - The Coppermind|Fortune]].[^30] [^31] [^32] The ability to see the future is related to the [Voidbinding chart](https://coppermind.net/wiki/File:TWoK_Rear_Endsheet.jpg "File:TWoK Rear Endsheet.jpg"),[^33] though the precise relationship between the powers and Voidbinding remains unclear.[^34]

Sphere Light

Instead of Lightweaving, Enlightened Truthwatchers are able to create a sphere of light the can show people's futures and souls.[^35] [^36] This could show a person an alternate version of themselves, similar to the effect of [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomantic]] [[Gold - The Coppermind|gold]] or [[Malatium - The Coppermind|malatium]].[^35] This manifests as an illusory sphere of light showing the target's alternate self.[^37] This ball of light has also been shown to have other effects, perhaps influencing the soul, such as when Renarin makes the spirit of a wounded [[Thunderclast - The Coppermind|thunderclast]] flee her body with one of these lights.[^22] The light responds better when given a [Command](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Command "Command").[^35]

Nahel Bond Abilities

Enlightened Truthwatchers and mistspren can feel each others emotions with a strong enough bond.[^24] [^38]

### Shardplate Abilities

Truthwatchers' [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]] is formed of [[Spren - The Coppermind|concentrationspren]].[^1] [^13]

## History

### The Ancient Radiants

The ancient Truthwatchers were often scientists, scholars and thinkers; this led to them being perceived as quiet and rather esoteric in their pursuits.[^7] They tended to be reserved, though a group of them was always dedicated to monitoring the actions of the people in power on Roshar, and making their opinions on said actions heard far and wide if necessary. They particularly concerned themselves with abuse of power, as well as finding out the truth, though what constituted "truth" was a subject of some debate.[^4]

Truthwatchers would often engage in enthusiastic discussions on the topic of philosophy, particularly concerning the truth, even if many such conversations would take place only in writing. They were also interested in the more obscure crafts and sciences, such as new [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]] or technologies.[^4]

### True Desolation

Modern Truthwatchers are few and far between, scattered around the world rather than united.[^7]
