## AonDor

**AonDor** is a manifestation of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] located on the [[17th Shard|planet]] [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] that is used by the [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian").[^3] It involves the use of variously shaped [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]], which draw energy from the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] and must be fine-tuned to the landscape.

## Prerequisites

Only an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") can use the [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]] to draw power from the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]].[^4] This means that to use AonDor, a person must originally be of [Aonic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aonic "Aonic") descent.[^5] The person must then be chosen by the [[Shaod - The Coppermind|Shaod]]. Their skin will glow, their hair will turn white, and they gain the ability to draw Aons that channel the Dor.[^3]

It is also possible to form this Connection by being invited into specific groups (presumably Elantrian groups, such as the [[Ire - The Coppermind|Ire]]), though the exact mechanics of this are not yet known.[^6] Someone can also [[Forgery - The Coppermind|stamp]] themselves to become an Elantrian.[^7]

## Drawing Aons

In order to draw a functional Aon, the Elantrian drawing it must intend to do so, or else the glowing lines used to draw the Aon won’t appear.[^4] Elantrians can use their fingers to draw Aons, with each finger giving the lines different widths. Other objects like a quill or stick can also be used to draw Aons.[^8] Aons can also be crafted out of other materials by Elantrians, like stone, wire, or [Light](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Light "Light").[^9] In this way they can make amulets and the like, that have an actual magical effect.[^10] It seems like some technology similar to this, Aonic in origin, has made its way into the Space Age of the cosmere.[^11] Leaving an Aon alone for a short time without completing it will cause it to disappear with no effects.[^12]

Each Aon has a unique effect, which is determined by its shape. The Aon "Aon" is the starting point for every other Aon.[^13] It appears that the energy can only get to the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] in certain pulse lengths and frequencies, so an Aon’s shape only allows for a certain frequency to escape, which has a specific effect.[^14] If an Aon is the wrong shape, it may do nothing, and the Aon will flash once and fade away.[^12] An Aon drawn incorrectly with an omission of basic lines would begin the process of the Aon, but stop in the middle of the process due to not having enough energy from the Dor.[^13] Sometimes if it is targeted at a person or group of people, it could cause them to appear like a Reod Elantrian.[^15] The size of an Aon can affect its power.[^16] Aons can also be combined together.[^17] Aons can be given more control or strength when the width of the lines match the proper locations that correspond with [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]].[^8]

Aons can only be destroyed by an Elantrian. Unless destroyed by an Elantrian an Aon will continue to function even if it is scuffed or erased.[^18] It is possible for AonDor to negate effects granted by [[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]].[^19]

## Aon Modifiers

While Aons are undoubtedly powerful, in order to specify certain traits, like how long a column of fire from an Aon Ehe will last, or where an Aon Tia will take you, additional lines must be drawn around the central Aon to modify its effects.[^10] [^13]

### Types of Modifiers

- Linking Aons - used to link subsections of Aon lists together, it controls the timing of when other Aons take effect.[^13] [^20]
- Setting off different chains of Aons.[^21]
- Modifying power levels of Aon effects.[^8]
- Modifying the range of Aons.[^22]

## Aon Equations

In extremely advanced uses of AonDor, multiple Aons can be used in conjunction to modify each other in Aon equations.[^23] These are used to channel the Dor in the most specific ways allowed by AonDor. With it, Elantrians could fabricate any object out of nothing, although the sheer skill and practice required makes Elantrians who can do this extremely rare.[^23] In addition to its regular effect, it appears that each Aon has a secondary effect that can be used in the Aon equations. Almost any effect in the cosmere can be replicated with the right equation,[^24] though AonDor struggles to fully imitate the effects of persistent bonds or [Anti-Investiture](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Anti-Investiture "Anti-Investiture").[^25]

### Curses

It is possible through complex Aon Equations to lay complex curses upon people with specific effects and conditions, even to the point of transforming someone into a completely different species.[^6] They can also completely change one's personality,[^26] limit their intelligence,[^27] and forbid one from being able to speak of their curse or how to break it.[^27] [^28]

The only known way to break a curse is to fulfill the conditions of the curse.[^6] These curses are difficult to break in any other way, even by the one who created it. Though it is possible to do so through unknown methods or editing the parameters of the conditions.[^29] [^6]

Cursed individuals have a ring of Aons surrounding them, which describes and makes up their curse.[^6] These appear to be difficult or impossible to see by those without access to AonDor.

## Relationship To Arelon

AonDor has a specific tie to the land of Arelon.[^5] The base Aon Aon is shaped like the land of Arelon, with its lines and dot representing the coast, Atad Mountains, and Lake Alonoe.[^12] Every Aon reflects some trait of the land, such as the Dii, the Aon for wood, contain circles where the forests of Arelon fall.[^8] This is a very fluid relationship; as the land changes so do the Aons. When an earthquake created a chasm in the south of Arelon, a new line was required in every Aon to make it functional.[^8] AonDor's power will diminish the further an Elantrian travels from the city of [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)").[^10] [^30]

It is possible to create another city similar to Elantris in other locations on Sel.[^31] The [[Ire - The Coppermind|Ire]] created a base similar to it in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]], though in order for this to work, Investiture from Sel needs to be piped to it.[^32] There are, however, other ways to achieve the same effect.[^33]

AonDor will not normally work on planets other than [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]],[^34] [^35] but it is possible to hack the magic system and get it to work on other planets.[^6] [^36] [^7] A Full [Feruchemist](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Feruchemist "Feruchemist") could use [[Nicrosil - The Coppermind#Feruchemical Use|nicrosil]] to create an [unsealed](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Unsealed_metalminds "Unsealed metalminds") [[Duralumin - The Coppermind#Feruchemical Use|duraluminmind]] [[Southern Scadrian - The Coppermind#Technology|medallion]] that allows an Elantrian to store their [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 18|Connection]] to Elantris for magic to work at full potential far from Elantris.[^37]

## History

### The First Elantrians

King [Rhashm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Raoshem "Raoshem") led his people from other lands to Arelon, discovering the city of Elantris completely abandoned. Despite superstition about Elantris being haunted, Rhashm made it his capital. After a few decades, Princess [Elashe](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elashe "Elashe"), one of his descendants, was the first Aonic person to be taken by the Shaod to become an Elantrian.[^38]

### The Reod

For centuries, AonDor functioned fine, Elantrians ruling Arelon from Elantris.[^39] However, when the earthquake caused the Chasm, the Elantrians became the target of the now malformed Rao that made up Elantris and the Outer Cities. This caused them to become cursed and foul looking, as well as robbing them of their access to the magic system.[^8] They didn’t know where the new line was, so they couldn’t use the magic. Most were slaughtered mercilessly by the terrified citizenry.[^40]

### Restoration of Elantris

[[Raoden - The Coppermind|Raoden]] managed to discover what was wrong with the AonDor during his imprisonment in the city, and he repaired the Rao and restored the city to its former glory.[^41] He, an Elantrian, took his place as king of Arelon, ruling from the city of Elantris.[^21]

### The Sorceress of Lumar

At some point in the future, [[Riina - The Coppermind|Riina]] settled on [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]] and became feared as the Sorceress. One source of her infamy as her various curses, which she laid upon people using complex AonDor arrays.[^26] [^6] [^42]
