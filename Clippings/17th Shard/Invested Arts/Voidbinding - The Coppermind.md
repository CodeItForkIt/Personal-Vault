## Voidbinding

**Voidbinding** is a manifestation of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] of which little is known. It is connected to [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] and his [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]],[^2] and predicting the future is said to be of it.[^3] [Khriss's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khriss "Khriss") writings in the [Ars Arcanum](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ars_Arcanum "Ars Arcanum") states that there are ten levels of Voidbinding and that it is cousin to the [[Old Magic - The Coppermind|Old Magic]].[^4]

## Prerequisites

Not much is known about how one becomes a Voidbinder under normal circumstances. It has been said that Voidbinding usually, though not always, originates with the Unmade.[^2] Parts of Voidbinding have been looked into in the past, but it likely has not been fully explored.[^5]

A possible example of a method of becoming a Voidbinder is by bonding with a [[Spren - The Coppermind#Corrupted Spren|corrupted]] [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel spren]]. [[Renarin Kholin - The Coppermind|Renarin Kholin's]] ability to see the future seems to be connected to Voidbinding,[^6] [^7] and it is likely he gained it through his bond to the corrupted spren [[Glys - The Coppermind|Glys]].[^8] How much Renarin's abilities relate to Voidbinding is currently unclear. Another method of gaining access to a Voidbinding ability is through taking a [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regal]] form, especially [Nightform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nightform "Nightform"). This form is known to give the ability of future-sight as well.[^9] The surges granted by Odium are sometimes referred to as Voidbinding.[^10]

It is "theoretically possible" for a [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrial]] to imitate at least some parts of Voidbinding.[^11]

## Mythology

One of the most common powers associated with Voidbinding is prophecy and foresight.[^3] [^12] According to the [ardents](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ardent "Ardent"), even the [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") denounced its use. Whether or not this is true or a myth is yet to be seen, although [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah]] believes this to be true. Regardless, in [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") nations, *any* type of prediction is considered to be taboo.[^13] The ardents worried that [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar's]] [visions](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dalinar%27s_visions "Dalinar's visions") were connected to Voidbinding, although since the visions come from [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]], that was an unfounded concern.[^14]

The Vorin reluctance to guess the future led to the rise of games like [breakneck](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Breakneck "Breakneck"), that don't require directly guessing the future.[^13] Many Vorins are distrustful of [stormwardens](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormwarden "Stormwarden"), since they predict when [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorms]] are going to occur.[^15]

## Abilities

Very little is known about the abilities of Voidbinding. While Khriss says that there are ten levels to Voidbinding and refers to "Voidbindings",[^16] it is unknown what this means.

[[Renarin Kholin - The Coppermind|Renarin's]] ability to see the future is related to the [Voidbinding chart](https://coppermind.net/wiki/File:TWoK_Rear_Endsheet.jpg "File:TWoK Rear Endsheet.jpg"),[^6] though the precise relationship between his powers and Voidbinding remains unclear.[^7]

While details on the ability are sparse, Renarin's future sight is shown to be imperfect, as the events he foresaw during the [[Battle of Thaylen Field - The Coppermind|Battle of Thaylen Field]] did not come to pass.[^17] It seems to also in some way be fueled by [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]].[^6]

### Voidish Surges

There are glyphs on the Voidbinding chart that share position with and are variations of the Surge glyphs on the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]] chart. Instead of the axial symmetry of the Surge glyphs, the glyphs on the Voidbinding chart exhibit rotational symmetry.[^18] [^19] It may be that these glyphs represent either alternatives to, or variant expressions of, the Surges in Surgebinding.

As Renarin Kholin is apparently unable to use the Surge of [Illumination](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illumination "Illumination") in the manner normal [[Order of Truthwatchers - The Coppermind|Truthwatchers]] do,[^20] it is possible that his visions of the future come from this hypothetical "Voidish" expression of the Surge of Illumination.

### Voidish Essences

The glyphs in the Voidbinding chart whose location correspond to the Ten Essences chart are polar transformation of standard glyphs, some of which are similar to glyphs for the ten fundamental surges [^21]. Whether or not this is significant is unclear.

### Regals

[[Regal - The Coppermind#Nightform|Nightform]], one of the [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regal]] forms of power, is known to have the Voidbinding power of future-sight.[^22] [^9]
