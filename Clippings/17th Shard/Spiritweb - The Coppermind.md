## Spiritweb

> “ *The actual outlet of the power is not chosen by the practitioner, but instead is hardwritten into their spiritweb.* ”
> 
> — [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss]] [^1]

The **spiritweb** is the network of [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connections]] and [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] which make up the soul of a creature, place, object, or planet.[^2] Residing in the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]], spiritwebs lack any tangible shape or size, with their borders defined by their [[Identity - The Coppermind|Identity]]. Contained within a spiritweb are various pieces, including **Spiritual DNA**, the **Spiritual aspect**, and one’s **Spiritual Ideal**.[^3] The Spiritweb is Connected to the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical]] and [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive aspects]], and contains a Connection between both.[^4] [^5]

## Mechanics

### Spiritual DNA

A person’s spiritweb, closely related to Spiritual DNA,[^6] makes up their presence in the Spiritual Realm. Measuring and quantifying a spiritweb is possible, if difficult, to do.[^7] Spiritual DNA can be passed from parent to child like regular DNA,[^8] yet does not function exactly the same as identical twins can have different Spiritual DNA.[^9] [^10] [^11] Some ways of utilizing Investiture are written into Spiritual DNA. Spiritwebs can be manufactured, though the process of how is unknown.[^12]

### Damaging and Healing

A person's spiritweb can be damaged either through physical or emotional trauma. This creates "cracks" in the soul. It is easier for Shards to [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invest]] in people who are damaged in this way, as Investiture can enter the soul through these "cracks". This is why [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancers]] need to undergo [[Snapping - The Coppermind|Snapping]] in order to awaken their powers, and is implied to be the reason why [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinders]] tend to have psychological problems.[^13] [^14] [^15] Spiritwebs can be damaged with weapons like hemalurgic spikes and Shardblades. Those wounds can then be healed with a graft of Investiture. The healing process does leave a permanent wound on a person's spiritweb.[^16] Investing an object or person for a long period of time will warp and change their spiritweb.[^17] In humans, this process leads to [[Savant - The Coppermind|Savantism]].[^18]

#### Healing the Physical

Most forms of healing work by using the three aspects of the user's self; their Physical, Cognitive, and Spiritual aspects.[^19] [^20] Healing typically heals the Physical version by matching it to their Spiritual Ideal, which is filtered through the lens of their minds (the Cognitive aspect).[^3] Almost all forms of healing in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], even natural healing, are filtered through the perception of the user, [^21] [^22] which means that if a person hasn't accepted a wound as a part of themselves, they can still heal it regardless of how long they've had it.[^23] However, even if a wound is recent, if the person has changed their mindset to see it as a part of themselves, they will not be able to heal from it.[^24] [^25] An example of this can be found when [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] was unable to heal his slave brand until he swore the fourth oath and recognized that it wasn't a part of him.[^15]

### Permanency

Even after death, a record of the Spiritweb of a person remains in the Spiritual Realm. It is not known if this can be interacted with by Invested Arts such as Hemalurgy.[^26]

## Interactions

All forms of Investiture which one can use are related to the spiritweb. Some manifestations of Investiture are built into a person's Spiritual DNA, such as [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]] and [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]]. Other abilities, such as [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]] and [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]], involve manipulating Spiritual DNA. A few types of Investiture even work by manipulating or overwriting the spiritweb directly, such as [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]].

### The Dor

#### AonDor

[[AonDor - The Coppermind|AonDor]] requires some innate Spiritual DNA, but the [[Shaod - The Coppermind|Shaod]] that turns someone into an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") involves some sort of change to the Spiritual DNA during the transition.[^27] [^28]

#### Forgery

[[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]] works by overlaying an object’s or person’s history and spiritweb with an alternative one. This may indicate that it is possible to remove the effects of a [soulstamp](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulstamp "Soulstamp"), though it is currently unknown.[^29]

### Canticle

#### Heat Sharing

People from the planet [[Canticle - The Coppermind|Canticle]] are able to exchange portions of their spiritweb similar to breath. Unlike breath, heat can only be exchanged with people of Canticle (including by invitation).[^30] It is unclear how this would interact with Allomancy, as the strength of Allomantic power is generally proportional to the amount of [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation's]] investiture an Allomancer contains.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

#### Cinderhearts

When a [cinderheart](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cinderheart "Cinderheart") is inserted deeply into a person's chest it consumes the connections and identity in a person's spiritweb, and connects them to other people who host a cinderheart.[^31] [^32] People who have a cinderheart placed more shallowly become connected to other people who host a cinder heart, but still maintain their original connections and identity.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

### The Metallic Arts

#### Allomancy

Multiple forms of Allomancy relate to the spiritweb. Allomantic [[Zinc - The Coppermind|zinc]] and [brass](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brass "Brass") will work only on creatures with a sufficient level of sapience and work better on creatures whose spiritweb has been damaged, as with Hemalurgic constructs.[^33] [^34] [^35] The ability to use Allomancy is passed down via Spiritual DNA.[^36]

#### Feruchemy

Several of the Feruchemical Attributes are deeply connected to the spiritweb, especially the Spiritual metals such as [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]] and [[Duralumin - The Coppermind|duralumin]], which allow for Identity and Connection, respectively, to be stored. Similarly to Allomancy, the ability to use Feruchemy is passed down through Spiritual DNA.[^37]

#### Hemalurgy

In Hemalurgy, portions of the spiritweb seem to be ripped off of one person and grafted onto another person's spiritweb.[^38] This causes lasting damage to the spiritweb of the creature in which a Hemalurgic Spike is placed.[^39]

### Surgebinding

A [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinder's]] [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] involves a [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] attaching themselves to a person's spiritweb, merging more fully as the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinder]] progresses in their [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Oaths]].[^40] <sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

#### Bondsmithing

A [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmith]] that is bonded to the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]] is able to read a person's spiritweb.[^41] The person's spiritweb appears to the Bondsmith as faintly glowing lines of varying thickness depending on the strength of the connection. It is currently unknown if other Bondsmiths share this ability.

#### Soulcasting

Although it does not directly alter the spiritweb, [[Soulcasting - The Coppermind|Soulcasting]] can alter a spiritweb if the Cognitive aspect stays altered for long enough.[^42]

#### Regrowth

[[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Regrowth]] allows for healing both the Physical form of a living thing and its spiritweb, as does a sufficient amount of Stormlight. This likely functions by restoring one’s physical form to the state of their Spiritual Ideal.[^19] Regrowth can even go so far as to reattach the soul of one recently deceased.[^43] [^44]

### Shardblades

A [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") can be stolen with Hemalurgy because it is Connected to a person's spiritweb.[^45] [^46] When a Shardblade cuts through living matter its edge fuzzes, leaving the physical body uninjured.[^47]

### Fabrials

There are fabrials that are capable of accurately [reading a person's spiritweb](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aura_fabrial "Aura fabrial"). However, these devices tend to go haywire if the person being read is heavily Invested such as a [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]], [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian"), or [[Yoki-hijo - The Coppermind|yoki-hijo]].[^48]
