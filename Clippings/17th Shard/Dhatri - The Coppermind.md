## Dhatri

> “ *There are potential allies out there. Moonlight's world, perhaps. Or the land of the aethers. Hell, maybe even Mythos.* ”

**Dhatri** [^2] [^3] is a [[17th Shard#Planets|planet]] in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] that is the origin of the [[Aether - The Coppermind|aethers]].[^4] [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] mentioned the planet to [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] as a potential ally for [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^1]

## History

At an unknown point before 348 [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|PC]], it was struck by a force or entity known as the [[Aether - The Coppermind|dark aether]], leaving the planet inhospitable to aetherbound.[^5] [^6]

At some point it had a [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]], but at an unknown point before the discovery of the perpendicularity on [[First of the Sun - The Coppermind|First of the Sun]] was made public, it no longer had one. Following the law of commerce, travel to Dhatri became very expensive.[^7] Dhatri is known to sell aethers, although they are expensive and heavily regulated.[^8]

## Culture

### Ethnicity, Fashion and Other

At least some people from Dhatri have brown skin, with light tan undertones and black hair, which is sometimes worn in a braid. Dhatrians often wear bright, colorful clothing. They also have their own flowing script.[^7] Having a dead aether is seen as terrible luck.[^9]

### Religion

#### Gods

They are known to worship the aethers as gods, which they believe predate not only the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]], but [Adonalsium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adonalsium "Adonalsium") in its entirety.[^5] They believe that aethers made all people, creating them to each think differently from one another.[^10]

#### Rebirth

They also believe humans were gifted with rebirth by the aethers to encourage, bless, and uplift their fellow people.[^11] Those that follow the aethers also hold that people return to the aethers upon their deaths to reflect upon their choices in life.[^6] It is believed that people who have made poor choices are cursed to wait a long time until their next rebirth.[^11] They often swear with phrases such as "by the first aether".[^12]

## Politics

It has at least twelve known kingdoms, each one seemingly associated with a specific aether.[^11] The exact nature of their governments is unknown, but they have a position known as a Raj, and a body known as the [Coriander Court](https://coppermind.net/edit/Coriander_Court?redlink=1 "Coriander Court (page does not exist)").[^11] It seems that Dhatri is part of a political entity known as the Dhatrian Planetary Network. It is unclear what this entails however.[^7]

## Trivia

- The planet is a "12 world" just like [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] is a 10-world and [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] is a 16-world.[^13]
- The planet is inspired by the Indian subcontinent.[^14]
- Brandon considers it to be one of the "core planets",[^15] [^16] and is planning a series set on the world.[^17]
- Brandon is being assisted in the world-building for Dhatri by individuals named Kalyani and Rahul.[^2] [^18]
