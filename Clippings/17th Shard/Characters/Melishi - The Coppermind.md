## Melishi

**Melishi** is a historical [Knight Radiant](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Knight_Radiant "Knight Radiant") of the [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Order of Bondsmiths]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^2]

## Attributes and Abilities

Melishi was bonded to the [[Sibling - The Coppermind|Sibling]],[^1] which allowed him to use the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of [Tension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tension "Tension") and [Adhesion](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adhesion "Adhesion").[^3] The Sibling was exceptionally selective regarding their bonded human, and mentions taking years to find a human that fit them exactly.[^4]

Melishi can presumably utilize the Surges similarly to others of his Order. For example, the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]] says that Melishi was able to see the [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connections]] between people as Dalinar does.[^5] He also seems to be an expert regarding [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]], based on the nature of Urithiru and the protections he put in place for the Sibling.[^1]

## History

> “ *\[Melishi\] could make no specific account of his process; it was related to the very nature of the Heralds and their divine duties, an attribute the Bondsmiths alone could address.* ”
> 
> — *[Words of Radiance](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Words_of_Radiance_\(in-world\) "Words of Radiance (in-world)")*, chapter 30, page 18 [^2]

Melishi is thought to have lived several thousand years ago, and was likely the only Bondsmith of his generation.[^6] Like the other Radiants, he fought against the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]] during the [[False Desolation - The Coppermind|False Desolation]]; the book *[Words of Radiance](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Words_of_Radiance_\(in-world\) "Words of Radiance (in-world)")* stated that he was determined to destroy them using abilities unique to the Bondsmith order.[^2] His plan was related to the nature of the [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") and he was not able to fully explain it to the other orders of Radiants.[^2]

The [Urithiru gem archive](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Urithiru_gem_archive "Urithiru gem archive") held some additional details about Melishi's plan. Melishi and other scholar Radiants of the time believed that the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]] had [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connected]] to the [parsh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Parsh "Parsh") [^7] and was supplying them with [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]], facilitating their [[Regal - The Coppermind#Forms of Power|Forms of Power]].[^8] The Radiants intended to capture Ba-Ado-Mishram using Melishi's abilities and a [perfect gemstone](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Perfect_gemstone "Perfect gemstone"),[^9] [^10] denying them Voidlight and thus bringing the war to an end.[^11] [^12] However, [Naze-daughter-Kuzodo](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Naze-daughter-Kuzodo "Naze-daughter-Kuzodo") was concerned about the unintended side effects of Melishi's actions.[^8] The specific outcome of Melishi's plan is not known, but the events surrounding this conflict are believed to be the basis of the legendary [[False Desolation - The Coppermind|False Desolation]].[^13] [[Sylphrena - The Coppermind|Sylphrena]] comments to [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] that when the parsh were "conquered", their Connection and [[Identity - The Coppermind|Identity]] were damaged, creating what was known as " [slaveform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Slaveform "Slaveform") " for several millennia.[^14]

> “ *I hate humans. Humans twist what is said and always make themselves out to be right. How long until you demand that I bond a human, give up my freedom, and risk my life? I'm sure you’ll have wonderful explanations as to why I should absolutely do that.* ”
> 
> —The Sibling, to Navani [^15]

Melishi is also the creator of the barrier surrounding the [[Sibling - The Coppermind|Sibling's]] pillar at the heart of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], which is powered by four gemstone nodes throughout the tower. The Stormfather refers to the Sibling being hurt by humanity in the past [^16] and the Sibling expresses significant bitterness towards Melishi, blaming him for their loss of freedom and risking their life.[^1] [^15] The Sibling also had an aversion to fabrials since they involved imprisoning spren.[^17] However, at least some of Melishi's actions proved necessary to protect the Sibling and Urithiru from invasion.[^1] The Sibling somehow knew to end their bond with Melishi voluntarily before the [Recreance](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Recreance "Recreance") so they would not become a [deadeye](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deadeye "Deadeye"),[^18] and they swore to never bond another human, only reluctantly bonding [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani]] out of necessity.[^17]
