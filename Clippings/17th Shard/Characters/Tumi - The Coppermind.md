## Tumi

> “ *I must find my own way, my own bond.* ”
> 
> —Tumi [^1]

**Tumi** is a [mistspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mistspren "Mistspren") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], bonded to [[Rlain - The Coppermind|Rlain]]. He's one of the true spren that have been Enlightened and transformed by [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat]].[^2]

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *Our bond will be strong, and you will be wondrous.* ”
> 
> —Tumi [^2]

Very little is known about Tumi's appearance. During the occupation of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], he hid in the [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemheart]] of a [[Cremling - The Coppermind|cremling]], remaining invisible.[^2] If he's similar in appearance to the other Enlightened [mistspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mistspren "Mistspren"), [[Glys - The Coppermind|Glys]], he likely appears as a red crystal dripping light upwards in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]].[^3] To [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat's]] sight, he looks like a glowing, shimmering light, constantly changing shape.[^1]

He's not hostile to [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] the way other [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] are, and considers it an honor to bond Rlain, even thinking him awe-inspiring. He's interested in exploring new things, even when they're potentially dangerous: he willingly underwent Sja-anat's transformation and expressed strong interest in the [Rhythm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm") of War, wanting to share the news of it with the rest of his siblings.[^2] He reveres Sja-anat as his mother, and is grateful to her for the opportunity to see the world from a different perspective. He's also supportive of Rlain and hopeful about their bond, convinced that it will be something wonderful. He remains comforting to everyone he knows, regardless of their relative power. However, he is also cautious; he hides while in Urithiru, and follows Rlain for a while before introducing himself.[^4] [^2] [^1]

## Abilities and Attributes

![[Tumi---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Marie Seeberger](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Marie_Seeberger "Coppermind:Artists/Marie Seeberger")

Tumi, as he might appear in Shadesmar

Tumi can hide from sight of others by entering gemhearts of cremlings; he seems to have some control over the creatures this way, guiding them on a specific path. This allows him to stay hidden from [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]] and [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] alike, even those designed to detect [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiant]] activity.[^2] At times, his speech pattern is somewhat similar to that of the [inkspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Inkspren "Inkspren"), using phrases like "salvation will be".[^4]

### Nahel bond

Tumi is a [mistspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mistspren "Mistspren"), and as such grants Rlain the abilities of the [[Order of Truthwatchers - The Coppermind|Order of Truthwatchers]]. However, he was corrupted by [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat]], changing the properties of the bond.[^2] While he should still grant Rlain the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surge]] of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Progression]], letting him heal others and grow plants, it's likely Rlain's [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Illumination]] will not function as it would for ordinary Truthwatchers.[^5] Most notably, he grants Rlain the ability to see visions of the future, and seems able to induce them, or at least encourage them to appear. Like with Glys, the visions take form of images made out of what looks like stained glass.[^4]

## History

### Enlightenment

> “ *I will go, Mother. To the tower, to this man Mraize, as you have promised.* ”

While the circumstances around Tumi's transformation are unknown, it seems he deliberately approached [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat]] to be Enlightened.[^2] When that happened is unknown; however, a year into the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind 1|True Desolation]], [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Sja-anat]] sent him to [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] to investigate members of the [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]] for a possible bond, due to [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize's]] efforts -- through [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] -- to get the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] to cooperate with him.[^6] While Sja-anat was hesitant to let Tumi go, wary of both Mraize and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], Tumi insisted on forming a bond and set out.[^1]

At roughly the same time, [[Renarin Kholin - The Coppermind|Renarin Kholin]], the only other Truthwatcher to have bonded an Enlightened spren, convinced his father to allow "corrupted" [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinders]] into the ranks of [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]]. When Glys asked who else would join their ranks, Renarin almost immediately suggested [[Rlain - The Coppermind|Rlain]], the only [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] within [[Bridge Four - The Coppermind|Bridge Four]].[^7] [^2] Somehow, Glys managed to pass this request on to Sja-anat, who in turn sent it onwards to Tumi. As such, the mistspren started looking into Rlain, examining him as a potential bondmate.[^4]

### Bonding Rlain

> “ *I have watched you and seen your worthiness. Speak the Words, and do not despair.* ”
> 
> —Tumi [^4]

Before Tumi could form a bond, the [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] and their forces invaded [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], the Knights Radiant were rendered unconscious, and Rlain was locked up for being an essai, or "human-lover".[^8] To hide from secretspren, Tumi entered the [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemheart]] of one of the cremlings inhabiting the tower, and began following Rlain.[^2] At some point, Rlain noticed it, and grew paranoid about the cremling's presence, suspecting that it was a [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]] trying to spy on him.[^9]

When the revolt against the Fused began in the tower, Tumi started following the group closer by, directing his cremling to go after Rlain as the singer led his group, including humans, [[Venli - The Coppermind|Venli's]] singers and Fused, to escape Urithiru. Impressed by Rlain's actions in bringing the three groups together, Tumi chose to bond him, and informed [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]], Venli's spren, about it. When Rlain asked Venli if she'd be willing to lend him one of the [Reachers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Reacher "Reacher") following her, she passed on the news: Rlain had already been claimed by another spren.[^4]

It was then that Tumi chose to reveal himself. Showing Rlain a vision of [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] swearing the Fourth Ideal and [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] coming alight, he bestowed the title of Bridger of Minds onto him and implored him to speak the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Immortal Words]] and form a bond.[^2] Rlain did so, becoming a Truthwatcher; from then on out, the two could communicate mentally, without speaking out loud.[^2]

Over the course of the following day, Tumi learned about Rhythm of War, combining [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor's]] and Odium's Rhythms similarly to how Tumi himself combined Honor and Odium's [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]], and grew interested in sharing news of it with his siblings. He also explained to Rlain that he approached him for a bond at Renarin's suggestion, though it was Rlain's own actions that convinced him it was worthwhile.[^2]
