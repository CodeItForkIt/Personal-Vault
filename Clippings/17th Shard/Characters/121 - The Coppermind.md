**121** is a [highspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Highspren "Highspren") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. He is bonded to [[Nale - The Coppermind|Nale]].[^1]

He does not approve of close relationships between spren and their [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiant]]; he thinks that these make the spren an "auxiliary" to human will. [12124](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Auxiliary "Auxiliary") likely derived his new name from that idea, as much of his treatment of [[Szeth - The Coppermind|Szeth]] was influenced by 121's such opinions.[^2]

121 and Nale have reached the Fifth Ideal of the Skybreakers.[^3]

When in [shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") form,[^4] [^5] he resembles Nale's " [swordglyph](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Glyphs/Index#Fundamental_Glyphs "Glyphs/Index") ".[^6] Any relation between the two is unknown.
