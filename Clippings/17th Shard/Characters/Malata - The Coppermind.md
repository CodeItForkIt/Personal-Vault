## Malata

> “ *Someone should have warned me how fun this would be. I’d have tried way harder to land the job.* ”
> 
> —Malata, regarding her job as the [Diagram's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diagram_\(group\) "Diagram (group)") [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knight Radiant]] [^1]

**Malata** is a woman from [Jah Keved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jah_Keved "Jah Keved") and a member of the [Diagram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diagram_\(group\) "Diagram (group)"). She is also a [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knight Radiant]] of the [[Order of Dustbringers - The Coppermind|Order of Dustbringers]], bonded to an [Ashspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ashspren "Ashspren") named [[Spark - The Coppermind|Spark]].

## Appearance and Personality

Malata is a Veden [[Lighteyes - The Coppermind|lighteyed]] woman of middle-age, with short, black, boyish hair. Her usual attire is described as a Thaylen style, with a skirt, leggings, and thick boots.[^1] She also wears gloves on both hands.[^2]

Having been informed by Spark of her Order's distaste for the term **Dustbringer**, she has adopted the Order's preferred epithet **Releaser** for herself,[^3] [^2] although those around her still refer to her as a Dustbringer.[^4] [^5]

Malata behaves in a very confident manner even in the presence of royalty. She is not afraid to challenge social conventions when she sees fit, as when she exposes her [safehand](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Safehand "Safehand") while demonstrating her powers. She does not seem to care about the ethical issues of the Diagram's actions, or that her spren wants to punish humans for the [[Day of Recreance - The Coppermind|Recreance]]. Rather, she seems to only be concerned about enjoying her work.[^1]

Whether or not this matches the attitude of ancient Releasers is currently unknown, although the [Diagram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diagram_\(literature\) "Diagram (literature)") indicates that Dustbringers are the most likely Order of Knights Radiant to accept the Diagram's cause.[^5]

## Attributes and Abilities

As a Releaser, Malata has access to the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Division]] and [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Abrasion]], although so far she has only demonstrated Division, the Surge of decay. Using Division, she can cause objects to degrade, burn, or turn to dust just by touching them with her bare hand. She has shown enough skill in this that she could quickly make an intricate engraving on wood via controlled burning.[^1]

She has also progressed enough in her [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Oaths]] to turn Spark into a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade"),[^2] which she has been using to control [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgates]].

## History

At some point after creating the [Diagram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diagram_\(group\) "Diagram (group)"), King [[Taravangian - The Coppermind|Taravangian]] of [[Kharbranth - The Coppermind|Kharbranth]] formed and managed a project to have some Diagram members attempt to bond a [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]].[^1] Malata was supposedly part of this project, although she claims to have approached Taravangian only because of a suggestion by [[Spark - The Coppermind|Spark]], implying that she was already bonded prior to joining Taravangian's team.[^4]

She was part of Taravangian's contingent in [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin's coalition]]. With some guidance from [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani Kholin]], Malata was able to use Spark to unlock and activate the Veden [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]], transporting herself and the rest of the contingent to [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], the base of operations for the coalition.[^2]

Having access to a Radiant [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade"), Malata was often given the responsibility of opening Oathgates for members of the coalition. Malata accepted this role obediently, although she once expressed distrust against the Alethi and was apparently skeptical of the claims regarding the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind 1|True Desolation]].[^4]

The Diagram later decided that Taravangian must replace Dalinar as leader of the coalition, and had Malata spy on the Blackthorn using Spark.[^5] Through this, they learned about Dalinar's visions where he talked to [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]]. They used the information about these visions, along with other secrets they discovered, to undermine the coalition's trust in Dalinar.[^6]

Later on, while the [[Battle of Thaylen Field - The Coppermind|Battle of Thaylen Field]] was taking place, Malata opened Kholinar's [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] upon orders from the Diagram to allow a strike force of Odium's [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] army into Urithiru, where they battled with Alethi soldiers. An agent of the Diagram disguised as a [[Bridge Four - The Coppermind|Bridge Four]] member was also able to enter Urithiru and steal [[Jezrien - The Coppermind|Jezrien's]] [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblade]] from [Eth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Eth "Eth"), killing him and injuring [Rock](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rock "Rock") and [Bisig](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bisig "Bisig") in the process. All this was part of Taravangian's plan to appeal to Odium so that he would agree to sparing part of humanity on Roshar.[^7] [^8] [^9]

In Urithiru, Malata is usually present in meetings of the coalition.[^10] She's there in the meeting where the coalition agrees to go and fight in [[Emul - The Coppermind|Emul]] and send an expedition to [Lasting Integrity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lasting_Integrity "Lasting Integrity") to convince more [honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren") to bond.[^10]

When Taravangian decided to disband the Diagram, he believed that Malata would accept the truth of it's disbanding, knowing that its purpose had been fulfilled.[^11]
