## Testament

**Testament** is a [deadeye](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deadeye "Deadeye") [Cryptic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cryptic "Cryptic") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] who is bonded with [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]].[^1]

## Appearance and Personality

Unlike living Cryptics, her head pattern is broken, with the same scraping over the eyes seen on other deadeye spren in the center of it. Instead of turning in a smooth pattern, the lines are twisted in jagged patterns, like broken fingers,[^2] giving the head a wilted look.[^3] Her stiff robes look dustier than [[Pattern - The Coppermind|Pattern's]].[^4]

According to Shallan, Testament was a kindly and friendly spren.[^5]

## Abilities and Attributes

Being a Cryptic, Testament is able to form [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bonds]] with humans. This gives the bonded human access to the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of the [[Order of Lightweavers - The Coppermind|Order of Lightweavers]], namely [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Illumination]] and [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Transformation]].

## History

Not much is known about Testament's past. For centuries, she was a friend and a business partner to an [inkspren shopkeeper](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Inkspren_shopkeeper "Inkspren shopkeeper") in [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar").[^2]

In 1165, Testament left [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar") to form a Nahel bond and would eventually bond to [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]]; as Testament felt that bonding to a child without any predispositions would be better for inspiring a new generation of Radiant.[^2] [^6] Testament was attracted to Shallan's lies, the ones where Shallan told herself that she was happy, strong and not terrified despite the abuse happening in her family and because of Shallan's unique parentage: that of being the offspring of a [Herald](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald").[^7] [^6]

In a duration of about two years Shallan and Testament's bond progressed enough for her to summon Testament as a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade"), which she later used to kill her mother in self-defense. Horrified by what she had done, still only a child and in a state of grief and trauma, Shallan broke her bond to Testament the same day, blaming her.[^8] [^9] Later, due to Shallan blocking her memories to cope with the pain, it was assumed that the Cryptic that Shallan was bonded to as a child was Pattern and that their bond had been weakened by the event due to Shallan's resentment of the sword. In reality, Shallan used Testament as a Blade, then abandoned their bond, causing Testament to become a deadeye. Testament, now manifesting in the physical realm as a [Dead Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade#Dead_Shardblades "Shardblade"), was then placed in a strongbox by [Lin Davar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lin_Davar "Lin Davar"), hidden behind a painting in his room. The Shardblade quickly turned to mist after being locked away, as it was still bonded to Shallan.[^8]

Pattern bonded Shallan for the first time years later, although Shallan believed that some of her powers somehow functioned in the gap between her spren bonds.[^1]

Testament's inkspren friend later found her as a deadeye, sitting on a far off island in the east. She seemed to want to travel towards [Nameless](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nameless "Nameless"), where the inkspren set up a shop; Testament stayed nearby.[^2]

In 1173, Shallan summoned Testament as a Dead Shardblade to kill [Tyn](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tyn "Tyn") in self-defense, after Tyn discovered Shallan's identity as [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin's]] ward and attacked her.[^10]

In 1175, Adolin found her in Nameless through [[Mayalaran - The Coppermind|Maya]]. She was later brought to [Lasting Integrity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lasting_Integrity "Lasting Integrity") by the [honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren"). While in Lasting Integrity, Shallan talked to her at least once.[^1] Testament does not hate Shallan for having made her into a deadeye.[^9]

Testament would accompany Shallan on her trip into the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]], where their bond would strengthen as Shallan remembered more about her past. Testament would eventually grow strong enough to speak again, and could communicate with Shallan in her mind as well.[^6]
