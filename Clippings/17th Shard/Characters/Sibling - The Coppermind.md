## Sibling

> “ *There is... a third sibling. They are not with us.* ”
> 
> —The Stormfather [^3]

The **Sibling** is a notable [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^4] They are of the same power level as the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]] and the [[Nightwatcher - The Coppermind|Nightwatcher]], and is the third spren historically associated with the [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Order of Bondsmiths]].[^5] [^6] The Sibling manifests physically as many fabrials within the tower of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] and has control over them, with the crystal pillar being the heart.[^7] They are currently bonded to [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani Kholin]].[^5] [^6]

## Personality and Appearance

### Appearance

![[Sibling---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Ben McSweeney](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Ben_McSweeney "Coppermind:Artists/Ben McSweeney")

Urithiru in the Cognitive Realm

The Sibling is essentially the tower of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] and by extension the complicated fabrial within and are able to manifest in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] as both metal and as [[Polestone - The Coppermind|Polestones]].[^7] Within the tower, they are capable of appearing as a column of light that appears from a glass disc in the ceiling.[^8] Those who can see into the Cognitve Realm are always able to see and hear the column of light when the Sibling's presence is nearby.[^9] [^10] The Sibling can speak to others via this column of light, or by speaking though the air vents.[^11] [^8]

In the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], the tower glows with a bright, multicolored, coalesced light.[^12] [^7] It is made of solid, glowing glass that perfectly replicates itself from its appearance in the Physical Realm.[^12]

### Personality

The Sibling does not trust humanity due to trauma stemming from feeling betrayal at the Recreance. Due to this, they do not want to bond with any human.[^13] They despise modern fabrials due to how they require that spren be imprisoned,[^14] though they are willing to compromise if the spren are treated well.[^14]

They do not identify with any gender, and do not understand why any spren identify with human genders.[^15] [^16] Despite living thousands of years, they remain somewhat naïve.[^17]

## Attributes and Abilities

The Sibling is known to have been alive for at least four and a half millennia and is functionally immortal.[^3] [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] spren follow the orders of the Sibling and refer to the Sibling as a parent.[^18]

### Investiture

> “ *In talking to Dalinar earlier, she’d learned that a Radiant leaving would lose Towerlight almost immediately. If a person carried it in a gemstone, the Light escaped faster than Stormlight did. Towerlight was a gift, but solely in Urithiru. But while they were here, it was omnipresent.* ”
> 
> —Navani [^19]

The Sibling is capable of producing their own [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] known as [Towerlight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Towerlight "Towerlight"), a mixture of [Lifelight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lifelight "Lifelight") and [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]], which they use to power the [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]] in Urithiru.[^20] They need to be able to harmonize the rhythms of [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] and [[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]] in order to produce Towerlight.[^5] Towerlight within Urithru powers Radiant abilities far longer than stormlight and is constantly replenished.[^21] When brought outside of Urithiru, Towerlight almost immediately completely leaks away from both gemstones and humans.[^22] [^19] The Sibling is able to cancel any power being sustained with Towerlight while in Urithiru.[^10]

### Nahel Bond

The Sibling is one of the three great [Bondsmith](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bondsmith "Bondsmith") spren.[^4] Forming a [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] with them grants one the power over the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of [Tension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tension "Tension") and [Adhesion](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adhesion "Adhesion").[^23] While a Bondsmith can use Adhesion the way a [Windrunner](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Windrunner "Windrunner") would,[^24] they can also apply their powers in different manner, using them more in the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual]]. Using Spiritual Adhesion, a Bondsmith can form a [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]] between themselves and another person, allowing them to speak their language.[^25] They also seem capable of hearing the spiritual aspects of objects and using this to mend broken things back whole.[^26] [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani]] is able to assist the Sibling in once again making [Towerlight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Towerlight "Towerlight") in large enough quantities to fully restore the functioning of Urithiru.[^5]

In order to bond with the Sibling, a person must be touching their crystal pillar.[^20] The Sibling knows how to end the Nahel bond, and has done so in the past.[^27] Many of the Sibling’s abilities rely on the Nahel Bond to function fully.[^10] The Radiant bonded with the Sibling cannot leave Urithiru for more than a few weeks without weakening their bond.[^19] If their Bondsmith is in the Spiritual Realm, this effect is lessened but will still cause issues.[^10] [^28] The Sibling and their Bondsmith are able to communicate with their minds.[^14] They are also able to speak directly into the minds of other Bondsmiths if their own Bondsmith is closeby.[^8]

While the [[Nightwatcher - The Coppermind|Nightwatcher]] and the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]] are known to be connected to [[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]] and [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] respectively, the Sibling is the child of both [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] and [[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]], created specifically to oppose [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].[^1] The Stormfather considers all three of them to be "siblings", and the fact that they are the only three spren that can bond with Bondsmiths [^29] indicates that they share some characteristics.

### Urithiru

> “ *She saw the tower now as an entity, with lines of garnet very like veins and arteries. And she inhabited that entity. It became her body. She saw thousands of closed doors the scouts had missed in mapping the tower. She saw brilliant mechanisms for controlling pressure, heat — No, stay focused.* ”
> 
> —Navani Kholin [^7]

The Sibling is essentially the Tower of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] and by extension the complicated fabrial within.[^7] They are capable of manifesting in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] as both metal and as [[Polestone - The Coppermind|Polestones]]. The Sibling can also fully power and control the various mechanisms of the fabrial that makes up the tower.[^30] The temperature becomes warmer since heating fabrials become more potent, for example.[^6] The Sibling is able to see and hear everything within the tower (including in the Cognitive Realm), though they aren't able to pay attention to all of it.[^1] [^7] [^14] [^10] Their Bondsmith is also able to do so, as well as comprehend the mechanisms of Urithiru and speak through the veins of garnet in the tower as the Sibling does.[^31] [^7] The Sibling is able to recreate sounds that they heard in the tower.[^10]

The red garnet vein within the wall strata serves as means of communication.[^32] It can light up and, upon being touched, the Sibling will be able to mentally speak with a person or spren,[^33] [^34] although this can be eavesdropped through unknown methods.[^17] This also allows people to communicate with each other if they are both touching the garnet vein.[^16] The Sibling can also talk to people in weak mental state.[^16] The line of garnet will give off a small amount of light when the Sibling wants it to.[^35] [^36] This can be moved within the garnet veins to guide people.[^1] [^37] The Sibling can open, close or lock the fabrial doors in Urithiru.[^33] They are even able to key them to certain people.

The tower is very bright and due to the highly Invested nature of the tower, it glows brightly in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], making it hard for people to see specific spren while there.[^32] [^7] Its highly Invested nature also makes it near impossible for the detection of kinetic Investiture.[^38] [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]] are unable to remain invisible while in the tower, though regular spren can.[^20] [^39] True spren that normally can't be seen are visible to others while in the tower.[^9] Highly Invested spren, such as the Unmade, are unable to hide from the Sibling while inside the tower.[^40]

Previously, the outer halls of the tower were coated in [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]].[^41] Lesser spren show up far more frequently in the tower.[^22]

Defenses

The Sibling, in conjunction with their Bondsmith, is able to create a defensive crystal around Urithiru.[^42] [^43] The crystal prevents anyone from entering or leaving the tower in the Physical Realm, and protects those inside.[^44] This is powerful enough to protect from the direct intervention of a Shard.[^42]

Doing so puts both the Sibling and their Bondsmith into a coma, with their Bondsmith being trapped in a crystal.[^43] This leaves the tower functional, with fabrials still working and Towerlight still available.

Without Towerlight

When [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]] was imprisoned, the Sibling lost the [rhythm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm") of Towerlight, which stopped most of Urithiru from functioning.[^16] In this state, the Sibling was only able to see patches of the tower and a small area around people who are Connected to them, mainly the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiants]].[^1] [^16] Without hearing the correct rhythms, their Bondsmith would also be unable to produce Towerlight.[^20] If the Sibling cannot generate Towerlight, they will be left open to be corrupted by [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]] and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]] tone.[^16]

Without Towerlight, most of the fabrials were dysfunctional.[^2] [^45] Functioning fabrial systems include those for increased pressure,[^45] temperature,[^46] and general conditions encouraging plant growth.[^45] Navani also theorized that the air circulating through various shafts was powered by some sort of fabrial.[^47] In addition, the fabrial elevators connecting Urithiru's floors begin running when gemstones were inserted.[^48] Basic suppression of Fused powers remained active.[^41] Gemstones to open walls still worked if infused with Stormlight, though the Sibling could not keep certain people from opening it, only able to lock or unlock them.[^49] [^33]

### Crystal Pillar

Deep inside the tower, there is a large monolith crystal pillar that serves as the Sibling's heart.[^7] It is made of thousands of different gems.[^50] [^51] The pillar is where the Sibling is most exposed, and is where they can be bonded or corrupted.[^50] [^20] While functioning normally, the pillar glows the color of Towerlight.[^50] If the Sibling is being corrupted with Voidlight, the color will darken.[^52] The Voidlight and corruption can be pushed out by the rhythm of [anti-Voidlight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Anti-Voidlight "Anti-Voidlight").[^5]

In order to protect the crystal pillar, [[Melishi - The Coppermind|Melishi]] created a system of four sapphire fabrial nodes that produce a blue barrier around the pillar.[^35] [^1] [^53] The barrier is strong enough to stop humans, singers, and even [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] from breaching it.[^1] It even has a way of silencing itself to [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]].[^38] It can also be used to corrupt and unmake the Sibling remotely by infusing the nodes with Voidlight.[^1] Since it was added later, the fabrial does not connect to the entirety of the tower and is powered by Stormlight instead of Towerlight.[^1] [^38]

The shield is made using the Surge of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Transformation]]; [[Soulcasting - The Coppermind|Soulcasting]] air and persuading it that it is glass.[^16] When attacked, the section of the shield will evaporate and will then restore itself almost immediately.[^53] Each node is accompanied by a glass globe, wrapped in metal wires.[^54] The globes have a reproduction of the crystal pillar room inside them, which helps the Soulcasting think the air around it is glass.[^53] This makes the Soulcasting not a full transformation, but more of a half transformation. It needs a constant stream of Stormlight via the nodes to keep functioning, and takes in much more than a typical fabrial of its size.[^16] [^1] If a node is destroyed, the shield will weaken.[^35] The more it is weakened and corrupted, the more its color goes from bright blue to dark violet.[^55] If all four are destroyed, the shield will be destroyed.[^16] [^52]

The first node was located close to the pillar and was destroyed by Navani.[^1] [^16] The second node was built near the second level of the tower, so it can easily absorb Stormlight from [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorms]].[^39] [^54] It was destroyed by [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]].[^54] The third node was in the center of the Breakaway market on the first floor.[^20] It was hidden in a well near the pump mechanisms and was destroyed by Kaladin.[^34] The fourth node was hidden in the infirmary room, inside the model of the tower.[^52] It was destroyed by [[Moash - The Coppermind|Moash]]. More nodes were meant to be installed, as well as higher up into the tower, but Melishi did not have time to do so.[^20]

### Suppression Fabrial

The Sibling has a large [suppression fabrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Suppression_fabrial "Suppression fabrial") built into Urithiru, much more powerful than a normal one.[^32] It is built into a construction of garnets and destroying them will remove its effects.[^56] It normally incapacitates Fused, [Regals](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Regals "Regals"), and Unmade while inside its range.[^57] [^7] Some of the Unmade are able to resist the effects, as well as other highly Invested individuals.[^35] [^57] This fabrial can have varying power levels, from merely stopping some of the Fused from [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]] to knocking them unconscious.[^41] Regals will also lose their forms.[^8] When in effect, rhythms are disrupted, and the rhythm of the Sibling can drive singers mad.[^32] [^8] Odium is unable to locate people while they are inside the tower.[^58]

Inverted

If the Sibling is corrupted, the suppression fabrial can be inverted, instead incapacitating Radiants.[^57] [^50] [^16] While in effect, Radiants inside of the range (roughly hundreds of yards from the tower) will fall unconscious.[^59] Their spren will also be pushed back into the Cognitive Realm, put into a deep sleep-like state.[^32] Those who have bonded a dead [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") will be unable to summon it, though an unbonded one will remain unaffected.[^60] [^61] The suppression fabrial also greatly limits the Stormfather's ability to see the tower and will inhibit his [visions](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dalinar%27s_visions "Dalinar's visions").[^60] [^62]

If a Radiant is far enough in their [oaths](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Immortal_Words "Immortal Words"), they may be able to resist the effects, as well as keep their spren in the Physical Realm.[^57] [^35] Those able to resist will instead feel an external oppressive pressure of darkness.[^32] [^34] They are still able to take in Stormlight and it will continue to heal them.[^63] The worse the corruption of the Sibling, the weaker the healing gets.[^53] [^34] If a Radiant is also a Regal, they will be mostly unaffected by the suppressor, even at the First Ideal.[^64] Those using an [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblade]] will still be able to Surgebind and summon their blade.[^65] [^52]

[Windrunners](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Windrunners "Windrunners") (due to their Surge of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Adhesion]] being a true Surge of Honor) will remain less unconscious, instead shaking and having muscle spasms.[^35] Those close to or above the Fourth Ideal will remain awake and will still be able to draw in Stormlight.[^16] [^61] They are also able to pull their [honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren") back into the Physical Realm, though they are unable to form a Shardblade for longer than a than a few seconds.[^32] [^66] Their honorspren's radius that they can travel from the Windrunner also decreases dramatically, going from miles to only about a floor.[^32] While their Adhesion Surge will still work, [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Gravitation]] will not.[^32] Due to Adhesion not being affected, it is likely that a Bondsmith would have similar resistance against the suppression.[^60]

[[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]], due to her [[Old Magic - The Coppermind|Old Magic]], will remain conscious and can continue to produce Lifelight.[^67] [^16] [[Wyndle - The Coppermind|Wyndle]] also remained in the Physical Realm, but was unable to become a Shardblade. Lift is still able to use the Surge of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Progression]], but not [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Abrasion]]. Lift is able to heal other Radiants, waking them up.[^68] If she doesn't re-heal them within at least ten hours, they fall back into unconsciousness.[^69] Despite this, her internal healing worked as a regular conscious Radiant's would and can get worse the longer the Sibling is corrupted.[^64]

Fabrials will stop working while inside the range of the suppressor fabrial.[^35] Fabrials made from [[Sja-anat - The Coppermind|Enlightened]] spren will still work, but only with Voidlight.[^70] [^53] The Oathgates remain unaffected, and can still be used by an Honorblade.[^32] [^56]

## History

> “ *My research into the cognitive reflections of the spren at the tower has been deeply illustrative. Some thought that the Sibling had withdrawn from men by intent—but I find counter to that theory.* ”
> 
> —The third [[Polestone - The Coppermind#Zircon|zircon]] in drawer 1-1 of the Urithiru gem archive [^2]

### Heraldic Epochs and Era of Solitude

The Sibling was created using a portion of the essence of [Stone](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stone "Stone"),[^11] mixed with the Investiture of Honor and Cultivation,[^1] around six-thousand years before the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows").[^11] They can, in a sense, be considered to be a child of Honor and Cultivation.[^11]

The Sibling was capable of forming a [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]]. There could never be more than three [Bondsmiths](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bondsmith "Bondsmith"); there were long periods where only one existed, which might have referred to the Sibling's Bondsmith, as they were vital for Urithiru to properly function.[^71] They were very particular with their bond, often spending years evaluating Bondsmith squires.[^20]

The Sibling was the key to certain magical aspects of the tower city of Urithiru that served as the base of the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]]. Notably, the Sibling's presence was linked to the climate of the city, allowing the temperature to stay unnaturally warm given Urithiru's elevation. This allowed the Radiants to cultivate crops in the city to sustain the large population there.[^2] [^45] At some point, the Sibling began to withdraw from the tower. The Sibling's withdrawal caused some—but not all— of the city's magical systems to fail; it soon became uninhabitable, and the Radiants were forced to abandon the city.[^72] [^45] [^73] It is not clear why the Sibling disengaged with mankind; an Elsecaller who left notes in the gem archive argued that the spren's disappearance was neither intentional nor a result of internal strife among the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]].[^2] [^45] [^74] The Sibling was wounded by the imprisonment of [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]]; this wound resulted in the Sibling losing the rhythm of [Towerlight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Towerlight "Towerlight"), which in turn stopped [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] functioning.[^16]

After the Sibling's departure, they were not hiding but rather "slumbering" in the millennia following the abandonment of Urithiru and the [Recreance](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Recreance "Recreance").[^3] The Stormfather has generally been reticent to discuss the Sibling with [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar]] because they were somehow harmed by mankind in the past.[^29] [^3]

### True Desolation

The Sibling first made contact with Navani Kholin through a purposefully placed spanreed ruby.[^75] Initially, Navani and the Sibling were at odds due to their differences in morals regarding fabrial technology.[^75] [^76] Throughout the occupation of Urithiru, the Sibling revealed that they have several nodes to protect them from enemy invasion.[^16] Their last Bondsmith wanted to install several more but was only able to install four nodes across the first few levels of the tower.[^20] The Sibling was hesitant to tell Navani the location of the nodes, and only gave them up when Navani convinced them it was vital to protect them from further corruption.[^53] [^20] However, the Sibling's fears were not unwarranted; after the reveal of the third node, Raboniel revealed that she had been listening in the entire time Navani and the Sibling were communicating.[^17] The Sibling then refused to talk to anyone until their final node was in danger, when they called on Kaladin for help to protect them from full corruption.[^37] While Kaladin fought against the Fused, Navani attempted to push the Sibling towards bonding Rlain, as suggested by Kaladin and Navani. However, there was not enough time to find Rlain, so Navani suggested herself.[^5] While hesitant initially, the Sibling accepted the bond, and working together they were able to expel the Voidlight corrupting the Sibling and restore Urithiru to full power.[^5] [^7] When the Sibling bonded Navani, they were finally able to create Towerlight after being unable to hear Honor's tone, or by extension that of Towerlight, since his death.[^5]
