## Masaka

**Masaka**, also known as **Chinikdakordich**, is a young [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] who assumes the role of a female [[Nightmare - The Coppermind|nightmare]] [[Painter - The Coppermind|painter]] on [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]].

## Appearance and Personality

Masaka takes the appearance of a short woman who wears her hair with bangs and tends to wear dark makeup on her face, especially around the eyes.[^1] She exclusively wears black clothing, and favors a black sweater.[^2] She dislikes showing her skin, to avoid revealing her nature as a [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] to others--she wears scarves or tops with a high collar in order to hide her neck, and sometimes wear sleeves long enough to pull her hands into them.[^1] [^2]

Masaka is usually very shy and quiet, even with her friends.[^1] She dislikes when people stare at her, and shows other signs of antisocial behavior.[^1] Despite her strange personality, Masaka makes an effort to be friendly to people she likes.[^1] Her odd behavior is likely a consequence of her limited experience being a human.[^2] Masaka is very adamantly tired of war,[^2] and tries to influence her friends toward being accepting of aliens, likely as a measure to prevent bigotry.[^3] She also thinks [[Nightmare - The Coppermind|nightmares]] can sometimes be cute, though she insists she dislikes horrific creatures.[^4] Masaka often draws on her sketchpad while hanging out with friends.[^1] As one can assume from the career she chose, she enjoys painting, and art in general.[^1] [^2]

She takes on the form of a human woman not just out of convenience, but due to actually finding happiness in that form and being perceived as such.[^2] [^5] She actively prefers the name Masaka to her Sleepless name, Chinikdakordich, and corrects those that refer to her otherwise.[^2]

She is an agonistes that shows a substantial lack of self-confidence about her ability to blend in as a human, doubting that she actually passes as such.[^2] But at the same time, she takes pride in how far she has come and how well she is doing.[^2]

## Attributes and Abilities

As a [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]], Masaka is able to send out hordelings away from herself to search and spy, though she does not do this regularly.[^2] Masaka is also a competent painter, having completed two years of upper school on the subject before being hired.[^1]

## History

Masaka previously used the name Chinikdakordich, and is the sixtieth horde of the [Natricatich strain](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Natricatich_strain "Natricatich strain") of Sleepless. She doesn't talk much about their past, but experienced a great deal of prejudice due to being a Sleepless. At some point she grew tired of wars being fought elsewhere in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] and so decided to disguise herself and live a normal human life. After years of breeding she was able to produce a convincing face, and took up residence on [[Kilahito - The Coppermind|Kilahito]]. When she arrived on Kilahito, she sent out hordelings to investigate her surroundings. She found circular regions which she could not enter, and avoided searching them further.[^2]

In upper school on Komashi, Masaka was rumored to have stabbed someone years prior, and been sent to become a painter by a court as part of her plea deal.[^1] During their schooling she became friends with [[Nikaro - The Coppermind|Nikaro]], [[Akane - The Coppermind|Akane]], [[Tojin - The Coppermind|Tojin]], and [Izzy](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Izzy "Izzy").[^1] She once made a hat for Nikaro for his birthday.[^6] She became a painter after graduating and typically works alongside Izzy.[^3]

When [[Yumi - The Coppermind|Yumi]] first arrived in [[Kilahito - The Coppermind|Kilahito]], Masaka drew a strange bunny for her as a gift.[^1] Later, [[Design - The Coppermind|Design]] recruited Masaka to help Yumi map out the planet beyond the [[Shroud - The Coppermind|shroud]]. Masaka was readily willing to help once she understood the issue was important, though she begged Yumi not to tell anyone about her nature and offered a knife as a gift when Yumi promised not to tell. Masaka was able to map out the locations of the circular areas in the shroud where they were unable to enter.[^2]

When Nikaro begged his friends to join him in defense of Kilahito, Masaka was the second of his friends (following Tojin) to agree that they should help.[^7] At the [Battle of Kilahito](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Battle_of_Kilahito "Battle of Kilahito"), Masaka was the first to notice the arrival of the stable nightmares.[^8] After [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] and Design left Komashi, Masaka wrote letters to the [Cryptic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cryptic "Cryptic") periodically with updates. Hoid was able to learn more about Komashi's fate, following the destruction of the [[Father machine - The Coppermind|father machine]], through this information. With the Nightmare Division disbanded, Masaka got a job as a server at the [Noodle Princess](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Noodle_Princess "Noodle Princess"), working for Painter and Yumi. According to Hoid, she became increasingly comfortable with more people knowing about her nature.[^5]

## Relationships

### Nikaro (Painter)

She used to be friends with [[Nikaro - The Coppermind|Nikaro]], giving him a spiked black helmet at his birthday and saying it was a 'birthday hat.' [^6] But they fell out when Nikaro lied to her and their common friends by saying that he's joining the [Dreamwatch](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dreamwatch "Dreamwatch").[^9] But even after the fallout, she was the second [[Painter - The Coppermind|painter]] to agree that they should help in the [battle of Kilahito](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Battle_of_Kilahito "Battle of Kilahito"), even if Nikaro is lying again.[^7] It's likely they went back to being friends again after the battle, as she works as a server at the [Noodle Princess](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Noodle_Princess "Noodle Princess"), Nikaro and [[Yumi - The Coppermind|Yumi's]] restaurant.[^5]

### Akane

She is close friends with [[Akane - The Coppermind|Akane]], often eating with her and the rest of her friends at the [[Noodle Pupil - The Coppermind|Noodle Pupil]] and other restaurants.[^1] She doesn't want her to learn that she is a [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]], and begs Yumi to keep it a secret after she found out.[^2]

### Design

[[Design - The Coppermind|Design]] is one of the only people that are aware of her secret identity.[^2] Her exact thoughts on Design are unclear, but Design shows an almost maternal fondness for her.[^2]

### Hoid

While her thoughts on [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] are unknown, Hoid himself shows respect and affection for her. He also respects her desire to be treated as a human woman, and encourages people to give her affirmation.[^5] He and Design kept in contact with her even after leaving [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]] behind.[^5]

### Yumi

She is close friends with Yumi, and works for her at the Noodle Princess. She quickly took a liking to her in their first meeting, drawing her a picture of a rabbit.[^1] She deeply appreciated Yumi still treating her as a normal woman even after finding out she was a Sleepless, and for promising not to disclose her secret.[^2] Yumi finds her rather odd, especially so after discovering her species, but she didn't let it change how she treated her.[^2]
