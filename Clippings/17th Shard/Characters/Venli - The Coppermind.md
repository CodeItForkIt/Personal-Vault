## Venli

> “ *I feel like a relic. Already I seem like a cast-off ruin from a nearly forgotten day.* ”
> 
> —Venli [^4]

**Venli** is a [[Listener - The Coppermind|listener]] scholar in [Narak](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Narak "Narak") and [[Eshonai - The Coppermind|Eshonai's]] sister. During the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind 1|True Desolation]], she bonds with [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]] and becomes a [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinder]] of the [[Order of Willshapers - The Coppermind|Order of Willshapers]].

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *Why should I continue to obey your orders, spren? You should obey me.* ”

Venli is slender, with a thin face and primarily red-and-white marbled skin, with small black patches on her neck; having all three colors is extremely rare among listeners [^6]. She wears her hair long, down to her waist, and ties it in three places. Her eyes are dark and glassy like all [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]], though they appear violet in the right light. During the [[Clippings/17th Shard/Events/War of Reckoning - The Coppermind|War of Reckoning]], she utilizes nimbleform as a substitute for the undiscovered scholarform.[^7] She later changes into [stormform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormform "Stormform") for the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]], making her bulkier and taller, and smiles often while wearing it.[^8] Following the battle, she transforms into [envoyform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Envoyform "Envoyform").[^9] In that form, she has visible breasts and is extremely tall, taller even than the [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") humans.[^10] [^11]

In any form she has, Venli seems to tend towards the fatalistic, putting the odds of things like the war ending or her own survival at rather low.[^7] [^11] While she wishes she had the power to fight, she does not consider herself to be skilled in this regard, and being on a battlefield distresses her.[^10] [^12] On the other hand, she's highly ambitious, and by and large does not care about the plight of her people, so long as she gets what she wants -- power and recognition.[^4] To that end, she can be extremely manipulative and deceitful; however, when those goals, and the respect they'd give her, are threatened, she grows angry.[^13] She seems jealous of her sister and her position, and tries to take over once the two are both in stormform.[^14] It remains unclear how much of Venli's ambition came from having Ulim, a Voidspren, inside of her gemheart for a long period of time.

Eshonai's death is a massive catalyst of change in her.[^15] Over time, Venli comes to understand how horrid her actions have been and their true cost, and regrets them deeply, beginning to care about the other singers and their plight. She acknowledges her responsibility for causing the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] to be summoned, and admits that this was an awful thing to do.[^4] [^16] However, she retains her prideful streak, and will not accept any compromise with humans.[^11]

## Attributes and Abilities

> “ *Venli's eyes always seemed to have an extra depth of secret knowledge to them.* ”
> 
> — [[Eshonai - The Coppermind|Eshonai]] [^7]

Venli is a highly skilled scholar and researcher; she has managed to create a functional writing system from scratch and has discovered, among others, the [nimbleform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nimbleform "Nimbleform"), as well as [stormform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormform "Stormform").[^7] She can think on her feet and make snap decisions.[^10] She also has some ability as a craftswoman, having constructed and decorated the home where she and her family lived in [Narak](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Narak "Narak").[^7] She doesn't, however, have much in the way of combat skills; though she mentions having used an axe before, it's unclear whether it was in battle, and she's unfamiliar with swords.[^12]

### Forms

As a [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]], Venli has a [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemheart]], and can bond with different [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] to attain different forms.

In [nimbleform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nimbleform "Nimbleform"), Venli is agile and precise, as well as keen-minded. Assuming [stormform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormform "Stormform") makes her significantly stronger and tougher, almost enough to rival a person wearing [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]]. It also lets her shoot red lightning from her hands.[^4] Those abilities go away once the [envoyform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Envoyform "Envoyform") is thrust upon her, though she grows even taller. Instead of casting lightning, she gains the ability to speak and understand any language, writing and signals, both human and singer, on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. She also mentions having other, lesser gifts, though those are unspecified.[^9]

### Surgebinding

With envoyform being one of the forms of power, Venli has a [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|voidspren]] in her gemheart; however, she's also bonded with [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]], a [Reacher](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Reacher "Reacher"), making her a [Willshaper](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Willshaper "Willshaper").

Though the voidspren within Venli's gemheart remains there, Timbre has it trapped. This allows Venli to retain her envoyform abilities and switch between red and normal eyes at will, letting her blend in with Odium's forces.[^17]

## History

### Early life

Venli is the sister of [[Eshonai - The Coppermind|Eshonai]], and though her age is unclear, she is younger by a year.[^18] When the two were children, Venli would often stay at home and help their mother, [Jaxlim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jaxlim "Jaxlim"), while Eshonai travelled.[^13] Both sisters wore [workform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Workform "Workform") at the time. At some point, Venli and [Demid](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Demid "Demid") became mates, and though the relationship did not produce any children, they remained together even after changing into different forms.[^7]

Eventually, Venli settled into the role of a scholar. After the listeners first made contact with humans, she created a written script, recognizing the value of recording information on paper. Venli received a gemstone from [Axindweth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Axindweth "Axindweth") which contained [Ulim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ulim "Ulim").[^19] Over the next five years, she and her team wrote down all listener song and stories to be preserved and cross-referenced. Through this, they also discovered nimbleform. Venli took it on and joined listener leadership as its representative.[^7]

### War of Reckoning

> “ *The daughter of traitors. Then a traitor to her own kind.* ”

On the day of the peace treaty with the [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") and the [[Assassination of Gavilar - The Coppermind|Assassination of Gavilar]] Kholin, the Alethi king, Venli and another listener, [Klade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Klade "Klade"), were informed by the [Herald](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") [[Nale - The Coppermind|Nale]] that Gavilar intended to betray the listeners; he directed her to purchase [[Szeth - The Coppermind|Szeth]], which led to the killing.[^20] [^21] Following that event, Venli and Ulim worked together on discovering new forms.[^10] Though she claimed to Eshonai that she was researching mediationform to treaty with humans, in reality, Venli sought to capture a [stormspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormspren "Stormspren") and unlock the associated [stormform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormform "Stormform").[^13] [^14] [^10]

Upon succeeding, Venli intended to test the form herself, and was angry when [The Five](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Five "The Five") decided to let Eshonai do it instead. Though they did so out of concern, fearing the loss of Venli and her talent if the form turned out to be defective, she was jealous, and thought that her sister was distrusting her, and trying to steal her discovery.[^22] After Eshonai's thoughts got altered by the stormform and she began to enact the plan to transform all listeners, Venli also reacted badly, believing her strategies to be too aggressive. She intended to seize control from her sister, and suggested killing the listeners who refused to adopt the new form.[^14]

### True Desolation

> “ *How long can you keep being two people, Venli? How long will you vacillate?* ”
> 
> —Venli imagining what Eshonai would think of her [^4]

After the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] was created, Venli, led by Ulim, sought to find her sister, gone missing during the battle. She was heartbroken upon finding Eshonai dead, and stunned when Ulim told her that he only sought the [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]] she'd been wearing at the moment of her death. The event somehow unlocked her ability to attune to the old, pre-stormform Rhythms. While stripping the Shardplate off Eshonai, Venli also spotted a strange [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] escaping from underneath the corpse.[^5]

When nine listeners were chosen by Ulim to receive "new forms," Venli and Demid were among them. In the Everstorm, the [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] replaced their souls and possessed their bodies, with the sole exception of Venli. [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] himself protected her, and she was given [envoyform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Envoyform "Envoyform") instead. After realizing that the others were effectively dead, Venli once more spotted [[Timbre - The Coppermind|the strange spren]], and hid it from the Fused.[^10]

Afterwards, Venli became Odium's chief propagandist, first in [Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar "Kholinar"), then in [Marat](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Marat "Marat"), telling the tragic tale of the listeners who, according to the new narrative, sacrificed themselves to free the [Parshmen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Parshmen "Parshmen") of their mindless slavery. During that time, she was kept apart from other singers to maintain her mystique; in lieu of contact with her people, she began chatting with her spren, whom she nicknamed [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]]. At Timbre's urging, she finally admitted that summoning the old gods was a horrible mistake, and that she wished she could be like her sister.[^9] [^23] [^4]

While she was in Marat, [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin]] pulled her into one of his visions, and attempted to negotiate; Venli, however, refused all overtures of peace. The conversation was cut short by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] breaking into the vision space, with Dalinar managing to send Venli out just before his arrival.[^11] After that event, Venli boarded one of the ships heading to [Thaylen City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_City "Thaylen City").[^24]

### Surgebinder

During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field - The Coppermind|Battle of Thaylen Field]], Venli remained mostly out of the fighting, serving as a translator.[^12] She accompanied Odium to witness Dalinar's presumed fall from grace.[^25] After he managed to recover and summon [[Honor's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Honor's Perpendicularity]] instead, she crept away and returned to the ships, where she swore the [First Ideal](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Immortal_Words "Immortal Words"), bonding with [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]] and becoming a [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinder]]. This allowed Timbre to live inside her [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemheart]] and capture the [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|voidspren]] there, letting Venli retain her Odium-derived red eyes.[^17]

Following the battle, Venli remained on the Thaylen ships. Upon hearing the former [Parshmen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Parshmen "Parshmen") note that they have no idea who their people are, she decided to tell them the real story of the [[Listener - The Coppermind|listeners]].[^16]

At some point, Venli comes into the employ of the Fused [Leshwi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Leshwi "Leshwi"), serving as her Voice. She also begins recruiting a small group of singers who plan to escape from the war, from both Odium's forces and the coalition.[^1] As an apology for refuting her desire to invade Urithiru, Leshwi offers Raboniel Venli to serve as her voice, an offer which Raboniel accepts.[^3]

### Urithiru

Venli was brought along with [Raboniel](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Raboniel "Raboniel") and a scouting troop of *makay-im* during the invasion of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]]. Upon the initial seizure, she was attacked by a human soldier, and killed him in a panic. Afterwards, both she and Timbre attuned the [Lost](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm"), although Raboniel merely admonished her to carry a knife in the future.[^26]

While she was the Voice of Raboniel, Venli regularly reported to [Leshwi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Leshwi "Leshwi"). Raboniel was aware of this, and offered to have someone deliver the reports for her in order to save time.[^27]

After learning that [secretspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Secretspren "Secretspren") and [chaosspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chaosspren "Chaosspren") didn't work, she realized that she could practice her powers as a Willshaper without being caught, and did so in a secluded corner of the tower.[^28]

> “ *Don't make this harder than it already is. I'm not here to help the humans. I can barely help my own kind.*”
> 
> —Venli to Timbre [^29]

After discovering that [[Rlain - The Coppermind|Rlain]] was still alive, she went to Raboniel to request his freedom. Before she could do so, though, [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize]] entered the room with [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]] in a cage, giving her to Raboniel as a gift in exchange for access to the Oathgates. Venli, discomforted by this, left to fetch Rlain. On her way there, she attempted to swear the Second Ideal of the Willshapers, swearing to free those in bondage, but [[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]] did not accept them. Upon further reflection, she realized that she couldn't truly swear such an Ideal after having just seen a human child in a cage.[^30]

Upon hearing that [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] was free and in the tower, she went to Leshwi to plead protection for [Lirin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lirin "Lirin") and [Hesina](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hesina "Hesina"), as both Venli and Leshwi knew that the [Pursuer](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pursuer "Pursuer") wouldn't hesitate to use them in a bid to lure him into a fight. Leshwi granted this request, and Venli used this opportunity to bring Lirin to tend to the unconscious Radiants. While visiting them with Rlain, [Dabbid](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dabbid "Dabbid") arrived, telling the four that Kaladin was asleep and wasn't waking up. Venli realized that Lift would likely be able to heal him, and used Cohesion to free her, hoping that the Fused would assume that a human Stoneward had survived. Instead of telling Rlain, Lirin, and Hesina that she was a Surgebinder, though, she claimed to have a Shardblade.[^18]

While serving as an interpreter between Raboniel and [Regals](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Regals "Regals") coming to deliver reports, Raboniel and Venli discussed the listeners. Venli admitted shame at being the one directly responsible for her people's downfall, asking Raboniel why there couldn't be peace. Raboniel commended her on her service and gave her a writ of authority and a map showing a settlement of listeners on the Shattered Plains. Venli realized that it might be the group that had refused stormform before the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]], and when Rlain found her crying, she told him the entire story of what she did. Rlain attuned Betrayal, a decision that she called smart, and he said that they should attempt to save the Radiants before seeking out the other listeners. She disagreed with this, and told [Dul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dul "Dul") and [Mazish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mazish "Mazish") that it was time to leave.[^31]

As Venli waited with Dul and Mazish for an Oathgate transfer to Kholinar, Timbre expressed disappointment that Venli was abandoning the Radiants. Ignoring this, Venli walked to the front of the line to find out what was taking so long, and discovered that [Vyre](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vyre "Vyre") had left to meet with Raboniel, taking with him the only Blade that the Fused had that was capable of working the Oathgates. Venli moved everyone inside and decided to smuggle Kaladin and his family out with herself, Dul, and Mazish, who agreed that it was a good idea. They headed for the atrium, where they found Kaladin fighting the Pursuer. Although Kaladin forced Lezian to retreat, Vyre dropped [Teft's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Teft "Teft") corpse on him, and Venli attuned the Lost as he grieved. The Pursuer attacked him again, and after killing him, Kaladin headed toward Venli. She feared that he was coming for her, but instead, he attacked the "shanay-im" holding his father.

Venli watched as the humans desperately defended the Radiants against the Pursuer's troops, and at Timbre's urging, revealed her status as a Radiant to Leshwi. Contrary to Venli's expectations, she was overjoyed, and asked Venli if Timbre knew of an honorspren named Riah. Venli relayed the message that most of the old spren were killed in the Recreance, but despite this, the fact that the spren had forgiven the singers was enough to convince Leshwi that the Radiants must be protected, and she joined the humans in fighting Lezian's troops.[^32] [^33] During the fighting, Rlain arrived in the atrium, setting up a triage in the aftermath of the battle. He took command, telling the group that they should head for an Oathgate on the misconception that Venli had a Blade. She quickly disabused him of this notion, and while he was contemplating what to do, Kaladin, having just sworn the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Fourth Ideal]], arrived and activated the Oathgate to the Shattered Plains for them.[^33] [^34]

### Return to the Listeners

After leaving the tower, Venli located the listener settlement on the map, finding that [Thude](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thude "Thude") and Jaxlim, among others, were still alive. Although the group treated Venli with suspicion, and in some cases, outright hostility, they allowed her through to see her mother, who was the only one besides Venli herself who remembered the songs. Having brought a Reacher in the hope that it would heal her mind, Venli began to sing the Song of Mornings, Jaxlim's favorite of the old songs. Just when Venli was about to lose hope, Jaxlim recognized her, and Cultivation finally accepted her Words.[^35]

Venli reconnects with her mother, who has now bonded a Reacher, along with several other Listeners and she and the Fused integrate into the life of the Listeners, and she learns about the Listeners' new peace with the chasmfiends.[^36] [^37] Thude sings her the Song of the Chasmfiends, and Venli realizes that there must be something about the Shattered Plains that leads the chasmfiends to have to pupate there.[^38] The chasmfiends shelter the Listener settlement from the last highstorm, and the stones tell Venli to seek Narak and the fallen fourth moon of Roshar.[^39] Venli, along with the Five, Leshwi and her followers, and several chasmfiends begin the journey.[^40]

Venli speaks with Leshwi, and helps her seek a new place among the Listeners, free from Odium.[^41] The group locate a tunnel going under the central plateau of Narak and find at the end of it Odium's Perpendicularity.[^42] [^43] [El](https://coppermind.net/wiki/El "El") and several other Fused find Venli's group of Listeners, who seek the aid of the chasmfiends to help win the [Second Battle of Narak](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Narak "Second Battle of Narak").[^44] Venli secretly goes to meet with [[Sigzil - The Coppermind|Sigzil]], the leader of the Coalition's forces in the battle, and the two work out a deal in which [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin]], as Queen of Alethkar, signs a treaty with the Listeners, giving them dominion over the Shattered Plains and thus keeping them from Odium.[^45] [^46] The Listeners join the battle on the side of Odium's forces, who take Narak.[^47] [^48] When the [[Contest of champions - The Coppermind|contest of champions]] begins, Venli reveals the treaty to El, who withdraws Odium's forces from the plains.[^49]

### Night of Sorrows

> “ **This may not be redemption,* Venli thought. *Not yet. Maybe just…atonement. The redemption comes later, after we see if I can keep improving.** ”
> 
> —Venli considering what she has done for the Listeners [^50]

After [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] ascends to [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]], he sends a messenger to Venli's group in Narak, and they learn how to receive [Warlight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Warlight "Warlight") from the everstorm. She creates a passage down to what is now Retribution's Perpendicularity using her Stoneshaping, and thinks about the powerful duty that her people now have.[^50]

## Relationships

### Jaxlim

Jaxlim is Venli's mother. Venli spends hours learning the Listener's songs from Jaxlim. Venli is annoyed by her mother on occasion but does respect her.[^6]

The deterioration of Jaxlim's memory is a driving factor in why Venli wants to discover new forms. Initially, she wishes to discover forms of power to help heal her mother.[^19] [^51] Venli later helps her mother bond with a Reacher to heal her.[^35]

### Eshonai

Eshonai is Venli's older sister. Venli feels jealous of Eshonai and resents how Eshonai explores while she stays home and learns the Listener songs from Jaxlim. When Venli discovers Stormform, Eshonai is chosen to test it, which brings up emotions of jealousy for Venli again. Their relationship is somewhat fraught by Venli feeling threatened by Eshonai.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

Eshonai's death has a profound impact on Venli. When she realizes that Ulim only wants to recover Eshonai's body to have access to her shardplate, Venli is able to hear the old Rhythms again.[^5] She later forms a Nahel bond with Timbre, the Reacher that was initially going to bond Eshonai. Even after Eshonai's death, Venli feels inferior to her. She wishes that Eshonai was in her place, and tells Timbre that she has the wrong sister because she cannot resist Odium.[^4]

### Demid

Venli and Demid are once-mates and have no children together. They remain close after leaving mateform and become research partners.[^7] She watches as one of the Fused takes over his body and is shocked by the transformation.[^10] She blames herself for his death.[^27]

### Rlain

Rlain is the first living Listener that Venli discovers after the rest become Fused. She feels guilty upon speaking with him because he does not initially know the role she played in the destruction of the Listeners.[^30] Venli works with Rlain and Dabbid to free Lift to heal Kaladin and reveals her powers to him.[^18]

### Ulim

Ulim helps Venli discover Stormform.[^9] Ulim often withholds information from her and lies to her about Odium's intentions. Their relationship can be antagonistic, as he mocks her sadness over Eshonai's death.[^5]

### Timbre

Timbre is the Reacher that Venli has bonded. Timbre generally resides in Venli's gemheart, which allows Venli to attune to either the old or new Rhythms.

Venli first sees Timbre when she discovers Eshonai's corpse and shoos her away.[^5] The second time Timbre appears to her, Venli hides Timbre from the Fused.[^10] Timbre encourages Venli to resist Odium. She eventually helps Venli swear the First Ideal during the Battle of Thaylen City.[^17] Timbre wants Venli to work with the other Radiants, encouraging her to free Lift [^30] and to help Kaladin [^52]. Timbre consoles Venli while she grapples with her past actions and encourages her to tell Lady Leshwi about their bond. At first, Venli resists this idea. However, she eventually reveals her bond with Timbre.[^33]

Timbre does not speak but communicates with Venli using the Rhythms.[^4]

### Leshwi

Venli serves Lady Leshwi as her Voice, speaking for her in meetings between the Fused.[^3] Leshwi tasks Venli with spying on Raboniel for her.[^3] Venli reveals her bond with Timbre to Leshwi. [^32] This revelation causes Leshwi to fight against the Pursuer and eventually join with the Listeners in abandoning Odium.[^34]

## Trivia

- Venli is the focus character of *[Rhythm of War](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm_of_War "Rhythm of War")*, with the flashbacks split between her and [[Eshonai - The Coppermind|Eshonai]].
- Brandon planned to kill off Eshonai and put more emphasis on Venli from very early on in the story's development, having always considered her to be the more interesting of the two sisters.[^53]
- At present, Venli is one of only four [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] viewpoint characters in the [Stormlight Archive](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormlight_Archive "Stormlight Archive"), alongside her sister, [[Rlain - The Coppermind|Rlain]], and [El](https://coppermind.net/wiki/El "El").
