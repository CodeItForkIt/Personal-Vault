## Battar

> “ *Sweet wisdom of Battar.* ”

**Battar**, known as **Battah'Elin** in [[Vorinism - The Coppermind|Vorinism]] and as **Batlah** amongst the [[Shin Kak Nish - The Coppermind|Shin]],[^2] [^3] is one of the ten [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") of the [Almighty](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Almighty "Almighty") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. She is the patron of the [[Order of Elsecallers - The Coppermind|Order of Elsecallers]]. She resided in [[Kharbranth - The Coppermind|Kharbranth]] as an [ardent](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ardent "Ardent") under the assumed name of **Dova**.[^6] [^7] [^8] She is currently with the other Heralds on Braize.[^9]

## Appearance and Personality

During the lead-up to the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]], Battar maintains a shaved head, as she likes the fact that looking like an [ardent](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ardent "Ardent") causes most people to overlook her.[^10]

## Attributes and Abilities

![[Battar---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Shuravf](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Shuravf "Coppermind:Artists/Shuravf")

Battar is an immortal [[Cognitive Shadow - The Coppermind|Cognitive Shadow]] who gains a [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|physical]] body upon each return to Roshar. After death, she is transported to [[Braize - The Coppermind|Braize]] to be tortured; when she or another Herald breaks, a new Desolation begins.[^11]

During the [[Heraldic Epochs - The Coppermind|Heraldic Epochs]], she wields an [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblade]]. It grants her access to the [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] of [Transformation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Transformation "Transformation"), which enables her to [Soulcast](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulcast "Soulcast"), and [Transportation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Transportation "Transportation"), which allows for travel to and from [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar").[^12] She has, however, abandoned it at [Aharietiam](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aharietiam "Aharietiam").[^13]

She seems to be fairly knowledgeable, being ascribed the attribute of Wise, and noticing signs of an upcoming Desolation years before the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]].[^14] [^7] During her pre-herald life, even Battar has flaws such as having conniving tendencies.[^15] That conniving turns into cruelty as her soul was warped over time by her immortal torture.[^16]

## History

![[Battar---The-Coppermind-2.jpg]]

by [mushimallo](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/mushimallo "Coppermind:Artists/mushimallo")

Battar was born on [[Ashyn - The Coppermind|Ashyn]] and made the trip to Roshar during the [[Expulsion - The Coppermind|Expulsion]].[^17] She was someone important in the pre- [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]] society -- a queen, scholar, or general.[^10] Along with the others, she approached [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] and formed the [[Oathpact - The Coppermind|Oathpact]]. Whenever a Desolation ended, she'd travel along with them to [[Braize - The Coppermind|Braize]], where she suffered torture until one of them broke. She broke at least once, starting a new Desolation.[^11]

Eventually, she and the other Heralds grew exhausted with this endless cycle of war and torture. When [[Ishar - The Coppermind|Ishar]] suggested that one person might be enough to hold the [[Oathpact - The Coppermind|Oathpact]], she and the others abandoned [Taln](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Taln "Taln") to his fate, leaving their [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblades]] behind. From then on out, they hid among the human populace.[^13] Most of her actions in the intervening four and a half millennia are unknown.

As Dova, she approached [[Taravangian - The Coppermind|Taravangian]] and warned him of a coming [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolation]], which prompted him to seek out [[Szeth - The Coppermind|Szeth]] after King [[Gavilar Kholin - The Coppermind|Gavilar's]] assassination.[^7] She has since been assisting the [Diagram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Diagram_\(group\) "Diagram (group)"). Sometime prior to Taravangian seizing the throne of [Jah Keved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jah_Keved "Jah Keved"), she reported that the frequency of [Death Rattles](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Death_Rattle "Death Rattle") had been declining further, hinting at [Moelach's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Moelach "Moelach") movement.[^6] She was later assigned with writing an anonymous essay revealing [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar]] and [[Elhokar Kholin - The Coppermind|Elhokar's]] decision to make the former a highking, to undermine Dalinar's authority along with the revelations of the translated [Eila Stele](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Eila_Stele "Eila Stele").[^18] With the fulfillment of the Diagram Taravangian instructed his followers to no longer trust Battar as he feared for her mental state after seeing the other Heralds.[^8]

According to [Gavinor](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Gavinor "Gavinor"), Battar visited [Aesudan Kholin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aesudan_Kholin "Aesudan Kholin") more than once, and he recognizes her as 'Aunt Dova' while in the Spiritual Realm with Dalinar and Navani.[^19] It is likely that this was taking place shortly prior to the Siege of Kholinar, as, due to his young age (five at the time of the vision), he would not retain memories from much earlier.

After Taravangian's ascension to [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], Battar is positioned to rule Kharbranth from the shadows. She seems to have abandoned morality for greed and corruption as demonstrated by her conversation with Odium and the treasures she keeps with her including “Priceless paintings, jeweled vases, \[and\] bars of aluminum”. Odium recruits her to switch allegiances in return for both her general service and "specifically, the art with [crystal spikes](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crystal_spike "Crystal spike") " that allows a person to sense investiture.[^10] She puts this art to use for him soon after, [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|putting spikes through the eyes]] of the blinded [[Moash - The Coppermind|Moash]];[^20] this allows him to see investiture and, specifically, spren.[^21]

After Taravangian ascends to [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]], Battar returns to Shinovar with the rest of the heralds to restore the Oathpact and protect the spren.[^22] While she had previously shown a willingness to serve Odium, it is unclear where her loyalties currently lie.

## Culture

> “ *Battah send that girl some sense to go with her intelligence.* ”
> 
> — [[Navani Kholin - The Coppermind|Navani Kholin]] [^23]

Battar has been worshipped since the [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver Kingdoms]] Epoch, with people swearing by her name even in-between the [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]].[^4] In the modern [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") tradition, she's known as **Battah'Elin**, and is associated with the number seven (Betab), the [Essence](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ten_Essences "Ten Essences") of Tallow, the [[Polestone - The Coppermind|gemstone]] Zircon, and the attributes Wise and Careful.[^14]

There is a temple to her in several major Vorin cities.[^24] [^25] The temple of Battah in [Thaylen City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_City "Thaylen City") is particularly notable -- it is the oldest in the city, and a must-see location for every arriving dignitary.[^26]

She is known as **Batlah** amongst the Shin.[^2] [^3]
