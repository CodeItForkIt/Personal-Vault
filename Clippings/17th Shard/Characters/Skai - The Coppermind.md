## Skai

**Skai** was the original [[Shards|Vessel]] of the [[Shards|Shard]] [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]].[^2] Sometime after the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]], he and [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona]] took up residence on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]].[^1] [[Edgli - The Coppermind|Edgli]] believes that, by doing so, they violated the original Vessels' pact to not settle on the same world as another Shard,[^3] [^4] though the exact terms of that agreement are somewhat unclear.[^5]

## Abilities and Attributes

![[Skai---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [zoethatcher\_art](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/zoethatcher_art "Coppermind:Artists/zoethatcher art")

Skai was a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], making him one of the most powerful entities in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]]. Prior to being splintered he was immortal, unaging, and incorporeal. The [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] is his and Aona's Shards pushed into the Cognitive Realm by Odium; it fuels the magics [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|AonDor]], [[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]], [[ChayShan - The Coppermind|ChayShan]], [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]], and [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealing]].[^6] [^7]

## History

Nothing is known about Skai's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]], other than that he came from [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]].[^8] For unknown reason, he and a group of others broke the [[17th Shard|cosmere's]] creator deity, [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces.[^9] Of those sixteen, he took up [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]], and departed alongside [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] for [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]].[^10]

Odium manipulated him into conflict with Aona and finished him off while he was weakened from it.[^11]

## Trivia

- Brandon once thought he might make Skai use she/her pronouns,[^12] but the very male-presenting Shards of Creation art (and image description) suggests that didn't make it into canon.
