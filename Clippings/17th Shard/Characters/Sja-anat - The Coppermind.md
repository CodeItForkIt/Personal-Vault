## Sja-anat

> “ *Of the Unmade, Sja-anat was most feared by the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiants]]. They spoke extensively of her ability to corrupt spren, though only "lesser" spren—whatever that means.* ”

**Sja-anat** is one of the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]],[^2] an ancient and terrible [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] of [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].[^3] Also known as the **Taker of Secrets**,[^2] Sja-anat is one of the sapient Unmade, fully self-aware and responsible for her own actions.[^4] Sja-anat is capable of corrupting spren with Odium's Investiture.[^2] Sja-anat has expressed interest in freeing herself from Odium's influence entirely and defecting from his forces.[^5]

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *Lore suggested leaving a city if the spren there start acting strangely. Curiously, Sja-anat was often regarded as an individual, when others—like [Moelach](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Moelach "Moelach") or [Ashertmarn](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ashertmarn "Ashertmarn") —were seen as forces.* ”

### Appearance

Sja-anat takes the form of a woman with waist-length black hair, wearing a sleeveless, flowing tunic with a simple belt at the waist.[^5] She can also appear as the shadow of a woman with long, flowing hair and white holes for eyes [^7]. Other times, she looks human but with the white holes for eyes of the shadow.[^8] So far, she has only been shown to be visible in mirrors, and only to the person she wishes to appear to.[^8] When human-like, the rest of the reflection is otherwise as normal, but when she fades into her shadowy variant, the rest of the reflection fades away as well.[^9] [^5]

### Personality

It is noted that Sja-anat wants to be a god in her own right.[^10] She is very deceptive, able to successfully trick Odium and aid the Radiants behind Odium's back,[^11] as well as deceive him on her personal objectives, such as successfully sending an Enlightened true spren to Mraize by hiding them in a group of Enlightened windspren.[^10] She is also independent and earnest; she does not want to be Odium's servant anymore and desperately wants Shallan to believe that she has defected from Odium.[^5] Sja-anat also cares for her children, the spren that she has Enlightened, and wants to find them a home, one that she would be able to share.[^8] [^10]

### Enlightening

Sja-anat's touch has the ability to corrupt spren that she comes into contact with, injecting them with [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]].[^2] [^13] Sja-anat refers to this process as **Enlightening**.[^11] Spren affected by Sja-anat begin to carry the taint of [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]],[^14] and in rare cases can even grant abilities associated with Odium's power,[^15] [^16] though the extent of his influence on the affected spren is unknown. Enlightened spren take on noticeably different appearances, typically changed in coloration and shape but still strongly connected to their original form.[^3] Other times the resemblance of an Enlightened spren to its previous appearance is much more tenuous, notably in the case of an Enlightened mistspren which is changed from appearing as a sparkle of reflected light [^17] to a more crystalline form that is structured like a shimmering red snowflake.[^18]

The lesser spren that Sja-anat Enlightens are capable of serving as her spies [^12] or delivering messages for her.[^11] Messages sent from Sja-anat are spoken by the Enlightened spren in Sja-anat's voice into the mind of the person to which they are sent. Sja-anat refers to the Enlightened spren as her children.[^8] [^19]

In the past Sja-anat often Enlightened lesser spren,[^2] [^12] but she has now displayed the ability to Enlighten true spren,[^20] such as spren capable of forming a Nahel bond like Glys, and the spren of the [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]].[^11] However, she does not usually Enlighten true spren without their consent.[^16] [^21]

Enlightening has "dire ramifications" for existing Nahel Bonds, so Sja-Anat usually Enlightens unbonded spren.[^22] Enlightened spren do not appear to be particularly dangerous, so it is unknown what impact Odium's influence has on the spren aside from their appearance. They work similarly to unEnlightened spren in [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]], although they were unaffected by the [[Sibling - The Coppermind|Sibling's]] [suppression fabrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Suppression_fabrial "Suppression fabrial") when its effect was inverted.[^23] It is theoretically possible for Sja-Anat to Enlighten deadeyes.[^24]

### Realmatics

Sja-anat exists between the Physical and Cognitive Realms, such that she experiences both simultaneously, but also such that both appear shadowy to her.[^10] She can only be seen in the Physical Realm through reflected light, such as in a mirror, though it is possible to catch fleeting glimpses in the shadows without a mirror.[^8] [^10] [^25]

## History

### Ancient History

Sja-anat's origin is unknown; thus it is uncertain when she came into being. She was involved in the past [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]],[^2] with her Enlightened spren serving as spies on mankind.[^12] In the past when her Enlightened spren were seen in a village, the people often fled their homes.[^2] Sja-anat herself was the Unmade most feared by the Radiants.[^1]

After [[Last Desolation - The Coppermind|Aharietiam]], Sja-anat remained on Roshar, although none of her Enlightened spren are known to have been sighted since the Final Desolation. In the time after [[Last Desolation - The Coppermind|Aharietiam]] she mostly slumbered, as it was difficult for her to think without her bond to Odium.[^10] At some point Sja-anat Enlightened the true spren [[Glys - The Coppermind|Glys]].[^20]

### The Gathering Storm

Once the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] appeared in [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar"), centuries prior to the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]],[^26] [^27] Sja-anat found herself revitalized, able to think and plan once again.[^10]

### The True Desolation

Following the arrival of the Everstorm in the Physical Realm and the beginning of the True Desolation on,[^28] Sja-anat became overtly active in the area around [Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar "Kholinar"). She began Enlightening spren in and around the city.[^2]

During the [Siege of Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Siege_of_Kholinar "Siege of Kholinar"), as a part of a trap for [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin Kholin]], [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]], [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]], and their spren, Sja-anat was forced to touch the spren of the Kholinar [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] to Enlighten it.[^5] When Shallan infiltrated the palace in the guise of a messenger, Sja-anat saw her.[^9] Sja-anat attempted to reach out to Shallan and tell her that she was not her enemy while she danced with the [Cult of Moments](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cult_of_Moments "Cult of Moments") [^29] and when she infiltrated the revel on the Oathgate.[^30]

> “ *Listen, Radiant. Listen to my plea. Ashertmarn fled on purpose. It is a trap. I was compelled to touch the spren of this device, so it will not function as you wish.*”
> 
> —Sja-anat to [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]] [^5]

During the assault on the [Kholinar Palace](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar_Palace "Kholinar Palace"), Sja-anat spoke to Shallan and revealed to her that the Oathgate had been Enlightened and that she was supposed to kill them if they activated the Oathgate.[^5] When Shallan and her companions decided to activate the Oathgate anyway, Sja-anat promised that she would try not to kill them,[^8] and when the gate was activated they were merely transported to Shadesmar.[^31]

After Shallan and her group were transported in Shadesmar, Sja-anat lied to Odium and told him that Enlightening the Oathgate had had an odd effect and that the Radiants had been sent far from the point of transfer.[^11] Despite this lie, Sja-anat believed that Odium still did not trust her. Shortly afterward, Sja-anat sent a Enlightened gloryspren to deliver a message to Shallan. It explained the lie that Sja-anat had told Odium, as well as the fact that Odium's servants were pursuing them in Shadesmar. It also revealed that Shallan's deception had worked, and Odium believed her to be an Elsecaller rather than a Lightweaver. At some point the [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]] became aware of Sja-anat's disloyalty to Odium and sent Shallan orders to either recruit her to their organization or capture her.[^32]

## Trivia

- As with many [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]], Sja-anat's name is derived from that of an ancient real-life deity; in her case, the inspiration is most likely [Anat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anat "wikipedia:Anat"), a Semitic war goddess.[^33]
