## Aona

**Aona** was the original [Vessel](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vessel "Vessel") of the [[Shards|Shard]] [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]].[^2] Sometime after the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]], she and [[Skai - The Coppermind|Skai]] took up residence on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]].[^1] [[Edgli - The Coppermind|Edgli]] believes that, by doing so, they violated the original Vessels' pact to not settle on the same world as another Shard,[^3] [^4] though the exact terms of that agreement are somewhat unclear.[^5] They were both [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] and their combined power forms the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]].[^1] [^6] [^7]

## Abilities and Attributes

Aona was a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], making her one of the most powerful entities in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]]. Prior to being splintered she was immortal, unaging, and incorporeal. The [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] is her and Skai's Shards pushed into the Cognitive Realm by Odium; it fuels the magics [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|AonDor]], [[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]], [[ChayShan - The Coppermind|ChayShan]], [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]], and [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealing]].[^8] [^9]

## History

Aona originated from [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]], and was a healer.[^10] [^11] Tanavast recalls her as always being kind to him, and being one of the best people among those that participated in the Shattering.[^11] [^12]

For an unknown reason, she and a group of others broke [[17th Shard|cosmere's]] creator deity, [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces.[^13] Of those sixteen, she took up [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]], and departed alongside [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] for [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]].[^14]

Odium manipulated her and Skai into conflict with each other and killed them while they were weakened from the conflict.[^11]

## Trivia

When [[Raoden - The Coppermind|Raoden]] enters the [[Devotion's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|pool]], he hears a voice, which may be that of Aona's [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 1|Cognitive Shadow]], though whether or not she even has a Cognitive Shadow is unknown.[^15] [^16]
