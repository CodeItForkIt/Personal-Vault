## Resonance

> “ *The effects here are more subtle than they are when mixing Surges on Roshar, but I am convinced that each unique combination also creates something distinctive. Not just two powers, you could say, but two powers … and an effect. This demands further study.* ”
> 
> —Khriss.[^1]

**Resonances** are additional powers or effects gained by people with access to more than one [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested Art]] or [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surge]].[^2]

## Mechanics

Resonances arise from the nature of how [[Shards]] combine.[^3] They are not simply a combination of the two base powers, but rather a separate, more distinctive, effect.[^1] The combination of powers can also cause slight variances in how the base powers are manifested, which is why [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]] of different orders may manifest their shared [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] slightly differently.[^4] The longer a person uses their two powers, the more likely they are to acquire a resonance.[^5]

An individual with many powers, such as a [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Mistborn]] or Full [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemist]], may lose this added effect.[^6] With so many powers, the individual powers effectively wash out any resonance. It is still possible for such people to have a resonance, but it would be much less pronounced.[^7] Temporarily gaining additional powers, such as from the [Bands of Mourning](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bands_of_Mourning "Bands of Mourning"), would not deprive someone of a resonance they previously had. Someone with one power who began using the Bands of Mourning might develop a resonance between their original power and the Bands.[^5] It is possible for a Surgebinder with multiple [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblades]] / [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bonds]] to develop new abilities and resonances between surges that aren't normally combined.[^8]

## Known Resonances

### Surgebinding

All orders of [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]] have access to two Surges,[^9] and therefore each order has a resonance of their own.[^10] [^11] If a Radiant were to gain access to other Surges, either through an [[Honorblade - The Coppermind|Honorblade]] or [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|bonding]] to another different [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]], resonances can form between Surges that wouldn't normally have them.[^8] Known [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]] resonances include:

- [[Order of Windrunners - The Coppermind|Windrunners']] resonance is an increased number of squires, more than any other order.[^12]
- [[Order of Lightweavers - The Coppermind|Lightweavers']] resonance is increased mnemonic ability.[^13] [^14] For [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]], this manifests as the ability to take a "Memory" of a scene, which she can recall at a later time with perfect accuracy; when she put it to paper as a painting or a sketch the Memory is completely removed from her mind.[^15] [^16] This is similar to the way an [[Copper - The Coppermind#Feruchemical Use|Archivist]] stores memories in their copperminds. There is a limit to how many Memories she can store at the same time.[^17] Memories are tied to [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual]] [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]].[^18] Lightweavers can experience light and color differently and may be able to minutely alter the mood of others.[^19]

### Metallic Arts

[[Twinborn - The Coppermind|Twinborn]], individuals who are both [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Ferrings]] and [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Mistings]], also have resonances, although those resonances are typically more subtle than the resonances between [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^1] [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]] will usually not create resonances, but it is possible for it to happen.[^20]

## Potential Resonances

- [[Order of Skybreakers - The Coppermind|Skybreakers']] resonance is unknown, though it does not involve telling guilty and innocent people apart.[^21]
- [[Order of Edgedancers - The Coppermind|Edgedancers']] resonance may be the ability to communicate especially well,[^4] as scholars considered the pre- [[Day of Recreance - The Coppermind|Recreance]] Edgedancers "the most articulate and refined" of the Radiants,[^22] and [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]] is easily able to speak and understand quite opaque street slang in [[Yeddaw - The Coppermind|Yeddaw]].[^23]
- [Elsecallers'](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elsecaller "Elsecaller") resonance may be an internal GPS, due to [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah]] being able to pinpoint the closest settlement after returning from Shadesmar without any equipment.[^24] [^13] Elsecallers' ability to [[Soulcasting - The Coppermind|Soulcast]] at a distance is not a resonance, as it is something that Lightweavers could learn as well.[^25]

## Trivia

- The nature of resonances was partially inspired by the [constructive interference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wave_interference "wikipedia:Wave interference") of waves.[^26]
- [Waxillium Ladrian's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Waxillium_Ladrian "Waxillium Ladrian") ability to create a " [[Steel - The Coppermind#Allomantic Use|steel bubble]] " to deflect metal moving around him [^27] was originally intended to be the result of him being a [[Savant - The Coppermind|savant]] in the resonance between his [[Steel - The Coppermind#Allomantic Use|Allomantic steel]] and [[Iron - The Coppermind#Feruchemical Use|Feruchemical iron]]. Brandon later indicated that may not still be accurate, as he realized Wax was not manifesting the usual consequences of savantism.[^28] [^3]
- There are 256 possible Twinborn resonances, with 16 of those being compounders.
- There are 45 possible Surgebinding resonances, with 10 coming from the standard Radiant orders.
