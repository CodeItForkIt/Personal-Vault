## Nightmare

**Nightmares** are a type of [[Cognitive Shadow - The Coppermind|Cognitive Shadow]] native to [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]] which emerge from the [[Shroud - The Coppermind|shroud]] to feed on the minds of people while they sleep. They are formed by the souls of dead Komashi people killed by the [[Father machine - The Coppermind|father machine]], and made into its servants.

## History

The nightmares did not originally exist, they were created by a disaster wherein the [[Father machine - The Coppermind|father machine]] killed the vast majority of the inhabitants of [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]], creating the nightmares as [[Cognitive Shadow - The Coppermind|Cognitive Shadows]] held captive by the machine.[^1] [^2]

Following this disaster, they were kept in a replica of different towns in [[Torio - The Coppermind|Torio]], to repeat the same day disguised as the people they once were. At night, they were left to travel through the shroud and feed upon the people of various surviving nations, most notably [Nagadan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nagadan "Nagadan"). In response to this, the profession of [[Painter - The Coppermind|painters]] developed, in order to turn them into a more harmless shape, and banish them back into the shroud.[^2]

After the father machine was destroyed and the shroud was dispelled, all of the nightmares, freed from the tyranny of the machine, dissolved into nothingness.[^3] [^4]

## Appearance and Behavior

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Nightmare---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Aliya Chen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Aliya_Chen "Coppermind:Artists/Aliya Chen")

A Nightmare changing into the shape of bamboo.

Nightmares are inky black creatures that can take on many forms.[^5] They begin as amorphous blobs, but over time, as they feed upon the thoughts and ideas of people, they start to take on more recognizable forms, gradually gaining more and more form as they pick up on more thoughts.[^5] They rarely have faces, the face usually taking on the form of an even deeper blackness, which drips a dark liquid.[^5] They constantly let of a gaseous blackness as they move around outside of the shroud, and if they fail to return to the shroud, they can evaporate away completely.[^5]

They have to go on multiple trips to a city in order to gain enough substance to be truly dangerous, usually around ten or so trips.[^5] If a nightmare has enough form, it would become something known as a **stable nightmare**, which are particularly dangerous, and require elite painters in order to neutralize them, known as the [Dreamwatch](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dreamwatch "Dreamwatch").[^6] Stable enough nightmares have pinprick white eyes dotting their face, and bone-white teeth.[^6] [^7] [^8]

The shape of a nightmare can be forcibly changed by trying to think of it as a certain form through the focus of painting. This causes them to take on the shape of the painting. The closer the painting is to something they resemble, the more power one has over them.[^5] One can return them back to their human form by making a painting of who they used to be, but this doesn't change them back into a flesh and blood human, and they will still fade away without the shroud.[^8]

Nightmares spend most of their time in the [[Yoki-hijo - The Coppermind|yoki-hijo]] prisons, hidden away in the shroud. While there, they mostly behave as they did when human, and do not think of themselves as Nightmares.[^9] [^10] But, when the hold of the machine becomes looser, they are able to wander and travel towards population centers, attracted to the Cognitive activity focused at such centers. The reason for their attraction to human cognitive activity, and for feeding upon it to take on form and substance, is due to lusting for the life they lost upon the activation of the [[Father machine - The Coppermind|father machine]].[^10] [^2]

## Nature and Abilities

Nightmares are usually almost entirely incorporeal, and are able to pass through solid substances such as walls.[^5] Their forms are also unstable, and they constantly let off an inky black gas, if they fail to return to the shroud in order to re-Invest themselves, they will evaporate away completely.[^5] There are stories of nightmares so stable that they begin to take on the color of flesh, but the truth of these stories are unknown.[^11]

They are capable of detecting thoughts, most notably fear, and to hide from one, one should think calm and placid thoughts. Fear is also capable of giving it more form and substance, as it feeds on them.[^5] [^6]

Nightmares are able to feed upon Investiture.[^7] [^12] They can also see normally invisible Cognitive Shadows.[^7]

It is theoretically possible to form a bond with a nightmare,[^13] although exactly how such a bond would function is unknown.
