## Wan ShaiLu

**Wan ShaiLu**, known as **Shai**, is a [[MaiPon - The Coppermind|MaiPon]] woman from [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]]. She has a reputation throughout the [[Rose Empire - The Coppermind|Rose Empire]] as being one of the best [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgers]] alive.[^4] She was imprisoned in her homeland, but escapes after restoring [[Ashravan - The Coppermind|Ashravan's]] health.[^1] She becomes a [[17th Shard|worldhopper]] and leaves Sel, and is unable to return.[^5]

## Appearance and Personality

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Wan-ShaiLu---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [botanicaxu](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/botanicaxu "Coppermind:Artists/botanicaxu")

Shai is small of stature like the rest of the MaiPon people. She has black hair that lays straight and long.[^6] [^7] She wears spectacles because of her bad eyesight.[^6] She is implied to have epicanthic folds, and has prominent cheekbones.[^7] Her arms are layered with shallow cuts from the [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealer]] [[Weedfingers - The Coppermind|Weedfingers]], who needs her blood to keep her imprisoned.[^8] Her voice is on the deeper end for a woman, and she uses the expression "Nights!" as an expletive.[^7]

She loves the thrill, the accomplishment and the wonder of Forgery. However, at times, she is tempted by a simple life where she doesn't have to hide or lie.[^9] She constantly quotes her **Uncle Won** and **Aunt Sol** although they don't really exist. They are figments of her imagination created for one of her Essence Marks.[^9] She believes her Forgeries are works of art and she gets angry when contradicted on that.[^10]

She worships the [Unknown God](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Unknown_God "Unknown God").[^6] She holds a deep hatred for [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]], and by extension [[Bavadin - The Coppermind|Bavadin]], due to her ideals and actions. This hatred is enough that it can sometimes cloud her judgement.[^7]

### Elantrian

As an Elantrian, she has a very high opinion of herself, seeing herself as a divinity worthy of praise and offerings. In this form, she has the glowing skin of an Elantrian and wears her hair up in a bun.[^11]

## Attributes and Abilities

Shai is a very talented artist,[^4] but generally uses her ability to make forgeries of paintings. She reads people very well and is a master manipulator.[^12] She has good control of her emotions. She is very knowledgeable in art history and has a very good understanding of [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realmatic Theory]].[^13]

Shai is one of the best [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgers]] ever to live, especially in [[Forgery - The Coppermind#Soul Forging|Soul Forgery]]. [[Forgery - The Coppermind#Resealing|Flesh Forgery]] is one of the few branches of Forgery she hasn't studied in depth because getting a Flesh Forgery wrong usually means the subject dying.[^6]

### Essence Marks

During her imprisonment in the [Imperial Seat](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Imperial_Seat "Imperial Seat"), Shai had five [Essence Marks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Essence_Mark "Essence Mark"), which took her over eight years to create.[^9] As of her time on Scadrial, she has created at least one more.[^11]

In the decades to centuries between her escape from the Rose Empire, and her work against the Set alongside Marasi, she gained a lot of skill in her use of Essence Marks. She is now able to create Essence Marks that last far longer than the mere day [^14] [^1] that her old ones last for, enough so that she believes they could be potentially permanent, even without hemerine reapplication.[^11] [^15] [^16] Therefore bypassing, through unknown means, the limitations posed by the shifting Identity of sentient beings.[^14] These are not necessarily permanent, and might wear off some day.[^16]

Shaizan

Shaizan, translated Shai of the Fist,[^9] is an extremely skilled warrior who spent the last fifteen years among the [Teullu](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Teullu "Teullu") people, who adopted and trained her in their martial arts. They also gave her the name Shaizan. Gaining access to Teullu training was very difficult. Shaizan was nearly killed a dozen times while training. She has a lean, hard body, short hair, and a scar that stretches from her nose down around her right cheek. Unlike Shai, she doesn't need spectacles. She doesn't know how to create [soulstamps](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulstamp "Soulstamp") and doesn't have scholarly inclinations. She also doesn't have the patience for witticisms or gibes. She is a creature of instinct and thrives on a challenge. It took Shai two years to create this Essence Mark.[^1]

The Scholar

Using this Essence Mark, she can speak a dozen languages, from [Fen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fen_\(language\) "Fen (language)") to [Mulla'dil](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mulla%27dil "Mulla'dil") and a few from [[Sel - The Coppermind|Opelon]] as well. She knows dozens of cultures and how to move in them. She knows science, mathematics, and many major political factions of the world.[^9]

The Beggar

Using this Essence Mark, she knows the ways of the streets and how to survive in a city's underworld. She is an incomparable lockpick and has years of experience as a contortionist. She is emaciated, with lost hair and scarred skin.[^9] Shai finds thinking like a beggar uncomfortable.[^17]

The Survivalist

This Essence Mark gives her keen skills as a woodsman and a hunter as well as many others useful for surviving in the wilderness.[^9]

The Way Out

She knows cooking, farmwork, and sewing. She visited a resealer and was given a remedy, which is actually the soulstamp for this Essence Mark, for an illness. She believes she will die unless she applies the soulstamp every morning. Her Aunt Sol and Uncle Won will send her letters, hundreds of which Shai already wrote and will give to a delivery service before using the soulstamp. She is afraid of traveling, thinking a trip to visit her relatives is too dangerous. Unlike Shai's other Essence Marks, this one doesn't know she is a Forger. She is Shai's way out to a simpler life. The Mark rewrites her years as a Forger and removes all knowledge of Forgery from her mind.[^9] Later, Shai promises to add Gaotona as "a kindly grandfather... who I respected very much" to the Essence Mark.[^1]

Elantrian

This Essence Mark changes her past so her parents moved to [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]] before she was born, making her an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian"). She refers to herself as Shai (pronounced as in Aonic), blessed of the [[Shaod - The Coppermind|Shaod]]. She regards this Essence Mark as dangerous, as it could become permanent.[^11] [^3]

### Universal Stamps

Shai carries two soulstamps that are designed to work on any object for a limited time. One creates a doorway, and the other repairs broken objects.[^18] This is still limited by the feasibility of a Forgery, to a degree. If a Forgery with a Universal Stamp is completely infeasible, the stamp would not properly take.

## History

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Wan-ShaiLu---The-Coppermind-2.jpg]]

by [automaticmoon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/automaticmoon "Coppermind:Artists/automaticmoon")

Shai attempts to recreate the soul of the Emperor Ashravan

Shai first started inquiring about being a Forger when she was eight years old.[^9] As an apprentice, she received training in a variety of mundane forgeries such as art and writing;[^19] she also learned information useful in Forgery, such as how to identify different types of stone.[^6] Her first trainer in Forgery was [Tao](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tao "Tao"), who taught her the value of respecting people even as you manipulated their perceptions.[^20] She noted that apprentice Forgers spend much of their time learning about human nature before they are even allowed to touch soulstone.[^14] She also had other mentors, and she learned the finer mechanics of creating and applying soulstamps.[^13] Her training was performed "half in secret", as is typical in MaiPon.[^19]

Over time, she created five different [Essence Marks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Essence_Mark "Essence Mark"). Each of these marks took years to create and were essentially irreplaceable.[^6] [^1] For example, as part of the creation of one of the Essence Marks, Shai spent a year training with the insular [Teullu](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Teullu "Teullu") people, a training so intense that she nearly died on many occasions.[^1]

### Infiltration of the Rose Palace

[Han ShuXen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Han_ShuXen "Han ShuXen"), an old painter who had tutored Shai as a child, asked her to destroy his masterpiece *[Lily of the Spring Pond](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lily_of_the_Spring_Pond "Lily of the Spring Pond")*. ShuXen hated the Rose Empire and didn't want his painting, which he had painted for a woman he once loved, in their hands. He helped Shai create a counterfeit.[^9]

Shai managed to destroy the original painting in the Arbiter [Frava's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Frava "Frava") office near the [Imperial Gallery](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Imperial_Gallery "Imperial Gallery"), replacing it with a fake and planting another lower-quality counterfeit in her room in order to hide the theft.[^14] With the help of the [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Imperial Fool]], she also broke into the [Rose Palace's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rose_Palace "Rose Palace") Imperial Gallery by impersonating a noblewoman to steal the [Moon Scepter](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Moon_Scepter "Moon Scepter"). However, the Fool betrayed her. He stole Shai's copy of the Moon Scepter, swapped it for the original, then escaped after giving Shai up to the palace guards. Shai was captured and sentenced to public execution for theft.[^6]

### The Emperor's Soul

A day before her execution, she was asked by the council of five arbiters to Forge a soul in exchange for her freedom. Shai didn't think it was possible to create a soulstamp for an entire person's soul in the hundred days they gave her, but she accepted their offer in the hope that it would give her time to escape. She was taken before the Emperor [[Ashravan - The Coppermind|Ashravan]] and told that he took a crossbow bolt in the head from assassins sent by the [Glory Faction](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Glory_Faction "Glory Faction") and despite surgeons [[Forgery - The Coppermind|resealing]] the wound, he is in a vegetative state. She was told to replace the emperor's soul with a [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]].[^6]

She was given a room barely better than a cell which, over time, she transformed into the finest room in the whole palace with Forgery.[^9] She received her Forgery tools except for her Essence Marks. [[Gaotona - The Coppermind|Gaotona]] volunteered to be her test subject for the soulstamps she created for Emperor Ashravan.[^14] The arbiters hired a [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealer]] to make sure of her cooperation.[^10] As they work edtogether, Gaotona and Shai grew fond of each other and eventually became friends. Shai started to collect information on Ashravan's personality and memory to use for the soulstamp.[^8]

Shai concluded that the other arbiters were planning to kill her before the soulstamp was done and have another Forger finish her work. She started lacing her notes with subtext about the arduousness of the task to deter the other Forger, [Nyen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nyen "Nyen").[^21] All the arbiters except Gaotona proposed that she create a backdoor to the emperor's soul that would allow him to be controlled by the arbiters. Shai played along but eventually refrained from doing it, even for herself.[^19]

As her hundred days passed, Shai was constantly tempted to escape but was driven by the challenge of the task.[^9] On day ninety-seven, just before she finished the soulstamp, she enacted her plan to get past the Bloodsealer. The next day, Captain [Zu](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Zu_\(Sel\) "Zu (Sel)") tried to kill her, a move she had expected from some of the guards and managed to evade by Forging traps into her room. She escaped and made her way to Ashravan's chambers, desperate to see if she had managed to Forge a soul from nothing. When it succeeded, Gaotona gave her back her Essence Marks and she used them to escape the palace.[^1] It later turned out that she didn't reForge Ashravan's soul exactly the way it was and made small changes, nudging him on the path to being a better ruler.[^17]

After she escaped, Shai planned to track down the Imperial Fool who betrayed her.[^17] She eventually tracked him down, and they kept an eye on each other for a long time.[^7] [^22]

### Resistance against Trell

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Wan-ShaiLu---The-Coppermind-3.png]]

by [Caio Santos](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Caio_Santos "Coppermind:Artists/Caio Santos")

Turning into an Elantrian

At some point after her release from the Rose Empire, she became a member of the Ghostbloods and worked on [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] in order to resist the oncoming threat of [Trell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Trell_\(Trellism\) "Trell (Trellism)"), working under the codename of Moonlight.[^23]

Six years after the first interaction between the Elendel Basin and the Southern Scadrians, she found [[Marasi Colms - The Coppermind|Marasi Colms]] in a series of underground caverns, crashing a [Set](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Set "Set") smuggling operation. She secretly gave her a letter, informing her that the Ghostbloods were watching her and were impressed by her accomplishments.[^24]

Several days later, she met with Marasi, acting as her driver for a sting operation on the Set smuggling operation in [Bilming](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bilming "Bilming"). Introducing herself as a member of the Ghostbloods using the code name Moonlight, she explained to Marasi the nature of Trell and [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]].[^7]

Shai assisted Marasi, [Wax](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wax "Wax"), and [Wayne](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wayne "Wayne") in their investigation and pursuit of the Set until being discovered by [Telsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Telsin "Telsin"), then an avatar of Autonomy.[^25] [^26] When the group separated, Shai was tricked by Marasi into bringing her back to the Ghostblood hideout in Bilming, which Marasi believed may have been her intention regardless.[^23] Shai introduced Marasi to the small crew of Scadrian Ghostbloods—including to Kelsier, via Seon—who believed that Shai had taken on Marasi as her apprentice.[^27] [^28]

With some research, Shai, the Ghostbloods, and Marasi discovered an entrance to the Set's underground [caverns](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wayfarer "Wayfarer"). She took TwinSoul with them to infiltrate the complex, and—at Kelsier's request—three large jars of purified Dor, which Shai looked upon with awe.[^28]

She was forced to use a Stamp to change her past and turn herself into an Elantrian, also changing her personality and giving her the abilities of an Elantrian. She used [[AonDor - The Coppermind|AonDor]] in order to create an illusion of Marasi, letting her escape.[^11] Following this, she vanished and the Ghostbloods sent out a mission to look for her.[^15]

## Relationships

### Bavadin

She holds a deep hatred for Bavadin, fundamentally disagreeing with her ideals and actions.[^7] They have met before at an unknown point.[^25]

### Kelsier

Kelsier is her mentor, and she holds a deep respect for him.<sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

### Marasi

She has a great interest in Marasi, and was the one who brought her into the Ghostblood's fold.[^7]

### Prasanva

She works with Prasanva in Bilming.[^27] She is willing to try to help him get back to his homeworld, but he rejects her offer, stating that it is too dangerous.[^18] <sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

### Gaotona

Gaotona held a strong dislike for Shai, seeing her forgery as an abomination, and felt that she was wasting her immense talent.[^14] But over time, she managed to change his view on art, and make him grow a fondness for her, making him wish to save her from execution, even if he didn't feel as if he was on her side.<sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

### Hoid

She despises Hoid for deceiving her and running away with the Moon Scepter, replacing it with her forgery.[^6] After escaping from the Rose Empire, she began to hunt after him to enact her revenge, an act that she eventually succeeded at.[^7] [^22] Their current relationship is unclear, but she does wonder to herself if she was keeping watch over him, or he over her, but she knows that they are in reality just keeping watch over the same third parties.[^7]

### Kaise

She works with Kaise in Bilming.[^27] <sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

- In one of *[The Emperor's Soul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Emperor%27s_Soul "The Emperor's Soul")* deleted scenes, [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] visits her in prison and discusses the [thing](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Moon_Scepter "Moon Scepter") she stole and how he stole it first.[^29]
