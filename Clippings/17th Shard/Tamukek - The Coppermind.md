## Tamukek

**Tamukeks** are a type of bone that can be used for inter- [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realm]] and interplanetary communication, similar to a [[Seon - The Coppermind|seon]].[^1] Most notably they are used to interact with [dragons](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\) "Dragon (cosmere)") for purposes of prayer.[^2] [^3] [^4] They have some similarities to [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]].[^5]

[[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] has possession of a tamukek as of the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows"), which he used to contact [[Frost - The Coppermind|Frost]] and his sister.[^6] He would later use another to contact " [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|the meditative realm of the dragons]] " for "wisdom of the ancient dead"; this gave him insight on [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin's]] recent actions and their implications.[^7] An interference cloud made by a [Sleepless interference machine](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sleepless_interference_machine "Sleepless interference machine") has the side effect of preventing dragons from convening with their followers through a tamukek.[^2]

## Trivia

- When writing *[Rhythm of War](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm_of_War "Rhythm of War")*, Brandon debated whether to have the communication box that [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize]] gave to [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] contain a seon or a tamukek, but eventually settled on making it a seon because they have personalities whereas tamukeks are just bones.[^1]
- Tamukeks (then spelled "Tamu Keks") originated in the book *[Dragonsteel Prime](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragonsteel_Prime "Dragonsteel Prime")*.[^4]
