## Classical Scadrial

**Classical Scadrial** or the **classical era of Scadrial** refers to the period of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial's]] history directly preceding the [Ascension of the Lord Ruler](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ascension_of_the_Lord_Ruler "Ascension of the Lord Ruler") and the beginning of the [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|Final Empire]].

## Geography and Ecology

> “ *The great mountains to the north -- with their bald snowcaps and forested mantles -- stand like watchful gods over this land of green fertility. My own lands to the south are mostly flat; I think that they might look less dreary if there were a few mountains to vary the terrain.* ”
> 
> —Alendi's logbook.

Classical Scadrial had a blue sky and a yellow sun. This ended with the Lord Ruler's ascension, which marked the end of the Classical age of Scadrial.

During this time Scadrial also had many of the same animals and plants as Yolen, including horses, flowers and dogs. The [Deepness](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deepness "Deepness"), which arrived at the end of the era, and the Lord Ruler's Ascension, destroyed many species.[^1] [^2]

### Notable Landmarks

- [Khlennium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khlennium "Khlennium") - A large city located somewhere in the middle of the northern continent; the lands around it were mostly flat.[^3]
- [[Terris - The Coppermind|Terris]] hills - A large mountain range in the north which was home to the [[Terris - The Coppermind|Terris]] people.[^3]
- [Darrelnai](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Darrelnai "Darrelnai") - A city or kingdom located on the northern continent. It survived the Deepness and was eventually conquered by the [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Lord Ruler]].[^4]
- The [Southern Islands](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Southern_Islands "Southern Islands") - A large collection of islands in the south of what would later become the [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|Final Empire]]. These islands were home to many volcanoes.[^5]

## Culture and Societies

### Forms of Government

Many nations during this time were ruled by monarchs, both kings and queens. Examples of nations ruled by monarchs are [Khlennium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khlennium "Khlennium") and "The fiery empire of the West." [^6] Not all nations were ruled by monarchs however: the Terris, for example, were not ruled by a monarch, although their form of government is unknown.

The various classical civilizations believed in a number of [prophecies](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Terris_Prophecies "Terris Prophecies"). These prophecies were about a Hero, called The Hero of Ages, who would one day save the world.[^7] The civilizations all had a different name for this Hero, with the Khlenni calling him Rabzeen, but the prophecies were the same.[^8]

### The Metallic Arts

#### Allomancy

During this time period, [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]] was almost entirely unheard of. Towards the end of the era, when the Deepness arrived, it began to snap people into Mistings;[^9] [^10] Mistborn did not appear until Rashek discovered and gave away the [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]] located at the [Well of Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Ascension "Well of Ascension").[^11]

#### Feruchemy

[[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]] was the most widespread of the [[Metallic Arts]], and even that remained isolated with the [[Terris - The Coppermind|Terris]] people.[^12] The Terris used Feruchemy for many things in their daily lives, from storing and tapping heat to pass through mountain passes to storing memories in their [[Copper - The Coppermind#Feruchemical Use|copperminds]]. [Worldbringers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Worldbringer "Worldbringer") in particular were known for using Feruchemy to record lore.

#### Hemalurgy

[[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]] was unknown to most, although piercings were seen as a means of communicating with a deity. For this reason the Hero of Ages wore Hemalurgically-charged piercings. This was a result of [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] manipulating the [Terris Prophecies](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Terris_Prophecies "Terris Prophecies").[^13]

### Technology

The general level of technology at the end of this era was more advanced than that of the [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|Final Empire]]. Before [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek's]] Ascension, the people of Classical Scadrial had achieved steam technology, and were close to achieving locomotive technology.[^14]

## History

The classical age of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] began with the planet's creation by the [[Shards]] [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]] and [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]].[^22] These Shards settled in the [Scadrian System](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Scadrian_System "Scadrian System") and created Scadrial together. During this process they not only created the planet, but they also created life, including humans. During the Classical era of Scadrial, the magnetic and geographic poles were in the same location.[^23]

Over time, technology advanced and attained an early industrial level of technological and cultural advancement.[^24]

Thousands of years after the creation of the planet, a young man called [Alendi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alendi "Alendi") was forced to flee his village due to unknown reasons. It is likely that this was exactly sixteen years before the Well of Ascension was refilled.[^25] He ended up in [Khlennium](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khlennium "Khlennium"), seeking a life of indistinction. Once there he met [Kwaan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kwaan "Kwaan"), a [[Terris - The Coppermind|Terris]] worldbringer. Kwaan employed him as an assistant during his first months in the city.[^26] Years later Kwaan connected all of the signs of the Anticipation to Alendi, and announced his discovery to the other Worldbringers, they didn't believe him however.[^8] [^27] So Kwaan became determined to convince them, insisting on traveling with Alendi to witness his journeys.[^28] After a while even the other Worldbringers became convinced that Alendi must be the Hero of Ages,[^29] as Alendi fueled these rumors to persuade the world of his cause,[^30] and Ruin slightly altered the prophecies to fit Alendi.[^31]

It was around this time that the Deepness arrived, providing a threat that drove men to desperation both in action and belief.[^32] The Deepness began ravaging the land, killing plants and animals alike.

At some point in time Alendi became king of Khlennium,[^6] possibly by marrying the previous king's daughter.[^33] Under the growing threat of the Deepness, Alendi united several nations (including Terris),[^34] some by diplomacy,[^35] and others by conquering them.[^36] Except for a few dissenters, Alendi was able to create a general unity on Scadrial under reign of Khlennium.[^35]

During his encounters with the Deepness, he Snapped and his Allomantic powers were awakened.[^10] He believed that he had sensed the Deepness's mind, and noted that it was not hateful, but would destroy the world as part of its nature. After Alendi spent a decade leading armies, the Terris Worldbringers decided that Alendi, the assumed Hero of Ages, should set forth to the Well of Ascension [^37] and give up its power in the name of the greater good.[^38] However, Kwaan realized that some being deceived them all by altering the prophecies, but he couldn't convince Alendi and the other Worldbringers [^39] that the power at the Well should not be released.[^40] Soon Alendi departed towards the Well of Ascension, giving no heed to Kwaan's warning since Alendi surrounded himself with other counselors who told him what he wanted to hear.[^41]

Upon arriving in Terris, Alendi replaced his normal attendants with Terris packmen who could carry his gear in the cold unforgiving mountains.[^42] They were a group of [[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemists]] led by [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek]].[^43] Alendi started keeping a [logbook](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alendi%27s_logbook "Alendi's logbook"),[^44] in which he recorded his quest for the Well, describing hearing pulses coming from the Well [^45] due to his abilities as a [[Bronze - The Coppermind|Seeker]].[^9] In this journal he also expressed regrets about the death and fear he has caused [^46] [^47], doubts about his own sanity [^48], but also his fear of the Deepness [^49], and his faith that it needed to be defeated [^50].

Unbeknownst to Alendi, Kwaan had instructed Rashek to do everything in his power to keep Alendi away from the Well of Ascension,[^39] and if necessary even to kill Alendi to prevent him from giving up the Well's power.[^39] Since the pulses coming from the Well drew Alendi closer with each beat,[^45] Rashek could not lead him astray and was forced to kill him shortly before arriving at their destination. He then entered the room with the Well and Ascended. In the following moments, before the power left him, he reshaped the world, thus ending the era of Classical Scadrial.
