## Siah Aimian

**Siah Aimians** are a non-human race of beings that originated in the [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver Kingdom]] of [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^1]

## Characteristics

The only known Siah Aimian is [Axies](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Axies "Axies") the Collector. Based on his description and viewpoint, Siah Aimians look nearly human but usually have pale white (nearly blue) skin, deep blue nails and "crystalline" blue eyes.[^2] [^3] However, their "true" form is unclear as they are able to sculpt their bodies at will; Axies uses this ability to temporarily suppress his sense of smell and to heal from both a hangover and some physical bruising.[^3] They can pose as human,[^4] likely using a combination of a disguise and their abilities. Siah Aimians can also create intricate tattoo-like markings on their skin; Axies uses this ability to take notes that cannot be stolen.[^3]

Axies also notes that humans may notice him because he casts a shadow in the wrong direction (towards light).[^3] This stems from a connection to the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]].[^5] [[Rysn Ftori - The Coppermind|Rysn]], perhaps influenced by local legends, refers to this as a " [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringer]] shadow".[^2]

Siah Aimians are characterized as "extremely long-lived".[^6] Although the specifics of their magical abilities have not been revealed, they are functionally immortal.[^6] [^7] Axies is flippant about being beaten, robbed, imprisoned, and tied upside down to part of a [[Tai-na - The Coppermind|Tai-na]]. He sees pain and blackouts as minor inconveniences and does not appear to have any fear of permanent injury or death.[^2] [^3]

Likely due to their long life, Siah appear to be able to speak multiple languages of Roshar, although they may have an unusual accent.[^2]

## History

Siah Aimians and the [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] (also known as Dysian Aimians) both lived in Aimia prior to the [[Scouring of Aimia - The Coppermind|scouring of Aimia]], which has been characterized by [Hessi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hessi "Hessi") as "total destruction".[^8] The two races are not related.[^9] Although Axies thinks it has not been long since the scouring, he later considers "a few centuries" to be a relatively short amount of time. Little is known about the current state of Aimia.

The etymology of "Siah" and "Dysian" is not known. Both races of Aimians still reside on Roshar and have been seen in various parts of the continent.[^1]

Axies refers to his bad luck that is a result of the " [Curse of Kind](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Curse_of_Kind "Curse of Kind") ", but it is not clear what the curse is or whether it affects all Siah.[^3]

## Relationships with Other Races

### Humans

At some point in history, Siah Aimians and humans created hybrid offspring,[^10] possibly reflected in the blue skin of the people of [[Natanatan - The Coppermind|Natanatan]] [^11] or the prominent blue veins of the women of [Babatharnam](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Babatharnam "Babatharnam").[^12] Human-Siah hybrids have shadows that are unusual in some way.[^13]

Most cultures on Roshar remain aware of Siah Aimians through legends and limited interaction, but the reaction to them varies from curiosity and unease to outright disgust. Rysn believes that Aimians are bad luck, [Kylrm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kylrm "Kylrm") believes they bring curses onto people, and [Nlent](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nlent "Nlent") believes they can remove their limbs and skin because they are evil in human form.[^2]

According to a report from [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin]] to [Vstim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vstim "Vstim"), there are a few Aimians of the variety that Rysn saw moving among the people of Roshar.[^14]

### Sleepless

The past and present relationship between the two races of Aimians is unclear. Both races are functionally immortal; although the timeline of Aimia is not well-defined, it is likely that most individuals coexisted on Aimia for many thousands of years before the scouring. A Sleepless named [Arclo](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Arclo "Arclo") refers to his ability to imitate humans almost as well as a Siah and mentions Axies in a familiar manner.[^4] After the scouring, some of the Sleepless continue to protect secrets in the Aimian capital of [Akinah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Akinah "Akinah"),[^15] but it is not known if Siah Aimians are involved.

While in [Kasitor](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kasitor "Kasitor"), Axies noted that "Aimians—even Siah Aimians—were rare" at the time, implying that Siah Aimians have historically outnumbered Sleepless on the Rosharan mainland.[^3] However, this does not provide much insight regarding the population of Siah Aimians, as only twenty-four Sleepless swarms are known to have lived on Roshar.[^16]
