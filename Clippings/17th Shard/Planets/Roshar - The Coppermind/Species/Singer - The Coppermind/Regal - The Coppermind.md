## Regal

> “ *Forms of power, yes, but also forms of obedience. The gods commanded, and we did obey, always. Always.* ”
> 
> — [Jaxlim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jaxlim "Jaxlim") [^1]

**Regals** are [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] who bond with lesser [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]], granting them a transformation called a **form of power**. In the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringer]] hierarchy, they are ranked above common singers, and below the [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]].[^2] Singers sometimes formally refer to Regals as **Chosen**.[^3]

## Mechanics

### Formation and Appearance

To become a Regal, a singer has to bond a [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|Voidspren]]; a non-sapient variety is used for this purpose.[^4] The form is assumed much like any other, by stepping into the storm with the mindset to bond an appropriate spren. Unlike a Fused possession, a form of power can be taken on both in a [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorm]] and the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]].[^5] [^6] In some cases, [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] may force a Regal form upon a singer personally while the latter is in the Everstorm.[^6]

During the [[False Desolation - The Coppermind|False Desolation]], [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]] was able to Connect to all singers on Roshar and grant a large portion of them forms of power as well as Voidlight. This is no longer possible since that Unmade does not have access to the power of [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].

The forms of power come in many different shapes; their precise number is unknown, though higher than ten.[^7] Regardless, they can always be recognized by having red eyes, as opposed to typical [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] black.[^8] [^9] Historically, they were associated with [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]] and [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]], though neither is necessary for their functioning.[^10] [^11]

### Abilities

Unlike ordinary forms, Regals possess powers that are explicitly supernatural, from shooting lightning from one's hands to understanding and speaking every language in the world (as such, a [[Kandra - The Coppermind|kandra]] wouldn't be able to mimic a Regal).[^12] These powers do not seem to require any sort of external [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] input, but at least some are powered by [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]].[^13] [^2] [^14] They are also generally stronger than normal singers or humans, including forms not intended for fighting.[^15] Moreover, in contrast to [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] possession, taking on a form of power does not strip the host of their personality, though it can alter their way of thinking.[^16]

On the other hand, the Regal form can be directly spoken to by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], though this seems to only occur if the singer is caught in the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]].[^17] Odium is also able to exert a significant amount of control over Regals, in a manner similar to the influence [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] has over those with [[Hemalurgic chimera - The Coppermind|Hemalurgic]] spikes.[^18] Most notably, they tend to become more aggressive [^13] and have an exceptionally difficult time hearing ordinary [Rhythms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm"), instead hearing those related to Odium.[^8] [^2] [^19] If a Regal has a [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] with a Radiant spren, they are more easily able to retain their ways of thinking.[^20] [^21] It is possible that Odium can force Regals to obey him.[^1] Despite their ties to Odium, Regals are not immune to effects tied to another Shard, such as being pulled into one of [Honor's visions](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dalinar%27s_visions "Dalinar's visions") by the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]].[^8]

## Known Forms of Power

Most forms of power are only known through [[Listener - The Coppermind|listener]] songs; several were recorded in the [Song of Secrets](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Secrets "Song of Secrets"), the [Song of Histories](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Histories "Song of Histories"), and the [Song of Winds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Winds "Song of Winds"). There are more than ten total.[^7]

### Stormform

> “ *Stormform is said to cause  
> A tempest of wind and showers  
> Beware its powers, beware its powers.  
> Though its coming brings the gods their night,  
> It obliges a bloodred spren.  
> Beware its end, beware its end.  
> *”
> 
> — fourth stanza of the [Song of Winds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Winds "Song of Winds") [^22]

A singer bonded to a [stormspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormspren "Stormspren") is granted stormform,[^23] a form of power that is commonly used for frontline soldiers and is a fearsome foe on the battlefield. This form was the first used by the listeners, allowing them to call the Everstorm into the Physical Realm [^24] and freeing the other singers from their captivity.[^25] With the coming of the Everstorm, stormforms are often used as elite troops, capable of reinforcing standard soldiers or serving in special units that carry out important tasks, such as the company that seized the Sibling's crystal pillar in Urithiru.[^26]

Instead of the bulky external carapace armor granted by warform, a singer in stormform is protected by a more subtle armor that is nearly as effective.[^27] The singer's skin is covered in rows of durable protective ridges that poke out through the skin, which stretches tightly in some places. Stormforms also have hairstrands that are absent in warform,[^13] more prominent physical differences between the sexes than other forms,[^28] and barbed carapace on the backs of their hands.[^29] Their face is protected by a carapace mask beneath the stormform's skin [^4] with ridges on the nose and cheeks, capable of deflecting a blade away from the face without causing too much damage.[^30] The ridged carapace breaks the skin above the cheeks and along the sides of the head,[^31] with a carapace gorget at the neck.[^29] The spiking armor plates [^32] present under their skin elsewhere on their bodies [^4] makes grappling with them difficult, jabbing attackers and preventing them from getting a decent hold.[^29] A stormform often has flashes of energy appear around them, such as sometimes flashing across their eyes [^33] or tiny nearly invisible lines of lighting flaring from their hands when they form a fist quickly.[^13] When they are preparing to summon their lightning, their skin begins to crackle with energy.[^29] As they fight, they cause flashes of light, [^34] sometimes emanating from their weapons when they connect with enemies.[^35]

Abilities

Despite looking comparatively lithe,[^32] a stormform Regal is in fact significantly stronger than a warform singer,[^13] and even other forms of power.[^26] They are able to leap across the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]] without a running start [^13] and possess a grip strength that allows them to easily hold the chasm walls when climbing them.[^4]

The most overt power that stormform grants a singer is the ability to create and manipulate a red lightning in a variety of useful ways. Stormforms are capable of unleashing grand bolts of lightning that electrocute and burn their enemies, cooking them alive in their armor.[^35] It is also capable of doing great damage to fortifications, with bolts being able to destroy stone structures.[^36] It takes a moment for a stormform to summon a bolt of lightning,[^28] [^37] and those nearby can sense the power building as the stormform's skin begins to crackle with the red lightning.[^37] [^29] This ability to throw lightning is most deadly in close range. The lightning itself is difficult to control and moves unpredictably, sometimes moving the wrong direction or channeling directly into the ground.[^35] The flash of lightning is blindingly bright and creates a loud clap of thunder that is sometimes used as a signal and intimidates enemies. [^38] This thunderclap can be permanently deafening in close quarters, but as it is quieter than normal thunder, those shielded from the noise usually suffer no permanent hearing loss.[^29] When a stormform is killed, if their power has not been expended it can escape from their corpse and kill those nearby, particularly those standing in puddles.[^39]

Stormform Regals are also capable of directly channeling the lightning into another person directly through physical contact, sending constant jolts of power through them,[^29] which creates an immensely painful burning sensation in the victim.[^37] Their opponents are unable to withstand the power of this attack without Stormlight healing to aid them.[^29] Unleashing lightning into a person still generates a great deal of heat, burning through clothes, with the shock causing the victim's hair to stand on end.[^37] Even when they are not intending to do harm, touching a stormform may cause the feeling of a little jolt of power.[^40] Stormforms have also experimented with their powers in less directly martial ways. They have discovered that they can rewrite the polarity of a magnet with their lightning [^41] and use it to ignite the contents of pressurized vessels.[^42]

This lightning power is not limitless though; subsequent blasts of lightning are often weaker [^37] than the first, and after their Voidlight has been expended, it is slow to renew and its absence may leave the stormform feeling limp.[^30] Another weakness of stormform is, ironically, water, which makes their ability to throw bolts of lightning even less reliable.[^36] If a stormform is sufficiently drenched in water, their power will be ruined and unable to renew properly until they are dry, slipping away from the stormform every time it starts to return.[^43] Their lightning may also be counteracted by placing lightning rods to draw the bolts of power themselves to protect soldiers fighting stormform Regals.[^40] Their powers may also be fully dampened by a [suppression fabrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Suppression_fabrial "Suppression fabrial"), which causes their lightning to vanish.[^38]

Another ability granted by stormform is some resistance to the effects of the storms,[^32] allowing them to move through the winds and darkness of a storm with relative ease,[^35] although they are not immune to the dangers the storms present.[^6] According to legend they were able to control and summon the storms themselves;[^44] it is uncertain if this refers to the power to summon the Everstorm from Shadesmar. Prior to its arrival in the Physical Realm, a stormform Regal was capable of summoning a small portion of the Everstorm themselves.[^32] They could cause a small tempest of wind, lightning and dark mist to appear briefly between their hands, although it is unknown if they maintain this power following the arrival of the Everstorm. It is uncertain whether summoning the Everstorm was a task only the stormforms could complete or whether they were simply chosen as the form best suited for the task.

### Envoyform

While not described in any known listener songs, envoyforms are an important non-combatant form of power available to the singers,[^21] often employed by the Fused as Voices,[^21] interpreters [^45] or propagandists.[^46] A singer becomes an Envoyform Regal by bonding with a [zealspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Zealspren "Zealspren").[^47]

A singer in envoyform is extremely tall [^2] but not particularly muscular,[^6] with long arms and legs.[^33] Envoyform Regals stand above tall [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") humans,[^8] common singers,[^21] and even some forms of power such as stormform,[^2] though still shorter than some brands of Fused.[^33] The form grants no carapace armor,[^21] but much in the way of decoration, inspiring awe among common singers and fear in humans that see an envoyform.[^48]

An envoyform's body is not extensively covered in carapace, and the carapace that is present on their body is primarily ornamental and fragile.[^21] It is present in places such as along the cheeks and in ridges on the back of an envoyform's hands. This light carapace is not as useful for insulating an envoyform from the wind or the cold as the more extensive carapace of other forms.[^3] An envoyform also possesses long hairstrands and more pronounced sexual characteristics, such as small breasts for femalen singers.[^6] An envoyform Voice is often dressed in striking fashions designed to augment their forms and skin patterns as well as inspire more attention in a crowded room.[^49]

Abilities

Envoyform grants a host of supernatural abilities primarily related to translation and interpretation.[^21] The most notable power is the ability to speak, read, and understand any language, whether human or singer.[^2] The ability presumably involves some manipulation of the power of [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]]. After hearing several sentences in an unknown language, an envoyform feels as though the words blur in their mind before shifting into something understandable and they gain the ability to speak and understand that language.[^6] While this power is capable of allowing a envoyform to understand the meaning of a name, it cannot give them any context or additional information beyond that.[^50] There are also times that this translation power may fail.[^51] Odium himself may prevent an envoyform from interpreting certain conversations or songs, leaving envoyforms with a feeling that they can almost understand but that their powers shy back from comprehending,[^50] and there are other times it seems to fail an envoyform.[^40] In order to properly interpret the meaning of writing, an envoyform must first know that the symbols they look at are writing before their translation powers activate.[^52]

Some envoyforms have a remarkable ability to infer intention and emotion from non-verbal cues.[^53] Given more time and practice holding this form, its abilities grow even grander, allowing an envoyform to intuit instructions from merely a hum,[^21] or a simple gesture,[^40] a talent particularly useful when serving a Fused master. This makes serving as the Voice of an important Fused one possible position for an envoyform singer, because of its ability to speak the ancient Fused language.[^21] Additionally, as many Fused do not bother to learn modern languages, envoyforms are necessary as interpreters, allowing Fused to communicate their will to common singers.[^45] They are also useful as propagandists, such as when Venli is used by Odium to help craft a foundation myth for the common singers of the Voidbringer forces.[^2] Not all envoyforms choose to be faithful interpreters. Some twist the words they purport to translate, changing the words themselves, using a different tone of voice, or even humming to different rhythms. Fused often have trouble distinguishing between the “lesser” rhythms, which can allow Voices to soften a Fused’s tone when translating their speech to common singers. Voices who are exposed as unfaithful translators face the threat of execution.[^46] This form is somewhat fragile [^8] and not as physically powerful or athletic as other forms of power,[^15] but in it, an envoyform Regal is incredibly nimble [^8] and remains noticeably stronger than the average human.[^15]

### Decayform

> “ *Decayform destroys the souls of dreams. / A form of gods to avoid, it seems. / Seek not its touch, nor beckon its screams, deny it. / Watch where you walk, your toes to tread. / O'er hill or rocky riverbed / Hold dear to fears that fill your head, defy it.* ”
> 
> —27th stanza of the [Song of Secrets](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Secrets "Song of Secrets") [^54]

A singer becomes a Decayform Regal by bonding with a [blightspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Blightspren "Blightspren").[^47]

Decayform Regals have thin carapace that is jagged, brittle, and asymmetrical.[^20]

Abilities

Decayform Regals can sap the vitality of other beings.[^20] This affects them both physically and mentally.

### Direform

![[Regal---The-Coppermind-3.jpg]]

by [Audrey Hotte](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Audrey_Hotte "Coppermind:Artists/Audrey Hotte") and [Ben McSweeney](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Ben_McSweeney "Coppermind:Artists/Ben McSweeney")

Stormform and Direform Regals

A singer in Direform is more obedient to Odium and their Fused commanders, even more so than other Regals. They are also more stubborn. A singer becomes a Direform Regal by bonding with a [callousspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Callousspren "Callousspren").[^55] Direform typically serve as head jailers [^56] or guards.[^3] [^55] Direforms are also used as heavy infantry.[^57] [^55]

Direform Regals typically stand over seven feet tall, covered in thick plates of stacked, interlocking carapace resembling a full suit of plate armor. These plates are covered in wicked, curved spikes, especially on the head and shoulders. Direforms have no hairstrands, as their carapace covers most of their head—along with the rest of their body, leaving gaps only at their joints and face.[^55]

Abilities

They are stronger, faster, and tougher than humans, with exceptionally strong carapace, and are stronger than most allies and enemies, but they are no match for Shardplate.[^58] [^59] They can use Voidlight to further increase their strength and speed.[^60] They expertly use their carapace spikes to punish foes foolish enough to engage with them in close quarters.[^55]

### Nightform

> “ *Nightform predicting what will be, / The form of shadows, mind to foresee. / As the gods did leave, the nightform whispered. / A new storm will come, someday to break. / A new storm a new world to make. / A new storm a new path to take, the nightform listens.* ”
> 
> —17th stanza of the [Song of Secrets](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Secrets "Song of Secrets") [^61]

A singer becomes a Nightform Regal by bonding with a [nightspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nightspren "Nightspren").[^47] Nightform Regals act as battle strategists and guards.[^62] [^63]

Nightform Regals have pliant leather-like carapace, which covers most of their body other than their face.[^62] It offers nearly as much protection as warform does. They have hood-like shell connects their forehead, back, and sides of the head to their shoulders and base of the neck. Though typically bald, some nightforms have hairstrands that grow from the scalp beneath this hood.[^62] The carapace is darker than other forms, closer to black in color. The skin underneath shimmers with small, iridescent patterns.[^20] [^62]

Abilities

Nightform Regals are able to use [[Voidbinding - The Coppermind|Voidbinding]] to gain prophetic visions.[^64] [^62] [^61] This can be used to see what enemies plan to do or to receive directives from Odium. They can process and absorb information faster than other forms. They also have enhanced night vision and can perceive rhythms better.[^20] [^62] The mere presence of a nightform prevents enemies with their own future sight from benefiting from the power, making any attempted glimpses of the future a black void.[^62]

### Relayform

A singer becomes a relayform Regal by bonding with a [[Voidspren - The Coppermind#hastespren|hastespren]].[^47] Relayform is a form of power with a sleek appearance that is often taken by scouts.[^19] Serving as messengers and scouts in Odium’s army, relayforms carry parcels and written messages through harsh environments and across Roshar. During the [occupation of Urithiru](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Occupation_of_Urithiru "Occupation of Urithiru"), relayforms also kept watch at balconies and scouted the halls for signs of resistance.[^65]

Relayform Regals have light carapace with smooth edges, and aerodynamic spikes on their backs, forearms, and calves.[^66] A relayform's light carapace slices through the air as they race across the storm-torn landscape, significantly reducing drag as they run.[^65]

Abilities

Relayform Regals have increased speed and stamina.[^66] A relayform can sprint for days on end without stopping or slowing. Their steps touch the ground too briefly to sink into grasping quagmires or shifting sand. Relayforms can use Voidlight for bursts of even greater speed, blurring past their opponents.[^65]

### Shadowform

Shadowform was a theorized singer form. [[Ba-Ado-Mishram - The Coppermind|Ba-Ado-Mishram]] tried to create them during the [[False Desolation - The Coppermind|False Desolation]], in an attempt to create half-corporeal assassins, but failed.[^67] [^68] A singer becomes a Shadowform Regal by bonding with a [shadowspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadowspren "Shadowspren"). Shadowforms act more on instinct, and will likely follow their original goal, such as killing Radiants.[^69]

Ba-Ado-Mishram’s Shadowform singers became Cognitive Shadows, with only their gemhearts remaining solid.[^69] [^70]

Abilities

If a person pierces their heart with the gemheart, the Shadowform Regal will take over their body.[^71] [^68] When these bodies die, the shadowspren is released.[^72] As long as their gemheart remains intact, they will keep existing after death. Their existence is between the Physical and Cognitive Realms, which causes them constant torment.[^68]

### Smokeform

> “ *Smokeform for hiding and slipping between men. / A form of power, like human Surges. / Bring it 'round again. / Though crafted of gods, / It was by Unmade hand. / Leaves its force to be but one of foe or friend.* ”
> 
> —127th stanza of the [Song of Histories](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Histories "Song of Histories") [^73]

> “ *Smokeform for hiding and slipping 'tween men. / A form of power—like Surges of spren / Do we dare to wear this form again? It spies. / Crafted of gods, this form we fear. / By Unmade touch its curse to bear, / Formed from shadow—and death is near. It lies.* ”
> 
> —51st stanza of the [Song of Secrets](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Song_of_Secrets "Song of Secrets") [^74]

Nothing is known of smokeform beyond its mentions in the songs of the listeners, which imply that this form may grant some sort of ability to hide or move nimbly.[^73]

### Unknown

[Axindweth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Axindweth "Axindweth") suggests to [[Venli - The Coppermind|Venli]] that there is an unknown form which grants healing.[^75]
