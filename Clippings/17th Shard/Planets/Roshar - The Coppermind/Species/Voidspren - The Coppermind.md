## Voidspren

**Voidspren** are spren that, instead of being connected to either [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] or [[Cultivation - The Coppermind|Cultivation]], are under [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]] control. They are similar to other types of spren in many ways, such as their ability to bond with things in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] and their various shapes and habits. However, these spren fight on the opposite side of the [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]], aiding Odium's goal to destroy the [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Rosharans]] and ultimately earn his freedom from [[Braize - The Coppermind|Braize]]. Once they were entrapped by the [[Oathpact - The Coppermind|Oathpact]], the Voidspren would torture the [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") present on Braize in an attempt to get them to bend the bars of [Damnation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Damnation "Damnation") and release them back to Roshar.[^1]

## Powers and Abilities

Certain Voidspren are able to make themselves invisible, even to people who can see spren.[^2] [^3] Voidspren do not need to bond to retain their cognitive functions in the Physical Realm.[^2] [^4] [^5]

### Bonding

Like the other types of bonding spren, Voidspren can only bond to certain individuals during certain times. For many Voidspren, the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] is needed for the bonding process, likely because of its output of [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]]. The lesser spren that create [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regals]] can bond during highstorms, likely because the Regals can't invest the Voidlight. The bonding process in the previous Desolations is unknown since this is the first Desolation when Odium summoned the Everstorm.[^6]

Some Voidspren, like [Ulim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ulim "Ulim"), are able to bond with a singer without changing their form, residing in their gemheart while leaving the other spren there.[^7] The Voidspren can leave and return from their gemheart at will, without the need for a highstorm.[^8] [^9] This will still influence the singer's emotions, though the influence will leave if the Voidspren exits their gemheart.[^8] While inside their gemheart, the Voidspren can communicate directly with the singer though their mind and even hear their thoughts.[^7]

Some Voidspren are able to enter any animal's gemheart, giving them some influence of control, depending on the animal's intelligence.[^10]

## Types

> “ *Hmm... There are many varieties, she says. Some of golden light, others are red shadows. Curious, yes.* ”
> 
> — [[Pattern - The Coppermind|Pattern]], translating for an [ashspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ashspren "Ashspren"), on Voidspren [^11]

There are many varieties of Voidspren, and each type, similar to the other bonding spren, serves a different purpose. Most are lesser Voidspren.[^12] Some of them can form bonds, and some haven't revealed whether or not they can. [Raboniel](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Raboniel "Raboniel") has mentioned a type of spren capable of cracking ciphers, but further details about this type of spren are currently unknown.[^13]

### Blightspren

Blightspren look like small yellow puffs of miasma. When bonded to a singer’s gemheart, they grant the Regal decayform.[^3]

### Callousspren

It is not known how callousspren appear. Singers who bond with callousspren gain the Regal direform.[^14]

### Chaosspren

Chaosspren are normally invisible, but under certain circumstances, like when they are inside [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], they leave a trail of red sparks and lightning in the air.[^3] [^15]

### Guidespren

> “ *That yellow spren isn't any better. Hurry up. Keep moving. She tells us that we're free, then with the very next breath berates us for not obeying quickly enough.* ”
> 
> — [Sah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sah "Sah") on the yellow Voidspren [^16]

These spren haven't shown any bonding abilities, and they serve Odium as guides and messengers. One such spren is [Yixli](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Yixli "Yixli"). They appear as glowing yellow, shaped like windspren with eyes like a [[Shin Kak Nish - The Coppermind|Shin]]. In order to put themselves at the height of humans or singers, they stand on a yellow pillar that rises from the ground. When they walk, another pillar will meet any moving foot, keeping them even.[^17] These Voidspren are seen guiding the multiple bands of newly made [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]] to settlements, cities and, in the case of [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin's]] group, to [Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar "Kholinar").[^18] They are able to detect the arrival of an [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] before it appears.[^17] They can also choose who can and cannot see and hear them in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]].[^19]

### Hastespren

Hastespren appear as torn purple insect wings tossed through the wind. When bonded to a singer’s gemheart, they grant the Regal relayform.[^3]

### Nightspren

It is not known what nightspren look like, however, legend has it that nightspren only appear in complete darkness. This has lead to rampant misinformation and generally unreliable descriptions. When bonded to a singer’s gemheart, they grant the Regal nightform.[^3]

### Secretspren

These spren are similar to guidespren in their color, being yellowish-white to vivid yellow, but instead of appearing similar to windspren, these look more like "yellow spheres crackling with energy." They are used during the [Siege of Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Siege_of_Kholinar "Siege of Kholinar") to locate any [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]] being used in the city. If a fabrial is turned on, these 'screamers' will wail, then start twisting over each other above the area, alerting a quickly-following [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]]. The screamers can also sense when a [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Lashing]] is used, but not when a [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind#Illumination|Lightweaving]] is created. Pattern explains that he and [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] are quieter than [[Sylphrena - The Coppermind|Syl]] and Kaladin.[^20] They can detect Stoneshaping, but not a Willshaper looking into [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar").[^21] They cannot detect illusions, as they burn through much less [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] than a [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Lashing]], and their senses are fully blocked by aluminum. They could also be blocked by certain [Aviar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aviar "Aviar") or [[Copper - The Coppermind#Allomantic Use|Allomantic copper]], as they are similar to life sense and [[Bronze - The Coppermind#Allomantic Use|Allomantic bronze]].[^22] These spren are useless within Urithiru, since the whole tower is made of fabrials and thus secretspren would have trouble locating a specific usage of Investiture.[^15]

Overall, secretspren can detect Adhesion, Gravitation, and Cohesion,[^21] but cannot detect Illumination, Transportation (at least when it is used only to look into Shadesmar),[^21] or a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") being summoned.[^20]

### Stormspren

This type of spren can bond with [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] during a [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorm]] or the Everstorm, granting them the [[Regal - The Coppermind|Regal]] power of stormform. The stormspren appear as rolling red lightning that moves across surfaces. Among the [[Listener - The Coppermind|listeners]], they are described as looking smoky,[^23] as well as giving off a "strange red light" and splinters of lightning.[^24] Despite a similarity in appearance, they are not of the same species as the spren [Ulim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ulim "Ulim"), who can in addition take on a human appearance.[^4]

Stormspren can be found naturally prior to the arrival of the Everstorm. Hundreds of stormspren will appear at where its lightning strikes the ground, and will linger a short while before disappearing.[^25]

### Shadowspren

It is not known how shadowspren look in both the Physical- or Cognitive Realms. Shadowspren are among the creatures featured in the stories that give the [Dirgehollow](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dirgehollow "Dirgehollow"), a slum inside the [Iriali](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Iriali "Iriali") city of [Rall Elorim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rall_Elorim "Rall Elorim"), its name and reputation. It is likely, although not confirmed, that shadowspren grant the Regal shadowform.[^26] [^27]

### Zealspren

Zealspren look like bright blue drops of oil crackling. When bonded to a singer’s gemheart, they grant the Regal envoyform.[^3]

### Other Regal spren

Other types of lesser Voidspren are likewise capable of bonding a [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] and grant Regal powers. Their appearance and regular behavior are by and large unknown, as they rest in the singer's gemhearts to provide them their powers.[^28]
