## Larkin

**Larkin** are small, highly-valued, flying crustacean animals native to [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. They can consume [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]] as well as other forms of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. They have been a critically-endangered species since the [[Scouring of Aimia - The Coppermind|scouring of Aimia]].[^1] They are known as the **Ancient Guardians**, or **Guardians of Ancient Sins**, and have traditionally guarded the [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshard]] on Aimia.[^2]

The larkin are associated with the [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Knights Radiant]], though the specifics of their connection remain a mystery.[^3] One effect of this connection is possibly that [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinders]] can sense a larkin's mood.[^4]

## Appearance and Anatomy

Larkin are generally between a hand and a small melon in size.[^1] They are somewhat similar to [[Cremling - The Coppermind|cremlings]] in appearance, although, unlike the cremlings' crabbish faces, the larkin have small faces, like those of [[Axehound - The Coppermind|axehounds]], with a snout, mouth, and shimmering eyes of solid silver, as well as wings that fold out of their backs. Their wings are strong enough that they can fly, at least short distances.[^1] [^5] [^4]

Larkin are capable of hearing [rhythms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rhythm "Rhythm"), similar to [singers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Singers "Singers").[^6] They are also able to understand human languages, and can even crudely replicate it.[^6] For an unknown reason, Surgebinders are able to sense their moods.[^7]

During their lifespan a larkin bonds a particularly large kind of [luckspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Luckspren "Luckspren") only found on [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]] and must migrate there every few years to do so. Larkin can grow to great sizes but most now remain small, likely because they aren't bonding luckspren. It is unclear what the difference between larkin and [lanceryn](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lanceryn "Lanceryn") is but they are very closely related.[^8] Of the few larkin that exist, [Chiri-Chiri](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chiri-Chiri "Chiri-Chiri") is the only known one to travel and bond a [mandra](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mandra "Mandra") in a long time.[^9]

Larkin may have some sort of special connection to the [[Tai-na - The Coppermind|Tai-na]]; [Vstim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vstim "Vstim") says that Chiri-Chiri is given to [[Rysn Ftori - The Coppermind|Rysn]] by [Relu-na](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Relu-na "Relu-na") itself.[^5] According to [Talik](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Talik "Talik"), a family of [Reshi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Reshi "Reshi") gods, the [Na-Alind](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Na-Alind "Na-Alind"), made an agreement to keep safe the few surviving larkin after Aimia fell.[^10]

### Feeding on Investiture

The most outstanding feature of larkin is their ability to feed on [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] (commonly [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]], as they originate from [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]).[^11] This ability is related to both [[Chromium - The Coppermind#Allomantic Use|Leechers]] and [[Nightblood - The Coppermind|Nightblood]], although they can't hold as much [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] as the latter.[^12] [^13] They can suck Stormlight out of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinders]] and dead [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]] at will, causing the Light to stream visibly through the air and into the larkin's mouth.[^4] [^14] [^7] Larkin can also eat Stormlight that has been stored in gems and drain the [[Voidlight - The Coppermind|Voidlight]] from a [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]], and even [Lifelight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lifelight "Lifelight") from [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]].[^4] After absorbing a large amount of Stormlight from the [King's Drop](https://coppermind.net/wiki/King%27s_Drop "King's Drop"), Chiri-Chiri becomes noticeably larger.[^15]

Since larkin feed on Investiture, they are incapable of pushing it *into* people or returning it to its source.[^16] [^7] Larkin cannot feed on the Investiture of a person clad in [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]] or Shardplate, though they can feed on the investiture powering the Shardplate itself.[^14] [^7]

A larkin would be able to prevent someone from becoming [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]] by ingesting the [divine Breath](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Divine_Breath "Divine Breath") as it attempted to enter the person.[^17] Larkin could also kill a Returned by feeding on all of their Investiture.[^18]

## History

In the past, larkin have guarded the [[Dawnshard - The Coppermind|Dawnshard]] on [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]], giving them the title Ancient Guardians.[^2] During this time, the larkin were given eight [Soulcasters](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulcaster "Soulcaster") as an offering, though it is unclear as to why.[^9] Since the scouring of Aimia, larkin have become extremely rare,[^1] but are not totally unknown; [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]] thinks of the phrase "rare as a larkin" when she has a moment of freedom [^19] and kings are willing to pay fortunes for their corpses.[^5]

After the disappearance of the larkin from Aimia, the [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] took over the task of guarding the Dawnshard, although they still revere the original protectors.[^9]

## Notable Larkin

- [[Elhokar Kholin - The Coppermind|Elhokar Kholin]] has the corpse of a larkin trapped inside a block of crystal to use as a paperweight in his conference room in the [Pinnacle](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pinnacle "Pinnacle").[^20]
- [Vstim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vstim "Vstim") trades a considerable quantity of goods for the corpse of a larkin.[^5]
- [[Rysn Ftori - The Coppermind|Rysn]] is given a live larkin by [Relu-na](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Relu-na "Relu-na").[^5] She names it [Chiri-Chiri](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chiri-Chiri "Chiri-Chiri").[^15]
- [[Nale - The Coppermind|Nale]] uses one to drain [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]] of [Lifelight](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lifelight "Lifelight") while attempting to capture her.[^4]
- [Kaza](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kaza "Kaza") briefly encounters a creature that fits the description of a larkin in its native [[Aimia - The Coppermind|Aimia]].[^21]

## Trivia

- [Brandon Sanderson](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brandon_Sanderson "Brandon Sanderson") and [Isaac Stewart](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Isaac_Stewart "Isaac Stewart") sometimes refer to them as "dragon bugs".[^22]
- The [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]] have crossbred their hordelings with larkin in order to gain the ability to drain [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]].[^9]
- The Sleepless know of three larkin that have survived.[^9]
