## Shattered Plains

The **Shattered Plains** are a region of [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] located on the border of the [Frostlands](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Frostlands "Frostlands") and [[Unclaimed Hills - The Coppermind|Unclaimed Hills]], in the former territory of the [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver Kingdom]] of [[Natanatan - The Coppermind|Natanatan]].[^1] [^2] They are named for their topography, consisting of a huge number of plateaus of various sizes surrounded on all sides by deep chasms. During one of [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin's]] dreams in which he flew with a [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorm]], he saw the entire area as a symmetrical pattern, radiating outwards from the center as if something massive had struck there.[^3] [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]], and later [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution's]], [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]] is situated under the Shattered Plains.[^4] [^5] [^6]

The Shattered Plains were formed by a clash between [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], with their pattern resulting from metallic remnants of Roshar's fourth moon.[^7] [^8] The fragments of the moon, and the metal they contain, buried themselves into the plains and shrouded them from the eyes of the [[Shards]].[^4] Notably, this metal was not [[Aluminum - The Coppermind|aluminum]], and was described by Honor as something greater.[^7]

## Topography

![[Shattered-Plains---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Nick van Kleef](https://www.artstation.com/buddhistbaseball)

A view of the Shattered Plains

The western edge is ringed by ten circular craters.[^9] Each crater's ridges have been worn away by [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorms]] where they connect to the Shattered Plains themselves.[^10] It is noted by [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar]] that from above, it is clear that the craters are the remains of large stone domes that have collapsed.[^11] [^12]

The edge of the Plains consists of smaller plateaus with chasms ranging generally from fifty to one-hundred-fifty feet in depth.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup> These westernmost plateaus can be bridged easily, and many permanent bridges have been constructed joining them together. Moving eastward, the Plains become much more varied in size and the chasms themselves can become much wider, making crossing from one to another much more challenging.[^13] The eastern edge of the Shattered Plains is so weathered by storms that it consists mostly of spindly pillars of rock, making it impassable.[^14] [^15]

On the eastern side of the explored portion of the Plains is a particularly large plateau known as "the Tower". The Tower is a favored location for [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]] to pupate.[^16] Less then twelve hours of walking from the warcamps are the remains of a large temple. The temple is embedded in the canyon wall, once hidden behind a layer of stone and crem.[^17] Recently, a chasmfiend chrysalis was discovered on the plateau above the shrine, resulting in a battle between humans and listeners. Between thousands of marching soldiers and natural erosion over time, the crem covering the shrine started to crumble. A recent highstorm resulted in a more dramatic collapse.[^18] Its lower level begins about 30 feet above the chasm floor, where columns flank an open doorway. Another 20 feet above that rises an upper level. The upper level appears to be completely on its side, looking like a building tumbled into the chasm before being frozen in stone.[^17] The temple contains many rooms, some of which are dedicated to the Knights Radiant, and it has two openings that lead to the surface.[^19] As it was never looted, the temple also contains a number of valuables, such as a Soulcaster [^20] Both the listeners and the Alethi made at least one, somewhat disastrous, journey to research the complex.[^19]

Due to its eastern position, Highstorms are particularly strong on the Shattered Plains, garnering the moniker 'the Stormlands'.[^21] As there is no shelter on top of the plateau, the chasms often flood and the highstorms are terribly strong, getting caught in a highstorm on the Plains is a death sentence. The flooding and the highstorms both contribute to the erosion that the plains experience, particularly on the eastern side.[^22] [^14] [^15] The [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] also contributed to the erosion and damaging of the Shattered Plains.[^23]

![[Shattered-Plains---The-Coppermind-2.jpg]]

by [Isaac Stewart](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Isaac_Stewart "Isaac Stewart")

The pattern of the Shattered plains, shown from an overhead view

Some accounts describe the Plains as looking as if something enormous had struck the center, causing the fracturing Plains to radiate symmetrically in all directions. Claims of lights in the center plateau where the Parshendi live have also been recorded.[^3] The Shattered plains exhibit a four-fold, rotationally symmetrical pattern, which becomes more obvious when looked at in a larger scale. However, due to erosion over time, the pattern is no longer perfect, particularly on its eastern side.[^22] This pattern bears noticeable similarity to cymatic patterns.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

The ruins of [[Stormseat - The Coppermind|Stormseat]] are in the center of the Shattered Plains. The [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] is located there on a circular plateau.[^24] The Oathgate had been hidden underneath centuries of built-up [crem](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crem "Crem").[^25] The city of Stormseat did not encompass the whole of the Shattered Plains, but had satellite cities and outbuildings.[^26] Some of Stormseat’s ruins are still in good shape, having fallen into crevices protected from the worst of the storm waters, while others are revealed only after catastrophic storms damage the chasm walls.[^27]

Underneath the Shattered Plains lie the fragmentary remnants of Roshar's fourth moon, which crashed into the planet before [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor's]] arrival in the system. Those fragments can obscure a Shard's vision and also react to direct conflict between Shards; during a clash between Honor and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], that reaction was responsible for forming the pattern of the Shattered Plains.[^7] [^8] Additionally, the [Well of Control](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Control "Well of Control") is also located beneath one of the plateaus.[^4] [^5] [^6] The Well of Control is an integral part of the greater ecosystem.[^8]

## Flora and Fauna

![[Shattered-Plains---The-Coppermind-3.jpg]]

by [Lyraina](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Lyraina "Coppermind:Artists/Lyraina")

The rich ecosystem within the chasms.

The Shattered Plains has a large variety of plants and animals, the most dangerous of which is its apex predator: the chasmfiend. However, [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]] believes the Alethi have hunted chasmfiends nearly to extinction for their enormous [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]].[^11]

The climate is moister than it is in the west. Plants have a tendency not to pull into their shells as swiftly after a storm.[^28] Clouds are less frequent; alternating between sun and highstorms.[^29] Outside the warcamps, [rockbuds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rockbud "Rockbud"), [knobweed](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Knobweed "Knobweed"),[^28] flowering [branzah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Branzah "Branzah"),[^30] [prickletac](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Prickletac "Prickletac"),[^30] pink and purple [fingermoss](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fingermoss "Fingermoss"),[^31] and other grasses are known to grow. Due to the harsh weather, the grasses near the Shattered Plains are typically yellow in color and only grow to six to ten inches in height.[^32]

During Shallan's travels to the Shattered Plains, she notices feral [[Axehound - The Coppermind|axehounds]], small eels living in ponds, and white trees with red leaves.[^33] There is also a small, tentacled, animal that lives in pools near the warcamps, called a [grasper](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Grasper "Grasper").[^31] The Shattered Plains are also home to nests of [shellmites](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shellmite "Shellmite").[^27]

Out on the plateaus themselves, rockbuds, moss, and [shalebark](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shalebark "Shalebark") are the most common plants.[^10] Shallan notes that the moss and common flowering rockbuds grow in similar patterns.[^34]

Within the chasms is a different environment all together. The chasms are more humid and damp than the rest of the Plains, with stagnant pools where [[Cremling - The Coppermind|cremling]] larvae grow.[^15] [Haspers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hasper "Hasper"),[^35] [^36] mosses, fungi, and [frillblooms](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Frillbloom "Frillbloom") [^37] [^38] all grow on the walls and in crevasses in the chasms. Translucent cremlings have been observed,[^39] along with larger carrion animals that are sensitive to light.[^15] The Plains are home to a crimson and yellow species that has a venomous bite, harmless in small doses but dangerous if a victim is overwhelmed.[^18]

The Well of Control fills an important role in the ecosystem, providing the Chasmfiends with an influx of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] to assist in their pupation and the metamorphosis process. Due to the presence of the perpendicularity, Chasmfiends are driven to the plateaus in the lead-up to their pupation.[^8] [^16] This does not, however, mean that they exclusively pupate upon the plateaus above the Well of Control.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

## Inhabitants

Prior to the [[Clippings/17th Shard/Events/War of Reckoning - The Coppermind|War of Reckoning]], the only known permanent inhabitants of the Shattered Plains were the [[Listener - The Coppermind|listeners]], who occupied the ten craters on the western edge of the Plains.[^40] During the war, the craters were occupied by various [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") armies fighting the Parshendi under the [Vengeance Pact](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vengeance_Pact "Vengeance Pact"). The Plains and the surrounding area are so barren that the warcamps are perhaps the only places where it is acceptable to pile refuse up high and allow the highstorm to carry it away, as there is no danger of the trash being carried into another city.[^41]

### Listeners

During the War of Reckoning, the listeners resided in the ruins of Stormseat, which they call Narak. After the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]], the Alethi claimed the city for themselves. The homes of the listeners are now barracks, and a watchtower has been erected on the tower where the Five met.[^42] During the last few days before the [[Contest of champions - The Coppermind|contest of champions]], when the [coalition of monarchs](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coalition_of_monarchs "Coalition of monarchs") realized they would not be able to defend the Shattered Plains, [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin]], as Queen of [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar"), signed a peace treaty with the listeners, surrendering the land to them.[^43]

### Warcamps

For a list of residents, see [:Category:Residents of the Alethi warcamps](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Category:Residents_of_the_Alethi_warcamps "Category:Residents of the Alethi warcamps").

The Alethi have occupied the crater-like areas of the Plains and constructed ten warcamps, a royal palace, and a market. The palace complex, also called the Pinnacle, rises from a hill beside the Outer Market. The Pinnacle was carved from the hill and embellished with Soulcast stone. Protected on most sides by a sheer drop, this palace is a fitting bastion for a wartime monarch. Surrounding the palace grounds lie gardens, the feasting basin and the dueling arena.[^44]

The warcamps are each a mixture of Soulcast buildings, shanties, and tents that are taken down before each highstorm.[^30] Each camp is self sufficient, with their own markets, taverns, brothels,[^45] and [spanreed](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spanreed "Spanreed") information stations to [Tashikk](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tashikk "Tashikk").[^46] Most of the buildings are Soulcast using topaz.[^47] The bunkers and barracks are typically long and narrow with a smaller end facing the highstorms to the east.[^48]

The Outer Market was a collection of buildings outside the warcamps, near the king's palace. The buildings here were mostly constructed of stone quarried from near the Plains. It expanded in a sprawling way, without plan or order. It became a place where people from all warcamps mingled and shopped from the Thaylen merchants. The market was largely unregulated but was patrolled and administered by Kholin scribes.[^49] The Outer Market is also home to a bakery known as the Crem Oven bakery. The bakery takes the appearance of a dilapidated stone building with a single chimney on the leeward side.[^50] In addition to this large Outer Market, each of the warcamps had their own little market. These markets are generally clustered near the opening of the warcamp’s crater wall to allow for easy loading and unloading of goods.[^51]

While the warcamps weathered the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] quite well, eight of the ten warcamps were evacuated and abandoned after the Battle of Narak--their occupants either moving to [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]] or disappearing into the [[Unclaimed Hills - The Coppermind|Unclaimed Hills]].[^11]

Roion

Highprince Roion’s warcamps hosts the Range, which is a space for archery competitions and staged “hunts.” Roion soldiers often set aside spheres to bid for a shot at the whitespines.[^12]

Sadeas

It is known as one of the least ordered and respectable warcamps, as opposed to the well-maintained and designed Kholin warcamp. Prior to the evacuation to Urithiru, Sadeas controlled the forests north of the warcamps.[^28] The poor and desperate people of this warcamp often seek aid and sanctuary from the other nine.[^12]

Aladar

Aladar’s camp is a hotbed of intrigue, with guards turning a blind eye to certain unsavory elements in the hope of baiting out sources of information. Gambling dens have also thrived in this permissive environment.[^12] The guards at the entrance of the Aladar warcamp don’t mind people coming and going.[^52]

Location of the [Red Rockbud](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Red_Rockbud "Red Rockbud").[^12]

Kholin

The barracks of this warcamp are organized into radials, with meeting areas and mess halls at the center of each battalion.[^45] These battalions then circle Dalinar's stone bunker complex and the living quarters of other ranking lighteyes and generals.[^53] The Kholin warcamp has more bunker-like construction than other warcamps, as Dalinar wanted his soldiers to remember they were at war.[^11]

After moving most of the camp, under the command of Brightness [Jasalai](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jasalai "Jasalai"), to Urithiru, Dalinar left a few platoons and some workers in this warcamp.[^12]

Location of the [Ornery Chull](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ornery_Chull "Ornery Chull").

This warcamp’s own market has an area called the **Spear Exchange**. Here, the merchants selling and repairing equipment are based.[^51]

Location of the [Blueworks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Blueworks "Blueworks")

Vamah

After the discovery of Urithiru, Highprince Vamah, together with Highprince Thanadal, started making the Shattered Plains into his own little kingdom. His camp reflects that, and Vamah indulged his lighteyed subjects with feasts.[^54] After the abandonment of the warcamps, a Vamah captain occupied the arena at the Pinnacle, demanding a tournament to legitimize Vamah’s claim to the Shattered Plains.[^44]

Ruthar

While nobles in this camp duel for politics, many enlisted men take part in brutal fights in secret underground rings. Soldiers often pool their earnings to create a prize pool for the fights, and the winners have been known to fund their own desertion.[^54]

Thanadal

After the discovery of Urithiru, Highprince Thanadal, together with Highprince Vamah, started making the Shattered Plains into his own little kingdom. His camp reflects that. To consolidate power, Thanadal created a spanreed network rivalling that of [Tashikk](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tashikk "Tashikk").[^54]

Hatham

Hatham has cultivated a strong ardentia presence in his warcamp. The camp’s Devotary of Kelek is perhaps the largest monastery on the Shattered Plains. Rumors circulate that beneath the monastery, a Soulcaster secretly experiments with unusual metals at Hatham’s behest.[^54]

Bethab

In a deliberate attempt to rival Navani’s scientific prowess, Bethan’s wife, [Mishinah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mishinah "Mishinah") hosts the [Bethab Society](https://coppermind.net/edit/Bethab_Society?redlink=1 "Bethab Society (page does not exist)"), a lighteyed ladies’ scholarly club. Its ornate chapterhouse rivals the highprince’s manor, and romantic trysts between the women working as scribes and scientists are not uncommon within its halls.[^54]

Sebarial

It is a very technologically advanced camp for the area. It has running water, although muddy water will run for the first few seconds.[^55] Highprince [Sebarial's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sebarial "Sebarial") goal is to become the only camp that produces food and utilities and have a monopoly. His camp is dedicated less to the training of military and more to the production of resources. After the evacuation to Urithiru, Sebarial claimed the nearby forests [^11] and is transporting lumber across the Plains to Urithiru.[^56] The camp mixes Soulcast bunkers with traditional buildings, and cultivated fields to the southwest produce [[Lavis - The Coppermind|lavis]] grain, reducing the camp’s reliance on Soulcasters.[^44] The Sebarial warcamp won’t allow anyone to enter without authorization, which generally comes in the form of the right glyph.[^57]

[[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]] stays with Highprince Sebarial.[^58] She is very impressed by the situation of the camp, as it does not reflect what [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah]] reported.

The warcamp is also home to ‘little [[Herdaz - The Coppermind|Herdaz]] ’, the largest Herdazian community outside the nation itself.[^44]

Brightness [Chanan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Chanan "Chanan"), a distant Sebarial cousin, runs an auction each dawn.[^44]

### Greatshells

The [[Chasmfiend - The Coppermind|chasmfiends]] inhabiting this region come here to pupate for energy they receive from Odium's perpendicularity.[^8] They regard singer and human meat to be not as fine as other types but are willing to consume it if needed. They are sapient, but think in terms of feelings and emotions.[^59]

When the Alethi and [[Listener - The Coppermind|listeners]] began hunting them for their [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]], they initially assumed that, as it would be in their society, it was an attempt to assert dominance over them and embraced the challenge. They were surprised and heartbroken, then, when they discovered that defenseless chrysalises were also being attacked. By the time of the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]], their population had been significantly reduced and a group of about fifty of them chose to join up with the listeners who had escaped [stormform](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormform "Stormform"), trading their strength and some of the food they hunt for peace.[^59]

## Politics

Since the relocation from the Shattered Plains warcamps to Urithiru, ownership of the Plains and the Oathgate has become extremely important and strategic to the success and survival of the inhabitants of Urithiru. Urithiru is not self-supporting yet, and resources are required from the warcamps to supply the city. Farming operations have begun in the Unclaimed Hills,[^60] but due to the climate, rockbuds grow smaller and crops are less productive than in [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar").[^11] The [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] are harrying caravans traveling to the Oathgate.[^56]

The Natans want ownership of the Shattered Plains and the Oathgate.[^61] [[Elhokar Kholin - The Coppermind|Elhokar]] proposed that Dalinar be made High King of Urithiru and the Shattered Plains.[^25]

In, the Shattered Plains were granted to the surviving listeners by Jasnah Kholin as part of a peace treaty.[^43]

## Trivia

- The Shattered Plains was originally envisioned as existing on the world of [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]], [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid's]] homeworld and the site of the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering of Adonalsium]], until Brandon moved it to Roshar in the redrafting of the Way of Kings.[^62]
