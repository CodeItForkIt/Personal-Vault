## Highstorm

A **highstorm** (Alethi: , romanization: *Kezeras*) [^1] is a massively [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested]] hurricane on the planet of [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. Prior to the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows"), It occurred once every few days, or at the command of [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]], and moved from stormward to leeward (East to West), starting at the [Origin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Origin "Origin").[^2] East of Roshar, the highstorms are incredibly destructive. They progress across the continent, though they weaken gradually as they cross, and are mostly broken by the mountains east of [[Shinovar - The Coppermind|Shinovar]].[^3] [^4] This weakness is caused by a combination of being over land instead of water, and infusing spheres with [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]].[^5]

## Description

A highstorm has three general stages. The leading edge, or stormwall, is the most dangerous part of the storm. Immensely powerful winds generate a wall of dust, water, and larger debris.[^6] It is shown that it is powerful enough to carry large trees long distances into the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]].[^7] The main body of the highstorm carries less debris, although the winds are still strong enough to hurl boulders. The trailing edge of the storm, known as the riddens, consists of light rains mixed with relatively mild gusts of wind.[^8] On average, a highstorm travels at roughly 370 miles per hour,[^9] and weakens as it moves across the continent; the [[Purelake - The Coppermind|Purelake]], for example, experiences less intense highstorms than areas to the east.[^10]

[[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] is able to lessen the impact of the storm in certain areas, and would do so frequently to those that prayed to him.[^11] Before Honor arrived at the planet, the storm itself would sometimes lessen if pled to, and seems to have been weaker in general.[^12]

There is natural cyclical variation in the height of highstorms. At times it barely reaches Urithiru in the mountains, and at other times it is taller than the tower.[^13] [^14]

When a highstorm and the [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]] hits a location at the same time, the combined power of the storms can cause even greater destruction than normal, destroying the land below them with enough force to send boulders flying hundreds of feet up in the air.[^15]

On the Rosharan subastral of the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], highstorms manifest in a non-violent manner, in contrast to its form in the Physical Realm. Instead of powerful winds and rain, the sky would simply light up,[^16] the clouds shimmering with a mother-of-pearl iridescence.[^17]

### Centerbeat

> “ *There was no lightning, no wind, no rain. The silence was unnatural.* ”
> 
> — [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin's]] experience in the highstorm [^8]

Some unlucky travelers who have been caught in a highstorm have spoken of a moment that occurs within the heart of the storm when the winds suddenly die away and everything becomes still for a brief moment, defying explanation. Termed the "centerbeat" by multiple cultures, scholars seem to lack scientific evidence of its existence.[^18] [^19] [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] experiences an unnatural stillness in the middle of the highstorm when he first sees the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]].[^8] He experiences a similar sensation when he is stuck in the chasms of the Shattered Plains with [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]].[^20]

This is caused by the bringing to bear of large enough quantities of Stormlight that time and space distorts and the [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|three realms]] are brought closer, and the Stormfather can appear and speak to people through this mechanism.[^21]

## Causes

![[Highstorm---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Marie Seeberger](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Marie_Seeberger "Coppermind:Artists/Marie Seeberger")

Many plants flourish only in the short period after the highstorm

[Stormwardens](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormwarden "Stormwarden") try to predict when highstorms occur,[^22] and have become somewhat accurate, though not always.[^23] The Stormwardens have poor reputations since predicting the future is forbidden under [[Vorinism - The Coppermind|Vorin]] doctrine.

Vorin mythology claims that each storm is born in the east at the Origin before traveling west across Roshar, but modern Stormwardens believe there exists a single highstorm that travels around the planet, with the length between storms decided by the speed it travels during that time of year.[^24]

## Ecology

Highstorms are the single most important force in Roshar's ecology, and life on the planet has adapted to them. Some plants, like lavis polyps, enclose themselves against the highstorm's fury; others retract or fold their relatively fragile leaves, vines, or roots away. The east side of hills is more barren since it directly faces the blow of a highstorm. Contrarily, life flourishes on the west side of hills or landscapes such as valleys, where they are shielded from the powerful winds.

Another effect of a highstorm is the [crem](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crem "Crem") in the storm water. This substance is deposited onto the land, accumulating over the millennia, even prior to the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]].[^25] Crem comes from minerals in the highstorm that harden, and it contains minerals and nutrients that are useful for plant life.[^26] Plants fed highstorm water do better than those given lake or river water, as discovered by farmers and Stormwardens.[^27] By the time the highstorm reaches Shinovar, it no longer contains the minerals that harden into crem.[^26] Many plants have outer coverings infused with highstorm [crem](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crem "Crem"), forming protective shells.[^28]

Rosharan fauna has adapted as well; many species like [[Axehound - The Coppermind|axehounds]], [whitespines](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Whitespine "Whitespine"), and [[Greatshell - The Coppermind|greatshells]] are covered in protective carapace. They also have [[Gemheart - The Coppermind|gemhearts]] that glow with [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]], enabling their symbiotic relationship with [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]].

For Roshar's native sentient species, the [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]], highstorms are a crucial part of their life cycles. Entering a highstorm allows them to bond a spren, changing their body into one of the dozens of specialized forms. In the past, this was done simply by entering the storm with a specific mindset; in the current day, the singers trap a spren in a gemstone beforehand and await the storm with it at the ready.[^29]

During the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows"), [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]] had the Everstorm consume the highstorm, causing a slowness bubble to form around the planet.[^30]

## Cultural Significance

![[Highstorm---The-Coppermind-2.jpg]]

by [Casey Fallon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Casey_Fallon "Coppermind:Artists/Casey Fallon")

A [[Singer - The Coppermind|singer]] transforming in a highstorm

People on Roshar live in cities and settlements that are protected from highstorms by natural or manmade structures. [Kholinar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kholinar "Kholinar") has its windblades [^31] —large stone formations that divert the highstorm's force— and [[Sesemalex Dar - The Coppermind|Sesemalex Dar]] has troughs that prevent the city from flooding.[^32] These structures are often city-wide and become an integral part of the city's culture.

Humans depend on highstorms not just for water, but also for the [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]] that they use in their [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]], the crem that they use as building material, and the regular weather patterns from which they base their calendars.

Other aspects of civilization are also affected by the highstorms. For example, sanitation regulations have been developed to prevent the trash of one town from being blown by the strong winds towards the next town. These regulations, however, do not apply on the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]] due to its isolated location, and so the refuse and litter of the [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") warcamps there are piled high to be carried away during each highstorm.[^33]

Many curse words and insults are related to the highstorms, but they are also a target of reverence. The Stormfather is venerated by many. Some folklore traditions equate the Stormfather with the Heralds [[Jezrien - The Coppermind|Jezrien]] and [[Kalak - The Coppermind|Kelek]]. Criminals have been known to be punished by being strung up outside during a highstorm in order to be "judged" by the Stormfather, who supposedly would let them live if they deserved it.[^6]

## Invested Arts

![[Highstorm---The-Coppermind-3.png]]

by [Ashley Coad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Ashley_Coad "Coppermind:Artists/Ashley Coad")

[[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] floating over a highstorm

Highstorms are highly [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested]],[^34] and are the primary source of Investiture on Roshar. Gemstones are infused with [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]] when left outside during a highstorm, though this only happens in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]].[^27] [^35] This magical source of illumination and power is used to Invest [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrials]] and fuel [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]]. If they are within about a hundred feet of a highstorm,[^36] Surgebinders can gain Stormlight directly from the storm, instead of using gemstones as an intermediary.[^15] The Investiture provided by the storm only functions when it is "whole", and it cannot split pieces off to reach into sheltered places, such as inside buildings. The Investiture comes at a certain moment, normally a few minutes after the storm wall, but it changes, this is how surge binders with gravitation can ride storm wall.[^13] The strength of the storm is not tied directly to the amount of Stormlight Invested into gemstones.[^37] Highstorms predate the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering of Adonalsium]],[^38] though they have been significantly altered from their original form by certain events,[^39] likely the Shattering itself, as well as both the arrival and [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintering]] of Honor.

The storms are a physical manifestation of the [[Stormfather - The Coppermind|Stormfather]], who has a degree of control over them and can send one at will, with his bonded [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiant]] sharing his abilities.[^40] [^41] [^42] The [center of the storm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Highstorm#Centerbeat "Highstorm") is unnaturally dark and quiet.[^8] In the center, the Investiture of the Stormfather is at its most potent, and the large amount of power can warp time and create bridges between [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|realms]].[^43] The [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]] can be accessed via the highstorm through the Cognitive Realm.[^16] The relationship between the highstorm and [[Honor's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Honor's Perpendicularity]] is unclear.[^44] [^45] The center of the storm is where the Stormfather can reveal himself to humans other than his [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmith]].[^41] [Honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren") also have a connection to the highstorms, as they can always predict their arrival [^46] and the Stormfather can also speak directly to [honorspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Honorspren "Honorspren") in the center of the storm.[^43]

Because of the strong winds, [[Spren - The Coppermind|windspren]] are especially numerous during storms. [[Spren - The Coppermind|Rainspren]] sometimes appear during the light rains of the riddens. More recently, [[Voidspren - The Coppermind|stormspren]] have also started appearing.[^47] There have been sightings of [gigantic glowing spren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Stormstrider "Stormstrider") striding through the land during highstorms.[^48] [^20] They have been described as sleek, moving on spindly legs.[^49]

In the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], sapient spren appear to act more energetically during highstorms, and it has been speculated that they get their sustenance from it.[^17]

## Development

In *[The Way of Kings Prime](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Way_of_Kings_Prime "The Way of Kings Prime")*, highstorms were much different.

- They varied in strength and duration based upon the seasons; a spring highstorm occurs during the battle in which Merin (proto-Kaladin) wins his Shards, which lasts for only a hour and is only windy enough to make fighting "inefficient", appearing to be little different from a regular storm from the real world. Summer highstorms are noted to be much fiercer, more in line with how they later appeared.
- The Stormfather was also absent from this draft.
- Crem was instead called crom.
