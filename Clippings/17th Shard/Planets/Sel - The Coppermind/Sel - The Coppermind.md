## Sel

> “ *Sel—home to multiple empires that, uniquely, have remained somewhat ignorant of one another. It is a willful kind of ignorance, with each of the three great domains pretending that the others are mere blips on the map, barely worth notice.* ”
> 
> — [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss]] [^5]

**Sel**, also known as **Seol**, is a [[17th Shard|planet]] in the [Selish System](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Selish_System "Selish System") of the [[17th Shard|cosmere]],[^5] and is the setting of the books [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(book\) "Elantris (book)") and [The Emperor's Soul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Emperor%27s_Soul "The Emperor's Soul"), as well as the short story [The Hope of Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Hope_of_Elantris "The Hope of Elantris").

Sel is inhabited by humans and artificially created beings called [[Seon - The Coppermind|seons]] and [[Skaze - The Coppermind|skaze]]. It is a relatively large planet, being 1.5 times the size of the [[Yolen - The Coppermind|cosmere standard]], and contains diverse landscapes home to multiple great civilizations including at least three empires.[^3]

People from Sel are called Selish.[^6]

## Astronomy

Sel is the second planet in the Selish system, orbiting a yellow star named [Mashe](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mashe "Mashe") while in turn being orbited by a single blue-white moon named **Oem**.[^7] Its size is 1.5 [[Yolen - The Coppermind|cosmere standard]] and its gravity is 1.2 cosmere standard.[^3]

Based on limited available information about cosmere standard measurements, Sel's radius is about 6,000 miles (~9,600 km) and the circumference is about 37,000 miles (~60,000 km).[^8] The gravitational acceleration on Sel is about 12 m/s <sup>2</sup>.[^1]

## Geography and Culture

### Opelon/Sycla

The story of *[Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(book\) "Elantris (book)")* and the short story *[The Hope of Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Hope_of_Elantris "The Hope of Elantris")* are both set on a continent on Sel called **Opelon** by those in the west [^9] and **Sycla** by those in the east.[^10] There is a vast desert to the south of the known nations of the continent.[^11]

#### Fjordell Empire

Most nations on Sycla have been subsumed into the [[Fjorden - The Coppermind|Fjordell Empire]]. The empire is ruled by a leader known as [Wyrn](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wyrn_\(title\) "Wyrn (title)"), who is also head of [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Shu-Dereth]], which has been used as a vehicle to dominate nations. The Fjordell Empire is, on the surface, completely Derethi. However, many of the people of the empire, especially those in backcountry areas like Hrovell, still secretly follow their old religions, like the [Jeskeri Mysteries](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jeskeri_Mysteries "Jeskeri Mysteries").

The Fjordell Empire is made up of the following nations:

#### Arelon

Located to the west of the Fjordell Empire, [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]] is the only remaining independent nation on Opelon. The capital of Arelon is the city of [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)"), which is a monarchy ruled by the [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") King [[Raoden - The Coppermind|Raoden]]. Under the king, there is a class of nobles who control various plantations throughout Arelon. Their primary function is to distribute food to the people of Arelon. Arelon controls the area to the west of the Atad Mountains, and south of the northern ocean. Arelon is mainly made up of followers of the [[Shu-Korath - The Coppermind|Shu-Korath]] religion, although there are still remnants of the old pagan religion that worshiped the Elantrians. There is also a small number of followers of the Jeskeri Mysteries.

### The Northern Continent

There is another continent north of Opelon, though its name is unknown. The [Northern Sea](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Northern_Sea "Northern Sea") appears to lie to the northwest of this continent, though the only known map of the region is imprecise.[^12]

#### Teod

The nation of [[Teod - The Coppermind|Teod]] is located on a small peninsula attached to the northern continent.[^12] The capital of Teod is Teoras, and its ruler is King [[Eventeo - The Coppermind|Eventeo]].[^13] Teod's martial strength exists mainly in the form of its navy, which dominates the northern sea, protecting Teod's borders from the Fjordell Empire. Unlike the rest of the northern continent, Teod is within Opelon's cultural sphere of influence. For example, the nation's state religion is [[Shu-Korath - The Coppermind|Shu-Korath]], which originated in Opelon. The Teo people are also of [Aonic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aonic "Aonic") descent and are therefore related to the [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelene]] and [[Duladel - The Coppermind|Dula]] people of Opelon.

#### Rose Empire

North of the Teod peninsula, beyond the mountains, is the [[Rose Empire - The Coppermind|Rose Empire]].[^14] This empire has many provinces, including but not limited to [Mulla'dil](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mulla%27dil "Mulla'dil"), [[MaiPon - The Coppermind|MaiPon]], [Dzhamar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dzhamar "Dzhamar") and [Ukurgi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ukurgi "Ukurgi"). The empire maintains a little-used mountain pass that leads to Teod.[^15] Unlike Teod, the Rose Empire has limited cultural exchange with the nations of Opelon, though it has some diplomatic relations with [[Svorden - The Coppermind|Svorden]] and [[JinDo - The Coppermind|JinDo]]. By the time of the assassination attempt on Emperor [[Ashravan - The Coppermind|Ashravan]], they seem to have made contact with the Fjordell Empire, as a southern ambassador with [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|red priest's armor]] is present while [[Wan ShaiLu - The Coppermind|Shai]] is rushing through the palace.[^15] Certain segments of the population seem to have a concept of [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realmatic Theory]].

The story of [The Emperor's Soul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Emperor%27s_Soul "The Emperor's Soul") is set in the Rose Empire.

### Aonic Calendar

The Aonic calendar is used by the people of Arelon and Teod. It has at least six months, with Eostek being the sixth, and at least six days in a week, with MaeDal being the second and OpeDal being the sixth.[^16] [^17] [^18]

## History

### Early History and Mythology

> “ *Both Devotion and Dominion were destroyed. Their Investiture - their power - was Splintered, their minds ripped away, their souls sent into the Beyond.*”
> 
> — [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss]] on the history of Sel [^3]

At an unknown date, over 7700 standard years before the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]],[^19] [^20] [^21] [^22] the planet was settled by [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] against the rules made by the original Vessels. [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] used this as justification to attack both of the Shards,[^23] this battle resulted in their Splintering. Their Investiture was then moved from the Spiritual Realm into the Cognitive to prevent anyone else from taking the Shards. This latent Investiture became dangerous plasma [^24] and was named the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]] [^3] and some of these [[Splinter - The Coppermind|splinters]] became the [[Seon - The Coppermind|seons]] and [[Skaze - The Coppermind|skaze]].[^25] [^26] [^27] [^28] [^29] [^30] They were created from the Investiture of Devotion and Dominion respectively.[^31] Because of this, the world's magic systems became region specific.[^32] These consequences were a side effect of Odium's actions, he didn't intend them to happen.[^33]

[Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)") was built centuries to over a millennium before its restoration.[^34]

Three major empires eventually formed on Sel. Due to the planet's size, these empires largely ignored each other and considered the others as irrelevant.[^3] One of the reasons for this isolation is the difficulty of travelling between the empires.[^35] [^36] Two such empires are the [[Rose Empire - The Coppermind|Rose Empire]] and [[Fjorden - The Coppermind|Fjordell]]. [[Shu-Keseg - The Coppermind|Shu-Keseg]] rose as a religion focused on the unity of all people.[^37] [^38] A schism later formed in the religion over a disagreement on what unity meant, this lead to the creation of the [[Shu-Korath - The Coppermind|Shu-Korath]] and [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Shu-Dereth]] traditions.[^38]

The original Fjordell empire was formed and the first [Wyrn](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wyrn_\(title\) "Wyrn (title)") was created. The empire eventually collapsed three hundred years before the events of Elantris.[^39] Around this time a schism formed in the Shu-Keseg faith forming [[Shu-Korath - The Coppermind|Shu-Korath]] and [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Shu-Dereth]].[^40] The remnants of the Fjorden empire blamed their collapse on a lack of true unity, as a result they adopted the newly formed [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Shu-Dereth]] as the state religion,[^40] it was hoped that by doing so they would prevent any future rebellions or political problems.[^41] They mixed the Shu-Dereth faith with Fjorden mythology and made Jaddeth the central deity of their religion.[^40] [^41] A movement was begun to update their religious and historical texts in order to make it seem that Fjorden was always of this faith and that the first Wyrn also worshipped Jaddeth.[^40]

### The New Fjorden Empire

The monasteries of Fjorden were formed and began training their members.[^42] These monasteries and internal Fjorden politics began to be influenced by the Skaze,[^43] [^44] in particular their want for power.[^45] Overtime this new empire expanded so as to include most of the continent, with only [[Duladel - The Coppermind|Duladel]], [[Teod - The Coppermind|Teod]] and [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]] resisting them. By this time the new empire had become stronger than the original ever was, though their strength was in the power of their priests rather than military might.[^39] [Wulfden](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wulfden "Wulfden") the fourth became the new Wyrn and leader of the Shu-Dereth church. Feeling threatened by this new Wyrn, the leaders of Shu-Korath named [Seinalan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Seinalan "Seinalan") as their new high priest because of his relative youth.[^46]

An earthquake happened near the city of [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)"), this earthquake then triggered an event known as the [[Reod - The Coppermind|Reod]], causing Elantrian magic to stop working and Elantrians to be changed. The earthquake was not due to natural causes.[^47] The native Elantrians became [hoeds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hoed "Hoed") and the Arelene government collapsed.[^39] The majority of damage and death during the resulting riots was caused by the countries servant class. After the riots eased the government was replaced with a system where the wealthiest ruled the country.[^39] The newly empowered merchant class blamed the riots on the servants of the city being too pampered.[^39] King [[Iadon - The Coppermind|Iadon]] was crowned in response.

Deciding that the new system of wealth based politics was unstable he secretly wrote a new law that was to take effect either fifteen years after it was written or after his death, whichever came first, he then entrusted this with Seinalan. The law removed the wealth based system of Arelon government and made its positions be determined by lineage like other nations, he based this model of Teod's.[^46] To foster peace with the Fjorden empire he also allowed for Shu-Dereth priests to preach to their people.[^39] Nevertheless the empire remained dissatisfied and a push was made by Fjorden to convert the leaders of Arelene from Shu-Keseg to Shu Dereth and have them pledge loyalty.[^39] The nation of Teod also spoke less with Arelon after the events of the Reod.[^48]

Gyorn [[Hrathen - The Coppermind|Hrathen]] was assigned to convert Duladel to the empire, as it was one of the few remaining nations offering resistance. Within six months he managed to lead a revolution that led to the deaths of the republican class and collapse of its government.[^42] In its place a monarchy was formed and Shu-Dereth pronounced the state religion at the expense of the [Jesker](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jesker "Jesker") religion.[^48] This event caused a renewed interest in an alliance between Teod and Arelon, a marriage was planned between prince [[Raoden - The Coppermind|Raoden]] and princess [[Sarene - The Coppermind|Sarene]].[^48] Having successfully converted Duladel, Hrathen was then dispatched to Arelon. Unbeknownst to him [[Dilaf - The Coppermind|Dilaf]], his old mentor,[^49] was already dispatched to the area and preparing to invade it.[^50] Upon meeting Hrathen failed to recognize him and also misunderstood his orders as to convert the people of Arelon, not destroy them.[^10] [^50]

Before the marriage of Sarene and Raoden could take place Raoden found himself taken by the Shaod.[^9] An effort was made by Sarene to oppose Hrathen's preaching and his attempts to instate [Telrii](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Telrii "Telrii") as the new King of Arelon. Telrii had agreed that if he became king he would make Arelon convert to Shu-Dereth. After Sarene discovered the King Iadon was part of the religious cult known as the [Jeskeri Mysteries](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jeskeri_Mysteries "Jeskeri Mysteries") where he sacrificed women the king was dethroned.[^51] After this event king Iadon killed himself.[^52] To try and prevent the nation of Arelon from falling into Derethi control it was decided that Sarene had to marry [[Roial - The Coppermind|Roial]] in order to legitimize his claim to the throne.[^52]

Before the marriage the funeral of King Iadon took place and as per his will a new law was pronounced by Seinalan that reformed the Arelene government again.[^46] Hrathen managed to make it appear Sarene was taken by the Shaod before her marriage stopping it.[^46] This led to Telrii being crowned king of Arelon.[^53] After this Telrii refused to follow through on the deal he made with Hrathen unless he was also made a gyorn as a gyorn held greater power in the Fjorden empire.[^54] He insisted on this despite Hrathen's protests that such a thing was impossible. Seeing this failure Dilaf then announced his true rank as Gragdet and the true nature of Hrathen mission. He then began the [invasion of Arelon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Invasion_of_Arelon "Invasion of Arelon").[^50] During the invasion Raoden worked out how to undo the Reod and restore Elantris.[^55] With their renewed power the Fjoredell army was removed and Raoden and Sarene were both crowned the new monarchs of Arelon.[^56]

The skaze for a brief time worked alongside Hoid.[^57] Hoid promised them that he could become an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian"); when his efforts failed the skaze mocked him for it.[^58]

### The Rose Empire

The Shu-Dereth religion never historically threatened the Rose Empire because they view its land as unholy.[^59] The Rose Empire was ruled by eighty factions, one faction for each sun that they believed existed.[^60] [^61] These factions then elect an emperor to sit on the Rose Throne.

Emperor [Yazad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Yazad_\(Sel\) "Yazad (Sel)") died and under pressure from his faction [[Ashravan - The Coppermind|Ashravan]] put himself forward for the position. This was accepted and he became the new emperor.[^62] Initially he hoped to lead a series of reformations to help the empire [^63] His desire waned however and he became distracted with the bureaucracy of the empire.[^61] [^64]

The [Glory Faction](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Glory_Faction "Glory Faction") of the empire moved in and assassinated emperor Ashraven. His wounds were fixed by a [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Resealer]], however he was left brain damaged.[^65] Because his wife died as well his faction, the [Heritage Faction](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Heritage_Faction "Heritage Faction") claimed that he was well and in his customary one hundred days of mourning. In desperation they turned to a [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forger]] known as [[Wan ShaiLu - The Coppermind|Shai]] in order to recreate the Emperor's soul.[^60] She succeeded in these efforts and also escaped the country on the 98th day of her imprisonment.[^15] Ashraven had their soul recreated and they quickly worked out what had happened,[^15] his soul was made so that he would remember his old ideals and bring about the reforms he had wanted to as a youth.[^66]

## Invested Arts

Each [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested Art]] on Sel use symbols, shapes, or forms as "code" to direct magical effects.[^67] They are all powered by the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]], making them end-positive.[^68] Because their power is coming from the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], the Selish Invested Arts are highly location dependent.[^3] The strength of each Invested Art decreases the farther one travels from its associated region,[^69] [^70] symbols in an Invested Art often come from the geography or culture of their region,[^71] [^56] [^63] and [[Initiation - The Coppermind|Initiation]] is often restricted to people from the associated region.[^72] [^73] If a sustainable population were to form a new nation, it is likely that a new Invested Art would manifest there.[^74]

- [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|AonDor]] uses symbols called [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]], which can be drawn directly in the air as glowing light, or in a large variety of physical representations, such as etched into metal plates, or incorporated into the design of buildings. Anyone can learn to draw Aons, but they only have power when created by an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian"). AonDor is tied to the land of Arelon and is only practiced within its borders.
- [[ChayShan - The Coppermind|ChayShan]] seems to be a type of meditative martial art practiced by the [[JinDo - The Coppermind|JinDo]], with the forms one moves through granting access to the Dor. It appears that, like Aons, these forms can be practiced by anyone without supernatural effect,[^13] with additional factors such as Intent or bloodline establishing the connection to the Dor.
- The monks of [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Dakhor Monastery]] use an Invested Art often referred to as [[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]]. Dakhor monks have unnatural and twisted bone structures that form patterns under the skin.[^75] These bones are inlaid with ancient Fjordell characters,[^56] which are likely the symbols used to access the Dor. Some Dakhor magics, such as instant transportation, require human sacrifice.[^49]
- [Forton's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Forton "Forton") potions are Invested,[^76] likely derived from the Invested Art associated with [Hrovell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hrovell "Hrovell"). This suggests that, despite Hrovell being under Derethi rule, it maintains an Invested Art of its own. It is not yet known what forms are used to create these potions.
- [[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]] involves shaping a specific form, by the carving of a sort of stamp, and then the application of ink to the stamp, and then applying a mark onto the desired object or person. Supposedly the forgery helps the object (or soul) remember a different past, or a likely destiny in order to change the actual essence of the object. The symbols on the front side indicate what a soulstamp does; the "setting mark" on the back side indicates that the stamp is complete and looks like [[MaiPon - The Coppermind|MaiPon]].[^63]
- [[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealing]], like Forgery, uses stamps, but they must be inked with fresh blood. Bloodsealers can mark a door, which will set their [Skeletals](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Skeletal "Skeletal") to attack the person from whom the blood came if they pass through the door.[^77]

People on Sel can also form bonds, similar to the [[Nahel bond - The Coppermind|Nahel bond]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], with [[Seon - The Coppermind|seons]] and [[Skaze - The Coppermind|skaze]].[^78] Unlike the participants in the Nahel bond, people bonded to seons are not shown to gain direct supernatural powers themselves, instead requesting that their companions use their abilities. What advantage a skaze bond grants is not clear at this time.

## Cosmere

For most of the Cosmere’s history, Sel is the most Realmatically knowledgeable of the known planets in the cosmere.[^79] It is home to two [[Shards]], [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]], and their powers have influenced many Selish traditions, religions, and languages.[^3]

At some point in Sel's distant past, [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] killed the [[Shards|Vessels]] of both Devotion and Dominion and Splintered the two Shards.[^80] The collective power of the Splintered Shards became somehow trapped in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], and is now what Selish people call the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]].[^3] Due to the Dor’s size, Sel experiences time dilation.[^81]

While travel to Sel is possible via the Cognitive Realm (for example, one can use a [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Perpendicularity]] that exists in the form of [[Devotion's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|a pool of sapphire-colored liquid]] near Elantris), the raging uncontrolled power of the Dor makes travel through the Selish Cognitive Realm incredibly dangerous.[^82] There is only one reliable way to travel offworld. This way is known by some Selish people, and knowing them is nearly the only way to worldhop from Sel.[^83]

[[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] has visited both Arelon and the capital of the Rose Empire.[^84] [^85]

## Trivia

- Initially, Brandon had the shadows of Elantris falling on the city of Kae at dawn. As Kae is to the east of Elantris, this indicated that the sun rises in the west on Sel. However, in the Anniversary Edition, this was identified as an error and corrected.[^9]
