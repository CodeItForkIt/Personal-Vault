## Shu-Keseg

**Shu-Keseg** is a religion practiced on the [[Sel - The Coppermind|Selish]] continent known as [Opelon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Opelon "Opelon") ([Aonic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aonic "Aonic") term) or [Sycla](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sycla "Sycla") ([[Fjorden - The Coppermind|Fjordell]] term), as well as the nearby nation of [[Teod - The Coppermind|Teod]].[^1] [^2] [^3] Two opposing sects, [[Shu-Korath - The Coppermind|Shu-Korath]] and [[Shu-Dereth - The Coppermind|Shu-Dereth]], later developed from this parent religion. [^4] [^5]

## Beliefs

Shu-Keseg's core ideals center around unity – specifically unity of mind – and acceptance.[^5] Its followers believe in a single, omnipotent God named Dashu.[^6] [^7]

## Origins & History

Shu-Keseg was founded by a [[JinDo - The Coppermind|JinDo]] man named [Keseg](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Keseg "Keseg") who preached the power of unity.[^5] [^6] Keseg was considered a great teacher, and was given the honorific name of KeHwo.[^8] The religion’s teachings are held in a book called the [Do-Kando](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Do-Kando "Do-Kando").[^9]

In [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]], Shu-Keseg was tolerated, but the godlike power of the pre- [[Reod - The Coppermind|Reod]] [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") precluded most people from converting.[^10]

### Sects

Keseg’s students, [Korath](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Korath "Korath") and [Dereth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dereth "Dereth"), left JinDo to spread Shu-Keseg, but they disagreed about how to achieve the central tenet of unity. Korath preached that mankind should be united through love, whereas Dereth taught of achieving unity through obedience. This schism resulted in the two opposing sects of Shu-Korath and Shu-Dereth.[^5]

Korath's teachings became popular in [[Teod - The Coppermind|Teod]] and it is considered the home of Shu-Korath.[^11] [^8] After the fall of its First Empire around three hundred years ago, the leader of Fjorden converted to Shu-Dereth.[^8] The new religion was then used as an excuse to conquer nearby nations under the guise of divine unification, creating the modern Fjordell Empire.[^12] [^13]

Due to the dominance of the Empire, Shu-Dereth is the state religion of all of Opelon, except for Arelon. Shu-Keseg is still recognized as the predecessor to Shu-Dereth and Shu-Korath,[^4] [^9] but it is only practiced (unofficially) in JinDo.[^5] After the [[Reod - The Coppermind|Reod]], the Fjordell Empire sought to conquer the last remaining bastion of Shu-Korath in Teod; it also planned to convert or destroy the remaining Korathi in Arelon along with any possibility of the Elantrians returning.[^14] [^15] [^16]
