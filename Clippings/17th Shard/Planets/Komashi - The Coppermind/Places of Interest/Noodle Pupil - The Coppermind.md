## Noodle Pupil

The **Noodle Pupil**, later known as the **Noodle Princess**, is a noodle restaurant in [[Kilahito - The Coppermind|Kilahito]] on [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]]. It contains both tables and a bar, the latter of which has heated circular nooks carved into the wood to keep the bowls of noodles warm.[^1] In addition to noodles, the establishment also serves rice, which is used to soak up leftover broth. While [[Design - The Coppermind|Design]] runs the establishment, she offers discounts of prime number percentages. Without a discount, a bowl of noodles generally costs two hundred [kon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kon "Kon").[^1]

## History

The Noodle Pupil was founded by [[Design - The Coppermind|Design]], a [Cryptic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cryptic "Cryptic") from [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] who bonded with [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]]. After arriving on the planet, Hoid froze in time due to protections he put on himself to stop his memories from being tampered with again. Design started the shop with help from the assistant cook [Namakudo](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Namakudo "Namakudo"), and attracted lots of nightmare painters who would visit after hours or during breaks. During the month that Yumi and Nikaro were swapping bodies, they visited many times. During one visit, [[Design - The Coppermind|Design]] lengthened Nikaro's [[Connection - The Coppermind|Connection]] to Yumi.[^2] On another visit, Design used a [[Fabrial - The Coppermind|fabrial]] of Hoid's to check Yumi's [[Spiritweb - The Coppermind|spiritweb]] and found that her [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] was off the charts, that she had had memories removed, and that she had very few connections.[^3] It was here that Nikaro recruited his friends to fight off the army of [[Nightmare - The Coppermind|nightmares]] that was coming.[^4] Once Hoid was unfrozen, Design willed the shop's ownership to Yumi and Painter, who later renamed it the 'Noodle Princess.'
