## Battle of Thaylen Field

The **Battle of Thaylen Field** is one of the first major engagements in the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]]. It's fought between the human armies, led by [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin]], and the [[Voidbringer - The Coppermind|Voidbringers]], mainly [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]], under direct command of [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].[^2]

## History

### Background

After the [[Battle of Narak - The Coppermind|Battle of Narak]] in, the Alethi discovered and inhabited the lost city of Urithiru, right as the True Desolation began. This gave them access to Oathgates, therefore giving them potential links to the various Dawncities across Roshar. However, they were incapable of using the Oathgates without first activating them on the opposite side, thus they did not receive an immediate strategic benefit. This motivated them to try and ally with nations that contained Oathgates, due to the strategic value they possess, as they allowed for travel to major Rosharan cities far faster than could be done by foot.[^3] [^4]

A major nation that they attempted to negotiate with was [[Thaylenah - The Coppermind|Thaylenah]], whose capital, Thaylen City, possessed the Thalath Oathgate. The leader of Thaylenah, Queen [Fen Rnamdi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fen_Rnamdi "Fen Rnamdi"), was suspicious of the Alethi forces and didn't want to give them free access into the center of their capital city.[^3] [^4] She eventually came to agree to join the coalition, and allowed them access to her Oathgate, therefore giving them the ability to quickly travel to and mobilize in Thaylenah.[^5] [^6]

Shortly before the battle, [[Taravangian - The Coppermind|Taravangian]], who was working for [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]], manipulated the government in order to motivate them to send off a large portion of the city's defense force, greatly reducing their military prowess and defensive capabilities. This left them reliant upon military aide from the Alethi in order to protect themselves from the Fused offensive.[^7]

After intel arose concerning a Fused offensive on Thaylen City, the Alethi forces moved in to protect their allies and the strategic advantage they possessed via their control of the Thaylen Oathgate. They were further motivated by the threat that the Fused would pose to the human resistance if they managed to take control of the Oathgate.

### Preparations

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Resonance---The-Coppermind-6-1.jpg]]

by [Marie Seeberger](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Marie_Seeberger "Coppermind:Artists/Marie Seeberger")

Human and singer forces facing off

Several thousand [[Singer - The Coppermind|Singers]] arrived at the shores of [Thaylen City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_City "Thaylen City") under the protection of an [[Everstorm - The Coppermind|Everstorm]], spawned by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] to mask their invasion and hasten their ships.[^2] As sailing during a storm was considered impossible, the human defenders of the city were caught unaware; furthermore, due to Taravangian's manipulations,[^7] much of the city's defense force had departed it shortly before, leaving only the [Thaylens](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen "Thaylen") and a portion of Alethi army under [Meridas Amaram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Meridas_Amaram "Meridas Amaram"). The Everstorm covering the advance of the enemy stalled over the city instead of continuing on,[^2] something never seen before.[^2]

At the same time, the souls of approximately ten thousand [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] gathered outside the [Thaylen City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_City "Thaylen City") [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]] in [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar").[^8] Due to the stalled Everstorm, the Voidbringer souls were able to pass into the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]], with two going into the ground and ripping themselves free to become [[Thunderclast - The Coppermind|thunderclasts]]. In addition, clad in a body made of golden carapace and clutching a regal scepter, Odium himself stepped onto the shore to personally lead the battle.[^2]

Even with such might backing them, some of the [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] expressed doubt over their chances of victory, largely due to the inexperience of their army of freed [Parshmen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Parshmen "Parshmen"), many of whom did not want to fight at all.[^2] With Amaram's forces being veterans of the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]] campaigns, the battle was believed to be winnable or even favorable to the humans. Odium, however, had different plans.

### Nergaoul Arrives

In the form of blood red mist, occasionally manifesting into images of combat, the [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]] [Nergaoul](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nergaoul "Nergaoul") took to the battlefield and filled Sadeas' troops with the [Thrill](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thrill "Thrill"), imbuing them with bloodlust and the urge to fight above all else. It was then that the voidspren that had come through the Oathgate into the physical realm seized new hosts, possessing humans as they could normally do to singers; something that had never happened in previous desolations. With this, the Alethi army turned against the city they were supposed to protect.[^2]

At the same time, Odium ordered one of the thunderclasts, [Yushah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Yushah "Yushah"), to assault the [Thaylen Gemstone Reserve](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_Gemstone_Reserve "Thaylen Gemstone Reserve") to deny the enemy the [King's Drop](https://coppermind.net/wiki/King%27s_Drop "King's Drop"), a [perfect gemstone](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Perfect_gemstone "Perfect gemstone") capable of capturing an [[Unmade - The Coppermind|Unmade]]. Yushah breached the wall and broke apart the entire building, scattering all the gemstones onto the battlefield.[^9] [^10]

Taking his copy of *[The Way of Kings](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Way_of_Kings_\(in-world\) "The Way of Kings (in-world)")* under his arm, [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin]] went alone to meet the enemy.[^10] Before the [Azish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Azish "Azish") forces left the city, sailing to [Vtlar Cove](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vtlar_Cove "Vtlar Cove") to wait out the storm, [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]] attacked her [guard](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vono "Vono") and snuck back to the city, since she was unwilling to abandon the others. When she reached Dalinar he instructed her to track down and recover the King's Drop from the Fused who had taken it.[^11] At the same time, [[Szeth - The Coppermind|Szeth]], hovering above the city along with [[Nale - The Coppermind|Nale]], chose to swear the Third Ideal of the [Skybreakers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Skybreaker "Skybreaker"), picking Dalinar as the ideal to follow. With this, he joined the battle, unsheathing [[Nightblood - The Coppermind|Nightblood]] to bring its full might onto the enemy and aid Lift.[^11] [^12]

### Unity

Despite the powerful combatants involved on the human side, Odium's forces were winning. Odium and Dalinar met in the middle of the battlefield, with [[Venli - The Coppermind|Venli]] observing as Dalinar challenged Odium to a [[Contest of champions - The Coppermind|contest of champions]] to have a chance at saving the city. Odium agreed, then explained how he had been grooming Dalinar for years to become Odium's own champion.[^12]

With this, Odium unleashed the full might of the Thrill upon Dalinar, bombarding him with pain of his past actions and promising reprieve if Dalinar gives in.[^12] Dalinar, however, refuses, accepting the responsibility instead. With this, he managed to attain the next [Bondsmith](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bondsmith "Bondsmith") ideal, declaring "I am Unity" and summoning [[Honor's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Honor's Perpendicularity]].[^13] All three Realms—Physical, Cognitive, and Spiritual—were united in an outpouring of [gloryspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Gloryspren "Gloryspren") and [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]], infusing every gem in the city and forming a passage to [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar"). His allies trapped there -- [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin]], [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]], and [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] -- used this to return to the Physical Realm and join the fight. By contrast, seeing Dalinar's feat, Odium fled, leaving his armies to fend for themselves.[^13]

### The Tide Turns

Shallan, using [Lightweaving](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Illumination "Illumination"), created an illusory army to distract the Sadeas forces, even managing to give the phantoms some substance. Meanwhile, [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah]] used the near-infinite Stormlight to annihilate the enemy forces with [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinding]], while [[Renarin Kholin - The Coppermind|Renarin]], having revealed his [[Glys - The Coppermind|corrupted spren]], rushed to join the fight.[^13]

In the center, with Dalinar no longer a possible ally, the sphere containing [[Yelig-nar - The Coppermind|Yelig-Nar]] went to Amaram, who attacked Dalinar, only to be rebuffed by Kaladin.[^13] Amaram then chose to bond [[Yelig-nar - The Coppermind|Yelig-nar]] by swallowing a gem, growing grotesque carapace under his [[Shardplate - The Coppermind|Shardplate]] and gaining access to all ten [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surges]].[^14]

With Kaladin handling all threats to Dalinar, the latter made his way into the center of the red mists that made up Nergaoul. In the sky, Szeth, with the help of Lift, had finally acquired the King's Drop, but was immediately tailed by numerous flying [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]]. Unable to escape them, he and Lift devised a strategy to fool them, and, with Shallan's help, created a fake King's Drop, which they then allowed the Fused to "steal" back. With the perfect gemstone thus secured, Lift entered the mist to bring it to Dalinar.[^14]

Meanwhile, Adolin, with the help of his [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") [[Mayalaran - The Coppermind|Mayalaran]] and Thaylen highguard [Hrdalm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hrdalm "Hrdalm"), engaged [Kai-garnis](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kai-garnis "Kai-garnis"), the other thunderclast, near the city's [[Oathgate - The Coppermind|Oathgate]].[^14] Though Adolin managed to seriously wound the creature, he eventually sustained too many injuries to fight effectively. It was then that Renarin arrived with healing and his own Blade. He and Hrdalm, the latter armed with Mayalaran, finally managed to bring the beast down, reducing it to inanimate stone. Renarin then made for the Oathgate, only to see it guarded by a dozen flying Fused.[^14]

### Confronting the Thrill

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Resonance---The-Coppermind-6-2.webp]]

by [Ashley Coad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Ashley_Coad "Coppermind:Artists/Ashley Coad")

Dalinar confronts Nergaoul

Over in [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]], a surprise attack aimed at distracting the Alethi from sending reinforcements had been underway until [Teft](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Teft "Teft") managed to speak his Third Ideal and manifest a Shardblade, allowing him to open the Oathgate to Thaylen City and bring in much-needed reinforcements. There, the light of the activated gate momentarily scattered the Fused giving the new arrivals an advantage of surprise.[^14] At the same time, back at the ships the singers had arrived upon, [[Venli - The Coppermind|Venli]] bonded [[Timbre - The Coppermind|Timbre]], becoming the first singer [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surgebinder]].[^14]

Within Nergaoul's mists, Lift reached Dalinar and delivered the King's Drop. Embracing the Thrill, Dalinar lured Nergaoul in, and used the Drop to trap him, removing him from the battlefield. With this, the soldiers of Sadeas' army returned to normal, either fleeing or collapsing in place.[^14]

Outside, Kaladin's battle with Amaram was still underway. He was almost killed after he ran out of Stormlight, but was saved by [Rock](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rock "Rock"), who killed Amaram with two shots from a Shardbow. With their last major fighter gone, [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]] army retreated, while Kholin forces and the [Windrunners](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Windrunners "Windrunners") swept the city of the last invaders. The day was won.[^14]
