## Emerald Sea

The **Emerald Sea**, also known as the **Verdant Sea**, is an ocean on [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]].[^1] [^2] The sea is made up of [[Aether spores - The Coppermind#Types|Verdant spores]], which drop from the Verdant [[Lunagree - The Coppermind|Lunagree]].[^3] Like all seas on Lumar, it is pentagonal in shape.[^4]

It borders the [[Crimson Sea - The Coppermind|Crimson Sea]] and [Rose Sea](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rose_Sea "Rose Sea"),[^5] [^6] and three other unknown seas. It might border the [[Sapphire Sea - The Coppermind|Sapphire Sea]], as the Sapphire Sea is supposedly easy to access from the Emerald Sea, though this hasn't been confirmed.[^7]

## Government

It is ruled over, at least partially, by a figure known as the [Verdant King](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Verdant_King "Verdant King").[^8]

They have a royal navy which tracks down criminals such as [deadrunners](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deadrunners "Deadrunners").[^9] The government also has tax collectors and inspectors who have the authority to control the coming and going of ships and cargo through the sea.[^10] [^11]

Their government is known to recognize the [Felony murder rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Felony_murder_rule), wherein, if somebody dies in the committing or furtherance of a dangerous or enumerated crime, the offender and their accomplices are held guilty of murder.[^9]
