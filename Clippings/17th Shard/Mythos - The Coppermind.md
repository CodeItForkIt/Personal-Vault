## Mythos

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

**The Coppermind has spoilers for all of Brandon's published works, now including *Isles of the Emberdark* and Cosmere RPG books. Information about books that have not yet been released, like *Mistborn* Era 3, is allowed *only* on meta-pages for the books themselves. To view an earlier version of the wiki without spoilers for a book, go to the [Time Machine](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Special:TimeMachine "Special:TimeMachine")!**

> “ *There are potential allies out there. Moonlight's world, perhaps. Or the land of the aethers. Hell, maybe even Mythos.* ”

**Mythos** is an off-world nickname used for a [[17th Shard#Planets|planet]] in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]].[^1] [^2] [^3]

After [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy's]] attack on [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] was repelled in 348 [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|PC]], [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] mentioned Mythos to [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] as a possible source of allies, though he apparently considered it a less likely option than [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] and [[Dhatri - The Coppermind|Dhatri]].[^1]

## Trivia\[edit\]

In the 2022 [State of the Sanderson](https://coppermind.net/wiki/State_of_the_Sanderson "State of the Sanderson"), a possible series set on Mythos was mentioned as a minor project.[^2] [^3]
