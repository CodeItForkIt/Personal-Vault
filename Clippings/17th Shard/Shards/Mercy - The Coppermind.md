## Mercy

**Mercy** is a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]] that has not been [[Splinter - The Coppermind#Formation|Splintered]] and whose Vessel is unknown.[^1]

## History

Mercy was created at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering of Adonalsium]] and picked up by an unknown Vessel.[^2] It is not known what Mercy did directly after their Ascension, or where they settled, if they settled at all.

Mercy was involved in a battle between [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] and [[Ambition - The Coppermind|Ambition]], which resulted in a wound upon the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]] and ultimately in Ambition's Splintering. This clash had profound effects on [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnody]].[^3]

Some time around the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]], [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] reached out to Mercy about the threat posed by Odium, at [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid's]] request, but became troubled by Mercy as a result of their exchange.[^1] [^4]

## Trivia

- Before [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona's]] Shard was known (and well before it was known that Mercy is a Shard), fans speculated that its Intent might be Mercy.[^5] It was later revealed Aona's Shard is Devotion.[^6] [^7]
