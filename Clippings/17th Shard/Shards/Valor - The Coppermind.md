## Valor

**Valor** is a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]] that has not been [[Splinter - The Coppermind#Formation|Splintered]] and whose original Vessel is the [dragon](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\) "Dragon (cosmere)") god [[Medelantorius - The Coppermind|Medelantorius]].[^2] [^1]

## History

Like all Shards, Valor was created at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], and picked up by Medelantorius.[^3] [^1] Sometime after the Shattering, a shard with a dragon as Vessel, presumably Valor, found a planet and created a race of [lesser dragons](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dragon_\(cosmere\)#Lesser_Dragons "Dragon (cosmere)"). These dragons are mortal, possess human-level intelligence, are incapable of shapeshifting and have been known to let humans ride them.[^4] It is unclear if she also created these humans, or if they were there before she arrived.

[[Tanavast - The Coppermind|Tanavast]] searched for her during the conflict with [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] 3,000 years after the Shattering, but could not find her. He did not believe that she had been killed, however, as he was confident he would have felt her death.[^1]

Valor has had dealings with [[Endowment - The Coppermind|Endowment]],[^5] and at some point reached out to receive her aid in an unknown matter. Endowment had acquiesced without going to Valor's location in person, thus maintaining the Shards' agreement to isolation.[^6]

Some time around the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]], on the advice of [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], Harmony reached out to Valor about the threat posed by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]]; their conversation left him with the impression that she is reasonable. He later encouraged Hoid to try approaching her again, as she felt it had been too long since their last conversation.[^7] [^2] Despite this, Hoid was apparently unable to locate Valor himself, and when he asked Endowment for information on her location, Endowment refused to help, claiming that Valor had no interest in speaking to Hoid again.[^8] [^9] In Endowment's view, both she and Valor are the only Shards who still maintain a degree of isolation.[^9]

After his [[Shards|Ascension]] to Odium, [[Taravangian - The Coppermind|Taravangian]] was also unable to locate Valor, finding that she and [[Reason - The Coppermind|one other Shard]] had somehow completely vanished from interaction with the other Shards.[^10]

Following his work assisting [Waxillium Ladrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Waxillium_Ladrian "Waxillium Ladrian"), Hoid intended to find Valor in order to assist the people of Roshar to resist [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]].[^11]

## Trivia

- The unpublished novella *[Adamant](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Adamant "Adamant")* includes in-world curses of 'Valor'.[^12]
