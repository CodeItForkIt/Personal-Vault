## Ambition

> “ *As for [[Uli Da - The Coppermind|Uli Da]], it was obvious from the outset that she was going to be a problem. Good riddance.* ”
> 
> — [[Endowment - The Coppermind|Endowment]] on Ambition's Vessel [^2]

**Ambition** is a [[Shards|Shard of Adonalsium]].[^3] The Vessel of Ambition was [[Uli Da - The Coppermind|Uli Da]],[^4] but she was killed shortly after the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]]. She at one point visited the [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnodite]] system.

It is uncertain whether or not Ambition once had an [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 4|Invested Art]] associated with her.[^3]

## Associated Magics

### The Evil

The Evil are [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splinters]] of Ambition, and were created when [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] fought Ambition.[^5] [^1] They are beings made of almost pure [[Investiture - The Coppermind#Anti-Investiture|anti-Investiture]] and act more like forces of nature.[^5] They tend to avoid populated areas in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], as they don't want to be changed and trapped by human perception. The only exception to this rule are the entities near the Grand Knell.[^5]

## History

The Shard Ambition was created when [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]] was Shattered by a group of people on [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]] around ten thousand years before the overthrowing of the [Cinder King](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cinder_King "Cinder King").[^6] [^7] Uli Da took up Ambition and Ascended; she then made her way to the [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnodite]] system.[^3]

Over seven thousand years before the True Desolation,[^8] [^9] the Shard [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] arrived in the system.[^3] Odium desired to be the sole remaining Shard in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] and considered Ambition to be a dangerous rival; as a result, he had her as number one on his list of Shards to eliminate.[^10] Ambition, Odium, and [[Mercy - The Coppermind|Mercy]] fought one another in the space between the planets, struggling in all three [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realms]].[^11] [^3] Odium gained the upper hand and mortally wounded Ambition by tearing off chunks of her power, which altered the people of Threnody as well as the planets in the system. Ambition managed to escape the system, but she was killed and [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] in another location despite fighting back.[^3] [^12] Odium tore her apart and removed her Intent.[^13] Her death left a wound on the Spiritual Realm and affected Threnody in "disturbing" ways,[^11] as well as having far reaching effects elsewhere in the cosmere.[^14] [^15] One of these effects was the creation of the [Grand Knell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Grand_Knell "Grand Knell") near Threnody. The Knell gives off pulses, creating a ‘Current’ that can be used for navigation in the Cognitive Realm.[^5]

## Influence

The Shard Ambition may have had a local manifestation of Investiture at one point in its past.[^3] [Nazh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nazh "Nazh") gave [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss]] records from the [Homeland](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Homeland "Homeland"), which indicate that there used to be some sort of Investiture present on the planet prior to Ambition and Odium's fight. On the other hand, this Investiture may have existed prior to the arrival of both Shards. It is unknown if this Investiture is still in existence or not after Ambition fled the system.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

There are several other notable sources of Investiture on Threnody that may be associated wholly or in part with Ambition.[^3] There appears to be excess Investiture in general, which infuses the spirits of some humans. The spirit of these people imprints upon this power, and when the person dies, the power remains behind in the form of a [shade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shade "Shade"), a [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 1|Cognitive Shadow]] with, in general, a low level of consciousness. In addition to the shades, the [Evil](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Evil "Evil") is also present on Threnody. This force of darkness destroyed an entire continent, sending people fleeing from the Homeland and other regions of the [Fallen World](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fallen_World "Fallen World") to the [Forests of Hell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Forests_of_Hell "Forests of Hell") for safety.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup> Eidoliths also threaten the rest of the cosmere, wandering through large parts of the Cognitive Realm.[^5] Khriss indicates that she believes that this may be the product of a chunk of Ambition's power, twisting the people and the planet itself. There is no stable [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]] on Threnody, although there are some unpredictable and unstable perpendicularities that are of morbid but unknown origin.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

It is unknown what, if any, impact Ambition's power may have had on the planets Monody and Elegy.[^3] Endowment believes that they are better off for Ambition being dead.[^2]

## Trivia

- Ambition would be mono black in *Magic: the Gathering*.[^16]
- While technically fulfilling the requirement of not being on a planet, Ambition is not the Shard Brandon was thinking of when discussing a Shard that did not inhabit a planet.[^17]
