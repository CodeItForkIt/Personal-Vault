## Devotion

> “ *Come. I give you release. Come. You can finally give up.* ”
> 
> —The voice in [the Lake](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Lake "The Lake") [^3]

**Devotion** is a [[Shards|Shard of Adonalsium]], originally held by [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona]].[^4] [^5] After the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]], she traveled to [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] with [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]], where the two were later [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].[^6] [^7]

## Intent

> “ *You see rank and distinction; you try to order the world so that everything has a place either above you or beneath you. To a seon, there is no above or beneath, there are only those we love. And we serve those we love.*”
> 
> — [Ien](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ien "Ien") [^8]

As Devotion was Splintered far in [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel's]] prehistory, it's unknown how precisely her Intent would manifest.[^9] She appears to wish to care for people and provide them safety from pain and harm.[^3] Ien, a Splinter of Devotion, speaks of a love and devotion which transcends hierarchy, and a desire to serve those he loves. He also speaks of finding reward not in money, but in the pride and satisfaction of seeing the one he loves grow.[^8] Furthermore, [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] stated that the Intent of Devotion is that of divine love and compassion.[^10]

Though not as directly hostile to each other as [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]] and [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]], Devotion and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] are at some level opposite to each other, yet their powers work together as the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]].[^7] Devotion and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] can also be argued to be opposite to each other.[^11]

## Associated Magics

### The Dor

When Odium Splintered Devotion and Dominion, he trapped their power in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] to prevent them from gaining another Vessel. Since, unlike in the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]], location exists in the Cognitive Realm, the power of the two Shards is trapped together as a giant, polarized storm of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] known as the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]], which makes [worldhopping](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Worldhopping "Worldhopping") to Sel very dangerous.[^12] [^7]

The existence of Dor has a profound effect on Sel, as rather than a single, planet-wide magic system, the planet has numerous ones, each making its home in a particular region, all influenced in some way by both Shards.[^1] An underlying similarity between all of them seems to be reliance on shape -- all forms of accessing the Dor are dependent on the user creating a specific shape, often based on the features of the land associated with a particular magic.[^13] [^14]

This tie between the location and magic also means that the user's physical position in relation to the system's home region influences the strength of the magic, and moving further away from its origin point will diminish its effects.[^15] [^7] [^16] Furthermore, in order to access a magic system, it appears one must be from the land associated with it.[^17]

Magic systems using the Dor include:

- **[[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|AonDor]]** - the magic of [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]] and surrounding area; the ability to draw symbols called [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]] in the air to produce a variety of magical effects. Aons can only be drawn by [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian"), people raised to power and immortality through the city of [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)").
- **[[ChayShan - The Coppermind|ChayShan]]** - the magic of [[JinDo - The Coppermind|JinDo]]; through taking specific stances and forming specific motions with one's own body, the user can access Dor for increased strength, awareness, and possibly other effects.
- **[[Dakhor - The Coppermind|Dakhor]]** - the magic of [Fjordell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fjordell "Fjordell"); through chants, the bones of a Dakhor monk are induced to change shape, which grants them abilities such as increased strength, bodily transformation, and even teleportation.
- **[[Forgery - The Coppermind|Forgery]]** - magic usable in the [[Rose Empire - The Coppermind|Rose Empire]], originating from [[MaiPon - The Coppermind|MaiPon]]; accesses Dor through creation of [soulstamps](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulstamp "Soulstamp"), which contain a specific shape that is then imparted onto an object, rewriting its [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual]] aspect to change its history and, through this, present behavior and appearance.
- **[[Bloodsealing - The Coppermind|Bloodsealing]]** - another Rose Empire magic, originating in [Dzhamar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dzhamar "Dzhamar"); using human blood, Bloodsealers can create wards and animate skeletons of the dead.

### Seons

[[Seon - The Coppermind|Seons]] are magical entities somewhat common in the [Arelish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Arelish "Arelish") region of Sel. They appear as bright spheres of light about the size of a melon, with a shining [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aon]] inside of them; each Seon has its own Aon, and is named after it.[^18] [^19] Those Aons are [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splinters]] of Devotion,[^4] and have at least some sense that they came from something larger and how they were created, which causes them to consider Devotion as something of a god or parent.[^20]

They are extremely similar to the [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] of [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], and much like spren, they bond with humans.[^21] They offer their masters unconditional love, and will be negatively affected by something happening to their humans, such as the transformation into a [[Reod - The Coppermind|Reod]] Elantrian.[^22] [^8] Unlike spren, seons do not grant their bonded humans any particular abilities, but they do have magical talents of their own. Most notably, two seons can link together to allow for instant communication between their masters, with each seon taking on the appearance of the person on the other side of the link.[^18]

Their equivalents formed from [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion's]] Investiture are the [[Skaze - The Coppermind|skaze]].[^7]

### Direct intervention

As a dead Shard, Devotion can no longer directly intervene into the affairs of Sel; however, something still speaks to the [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") who go into [[Devotion's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Devotion's Perpendicularity]]. It presents Elantrians with a choice to either be dissolved, effectively dying, or carry on living.[^3]

## Vessels

Devotion was originally picked up by Aona shortly following the Shattering of Adonalsium.[^25] Roughly 8,000-10,000 years before the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows"), Devotion clashed with Dominion, and the two were finished off by Odium while they were weakened.[^23] [^24] [^25]

Afterwards, Odium pushed the remnants of both Shards into Sel's [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] to prevent anyone from taking up the power or the powers themselves from achieving sentience.[^12] [^26] [^6] This created a mass of plasma-like substance in the Cognitive Realm, known as the Dor.[^27] [^7] Over time, the Dor has begun to attain self-awareness of its own, but due to being in the Cognitive Realm it has attained only rudimentary emotions.[^28] [^7]

## History

![[Devotion---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [zoethatcher\_art](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/zoethatcher_art "Coppermind:Artists/zoethatcher art")

For history of the Vessel prior to becoming a Shard, see [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona]].

> “ *In case you have turned a blind eye to that disaster, know that Aona and Skai are both dead, and that which they held has been Splintered.* ”
> 
> — [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] [^29]

Very little is known about Devotion, as she was splintered roughly 8,000-10,000 years before the [Night of Sorrows](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Night_of_Sorrows "Night of Sorrows").[^23] [^24] [^25] The Shard was created at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering of Adonalsium]], along with fifteen others, and picked by [[Aona - The Coppermind|Aona]].[^30] She and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]], for an unknown reason, decided to strike out together to [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]], where they passively influenced a number of local cultures, though ultimately decided to take on a more laissez faire approach to human development.[^7] At some point early on, [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] came along and, possibly with some assistance from [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]], killed both Shards and [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] them.[^29] [^31] [^7]

Once the [Vessels](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vessel "Vessel") were dead, the remnants of the two Shards were pushed together into Sel's [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] to prevent anyone from taking up the power or the powers themselves from achieving sentience; this created the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]].[^12] [^26] This plan was not entirely successful, as over time, the Dor has begun to attain self-awareness of its own.[^7]

## Influence

![[Devotion---The-Coppermind-2.png]]

by [Heatherly](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Heatherly "Coppermind:Artists/Heatherly")

> “ *Merciful Domi!* ”
> 
> — [Arelish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Arelish "Arelish") exclamation [^22]

Historically, Devotion and Dominion were not directly involved in human civillization on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]]; however, the discovery of their powers drove its development nonetheless. Both were fundamental in early Selish cultures, and many traditions and religions on the planet are derived from the two of them. Among others, multiple languages and writing systems on Sel are based on Devotion and Dominion's power, with the prime example being the [[Aon - The Coppermind|Aons]].[^7] To this day, worship of Devotion is the primary religion of [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]], where she is known as [Domi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Domi "Domi"); some aspects of [Jaddeth](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jaddeth "Jaddeth") are likewise inspired by her.[^5] The [[Ire - The Coppermind|Ire]], or at least some of its members, also seem to revere her, directly under her Shardic name.[^32]

Due to her early death, Devotion is not particularly influential in modern-day [[17th Shard|cosmere]], but her corpse, in form of the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]], has made travel to and from [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] through the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] highly difficult.[^7] It's possible some remnant of her is still present within it, or at least within her [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]], [the Lake](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Lake "The Lake"), as it speaks to [Elantrians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") thrown into it.[^3]

During the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] sends [a letter](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Letters#The_Sixth_Letter "Letters") to [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], in which he expresses how troubled he is by Devotion's and Dominion's deaths, as he had not known that Shards could be [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] in such a way.[^33]

## Trivia

- In *[Magic: The Gathering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic:_The_Gathering "wikipedia:Magic: The Gathering")* colors, Devotion would be green/red.[^34]
- The pairing of Devotion and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] is somewhat inspired by yin and yang.[^35]
- Brandon has used "Love" as a synonym for Devotion's Intent.[^36]
