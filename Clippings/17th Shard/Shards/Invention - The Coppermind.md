## Invention

**Invention** is a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]] that has not been [[Splinter - The Coppermind#Formation|Splintered]] and whose original Vessel is [[Chan Ko Sar - The Coppermind|Chan Ko Sar]].[^1] The Vessel may be one of the scholars [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] questioned about talent, who answered that the highest talent is the invention of marvelous devices.[^3]

Invention created a number of planets, and [Starling](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starling "Starling") believes that they are more technologically advanced than [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^4] The lawnarks native to the [[Grand Apparatus - The Coppermind|Grand Apparatus]] are mentioned to be 'of Invention'.[^5]

## History

Like all Shards, Invention was created at the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], and picked up by the Vessel [[Chan Ko Sar - The Coppermind|Chan Ko Sar]]. After Ascending, Chan Ko Sar traveled the cosmere creating "great marvels." [^1]

Some time around the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|True Desolation]] [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]], on the advice of [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], reached out to Invention about the threat posed by [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]]. After that initial contact, Harmony was unable to locate Invention again.[^6] [^2]

## Trivia

- If Brandon were to become a Vessel, Invention is one of the Shards he has said he might choose.[^7]
