## Preservation

> “ *The infinity of a note held perfectly, never wavering. The majesty of a painting, frozen and still, capturing a slice of life from a time gone by. It was the power of many, many moments compressed somehow into one.* ”
> 
> — [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] [^2]

**Preservation** is a [[Shards|Shard]] of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], one of the two located on [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]]. Its original [Vessel](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vessel "Vessel") was [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]], but has since been held by [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] and [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] before [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]] took it and mixed it with [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]], becoming [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]].[^3] In the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|Terris]] religions Preservation is known as **Terr**, or "to preserve"; in [Southern Scadrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Southern_Scadrial "Southern Scadrial"), it's known as **Frue** and believed to be feminine.[^4] [^5] Preservation’s [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] is typically associated with the color white.[^6] [^7]

## Intent

> “ *If he had his way you'd all be frozen in time, unable to act lest you harm one another.* ”

Preservation seeks to provide stability and preserve the world and its inhabitants in their current state. It values the status quo, regardless of whether or not the people living underneath it are happy, and supports those who seek to keep it.[^9] It has distinct difficulty acting in a destructive way; it causes the Vessel almost physical pain to wound or kill, even if doing so would likely preserve more than the act itself would destroy.[^10] Preservation is not just about stability and stasis of the world, but also operates on a psychological level.[^11]

Preservation is the perfect opposite and complimentary Shard of [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]]. This is rather unique in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], as most Shards cannot be paired off in this way.[^12]

### Allomancy

Preservation fuels [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]], one of the three [[Metallic Arts]] of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]]. Unlike most forms of magic in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], the power is inherited rather than gained; an Allomancer can be a Misting, able to use only one allomantic ability, or a Mistborn, capable of utilizing all of them.[^13] Power is gained by consuming and then "burning" a metal, but the metal itself is not the source of the power. Rather, it acts as a key, unlocking access to Preservation's power, which can be used differently depending on which metal has been burned.[^14]

There are sixteen "base" metals that can be used in Allomancy, divided into four categories: **physical metals**, which alter a person or their surroundings, **mental metals**, which interact with emotions and supernatural senses, **temporal metals**, which change the perception and passage of time, and **enhancement metals**, which change the behavior of other allomantic abilities.[^15] Additionally, there exists a number of metals that are derived directly from [[Shards]], among them [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]], [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]] and [[Trellium - The Coppermind|trellium]]. Though most are not of Preservation, they and their alloys can also be burned by some Allomancers.[^16]

### Feruchemy

[[Feruchemy - The Coppermind|Feruchemy]] is a Metallic Art created from both Preservation and [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]].[^17] Much like Allomancy, it is inheritable, and a person can be either a Ferring, with access to only one power, or a Feruchemist, capable of using all of them.[^18] A Ferring or Feruchemist stores an aspect of themselves, such as their strength or speed, in a piece of metal dubbed a metalmind, with the metal used determining the atribute stored. When storing, this aspect is diminished in them -- for example, storing strength makes one physically weaker. Later, the same person can withdraw the power from a metalmind, or tap the metalmind, to increase that attribute in themselves.[^19]

Feruchemy utilizes the same base sixteen metals as Allomancy, albeit the effects produced are slightly different. The metals in Feruchemy are divided into **physical metals**, which store physical attributes of a person, **cognitive metals**, which store attributes of the mind, **spiritual metals**, which store attributes of the soul, and **hybrid metals**, which store attributes related to bodily processes, like health or energy.[^20] [[Shards|Shardic]] metals can also be used to store other attributes.

### Lerasium

[[Lerasium - The Coppermind|Lerasium]] is the [God Metal](https://coppermind.net/wiki/God_Metal "God Metal") of Preservation, or the metallic form of its body. Preservation's Vessel can create them deliberately; it's unknown whether it'll form on its own.[^21] Unlike most metals, Lerasium can be burned by any person who ingests it. Doing so transforms a person into a Mistborn of power exceeding that of a natural-born Allomancer; if a Mistborn burned it, it would enhance their pre-existing powers.[^22] It can also be alloyed with any of the base sixteen metals to make someone a Misting of the alloyed metal.[^15]

### Mists

The [mist](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mist "Mist") of Scadrial is the gaseous form of Preservation's [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. Unlike [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]] and similarly to [Breath](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Breath "Breath"), the mist can be programmed to automatically perform specific tasks, albeit the only one capable of doing so is Preservation itself. Under [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras']] command, the mists would go out at night and seek out potential Allomancers, then Snap them to awaken their power; this process continued even after Preservation has been killed, up until [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]] took on the power.[^23] Under [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]], the Mists only come out at specific nights, and turned from a continent-wide pattern into localized phenomena.[^24] They are no longer used for Snapping, but some believe that [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] can use them to observe the world.[^25] [^26]

The mists can be used by Preservation to manifest as a so-called **mist spirit**. The spirit has limited power to influence the world around it, curtailed further by Preservation's natural disinclination towards acting in general; however, under right circumstances it is able to interact with physical objects.[^10] Both Preservation and later Harmony can also use the mists to fuel the Allomancy of people they chose to empower, circumventing the need for metals.[^27]

### Direct intervention

> “ *They say that You come to all people when they die.* ”
> 
> — [Wax](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wax "Wax") [^28]

Having directly created [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] along with Ruin, Preservation has vast amount of control over the planet. A person holding the Shard's power can rearrange continents, recreate extinct forms of life, and even move the planet within the solar system, permanently changing its orbit. Additionally, Preservation is Invested in every Scadrian, and can alter the way their bodies function.[^29] [^30] Direct killing is, however, directly against its Intent and thus extremely difficult bordering on impossible.[^10]

Preservation is able to hear the voice of any living Scadrian, but cannot speak to them; full two-way communication is only possible through the use of both it and [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]].[^31] It can manifest a body in Scadrial's [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], which both [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] and [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]] use to speak with the recently-deceased before they pass on through to the [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 2|Beyond]].[^9] [^28]

## Vessels

Preservation had many Vessels over its history, being passed between three people during the Scadrian year 1025 [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|FE]] following the death of its original Vessel.

Preservation was originally picked up by Leras shortly following the Shattering of Adonalsium, who held it for around 10,000 to 11,000 years.[^9] [^32] It was eventually taken up by Kelsier, who, as a Cognitive Shadow without any connection to the Physical Realm, was incapable of fully using its power.[^33] However, he held it for a short period, until he could pass it to Vin Venture, so that she would be capable of fighting against Ruin and thus protecting Scadrial.[^34] [^35] Vin perished in the battle, her Shard being released.[^36]

Following the deaths of the previous Vessels of Preservation and Ruin, both Shards were taken up by [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]], uniting as the Shard of [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]].[^29] [^37] This was the first instance of one Vessel taking up more than one Shard at a time, and it would be the only instance of such an occurrence of this until the formation of Retribution over 300 years following the birth of Harmony.[^38]

## History

For the history of Leras prior to becoming Preservation, see [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras#History]].

### The deal with Ruin

> “ *I was able to trick Ruin before, lock him away, by fooling him with our agreement.* ”
> 
> — [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] [^39]

Following the death of [[Adonalsium - The Coppermind|Adonalsium]], [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] and [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati]] picked up the Shards Preservation and [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]]. They departed [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]] together, before eventually finding their way to a star system empty of inhabitable planets.[^40] The two Shards found themselves at a stalemate; both knew they could only create something together, yet with one wishing to preserve, and the other to destroy, they could not easily cooperate.[^41] Eventually, Preservation offered a deal -- the two would create a world together, and Ruin would eventually be able to destroy it. Preservation [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested]] more power than Ruin into humans, thus guaranteeing for Ruin that he would one day be able to overcome and destroy it.[^42] Thus, [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] was formed. However, Ruin was unaware of Preservation's true plans, who realized he needed to create humans that would manifest both Preservation's protective ability and Ruin's destructive ability.[^36]

Some time later, Preservation exploited a loophole in the pact and trapped Ruin's mind in Preservation's own [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]], transforming the pool into the [[Well of Ascension - The Coppermind|Well of Ascension]]. The [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]] was altered into a prison of impotence, preventing Ruin from influencing Scadrial and humanity in major ways. Simultaneously, Preservation tore away Ruin's body -- representing most of his power -- and sequestered this into an Investiture cycle tied to the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]]. This cycle forbade Ruin from reabsorbing his body's Investiture, which thereon manifested in the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] as the God Metal [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]]. The region connected to this cycle became known as the [Pits of Hathsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pits_of_Hathsin "Pits of Hathsin").[^43] [^44] [^45] [^46] [^47] This made the two powers all but equal, but at the cost of Preservation's mind.[^48] Leras was, for all intents and purposes, dead; it was only a matter of time before his consciousness would dissipate, leaving the Shard free to be taken or fall apart.[^2]

However, before imprisoning Ruin, Preservation saw into the distant future and formulated a series of preparations to orchestrate Ruin's downfall. One preparation was gifting the original [Terris Prophecies](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Terris_Prophecies "Terris Prophecies") to the original Terris people. They spoke of The Hero of Ages - a hero who would come to the Well of Ascension every thousand years to be a protector of humanity; with risk of either protecting or destroying the world, while holding the future of the entire world on their arms. Unbeknownst to Ruin, [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] hid layers referencing the Hero who would span the Ages - a Feruchemist who could fully tap their copperminds while taking up both Preservation and Ruin.[^29]

The other preparation was the [mists](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mists "Mists") of Scadrial, designed to awaken [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]] every time the Well nearly filled up. Leras intended for the mists to gradually set up an army of atium Mistings, who would burn away all of Ruin's atium millennia into the future. Furthermore, they were meant to be fully drawn by a human after taking the Well of Ascension’s power. For that human was meant to take in all the mists, briefly Ascend to the new Vessel of Preservation, and kill Ruin. [^49]

Over time, Ruin changed the prophecies and mists, but the core of the plan did remain.[^10] [^50] For example, Ruin twisted the mists into the dangerous [Deepness](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deepness "Deepness").[^23]

As millennia passed, the increasingly impotent and mindless Preservation tried to faciliate the plan to the best of his ability. For example, he hid hints in all of Scadrial's religions from Ruin, intending for the Hero of Ages to find them.[^29] Millennia before the invasion of [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]], he created at least eleven beads of lerasium placed near the Well of Ascension.

### Rashek

As the power in the Well of Ascension continued to build, it had to be taken up and used roughly once per millennium.[^51] However, to give the power up altogether would release Ruin; as such, [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati]] twisted the prophecies to suggest that that was what had to be done.[^52]

In one such cycle, [Alendi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alendi "Alendi") travelled to the [[Well of Ascension - The Coppermind|Well of Ascension]], with the intent of releasing the power. Preservation attempted to stop him, and its mist spirit murdered one of Alendi's travelling companions. Afterward, [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek]] took up the power in the Well to prevent the release of Ruin, and used it to reshape the world of Scadrial in an attempt to get rid of the mists. This was only partially successful, as he ended up moving the planet too close to the sun, and had to make further changes to compensate.[^53]

Eventually, Rashek used the power of the Well to make himself a [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Mistborn]] in addition to being a [Feruchemist](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Feruchemist "Feruchemist"). He also empowered a cadre of allies and friends who would become the foundation of the nobility, leading to the creation of [The Final Empire](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Final_Empire "The Final Empire").[^54]

### The release of Ruin

A thousand years later, after [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] was killed by [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek]] -- now known as the Lord Ruler -- he found himself in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] and face to face with an apparition of Leras. After promptly punching it in the face, Kelsier tricked Preservation into revealing how to become a [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 1|Cognitive Shadow]], and was subsequently trapped in the [[Well of Ascension - The Coppermind|Well of Ascension]] as it began to fill once more.[^9]

Both Preservation and Ruin picked [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] as one of its unwitting agents many centuries prior, though Ruin attempted to stifle Preservation's influence by piercing child Vin with a [[Hemalurgic chimera - The Coppermind|Hemalurgic]] spike at birth. The first sign of [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin's]] connection to Preservation was when she attempted to fight the Lord Ruler and drew on the mist to fuel her [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Allomancy]].[^55] A year later, she began to hear the beat of the Well as it called for someone to once more take up the power. At the time, Preservation's mist spirit began to appear to her on multiple occasions as Leras was looking for a way to stop her from releasing the power.[^56]

Ultimately, Vin and [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") found themselves at the Well of Ascension, where, unseen to them, Kelsier's spirit had just learned of the danger in releasing the power. In a last bid to stop Vin from freeing Ruin, Kelsier grabbed Leras by the arm and forced him to slash [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend"), dealing him a deadly stomach wound. However, this was for naught, as Vin elected to release the power anyway out of a sense of duty. To prevent Elend's death, Leras led Vin to the last remaining bead of [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]], making him a Mistborn.[^52]

### The death of Leras

> “ *Odd. After all these years appearing for others as they died, I never expected... that my own passing would be so cold and lonely....* ”
> 
> — [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]] [^33]

Following Ruin's release, Kelsier was once more able to move freely about Scadrial's Cognitive Realm. Resigned, Preservation led him to [Khriss](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khriss "Khriss") and [Nazh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nazh "Nazh"), a pair of worldhoppers with knowledge of the greater [[17th Shard|cosmere]] mechanics. Though Khriss insisted that they could not help, Nazh pointed Kelsier towards the [[Ire - The Coppermind|Ire]], an [Elantrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantrian "Elantrian") organization aiming to take the power of Preservation for themselves.[^39] [^57] In their fortress, Kelsier managed to acquire the [Ire's orb](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ire%27s_orb "Ire's orb"), a sphere filled with a substance that could grant [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 18|Connection]] to Preservation.[^58]

At the same time, Leras was rapidly dying. As the mist spirit, he appeared one last time to [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") as the latter was almost ready to give up when escorting a large group of captured koloss back to [Fadrex City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fadrex_City "Fadrex City"). By this time, Preservation was too weak to perform any significant actions and could only vaguely communicate with Elend, attempting to convince him not to besiege Fadrex.[^59] Shortly after Elend departed, Leras died, and his body fell back into the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]].[^60]

### Kelsier

For the history of Kelsier prior to becoming Preservation, see [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier#History]].

> “ *Do you know why I always won at card tricks, Ruin? It's because I could always. Force. People to choose. The card **I wanted them to.*** ”
> 
> — [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] [^34]

Aware of Leras' death, Kelsier broke the orb and used its power to ascend to Preservation himself before the power could be [[Splinter - The Coppermind|Splintered]] by Ruin. However, the power did not obey him fully, as he was not one of the people intended to use it.[^61] Kelsier resigned himself to affecting the world around him in minor ways, and eventually formed a plan to get the power to [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]], who was held back by the Hemalurgic spike in her ear.[^33] He finally managed to contact [Spook](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Spook "Spook"), who sent a message informing Vin about the spike.[^62]

The message was not, however, meant for Vin -- rather, it was meant for [Marsh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"), who was hunting Vin. When Marsh saw the letter, Kelsier put the whole of his power against Ruin to distract Ati. This allowed Marsh the freedom to understand what the letter meant and rip the earring out of Vin. Kelsier then gave Preservation over to her.[^34]

### Vin

For the history of Vin prior to becoming Preservation, see [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin#History]].

> “ *You created the thing that can kill you, Ruin.* ”
> 
> — [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] [^36]

Initially, Vin was using Preservation's power with little skill, much like Rashek had, and at every turn, her actions were countered by Ruin.[^27] [^35] At the same time, Elend realized that the people being snapped by the out-of-control mists were in one-sixteenth [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]] Mistings, who Leras planned on to burn atium, denying Ruin its own power.[^36]

At the [Battle of Hathsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Battle_of_Hathsin "Battle of Hathsin"), as Marsh and Elend fought, Vin fuelled Elend's Allomancy to allow him to keep up. During the fight, he burned [[Atium - The Coppermind|atium]] with [[Duralumin - The Coppermind|duralumin]], which allowed him to perceive all of Preservation's plan. Now aware of what had to be done, Elend let Marsh kill him. Vin, now with her last reason to live gone, likewise realized why she held the power. As someone new to it, she was able to use it in destructive ways, as it has not yet warped her mind to its Intent. She rammed into Ruin with the whole of her power, killing them both.[^36]

### Sazed

For the history of Sazed prior to becoming Preservation, see [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed#History]].

> “ *I am, unfortunately, the Hero of Ages.* ”
> 
> — [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]] [^63]

As [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]] observed the battle drawing to a close, he ran out to find Vin, unaware that she was no longer alive. He eventually managed to find her and [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati's]] corpses. Distraught, he called out to Preservation, raging that despite Ruin's death, the world was still ending. It was then that the two bodies began leaking the powers of Preservation and Ruin. Though initially doubtful that he could hold them, Sazed eventually realized that he was, in fact, the prophesied [Hero of Ages](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hero_of_Ages "Hero of Ages"), bound to take up both powers and restore the world. Thus, he grabbed both powers and used them to restore Scadrial, merging the two Shards together into [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]].[^29]

This was the first instance of two Shards being united into a singular Vessel, and would remain as the only such instance until over three-hundred years later with the formation of [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution]], uniting [[Honor - The Coppermind|Honor]] and [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]].[^64]

For the history of the Shard following its merging with Ruin, see [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony#History]].

## Influence

> “ *When something grows, or dies, the Jaggenmire make that happen. There is Herr, and his sister Frue, who is also his wife. And she makes things stop, and he makes things go.* ”

Preservation is worshipped as a god by many Scadrian cultures. The [[The Coppermind Wiki - 17th Shard, the Official Brandon Sanderson Fansite 6|Terris]] have perhaps honored it the longest; they were worshipping it before [The Lord Ruler](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Lord_Ruler "The Lord Ruler") as **Terr**, and have kept it up even centuries after the [Final Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Final_Ascension "Final Ascension"), when the Preservation was already merged into [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]].[^65] [^66] In the [Southern Scadrial](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Southern_Scadrial "Southern Scadrial"), Preservation is worshipped as part of a pair of gods with [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]]; the two are known together as a single entity, the **Jaggenmire**, with Preservation's part of it being called **Frue**. Frue is considered to be female, and the Jaggenmire are believed to be both siblings and spouses.[^5]

The [[Kandra - The Coppermind|kandra]] have some knowledge of Preservation -- or, as they call it, the power of [Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ascension "Ascension") -- and its bearers. For an unknown reason, they're only aware of its last four Scadrian holders: [[Rashek - The Coppermind|Rashek]], [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]], [[Vin - The Coppermind|Vin]] and [[Sazed - The Coppermind|Sazed]].[^67] They might be unaware of [[Leras - The Coppermind|Leras]], or see him as something different.

Preservation is the author of the [Terris Prophecies](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Terris_Prophecies "Terris Prophecies"), a set of legends that foretold the coming of the [Hero of Ages](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hero_of_Ages "Hero of Ages"). Leras created them as part of his plan to ultimately defeat Ruin, and although [[Ati - The Coppermind|Ati]] twisted the prophecies over time to serve his ends instead, they were successful in this regard and have led to the creation of Harmony.[^68] On a smaller scale, Leras, with aid of [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]], was responsible for stabbing [Elend](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elend "Elend") at the [[Well of Ascension - The Coppermind|Well of Ascension]], leading to him becoming a [[Allomancy - The Coppermind|Mistborn]], and the mist spirit snapped Allomancers for many centuries.[^10] [^69]

## Trivia

- The Southern name for Preservation, Frue, is the Danish word meaning "lady" or "Mrs", and is close to German Frau ("Miss"), matching the name for Ruin, Herr (German for "Mister").
- In *[Magic: The Gathering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic:_The_Gathering "wikipedia:Magic: The Gathering")* colors, Preservation would be white.[^70]
- Out of all of the Shards, Preservation has the highest amount of known of Vessels.
