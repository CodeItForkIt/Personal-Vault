> “ *Travel to Bjendal has been completely upset. That’s four primary systems we can’t visit without extreme danger, if you count Roshar.* ”

**Bjendal** is a [[17th Shard#Planets|planet]] in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]], and its system is considered one of the primary systems.[^1]

The [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]] consider the planetary system of Bjendal to be of primary importance, likely meaning that a shard resides or has resided there. In 348 [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|PC]], interplanetary travel to Bjendal through the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] was disrupted, likely as a result of something happening to the [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularity]] on Bjendal.[^1]
