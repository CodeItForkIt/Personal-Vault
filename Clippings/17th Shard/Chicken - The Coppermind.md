## Chicken

**Chicken** is the name used by many [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Rosharan]] cultures -- excluding the [[Shin Kak Nish - The Coppermind|Shin]] and the [Unkalaki](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Unkalaki "Unkalaki") -- to refer to all species of birds.[^1] Only some are what would be called chickens in any other part of the [[17th Shard|cosmere]],[^2] such as [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^3] The term coming to mean all birds is a result of [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") languages tending towards generalization, similar to how all alcohols became wines and all small creatures became [[Cremling - The Coppermind|cremlings]].[^4]

Chickens are not native to [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], but were brought to the planet during the initial human migration from [[Ashyn - The Coppermind|Ashyn]].[^5] They live mainly in [[Shinovar - The Coppermind|Shinovar]] and are plentiful there.[^6] Very few birds are found in most of eastern Alethkar; wild sightings are essentially unheard of in [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar").[^7] There are some exceptions, as wild geese live in the [[Horneater Peaks - The Coppermind|Horneater Peaks]] and [Felt](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Felt "Felt") notes that other wild birds range as far east as the [Valley](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Valley "Valley").[^8] [^9]

Most easterners have a very poor understanding of the anatomy and behavior of chickens.[^10] By the time of the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]], more people began to recognize the differences between various species of birds, but the term chicken was still firmly entrenched in the Vorin vernacular.[^11] [^12]

## Uses

Chickens are often edible, and are therefore an export of Shinovar.[^13] [Vstim](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vstim "Vstim") trades with [Thresh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thresh "Thresh") (and likely other Shin) for chickens that he sells to customers in the east at high prices.[^6] Chicken as a food is therefore relatively uncommon in the [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") nations, and is even served at royal feasts.[^13] [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin Kholin]] believes that eating chicken brings good luck and always eats some before his duels.[^14]

Very expensive arrows may be fletched with chicken feathers.[^15] The elder [Tanalan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tanalan%27s_father "Tanalan's father") wore decorative feathers on his battle helm, which Dalinar found odd.[^16] Since wild geese live in the Horneater Peaks, [Unkalaki](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Unkalaki "Unkalaki") arrows are often fletched with geese feathers [^17] and the [Unkalaki language](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Unkalaki#language "Unkalaki") has a word for goose.[^8]

## Notable 'Chickens'

- [Jeksonofnone](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jeksonofnone "Jeksonofnone"), a bright green chicken brought by a [Thaylen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen "Thaylen") merchant to the [[Middlefest Fair - The Coppermind|Middlefest Fair]] which could say its name upon command.[^11]
- [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize]] owns an [Aviar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aviar "Aviar") which is referred to as a 'green chicken' by Shallan.[^18]
- [[Lift - The Coppermind|Lift]] finds another Aviar, a red 'chicken' that belonged to an [old Feruchemist](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Gereh "Gereh") who was killed by [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize]] during the occupation of [[Urithiru - The Coppermind|Urithiru]]. She is now raising the bird.[^12] [^19]
- [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] observes a 'chicken' with red, blue, and green feathers when she is at the Shattered Plains menagerie with [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin]] and [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]].[^10]

## Trivia

- The use of the word 'chicken' to refer to all birds is known as a [synecdoche](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synecdoche "wikipedia:Synecdoche"), or more specifically in this case, *[pars pro toto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pars_pro_toto "wikipedia:Pars pro toto")*.
