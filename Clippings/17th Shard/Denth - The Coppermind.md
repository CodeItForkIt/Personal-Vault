## Denth

> “ *I've tried going back. But the darkness... it's inside. I can't escape it. My laughter has an edge to it. I can't forget.* ”
> 
> —Denth to [[Vasher - The Coppermind|Vasher]] [^2]

**Denth** or **VaraTreledees** [^4] [^5] is a [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]] from Nalthis. He is also a [worldhopper](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Worldhopper "Worldhopper") and one of the [[Five Scholars - The Coppermind|Five Scholars]].[^6] He may also be a member of the royal line of [[Idris - The Coppermind|Idris]].

## Appearance and Personality

As a Returned Denth can change his appearance by will. During the Pahn Kahl rebellion Denth appears as man in his thirties. He dresses in nondescript brown leather, but he keeps some color, such as his red vest. He has a long face and styled hair pulled back into a neat short ponytail.[^7] He keeps himself well groomed and is clean-shaven.[^8]

His overall outlook is cynical, likely colored by his own frustration that he finds himself having to do things he feels bad about.[^9] <sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

## Attributes and Abilities

As a [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]] Denth is faster and stronger than other people. He is also immune to most toxins and cannot die of old age.

Denth is a superb swordsman. He is so quick he can kill most people, even trained warriors, before they can react. Even accomplished fighters such as Vasher are clearly outmatched by him.[^10] [^2]

Denth understands [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakening]] on an advanced level, as one of the Five Scholars.[^2] He is also a very skilled awakener.

Denth is a consummate actor and manipulator, able to deceive [[Vivenna - The Coppermind|Vivenna]] and the other Idrians for months into setting up a war for him.

He has not travelled the cosmere widely.[^3]

### Pre Rebellion

Little is known of Denth's life before he shows up in [[Hallandren - The Coppermind|Hallandren]]. Denth was originally known as VaraTreledees and was a scholar before the [[Manywar - The Coppermind|Manywar]]. He initially tried to stop the war, but ultimately took Vasher's side.[^11] He now hates [[Vasher - The Coppermind|Vasher]] for killing his sister [Shashara](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shashara "Shashara") and does not care that Vasher did it to protect the secret of [[Awakening - The Coppermind|Awakening]] steel.

Some time after the Manywar he took the name Denth and created a mercenary band with [[Tonk Fah - The Coppermind|Tonk Fah]], [[Jewels - The Coppermind|Jewels]], and [[Arsteel - The Coppermind|Arsteel]]. At some point Arsteel encountered Vasher and was killed by him. Denth took this opportunity to see how a dead Returned works as a [[Lifeless - The Coppermind|Lifeless]], and they renamed him [Clod](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Clod "Clod").

Denth has also spent time on the planet [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]], though when and for what purpose remains unclear.[^3]

### Pahn Kahl rebellion

> “ *Unfortunately, Princess, we're here to kill you.* ”
> 
> —Denth to Vivenna [^12]

Denth conspires with the [[Pahn Kahl - The Coppermind|Pahn Kahl]] rebels to overthrow the Hallandren government. Denth and his band infiltrate the [Idrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Idrian "Idrian") spy network to kidnap [[Vivenna - The Coppermind|Vivenna]].[^13] Acting as mercenaries working for [Lemex](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lemex "Lemex") they befriend Vivenna after poisoning Lemex to make it look like he died of illness. Denth uses Vivenna to increase tensions between [[Hallandren - The Coppermind|Hallandren]] and [[Idris - The Coppermind|Idris]] to drive [[Hallandren - The Coppermind|Hallandren]] to war.[^14] Later, after Vivenna is kidnapped by [[Vasher - The Coppermind|Vasher]], Denth and [[Tonk Fah - The Coppermind|Tonk Fah]] torture and kill [Parlin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Parlin "Parlin"), Vivenna's friend to try and discover where she has gone. When Vivenna escapes from Vasher and returns to the safe house, she uncovers the truth about Denth and [[Tonk Fah - The Coppermind|Tonk Fah]].[^15] In the ensuing confrontation, Denth is attacked by [Clod](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Clod "Clod") on [[Vivenna - The Coppermind|Vivenna's]] command, and she escapes. Denth catches Vasher while he is attemping to rescue Vivenna's sister [Siri](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Siri "Siri") who is living in the god king's palace. He has Vasher's sword [[Nightblood - The Coppermind|Nightblood]] thrown in the water so it can cause no further harm, then has Vasher hung by his wrists in a room with no color in the god king's palace to interrogate him.[^16] [^17] After retrieving Nightblood, Vivenna assists in [[Vasher - The Coppermind|Vasher's]] escape. Denth is killed by Vasher in a duel; Vasher stabs Denth after transferring what [Breath](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Breath "Breath") he had left into Denth in order to stun him. Vasher was not a better swordsman than Denth, and this was known by both characters.[^2]

### Death

During the [[Pahn Kahl rebellion - The Coppermind|Pahn Kahl rebellion]], Denth fights a duel with Vasher to prove that Vasher is not a better swordsman than [[Arsteel - The Coppermind|Arsteel]] or himself, and to find out how he won. Denth's fight with Vasher reveals that Denth is the better swordsman, however Vasher is more cunning. Vasher gives his [Breaths](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Breath "Breath") to Denth, effectively stunning him for a moment and Vasher runs him through with his dagger. As Denth dies, his hair flashes between colors, eventually falling to stark white.[^2] This reveals that Denth has the [Royal Locks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Royal_Locks "Royal Locks"), independent from being a [[Returned - The Coppermind|Returned]].[^18]

## Relationships

- Denth was one of the Five Scholars. He was the brother of Shashara and sworn enemy of Vasher, another of the Scholars.[^2]
- He is the leader of a mercenary band including [[Tonk Fah - The Coppermind|Tonk Fah]], [[Jewels - The Coppermind|Jewels]], and [Clod](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Clod "Clod").[^8]
- He temporarily pretends to be employed by and allied with Vivenna.[^15]
- He's actually in the employ of [Bluefingers](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bluefingers "Bluefingers").[^5]
- He hates [[Vasher - The Coppermind|Vasher]] intensely for his past crimes, such as instigating the Manywar and killing [Shashara](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shashara "Shashara") and [[Arsteel - The Coppermind|Arsteel]], even though they were once friends.[^19]

## Trivia

- Brandon considers Denth to be "the anti- [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] ", as they are both "glib, smart, and hired to do impossible tasks"; however, Denth, unlike Kelsier, is the antagonist.[^20]
