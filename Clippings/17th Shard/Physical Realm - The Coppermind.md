## Physical Realm

The **Physical Realm** is the realm of existence in the [[17th Shard|cosmere]] that contains what is perceivable to the physical senses.[^1] Of the three [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realms]], it is the most readily understood. It is made out of a very large number of [axi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Axi "Axi").<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

The cosmere is a large star cluster within a dwarf galaxy,[^2] [^3] or potentially a galaxy in of itself,[^4] or the universe.[^5] [^6] [^7] [^8] Galaxies are organized into galactic clusters, which are themselves organized into galactic superclusters.[^2] This article lists all the known celestial bodies in the cosmere as they appear in the Physical Realm, including planets, stars and moons.

## The Selish System

*Main article:* [Selish system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Selish_system "Selish system")

The Selish system orbits a yellow star named [Mashe](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mashe "Mashe"). There are five planets in this system: two within the habitable zone, then two gas giants named Ky and Ralen, and finally an unnamed dwarf planet.

### Donne

Donne is the closest planet to the sun. While it is in a habitable region, it is barren and suffers from terrible dust storms.[^9]

### Sel

*Main article:* [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]]

The second planet from the sun, Sel is an Earth-like planet inhabited by humans and artificially created beings called [[Seon - The Coppermind|seons]] and [[Skaze - The Coppermind|skaze]]. The planet was the location of [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(book\) "Elantris (book)"). Sel was dominated by the [Fjordell Empire](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fjordell_Empire "Fjordell Empire"), with [[Arelon - The Coppermind|Arelon]] and [[Teod - The Coppermind|Teod]] being the only remaining free nations. All these nations (except Teod which is located on a continent to the north) were situated on the continent of Opelon (named Sycla by the Fjordell Empire).[^9] [^10] Sel is also notable as, in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]], the shattered remnants of [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion]] and [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]] have mingled to form the [[Dor - The Coppermind|Dor]], which results in Sel having a great variety of [[Invested Arts]], which are primarily form-based.[^9]

#### Moons

Sel has a single moon named Oem.[^9]

## The Scadrian System

*Main article:* [Scadrian system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Scadrian_system "Scadrian system")

The Scadrian system has a yellow sun. It has five planets: Scadrial within the system's habitable zone; two gas giants, named Aagal Nod and Aagal Uch; and finally two unnamed dwarf planets.[^11]

### Scadrial

*Main article:* [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]]

The planet was created by the Shards [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin]] and [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation]]. The orbit of the planet has been changed by people using [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. Its size and gravitation are 1.0 cosmere standard.[^11] It is also one of the most technologically advanced planets in the Cosmere, with only Taldain able to rival it. [[Hemalurgy - The Coppermind|Hemalurgy]] also originates here, and has had important effects on the rest of the Cosmere. Scadrial has no moon.

## The Taldain System

*Main article:* [Taldain system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Taldain_system "Taldain system")

The Taldain system is a binary system. The two stars in it are a weak white dwarf star, which is surrounded by a particulate ring, and a blue-white supergiant.[^12]

### Taldain

*Main article:* [[Taldain - The Coppermind|Taldain]]

Taldain is a tidally locked planet. It is the only planet in this system. It is kept in an unstable orbit around [AisDa](https://coppermind.net/wiki/AisDa "AisDa") by the gravitational pull of [Ridos](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ridos "Ridos"), and by some other, unknown force, presumably [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]]. This astronomically unstable orbit is known as "Wombear's Saddle".[^13]

It is orbited by a single moon known as **Nizh Da**.

### AisDa

**AisDa** is a blue-white supergiant star which lights the Dayside of Taldain.[^13]

### The Eye of Ridos

The **Eye of Ridos** is a weak white dwarf star surrounded by a particulate ring. It is the primary source of light and radiation for [Darkside](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Darkside "Darkside").[^13]

Every seven days, it sends out a pulse of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] which Invests the [Starcarved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starcarved "Starcarved").[^13]

## The Threnodite System

*Main article:* [Threnodite system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Threnodite_system "Threnodite system")

This planetary system has four planets. The habitable zone has three planets named Monody, Elegy, and Threnody. Elegy has a single moon named Coronach. Beyond the habitable zone is a single gas giant named Purity.[^14]

### Threnody

*Main article:* [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnody]]

Threnody, the third planet in the Threnodite system, contains the [Forests of Hell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Forests_of_Hell "Forests of Hell"). It is the setting for the story [Shadows for Silence in the Forests of Hell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadows_for_Silence_in_the_Forests_of_Hell "Shadows for Silence in the Forests of Hell"). It has many features that are similar to Earth, including forests, mountains and oceans, but also some supernatural elements, such as the dangerous cognitive shadows, called [Shades](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shade "Shade"), which roam the Forests of Hell. [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver]] seems to be a fairly common resource on the planet, but because of its importance in fighting Shades, and therefore to survival, it is still finite and valuable. Threnody has no moon.[^14]

## The Drominad System

*Main article:* [Drominad system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Drominad "Drominad")

The Drominad system has seven planets. There are four planets in the habitable zone, three of which have developed human societies. An asteroid belt lies just beyond these planets. Then there are three gas giants. All these planets are named for their position from the sun (First of the Sun, Second of the Sun, etc.).[^15]

### First of the Sun

*Main article:* [[First of the Sun - The Coppermind|First of the Sun]]

First of the Sun is a minor world consisting mainly of a large ocean and island systems. It has no Shards in residence. It has developed unique flora and fauna, including a type of [bird](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aviar "Aviar") that forms a Nahel Bond.[^15]

#### Moons

First of the Sun has a single moon named First of the First.

## The Rosharan System

*Main article:* [Rosharan system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rosharan_system "Rosharan system")

Greater Roshar is a planetary system containing thirteen planets. Some have life, sentient and not, others are barren. There are three planets in the habitable region and ten gas giants, the two separated by an asteroid belt. The gas giants are named after Vorin numerals.[^16]

### Ashyn

*Main article:* [[Ashyn - The Coppermind|Ashyn]]

Ashyn is the first planet from the system's sun.[^17] It has sentient life.[^18]

The book [The Silence Divine](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Silence_Divine "The Silence Divine") is set here.[^18] The magic system involves people who gain magical powers from diseases.[^19]

Ashyn is mostly barren, due to an ancient disaster, with a few fertile patches.[^18] It currently has no [[Shards|shard]].[^20] [^21]

### Roshar

*Main article:* [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]

Roshar is the second planet from the Rosharan system's sun. It has a gravity of 0.7 Cosmere Standard, and a year 500 days long, with each year being 1.1 earth years. The planet consists of one large super continent.

#### Moons

Roshar has three moons. The first moon is Salas. It is violet, and is the smallest moon. The middle moon is Nomon, which is a bright, pale blue, and the final moon is Mishim, which is small and green.

### Braize

*Main article:* [[Braize - The Coppermind|Braize]]

Braize is the third planet from the sun. [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium]] settled on Braize, although he is still affecting [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]]. Braize is unlikely to get its own books, but scenes from [The Stormlight Archive](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Stormlight_Archive "The Stormlight Archive") will be set on Braize.<sup><i><a href="https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Cite" title="Help:Cite">[citation needed]</a></i></sup>

Braize is known to the [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") people as [Damnation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Damnation "Damnation").

## The Nalthian System

*Main article:* [Nalthian System](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nalthian_System "Nalthian System")

The Nalthian System orbits a yellow star. There are three planets in the system: [[Nalthis - The Coppermind|Nalthis]], the gas giant Farkeeper the Bright, and the dwarf planet Nightstar the Hidden. Much farther from the star, there is a comet belt. There is also a [Cognitive anomaly](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cognitive_anomaly "Cognitive anomaly") in Nalthis' orbit.[^22]

### Nalthis

*Main article:* [[Nalthis - The Coppermind|Nalthis]]

On Nalthis, [[T'Telir - The Coppermind|T'Telir]] is the capital city of [[Hallandren - The Coppermind|Hallandren]] and its bordering mountain state of [[Idris - The Coppermind|Idris]]. The jungles are situated in a large valley surrounded by mountains and a sea.

#### Moons

A single moon named Rrendos orbits the planet.[^22]

## The UTol System

*Main article:* [UTol system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/UTol_system "UTol system")

The UTol System orbits a red-orange star.[^23] There are two planets in the system: [[UTol - The Coppermind|UTol]], and [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]]. They are in dual orbit with the star.[^24] The [Iron Seven Waystation](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Iron_Seven_Waystation "Iron Seven Waystation") is in the vicinity of the system.[^24]

### UTol

*Main article:* [[UTol - The Coppermind|UTol]]

UTol is one of the only two known planets that are known to be inhabited by [[Sho Del - The Coppermind|Sho Del]], alongside [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]].[^25] It is covered primarily by oceans with relatively small land masses.[^25]

### Komashi

*Main article:* [[Komashi - The Coppermind|Komashi]]

Komashi is a planet that's inhabited by humans and [[Hijo - The Coppermind|hijo]].[^26] Humanity has inhabited very little of the planet, with the two largest nations being only fifty Rosharan miles across.[^27]

## Other Planets

### Bjendal

*Main article:* [[Bjendal - The Coppermind|Bjendal]]

Little is known of Bjendal, and nothing is known of its peoples, or any [[Shards]] that may be located there. It does contain a perpendicularity.[^28]

### Canticle

*Main article:* [[Canticle - The Coppermind|Canticle]]

Canticle is a very small planet, estimated at only 322km in diameter,[^29] but with gravity within the ranges that would be expected for larger planets, caused by its highly Invested core.[^30] The surface is blasted every day by intense sunlight capable of melting the land.[^31]

### Grand Apparatus

*Main article*: [[Grand Apparatus - The Coppermind|Grand Apparatus]]

The Grand Apparatus is a planetary [^32] [^33] megastructure comprising cities made of thousands of communal moving rooms controlled by a giant centralized [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Invested]] mechanism. It is controlled by the [[Sleepless - The Coppermind|Sleepless]], who experiment on its peoples.[^34]

### Lumar

*Main article:* [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]]

Lumar is a planet of islands with twelve moons hanging in equidistant, geostationary orbits oppressively close to the surface, that drop [[Aether spores - The Coppermind|aether spores]].[^35] It has a whole ecosystem beneath the surface with nothing known about it.[^36]

### Mythos

*Main article:* [[Mythos - The Coppermind|Mythos]]

Nothing is known of Mythos, its peoples, or any [[Shards]] that may be located there, only that it exists.

### Obrodai

*Main article:* [[Obrodai - The Coppermind|Obrodai]]

Little is known of Obrodai, but [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]] has claimed it, manifested an avatar there, and warned [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] against returning.[^37]

### Vax

*Main article:* [[Vax - The Coppermind|Vax]]

Nothing is known of Vax, its peoples, or any [[Shards]] that may be located there. All that is certain is that it has a manifestation of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]], and that it hasn't developed vaccines yet.[^38]

### Yolen

*Main article:* [[Yolen - The Coppermind|Yolen]]

Yolen is the original home of three sentient races [^39] and was the origin of all humans in the cosmere.[^2] The original sixteen Vessels were from Yolen.[^39]

## Stars and Constellations

### Starbelt

The [Starbelt](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starbelt "Starbelt") is an unusually bright patch of stars in the sky of [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnody]].[^40] It is useful for navigation in the [Forests of Hell](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Forests_of_Hell "Forests of Hell") during the night; the light usually filtered through the trees fairly well.

The Starbelt is the same patch of unusually bright stars that is visible from [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]].[^41] It is visible from other worlds in the cosmere as well.[^42]

### Taln's Scar

[Taln's Scar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Taln%27s_Scar "Taln's Scar") is a great swath of red stars that stand out against the white ones.[^43]

### The Tear

The [Tear](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tear "Tear") is a particularly bright star in the Rosharan sky. Its name comes from the single tear shed by [Reya](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Reya "Reya") in Rosharan Mythology.[^44]

### Mashe

[Mashe](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mashe "Mashe") is the star around which [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]] and several other planets orbit.[^9]
