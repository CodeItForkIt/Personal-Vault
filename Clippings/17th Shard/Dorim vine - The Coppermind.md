## Dorim vine

**Dorim vines** are flora native to the [Dayside](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dayside "Dayside") of [[Taldain - The Coppermind|Taldain]], primarily found in the [[Kerla - The Coppermind|Kerla]]. They are used by Daysiders as an important water source.[^1]

## Ecology

Like most flora of the Kerla, the ecosystem of dorim vines exists below the sands. The vines thrive in the [[Kerla - The Coppermind|Kerla]] because of its high water table.[^2] The vines are capable of rapid movement through the sand and will quickly seek out any available water.[^2] Dorim vines can also live in [deep sand](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deep_sand "Deep sand"), but they grow very far below the surface.[^1] [[Lossand - The Coppermind|Lossand]] has shallow sands, and dorim vines do not grow there.[^3]

Dorim vines carry a significant amount of water within themselves as a defense mechanism against certain creatures native to Dayside, primarily [[Sandling - The Coppermind|sandlings]]. These creatures have skin or carapaces that dissolve if they come into contact with water, even if they are [[Sand mastery - The Coppermind#Terken|terken]].[^2] [^4] In areas where dorim vines are common, sandlings grow to smaller sizes so they do not accidentally run into the vines.[^5]

## Use and Culture

Dorim vines are a critical water source for Daysiders. Vines can be located by pouring water onto the sand, as they will quickly come to the surface to absorb it. When the vine is cut, water flows out and can easily be collected in a canteen or other vessel.[^2] Despite much of Dayside being covered in sand, only areas where dorim vines are not available are considered to be desert; this includes areas of deep sand where the vines cannot be located by humans.[^2]

The vines are particularly important to [Kerztians](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kerztian "Kerztian"), since their nation largely consists of Kerla sands. The presence of dorim vines allows Kerztians to live and travel where they wish, while Lossandin culture is limited to the [Ry'DoAli](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ry%27DoAli "Ry'DoAli") river basin.[^3] Kerztians consider Lossand to be a desert due to its lack of dorim vines.[^1] They consider rivers to be cursed, since so much water in one place is unholy; the name Ry'DoAli is Kerztian for "the vein of cursed waters".[^2] [^3] [^1] Lossandins who travel to other parts of Dayside for trade or hunting are familiar with the vines since they are necessary for survival.[^5]

## History

[Daazk](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Daazk "Daazk") tried to explain how to find dorim vines to [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khrissalla]] and her traveling party, but he did not know enough [Dynastic](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dynastic "Dynastic") to explain why he needed to pour water onto the sand.[^4] [[Kenton - The Coppermind|Kenton]] later showed dorim vines to the [Darksiders](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Darkside "Darkside"); they were fascinated by them since they were unlike anything from their part of the world.[^2]
