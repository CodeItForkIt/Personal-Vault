**Ker'reen** is the predominant religion of [[Kerzta - The Coppermind|Kerzta]].

The philosophy calls for worship of the sun, teaching that it is the manifestation of the [[Sand Lord - The Coppermind|Sand Lord]] and the source of autonomy and independence. The Ker'reen philosophy denounces the use of [[Sand mastery - The Coppermind|sand mastery]] as heresy, which has led to war between Kerzta and [[Lossand - The Coppermind|Lossand]] in the past.

The priest [[DaiKeen - The Coppermind|DaiKeen]] oversees the religion among Kerztians. They are led by the **A'kar**, or high priest. Members of the priest DaiKeen are traditionally marked by square-shaped scars on their foreheads, though some have recently been found to wear X-shaped scars similar to the tattoos of the warrior DaiKeen. Members of the clergy speak [Karshad](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Karshad "Karshad").[^1]

The **KerKor** is the primary religious text of the Ker'reen religion.[^2]
