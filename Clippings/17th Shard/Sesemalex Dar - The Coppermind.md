## Sesemalex Dar

> “ *Yes, it is the capital of the kingdom of Emul, and is one of the most ancient cities in the world.*”
> 
> —Sigzil

**Sesemalex Dar** is the capital of the kingdom of [[Emul - The Coppermind|Emul]] [^1] on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude - The Coppermind|Era of Solitude]].[^2]

## Geography

The city of Sesemalex Dar sits on a stone plain on the west coast of Emul.[^3] It borders on [Tukar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tukar "Tukar") and is northeast of [Torriqqam](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Torriqqam "Torriqqam").[^2] [^4]

The design of Sesemalex Dar is intricate and often said to be spectacular,[^1] built in enormous gashes in the ground to protect it from [[Highstorm - The Coppermind|highstorms]].[^3] Each trough has a lip of rock diverting water from flowing into the city and a complex drainage system of cracks to help it weather the storms,[^3] as well as stone of a unique composition that helps with the drainage.[^1] The pattern of the Sesemalex Dar is central to the Emuli religion.[^1] The structure of the city is very similar to [[Yeddaw - The Coppermind|Yeddaw]], and many consider visiting Yeddaw to be the closest thing to seeing Sesemalex Dar itself.[^5]

## History

Sesemalex Dar was founded at an unknown time. One of the most ancient cities in the world, some believed it to be a [Dawncity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dawncity "Dawncity").[^1] [^3] The Emuli made the city their capital at some point, regarding its design as central to their religion. However, the Tukari also desired the city for its use as a port, and they were able to use the status of their ruler, [[Ishar - The Coppermind|Tezim]], as a justification for attempting to take it.[^1] This resulted in the [[Eighty's War - The Coppermind|Eighty's War]], a conflict centered around the city which lasted several years.[^6]

At the advent of the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]], the Eighty's War was swallowed up by the conflict with [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]] forces. The [[Fused - The Coppermind|Fused]] and [[Singer - The Coppermind|singers]] took the southern part of Emul, including Sesemalex Dar.[^7] The city was freed from this occupation when the [coalition of monarchs](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coalition_of_monarchs "Coalition of monarchs") successfully regained control of Emul in.[^8]

## Culture

It is said that the city of Sesemalex Dar--and the entire kingdom of Emul--was named by [[Jezrien - The Coppermind|Jezrien]] himself. Some people such as [Au-nak](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Au-nak "Au-nak") believe Sesemalex Dar to be a [Dawncity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dawncity "Dawncity"). The Emuli believe that Sesemalex Dar is their ancestral homeland given to them by the [Heralds](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Heralds "Heralds") and often speak of its religious significance.[^1]

The citizens of Sesemalex Dar are well known for their marvelous [crem](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crem "Crem") pottery, and the city itself is a major waypoint and trade city of the south. Due to the wealth of trade in Sesemalex Dar, the bordering nation of [Tukar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tukar "Tukar") wants the city for its fantastic port; this is the conflict at the root of the [[Eighty's War - The Coppermind|Eighty's War]].[^1] As such, the city has been a war zone for a long period of time.[^5]
