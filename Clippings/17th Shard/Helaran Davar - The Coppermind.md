## Helaran Davar

**Helaran Davar** is a [Veden](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Veden "Veden") [Shardbearer](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardbearer "Shardbearer") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^3] Helaran is the oldest child of [Lin Davar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lin_Davar "Lin Davar"). Unbeknownst to most of [[House Davar - The Coppermind|House Davar]], he is likely a bastard, making him the half-brother of the four children his father has with the [Herald](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Herald "Herald") [[Chanarach - The Coppermind|Chanarach]]: [[Balat Davar - The Coppermind|Balat]], [[Wikim Davar - The Coppermind|Wikim]], [[Jushu Davar - The Coppermind|Jushu]], and [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]].[^4] Originally known as **Nan Helaran Davar**, identifying him as the first son and heir of his House, until he is stripped of the title Nan when his father disowns him.

## Appearance and Personality

> “ *Helaran's grin was so wide, it practically glowed. It was hard to frown in a room where he was smiling.* ”
> 
> — [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan's]] thoughts on her brother.[^3]

Helaran is tall, muscular, and square-chinned, although his father is taller than he is.[^3]

Of all the Davar children, he is considered to be the most responsible and the only true adult, even after his younger brother [[Balat Davar - The Coppermind|Balat]] comes of age. He and [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] are his father's favorite children.[^5]

Helaran is resentful and angry at his father, who he believes murdered his step-mother and her lover in cold blood.[^3]

## Attributes and Abilities

His step-mother had intimate contact with a [Skybreaker](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Skybreaker "Skybreaker") acolyte and it is possible [[Nale - The Coppermind|Nale]] knew a member of the house was close to bonding a [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]]. It is believed they thought it was Helaran. He was recruited because Nale was impressed with him. They recruited him with displays of great power and Shards.[^6]

## History

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Helaran-Davar---The-Coppermind-1.jpg]]

by [Vedtom](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Vedtom "Coppermind:Artists/Vedtom")

Helaran on the Alethi battlefield, moments prior to being killed by Kaladin

Around or shortly before, Helaran acquires a [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") from the [[Order of Skybreakers - The Coppermind|Order of Skybreakers]].[^3]

Later that year, he visits [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] and brings her sketching tools from [Northgrip](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Northgrip "Northgrip") as a present. He had also given [[Balat Davar - The Coppermind|Balat]] a new [[Axehound - The Coppermind|axehound]] pup, but Balat had abused and possibly killed it.[^3] During the visit, he tells Shallan and his father that he must leave to attend to affairs, but [his father](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lin "Lin") disagrees and confronts him. Helaran summons his Shardblade, threatens his father, and leaves, but promises to return and check on the family.[^3]

After this confrontation with his father he visits the Davar estate sporadically and for only short periods at a time, bringing [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]] more drawing supplies but avoiding his father.[^5]

Of all the Davar children, he knows the most about his father's secrets and plans and stands up to him the most. He has some connection to [Wit](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wit "Wit"), whom he asks to deliver the message that "he has eyes nearby and is watching". Upon hearing this, his father declares him disinherited and start referring to Tet Balat as Nan Balat, thereby naming Balat the eldest son.[^7]

Two years later, he returns to [Jah Keved](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Jah_Keved "Jah Keved") and meets with Balat. Helaran warns his brother that this time when he leaves, he might not be coming back for a long time, and asks him to watch over the family. When his father finds out, he sends one of his new guards, [Rin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Rin "Rin"), to kill him.[^8]

In, he appears on a battlefield in [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar") with a full set of Shards. Helaran's goal is to kill Highlord [Meridas Amaram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Meridas_Amaram "Meridas Amaram"), but he is killed by [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] during the battle.[^1]

Helaran had not yet bonded a [highspren](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Highspren "Highspren") and so his [Shardblade](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shardblade "Shardblade") still had a glowing gemstone. Likely, Helaran was sent to kill Highlord [Meridas Amaram](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Meridas_Amaram "Meridas Amaram") as a test—either that or he took it upon himself as a way of proving his worthiness for Knighthood.[^6]

Although it is Kaladin's right to claim Helaran's Shards, Amaram betrays Kaladin, enslaved him, and claims the Shards for himself instead.[^9] [^10] [^11] [^12]

## Relationships

![[Clippings/17th Shard/attachments/Helaran-Davar---The-Coppermind-2.jpg]]

by [Marie Seeberger](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Artists/Marie_Seeberger "Coppermind:Artists/Marie Seeberger")

With his siblings

### Shallan

Helaran is protective of [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan]]. He encourages her drawing, as he believes her to have the potential to be great at it.[^3] After leaving, he continues to send her gifts, including [[Jasnah Kholin - The Coppermind|Jasnah Kholin's]] book and high quality paper for her drawings.[^13]

Shallan looks up to her brother and feels that her rightness comes from him, from "the best person she knew".[^14]

She wants to find information on his past and motives, but doesn't make much progress until after becoming affiliated with the [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]].[^6]

### Balat

When Helaran leaves the house for the first time he doesn't think [[Balat Davar - The Coppermind|Balat]] is ready to lead their brothers.[^3] However, several years later, before leaving to the war at [Alethkar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethkar "Alethkar"), he does ask him to watch over his family. Balat considers Helaran to be strong and brave and doesn't consider himself to be as good as he.[^8] When Helaran and Balat are together, Balat "puffs up" and acts more confident.[^15]

### Lin Davar

> “ *There are more important things in this world, more important even than you and your crimes.* ”
> 
> —Helaran to his father before leaving house Davar [^3]

Helaran is sure his father killed his step-mother and her lover. He confronts him as he leaves his house.[^3] Even though they don't see each other again, they grow further apart; Helaran sends him a message stating that he has eyes nearby, and is watching, causing himself to be disinherited and to lose his title of Nan.[^7] Later, Lin Davar finds that Helaran talked to Balat and sends a guard to kill him.[^8]

## Trivia

The reason behind Helaran's attempt on [Amaram's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Amaram "Amaram") life is unknown; however, [[Mraize - The Coppermind|Mraize]] of the [[Ghostbloods - The Coppermind|Ghostbloods]] believed the attack was a strike against the [[Sons of Honor - The Coppermind|Sons of Honor]].[^6]
