## Perpendicularity

> “ *At these points, Physical matter, Cognitive thought, and Spiritual essence become one--and a being can slide between Realms.* ”
> 
> —From [[Khrissalla - The Coppermind|Khriss's]] essay on the [Drominad system](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Drominad_system "Drominad system") [^1]

A **perpendicularity** is a junction between the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical]], [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive]], and [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realms]]. They are used by [[17th Shard|worldhoppers]] such as [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] to go from planet to planet. They are formed through the concentration of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]]. Because of this, they are most commonly found at places where a Shard's power is focused, often in the form of a pool of liquid. A Shard can control where their perpendicularity manifests.[^2] A perpendicularity may manifest as the Shards color.[^3]

## Creation

Perpendicularities occur naturally when a [[Shards|Shard]] spends a prolonged length of time Invested in a planet.[^4] The amount of concentrated investiture creates a "friction" between the physical and cognitive realm, causing a tunneling effect.[^1] Not all perpendicularities are created by the Shards however.[^5] Perpendicularities tend to begin as small pools, about ten feet in diameter.[^6] They can be expanded.[^7] [^6] They also tend to appear in shadowed or cavernous regions.[^8] Shards have a great deal of control over the location of their perpendicularity.[^9]

Generally, leaving a planet would cause the Shard's perpendicularity to disappear.[^10] As a result, their presence is often used to indicate the occupancy of a Shard.[^11] Special circumstances, however, can prevent the perpendicularity from disappearing, and there is at least one Shard that fits these circumstances.[^12] [^13] [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]] is capable of creating perpendicularities unexpectedly across multiple planets in a way that goes against the laws of [[Realmatic Theory, Miscellaneous|Realmatic Theory]] as it is currently understood by arcanists.[^14] This requires specific conditions though.[^15]

Perpendicularities can be created with Intent through various forms of Invested Art.[^16] [^17] It is more difficult to create a perpendicularity in the Spiritual Realm, than it is in the Cognitive or Physical Realms.[^18] [^19] However, it is not impossible to do so for some entities.[^20] [^21]

Some perpendicularities can be forcibly closed by having people utilize the massive amount of Investiture coming from it.[^22] [[Nightblood - The Coppermind|Nightblood]] is able to collapse a perpendicularity.[^23] [^17] [[Lights|Anti-Investiture]] of the perpendicularity's Shard touching the perpendicularity will cause it to collapse.[^24] [^25] Anyone who enters the perpendicularity while this is happening will be transitioned to the Spiritual Realm.[^26]

## Mechanics

Perpendicularities are formed at locations with a large concentration of Investiture. The concentration of Investiture brings the three realms closer together and allows travel between them.[^1] Perpendicularities will not transport people between realms unless they travel through it with Intent.[^22] [^27] They can be used to see, and travel to, both the Cognitive [^28] and the Spiritual Realms.[^29] [^30] However, to transport oneself to the Spiritual Realm, one must push through it with their mind alone, lest they would travel to Shadesmar.[^29] Perpendicularities typically act as stabilizing points between the Physical and Cognitive Realms, allowing their location to be an exact overlap.[^31]

The proximity of the realms around and in a perpendicularity also makes [[Soulcasting - The Coppermind|Soulcasting]] easier.[^32]

[[Aluminum - The Coppermind|Aluminum]] can be used to take liquid from a perpendicularity, and it will stay Invested.[^33] Some perpendicularities are more Invested than others.[^34] The enormous concentration of Investiture in a perpendicularity makes it possible to become a Cognitive Shadow by entering one.[^35] In at least some circumstances, a perpendicularity's liquid Investiture may be unkeyed.[^22] Devices can be made to point towards a perpendicularity.[^36]

## Known Perpendicularities

Scadrial

- **Ruin**: the pool below the [Pits of Hathsin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Pits_of_Hathsin "Pits of Hathsin") was [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin's]] perpendicularity; it was the main path used by inter-world traders before it was destroyed by [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]].[^37]
- **Preservation**: [[Preservation - The Coppermind|Preservation's]] perpendicularity was the [Well of Ascension](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Ascension "Well of Ascension"). It served as [[Ruin - The Coppermind|Ruin's]] prison, making it extremely dangerous.[^37]
- **Harmony**: [[Harmony - The Coppermind|Harmony]] has a perpendicularity somewhere in the south, it is known to be under careful watch by the Malwish.[^14] An article in *[The House Record](https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_House_Record "The House Record")* mentions a woman coming across a perfectly blue pool somewhere in the mountains in the Southern [Roughs](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Roughs "Roughs"), and a sudden appearance of a strange figure by the pool. It's possible, though not confirmed, that this could be Harmony's perpendicularity.[^38]
- **Autonomy**: Autonomy briefly had a perpendicularity in [Bilming](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Bilming "Bilming"), that was since destroyed by [[Marasi Colms - The Coppermind|Marasi Colms]], [Armal](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Armal "Armal"), and several other Allomancers.[^14]

Sel

- **Devotion**: [[Devotion's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|The Pool]] near [Elantris](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elantris_\(city\) "Elantris (city)") is [[Devotion - The Coppermind|Devotion's]] perpendicularity.
- **Dominion**: There is a second perpendicularity on [[Sel - The Coppermind|Sel]],[^39] presumably of [[Dominion - The Coppermind|Dominion]].

Threnody

- [[Threnody - The Coppermind|Threnody]] does not have a stable perpendicularity, but rather a number of unstable, unpredictable ones of a morbid origin.[^40] They may possibly have something to do with the creation of [Shades](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shade "Shade"), although the fact that the Shard of [[Ambition - The Coppermind|Ambition]] has died near the system has far greater influence on their creation.[^41] It is more of a circumstance than a location.[^42]

First of the Sun

- [[First of the Sun - The Coppermind|First of the Sun]] is notable for having a perpendicularity, [Patji's Eye](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Patji%27s_Eye "Patji's Eye"),[^43] without a Shard in residence.[^1] However, [Patji](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Patji_\(being\) "Patji (being)") is an avatar of [[Autonomy - The Coppermind|Autonomy]],[^44] which is likely the cause of the perpendicularity.

Roshar

- **Cultivation**: [Cultivation's Perpendicularity](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cultivation%27s_Perpendicularity "Cultivation's Perpendicularity") was located in the [[Horneater Peaks - The Coppermind|Horneater Peaks]], under the surface of one of the [Horneater Oceans](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Horneater_Oceans "Horneater Oceans").[^45] For [[17th Shard|worldhoppers]] of Roshar, it's the main method of travelling between Roshar and its subastral, [Shadesmar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shadesmar "Shadesmar").[^46] It vanished after Cultivation fled the planet.[^10]
- **Honor**: [[Honor's Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|Honor's Perpendicularity]] is well-known among worldhoppers and [[Spren - The Coppermind|spren]] to be unstable and dangerous.[^46] The exact pattern of its appearance is uncertain, although [[Order of Bondsmiths - The Coppermind|Bondsmiths]] are able to open it.[^47] [^46] [^48] [^49] [^17] The perpendicularity closes on its own within a few minutes.[^47]
- **Odium** and **Retribution**: [[Odium - The Coppermind|Odium's]], and later [[Retribution - The Coppermind|Retribution's]], perpendicularity is the [Well of Control](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Well_of_Control "Well of Control"). It is located underneath the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]].[^50] [^51] [^52] Odium is also capable of temporarily creating a miniature perpendicularity.[^53]
- **Transportation:** The [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Surge]] of [[Surgebinding - The Coppermind|Transportation]] temporarily creates a miniature perpendicularity, allowing an [Elsecaller](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Elsecaller "Elsecaller") or [[Order of Willshapers - The Coppermind|Willshaper]] to travel into Shadesmar from anywhere.[^48] [^16] However, returning back to the [[Physical Realm - The Coppermind|Physical Realm]] presents a far greater challenge, and can only be performed in some places.[^54] [^55] It is unclear if this allows travel to the Spiritual Realm.
- **Knights Radiants**: Whenever a [[Knights Radiant - The Coppermind|Radiant]] swears an Ideal, they create a miniature perpendicularity allowing them to take in an influx of Investiture from the [[Spiritual Realm - The Coppermind|Spiritual Realm]].[^56]
- **Oathgates:** Oathgates make use of perpendicularities in order to transport individuals between Realms and to different Oathgates across the planet.[^57] [^58]

Nalthis

- **Endowment**: The location of [[Endowment - The Coppermind|Endowment's]] perpendicularity is unspecified. It is, however, located somewhere in the jungles of [[Hallandren - The Coppermind|Hallandren]], in a shadowed, cavernous region.[^8] The [Tears of Edgli](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tears_of_Edgli "Tears of Edgli") owe their existence and their unusual properties as dye material to soaking up the [[Investiture - The Coppermind|Investiture]] flowing up from the perpendicularity.[^59] There are enough cosmere-aware people on Nalthis for there to be customs at this perpendicularity,[^60] and it is used for trade.[^61]

Bjendal

- **Unknown Shard**: There is a perpendicularity on [[Bjendal - The Coppermind|Bjendal]]. By 348 [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|PC]], events on Bjendal made travel to the planet through the perpendicularity extremely dangerous.[^61]

Dhatri

- [[Dhatri - The Coppermind|Dhatri]] had a perpendicularity in the past.[^62]

Yolen

- Yolen is one of the three places in the Physical Realm that the [[Silverlight - The Coppermind#Silverlight Nexus|Silverlight Nexus]] leads to.[^6] [^63] It is located within the upper atmosphere of the planet.

General Cosmere

- **Autonomy**: Autonomy has an ability to create and collapse perpendicularities in a way that goes against the understanding of Arcanists.[^14] [^64] These have specific constraints and conditions in order to be produced.[^15] Despite the specific conditions, she is known to have created perpendicularities across multiple worlds across the cosmere.[^14]
- **Silverlight**: [[Silverlight - The Coppermind|Silverlight]] has a perpendicularity known as the Silverlight Nexus.[^65] The perpendicularity leads to three different places in the Physical Realm, one of which is Yolen.[^6] The perpendicularity causes time dilation around Silverlight, making each day in the city equal to ten days outside of it.
