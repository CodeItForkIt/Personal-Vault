## Sorceress's tower

The **Sorceress's tower** is a spaceship in the possession of [[Riina - The Coppermind|Riina]]. It is the ship she used and lived in whilst on [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]], landing on the [[Sorceress's island - The Coppermind|Sorceress's island]] in the [[Midnight Sea - The Coppermind|Midnight Sea]].[^1] It is controlled by an entity named **Seslo**, who follows the orders of Riina.[^2] The spaceship itself is built like a conventional rocket with a single booster at the bottom, when landed on her island this booster is not visible thus causing locals to mistake the structure for the 'tower.' [^1]

The outside walls are made out of a single layer of silver, this makes the structure impossible to scale or stick to.[^3] [^4] Due to the silver any aethers that touch the outside the tower die, which protects the tower from any enemy [sprouters](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Sprouter "Sprouter") or rogue [[Midnight Essence - The Coppermind|Midnight Essence]].[^5] [^6] There are only two entrances and exits into the building, the first the main entrance that she walks through and one for her 'ravens' that she uses as familiars. Both doors are locked by modern means but as local Lumarans do not understand the technology they presume it to be another case of the Sorceress's magic.[^3] [^4]

Internally the tower resembles an office building with clear hallways with a single red carpet leading through them. Along these halls there are various aonic symbols carved into the structure. It is unclear if these served any purpose or are purely decorative. Although resembling a hospital room, the Lumarans generally consider the structure beautiful as they have no equivalent elsewhere. The inside consists solely of this hall and a staircase up to the top of the tower, where there is a bridge for the Sorceress to be based in.[^2] There is presumably another room elsewhere in the vehicle that can store her metal army.[^4]

From her tower, Riina watches over much of Lumar through hidden surveillance cameras.[^2] This includes a camera on [Fort's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Fort "Fort") board, as its security hadn't been turned on.[^2]

When [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]] and [[Tress - The Coppermind|Tress]] force Riina to leave Lumar, she does so in her tower, removing it from the planet.[^1]
