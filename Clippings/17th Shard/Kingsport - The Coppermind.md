## Kingsport

**Kingsport** is a city presumably in the [[Emerald Sea - The Coppermind|Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar - The Coppermind|Lumar]];[^1] it is also presumably the residence of the [Verdant King](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Verdant_King "Verdant King"), and as such is located on the [[Core Archipelago - The Coppermind|Core Archipelago]].[^2] Reports about possible [deadrunners](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deadrunner "Deadrunner") are sent to the port,[^1] which is located far from [Shimmerbay](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Shimmerbay "Shimmerbay").[^3]

[[Charlie - The Coppermind|Charlie]] claimed that there was constant war at the port, to the extent that gardeners must be trained in the art of warfare; however, it is likely that this was merely another of his stories.[^4] After Charlie's disappearance, [[Tress - The Coppermind|Tress]] planned to go to Kingsport to plead that the king pay Charlie's ransom. When she had the opportunity to do so, however, she instead remained with the crew of the *[[Crow's Song - The Coppermind|Crow's Song]]*.[^5] [^3]

[Crow](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Crow "Crow") had news via a raven from a contact in Kingsport that the remnants of the *[Oot's Dream](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Oot%27s_Dream "Oot's Dream")* had been found, but that there were no survivors, just the corpse of the one man that Crow had left alive.[^1]
