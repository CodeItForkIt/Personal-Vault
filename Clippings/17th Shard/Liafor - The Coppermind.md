## Liafor

> “ *I do think the coat is splendid, but I worry that the short cut might be trending out of fashion. What is the latest from Liafor?* ”
> 
> — [Torol Sadeas](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Torol_Sadeas "Torol Sadeas") to [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin]] on fashion [^1]

**Liafor** is a Makabaki kingdom on a peninsula in south-west [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^2] It is known as a center of fashion on Roshar.[^3]

## Geography and Ecology

It borders [Tashikk](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tashikk "Tashikk") to the east, [Yezier](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Yezier "Yezier") and [Alm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alm "Alm") to the north, the [Aimian Sea](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aimian_Sea "Aimian Sea") to the northwest, [Steen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Steen "Steen") to the southwest, and an unnamed body of water to the south. The city [Berizhet](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Berizhet "Berizhet") lies in northeastern Liafor, close to Tashikk. A major river runs west through Liafor, starting near Berizhet and ending in the Aimian Sea.[^2] During the [[Silver Kingdoms - The Coppermind|Silver Kingdoms]] era, it was part of [[Makabakam - The Coppermind|Makabakam]].[^4]

## History

During the Silver Kingdoms period, it was part of Makabakam.[^4] Eventually, Makabakam collapsed and Liafor eventually formed from its remains.<sup title="This section needs to be expanded with more information"><i>[expand]</i></sup>

During the Era of Solitude, Liafor remained a free nation, rather then becoming a member state of the Azish Empire.[^5] However, they often follow the Azish in foreign policy regardless.[^6] At some point it became famous across Roshar for its fashion.[^3]

## Politics

Liafor is headed by a monarchy run by a prince and other rulers.[^7] [^8] The prince's cousin, [Kaza](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kaza "Kaza"), was trained from a young age in the use of their [soulcaster](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Soulcaster "Soulcaster"), which is always kept in the royal family. Whoever is the soulcaster is locked up yet given comforts in an attempt to placate them. Their soulcaster only has the ability to turn objects into smoke. The soulcaster was lost on the island of [Akinah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Akinah "Akinah") when Kaza ran off seeking a cure for her [[Savant - The Coppermind|condition]].[^7] Liafor tends to eagerly join whatever councils Azir is in, though they prefer to cater meetings themselves.[^6] At the start of the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]], they and Yezier were the only two Makabaki countries [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar]] thought in a stable enough position to approach about the coalition.[^9]

## People

The Liaforan people have deep brown skin and dark brown eyes, like many other Makabaki peoples.[^7]

## Culture

Liafor, along with Yezier, are known as the origin of fashion on Roshar.[^3] They produce fashion folios that are read even on the opposite side of Roshar at the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]].[^10] Each folio page shows a few alternate styles of outfit for one particular demographic, such as [Azish](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Azish "Azish") male civil servants.[^11] The popularity of these fashion folios comes from an ability to use local models and market towards local fashion trends. Folios marketed towards the [Alethi](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alethi "Alethi") and [Veden](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Veden "Veden") market use Alethi models and show traditional styles such as the [Vorin](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Vorin "Vorin") [havah](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Havah "Havah") rather than the more daring styles popular in the west.[^12] [^13] Similarly, the styles shown in [[Thaylenah - The Coppermind|Thaylenah]] take inspiration from the style of [Queen Fen](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Queen_Fen "Queen Fen").[^14] The folios sent to the Shattered Plains intentionally had out-of-date styles in order to sell military uniforms to the soldiers, which [[Adolin Kholin - The Coppermind|Adolin]] was appalled to learn.[^15]

Fine Liaforan boots are an indication to [Hauka](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Hauka "Hauka") in Tashikk that a man is not a poor farmer as he claims.[^16] Other products to come out of Liafor include Liaforan handicrafts, discussed by Tashikki women,[^17] and a Liaforan word puzzle, done by [[Rysn Ftori - The Coppermind|Rysn]] in [Thaylen City](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Thaylen_City "Thaylen City").[^18]
