## Khrissalla

Duchess **Khrissalla**, better known as **Khriss**, is a [worldhopper](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Worldhopper "Worldhopper") originally from the country of [[Elis - The Coppermind|Elis]] on the [Darkside](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Darkside "Darkside") of [[Taldain - The Coppermind|Taldain]].

## Appearance and Personality

### Appearance

Being from the Darkside of Taldain, Khriss has brown skin, allowing her to also pass as [Makabaki](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Makabaki "Makabaki") on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]],[^2] [^1] or as a Terriswoman on Scadrial.[^3] [^4] She has brown eyes and wears her hair in braids. While on Dayside, Khriss wears a pair of dark, heavy sunglasses as she is not used to the sunlight of Dayside. She also wears a wide-brimmed hat to keep the sun's rays from her face.[^5]

### Personality

Khriss is a university-educated scholar and is extremely inquisitive. She seeks to understand and find scientific explanations for all that she can. She believes in freedom of information, and is willing to assist any who come to her for help if she perceives their intentions to be good.[^6]

## Attributes and Abilities

Khriss is one of the most accomplished scholars of [[Investiture - The Coppermind|investiture]] in the cosmere. She knows more about the cosmere and its magic systems than [Nazh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nazh "Nazh"), her assistant, and even [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Hoid]], who has been alive since before the [[Shattering - The Coppermind|Shattering]].[^7] She collects her knowledge in the [Ars Arcana](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Ars_Arcanum "Ars Arcanum").[^8] The most important question she wants answered is what happens in the [[Beyond - The Coppermind|Beyond]], followed by how to get [[Stormlight - The Coppermind|Stormlight]] off [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]].[^9] She is sometimes credited as a 'Professor of Arcanist Studies'.[^10]

Khriss has [starmarks](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Starmark "Starmark").[^11]

## History

### Taldain

Khriss was engaged for a time to [[Gevalden - The Coppermind|Gevalden]] --or Gevin, as he preferred in informal settings [^12] --a Prince of Elis. Before their wedding, Gevalden traveled to Dayside in search of the mythical Sand Mages, where he was shot by [[Dynasty - The Coppermind|Dynasty]] assassins. His linguist and assistant, [Wilheln](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Wilheln "Wilheln"), wrote to Khriss to report on his death.[^13]

Khriss, determined to seek out what her fiancé was trying to discover, traveled to Dayside with [[Baon - The Coppermind|Baon]], [[Allstren Cynder - The Coppermind|Allstren Cynder]], and [[Jon Acron - The Coppermind|Jon Acron]] and some Elisian soldiers.[^13] They arrived in the [Kerztian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Kerztian "Kerztian") port of [Dosha-Har'Ken](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dosha-Har%27Ken "Dosha-Har'Ken"), on the northwest coast of Dayside, and purchased supplies from a merchant that spoke Dynastic, who also offered the services of his nephew [Daazk](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Daazk "Daazk") as a guide to [[Lossand - The Coppermind|Lossand]].[^13]

#### Crisis in the Diem

She met [[Kenton - The Coppermind|Kenton]] shortly after the Kerztian attack on the [[Sand mastery - The Coppermind|sand masters]],[^5] and he agreed to be her guide to Lossand's capital, [[Kezare - The Coppermind|Kezare]], where they parted ways.[^14] In the Darksider district of [Lonzare](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lonzare "Lonzare") Khriss met with [Loaten](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Loaten "Loaten") and attempted to get answers about Gevin's attempt to contact the sand mages, but their meeting was interrupted by [[Nilto - The Coppermind|Nilto]].[^12] She then sought the [Taisha](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Taisha "Taisha"), assuming Gevin would have met with them, but failed to get meetings with any of the Taishin before finally discovering Kenton was the [[Taishin - The Coppermind#The Lord Mastrell|Lord Mastrell]].[^15]

After recovering from her initial anger at his deception, Khriss decided to help Kenton in his mission to preserve the [[Diem - The Coppermind|Diem]]. She reasoned that the Diem's sand masters could not support Elis if they were disbanded, and used her diplomatic skill to help Kenton negotiate with the [Lord Artisan](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lord_Artisan "Lord Artisan").[^16] Using a letter of introduction from the Diem, Khriss was also able to meet with [Lady Judge](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lady_Judge "Lady Judge") [[Heelis - The Coppermind|Heelis]] who suggested she speak to the Lord Beggar Nilto to help sway public opinion for the Diem.[^17]

During their meeting, Nilto gives her Prince Gevalden's signet ring and pistol, but refuses to explain how he acquired the items.[^17] Because Kenton withheld many details of sand mastery, Khriss attempted to [research](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khrissalla%27s_Journal#Sand_Experimentation "Khrissalla's Journal") white sand and the mechanics of Sand Mastery based on observation.[^11] [^18] Khriss eventually discovered that it was Baon that killed Captain [Deral](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Deral "Deral") and his lieutenant, and that he was sent to Elis by the Dynasty.[^19] Following Baon's departure, Khriss decided to move to the Diem so that she could try to learn sand mastery; but was ultimately unable to display any potential in the [sand mastery test](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Khrissalla%27s_Journal#Sand_Mastery_Test "Khrissalla's Journal").[^20] [^18]

Trying to find Baon before he left the city, she asked [[N'Teese - The Coppermind|N'Teese]] to arrange a meeting with Nilto for her, to ask if he knew Baon's whereabouts.[^20] During this meeting Nilto called her "Khriss", causing her to realize his identity as Gevin. She confronted him at the Kezare docks as he was attempting to leave the city when Professor Acron revealed himself as an assassin sent by the Dynasty to kill Gevalden. Acron shot Baon when he interfered to allow Gevin and Khriss to escape. While running from Acron, Khriss used a crane to knock him into the harbor. She asked Gevin to return with her to Darkside, but he refused in favor of continuing to help people on Dayside.[^21] Baon returned to her service as bodyguard since there was no conflict with his mission for the Dynasty.

When the [Lord Merchant](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lord_Merchant "Lord Merchant") [[Vey - The Coppermind|Vey]] disappeared, she pressured Gevin into using his contacts as Nilto to discover where he had gone if he wanted her to keep silent about his survival. She then traveled with Kenton to [[Lraezare - The Coppermind|Lraezare]] to find the Lord Merchant, and they returned to Kezare once Kenton made his deal with him.[^22] Searching for the [Lord General](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Lord_General "Lord General"), Khriss was present when they discovered his body in [The Tower's](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Tower_\(Taldain\) "Tower (Taldain)") wine cellar.[^23]

Khriss was present at the Diem when Kenton dueled [[Drile - The Coppermind|Drile]], and Baon prevented her from interfering.[^24] After the Diem was restored, Khriss returned to Darkside with multiple barrels of white sand and a few sand masters to aid her experiments in making sand mastery work on Darkside.[^25]

### Silverlight

At an unknown point after the crisis in the Diem, Khriss discovered how to leave Taldain and began to travel the cosmere. Eventually, she came to find [[Silverlight - The Coppermind|Silverlight]], where she was involved in the founding of a university.[^26] She eventually became a well known and regarded [arcanist](https://coppermind.net/edit/Arcanist?redlink=1 "Arcanist (page does not exist)"), possibly also founding her own order. Amongst other projects, Khriss was obsessed with researching [[Shards|Shardless]] [[Perpendicularity - The Coppermind|perpendicularities]].[^27]

### The Death of Preservation

> “ *You've stumbled into something far, far bigger than you, your politics, or your little planet.*”
> 
> —Khriss to Kelsier [^3]

Khriss and [Nazh](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Nazh "Nazh") were discovered by [[Kelsier - The Coppermind|Kelsier]] in the [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Cognitive Realm]] of [[Scadrial - The Coppermind|Scadrial]] in 1023 [[Final Empire - The Coppermind|FE]]. The pair enlightened Kelsier on how the events on Scadrial were affecting the cosmere. They remained quite vague as to why they were there - although Khriss mentioned wanting to see what the death of a Shard looks like - or about other events elsewhere in the cosmere that were unrelated to the current conflict. Khriss refused his requests for help and they left abruptly after Kelsier told them of his meeting with [[Hoid - The Coppermind|Drifter]].[^3] Since then, she has worked with Kelsier on research at least once.[^28]

### The True Desolation

On Roshar, over three centuries after the [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|Catacendre]], around and during the [[True Desolation - The Coppermind|True Desolation]], she took an interest in various figures and concepts, including fledgling Radiants, such as [[Kaladin - The Coppermind|Kaladin]] and [[Shallan Davar - The Coppermind|Shallan Davar]];[^29] [^30] in the nature of Vorin [[Glyphs - The Coppermind|glyphs]],[^31] [^32] [^30] [^33] in the pattern of the [[Shattered Plains - The Coppermind|Shattered Plains]],[^34] and of the Rosharan subastral.[^35] [^36]

It is unclear if she travelled to Roshar herself, or if she sent Nazh to the planet alone.

### New Seran

After the True Desolation, she appeared at a party in [New Seran](https://coppermind.net/wiki/New_Seran "New Seran") in post- [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|Catacendre]] Scadrial, 342 [[Catacendre - The Coppermind|PC]].[^4] There she stole a dance with [Waxillium Ladrian](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Waxillium_Ladrian "Waxillium Ladrian") and asked him questions about his powers and how they related to physics. She was also mentioned in a broadsheet ad, telling people who hear talking tools to contact Khriss and Nazh.[^37]

### Space Age

At some point before the overthrowing of the [Cinder King](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cinder_King "Cinder King") on [[Canticle - The Coppermind|Canticle]], within a century of the discovery and proliferation of faster-than-light travel,[^38] her theories regarding Investiture and how it operates became popular, with her Laws becoming widely recognized.[^39]

Around the time of the Scadrian pressure campaign on [[First of the Sun - The Coppermind|First of the Sun]], Khriss linked historical records of [aviar](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Aviar "Aviar") to the planet and, citing reports of people encountering a traversable well of power in [[Cognitive Realm - The Coppermind|Shadesmar]], theorized that the birds originated there and were brought through a Shardless perpendicularity. The theory drew ridicule from some arcanists for the seeming impossibility of a perpendicularity forming on a world with no known Shard and she was never able to prove it herself as she was not able to provide coordinates. By the time knowledge of the perpendicularity on First of the Sun became widespread, she was otherwise occupied on Taldain.[^27]

## Relationships

### Gevalden

Before she left Darkside for the first time, she was engaged to Prince [[Gevalden - The Coppermind|Gevalden]] of [[Elis - The Coppermind|Elis]]. They appear to have been quite close, enough so that Khriss organized an expedition to fulfill his mission of finding the Sand Mages after his reported death on Dayside.[^13] After discovering his identity as Nilto, she asks him to return to Darkside with her. He refuses, saying he will not return after being injured, and that he and Khriss have both changed too much to have a relationship.[^21]

### Nazh

The mapmaker Nazh is in her employ.[^40] [^27] She commonly sends him out to procure maps, documents and other illustrations for her, particularly in high-risk environments. Although the two argue fairly often, they are good friends and Nazh is fiercely loyal to her.[^41] By the time of the Scadrian pressure campaign on First of the Sun, Nazh is no longer in Khriss' employ, though they retain some amount of contact.[^27]

### Hoid

She has some history with Hoid, but the exact nature of her relationship with him is unknown.[^42] But it is such that she cursed upon learning he stole a bead of [[Lerasium - The Coppermind|lerasium]].[^3]
