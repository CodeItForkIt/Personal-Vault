> “ *Eleven years of war, and nine out of ten people I once ruled are dead. Do we even have kingdoms to lead any longer?* ”

**Eiliz** was a kingdom on [[Roshar - The Coppermind|Roshar]] during the [[Desolation - The Coppermind|Desolations]] of the [[Heraldic Epochs - The Coppermind|Heraldic Epochs]].[^1]

In one of Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin - The Coppermind|Dalinar Kholin's]] [visions](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Dalinar%27s_visions "Dalinar's visions"), King [[Nohadon - The Coppermind|Nohadon]] spoke of Eiliz to Dalinar, who had taken the appearance of [Karm](https://coppermind.net/wiki/Karm "Karm"). Nohadon believed that Eiliz, like [[Tarma - The Coppermind|Tarma]], would likely not survive the aftermath of the Desolation as too many of its people had perished.[^1]

Eiliz was not well known by the [[Era of Solitude - The Coppermind|Era of Solitude]]; Dalinar had never heard of the place.[^1]
