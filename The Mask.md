Why do I cry when others read their poems? Why do I send long texts when saying things important? Why do I act the way I do? 
Do I do these things, act this way, because it is me? 
Or do I just do these things, act this way, because it is who others know me as? 
Do I act as myself or a role made for me by others? 
A mask
A mask made by others
A mask made by me
A mask I wear in front of others
The face they all see
I ask; Why do I wear this mask?
Is the mask a role I act in some grand play?
Or is the mask me?
Is the mask even real?
This mask I wear, does it fool even me?
The mask can be a shield
The mask can be a wall
I let you see through my mask
And you saw me
Why must I wear this mask?
Why can't I show others me?
Thank you for seeing through my mask
Thank you for seeing me
One day I will tear down this mask
So everyone can see me
