# Chull (Huge)

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** huge  
**Attributes:** Strength 4, Speed 0, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 30/30, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

Immensely docile, a Chull (Huge) almost always retreats into their shell with their **Defense Curl** during combat. However, once pushed to the brink, they use their large claws to defend themselves; a pinch from a chull hurts.

### Beast of Burden
*1*

The Chull (Huge)'s carrying capacity is 1,500 lbs.

### Plodding Pace
*1*

The Chull (Huge) can only use the Move action once per turn.

### Strike: Pincer
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Keen average`

### Defense Curl
*2 act*

The Chull (Huge) retracts their head and legs to defend themself until the start of their next turn. While retracted, the Chull (Huge)'s deflect value increases to 6 and they can use the Brace action as if they were in cover. At the start of each of their turns, the Chull (Huge) can spend 1 focus to maintain this effect for an additional round.
