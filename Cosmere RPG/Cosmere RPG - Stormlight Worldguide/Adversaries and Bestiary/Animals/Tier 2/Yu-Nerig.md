# Yu-Nerig

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** large  
**Attributes:** Strength 5, Speed 3, Intellect 2, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 144/144, Focus 5/5, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, INM 3, PRC 1

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with the Yu-Nerig:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to prevent the Yu-Nerig from using their Hurl and Vital Toss actions until the end of the Yu-Nerig's next turn.

**Complication.** The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to have the Yu-Nerig use their Hammer Claw action as r.

##### Tactics

A yu-nerig is a brutal combatant who grips an enemy in their deadly Claw. Depending on the battle, they might Hurl this enemy at another target, or they might use their Vital Slam for massive damage. When surrounded, the yu-nerig attacks with their Hammer Claw, while their Thick Carapace provides powerful defense.

### Hammer Claw
*2 act*

**Attack** +8; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** up to five;

**Graze** `2d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `2d8 + 8 Impact average`.

The Yu-Nerig can't use this action if both claws are restraining enemies.

### Hurl
*1 act*

The Yu-Nerig throws an enemy who is Restrained in their claw to a space the Yu-Nerig can sense within 30 feet of them. The thrown enemy must make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`*. On a failure, the thrown enemy takes `1d8 Impact average` for every 10 feet they were thrown. On a success, the thrown enemy takes half that much damage.

If the enemy is thrown at a space occupied by another character, the thrown enemy lands in the nearest unoccupied space, and the other character must succeed on a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or take the same damage as the thrown enemy.

### Vital Slam
*2 act*

The Yu-Nerig flips an enemy who is Restrained in their claw and slams them against the ground. The target must make a *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`*. On a success, the target takes `1d10 Impact average`. On a failure, the target takes `3d10 Impact average` and is knocked Prone.

### Vital Slam (Test Succeeded)
*2 spe*

The Yu-Nerig flips an enemy who is Restrained in their claw and slams them against the ground. The target must make a *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`*. On a success, the target takes `1d10 Impact average`. On a failure, the target takes `3d10 Impact average` and is knocked Prone.

### Strike: Claw
*1 act*

**Attack** +8; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `2d6 + 8 Keen average`, and the target becomes Restrained by one of the Yu-Nerig's claws while the Yu-Nerig remains within 5 feet of them. As 2, the target or a character who can reach them can make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`*, ending the Restrained condition on a success.

The Yu-Nerig has two claws, each of which can restrain one enemy. While restraining an enemy in a claw, that claw can only attack the Restrained enemy.

### Boss
The Yu-Nerig can take both a fast turn and a slow turn each round. After an enemy finishes a turn, the Yu-Nerig can spend 1 focus to immediately use an extra 1 or 0. Additionally, they can spend 1 focus on their turn to remove a condition from themself.

### Thick Carapace
Before the Yu-Nerig is hit by an attack, they can spend 1 focus to turn the hit into a graze (no action required).
