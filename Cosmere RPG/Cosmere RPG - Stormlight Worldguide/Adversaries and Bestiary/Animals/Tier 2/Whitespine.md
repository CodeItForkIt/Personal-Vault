# Whitespine

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** large  
**Attributes:** Strength 4, Speed 3, Intellect 0, Willpower 2, Awareness 4, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 42/42, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Senses:** 40 ft.  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, STL 3, INM 3, PRC 2, SUR 2

##### Tactics

As a dangerous predator, a **whitespine** stalks their prey with intent to ambush and surprise them. The whitespine charges at weak opponents in the distance, dealing extra damage with their Tusks, then they follow with their Claws to leave prey bleeding out. Whitespines don’t fear leaving an enemy’s reach to charge another, as only precise blows can avoid the whitespine’s Spined Carapace. When a fight goes poorly, whitespines use Pheromone Release to stupefy their opponents so the whitespine can either retreat or overwhelm them.

### Goring Tusks
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d10 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d10 + 7 Impact average`.

If the Whitespine hits with this attack immediately after moving at least 15 feet, the target takes an extra `1d10 Impact average` and must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=14`* or be knocked Prone.

### Pheromone Release
*2 act*

The Whitespine releases foul pheromones. Each non-Whitespine character within 10 feet of them must succeed on a *Test: `skill=dis dc=15`* or become Stunned until the end of the Whitespine's next turn.

### Spined Carapace
*1*

After an enemy within 5 feet of the Whitespine grazes them with a weapon attack, that enemy takes `1d6 Keen average`.

### Strike: Claws
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 7 Keen average`, and the Whitespine can spend 1 focus to make the target Afflicted [`1d4 Vital`] until the target regains at least 1 health.

### Enhanced Senses
The Whitespine gains an advantage on non-attack tests that rely on smell.
