# Ryshadium

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** large  
**Attributes:** Strength 4, Speed 5, Intellect 1, Willpower 2, Awareness 4, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 48/48, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, INS 3, PRC 2

##### Tactics

Few beasts are as intelligent, loyal, and strong as a **Ryshadium**. This fierce steed uses **Loyal Companion** and **Danger Sense** to stay within range of and aid their chosen rider, but they’re also dangerous foes on their own. A Ryshadium’s **Hooves** inflict a powerful blow, and their **Kick** controls the battlefield and gives them and their rider relief. In an opportune moment, the Ryshadium’s **Charge** can be devastating, leaving multiple enemies reeling.\

### Kick
*2 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 7 Impact average`, and the target is knocked back 15 feet.

### Charge
*2 act*

The Ryshadium moves up to 40 feet in a straight line without triggering Reactive Strikes. When they do, they can move through the spaces of unwilling characters. Enemies in this line must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=17`* or be knocked Prone and take `2d10 type=Keen average` as they are trampled underfoot.

### Loyal Companion
*1 fre*

While within 60 feet of their chosen rider, the Ryshadium can use the Move action as 0.

### Danger Sense
*1 rea*

Before the Ryshadium's chosen rider is attack by an enemy the Ryshadium can sense, the Ryshadium can increase the rider's Physical defense by 5 against the attack if the rider is within 5 feet of the Ryshadium.

### Strike: Hooves
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d10 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d10 + 7 Impact average`

### Beast of Burden
*1*

The Ryshadium's carrying capacity is 1,000 lbs. They ignore the weight of Shardplate worn by their chosen rider.
