# Sleepless Hordeling

**Tier:** 3  
**Size:** small  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 3, Intellect 4, Willpower 4, Awareness 6, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 5/5, Focus 6/6, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 3, STL 4, DIS 3, INM 4, LOR 2, DEC 3, INS 4, PRC 4

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with the Sleepless Hordeling:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to prevent the Sleepless Hordeling from using their Ravage action or Swarm Weapon reaction until the start of the enemy's next turn.

**Complication.** The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to use the Unsettle action as r against one or more enemies within 30 feet of them. The Sleepless Hordeling must spend 1 focus per enemy targeted (maximum 5).

##### Tactics

In combat, a **Sleepless** aims to rapidly destabilize and overwhelm foes. First they **Ravage** to begin ongoing damage, then **Overwhelm** in an attempt to smother their foe. Each time they take impact or keen damage, they use **Divisible** to send out a hordeling. If the combat goes awry, their **Swarm Weapon** can give them an edge. When they have an action to spare, they often **Unsettle** their foes.

Outside combat, the Sleepless uses their **Divide** when they need to create one or more hordelings for reconnaissance.\

### Divisible
*1*

After the Sleepless Hordeling takes impact or keen damage, they can immediately use their Divide to send out one hordeling (no action required).

### Overwhelm
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `2d6 + 10 Keen`, and if the Sleepless Hordeling has at least 25 health, they can smother the target. While smother a target, the Sleepless Hordeling occupies the target's space and can't attack other enemies. At the start of each of the Sleepless Hordeling's turns, a smothered target takes `2d6 Vital average`. If the Sleepless Hordeling takes more than 20 damage from a single attack or is reduced below 25 health, they release the smothered target, fall Prone within 5 feet of the target, and no longer occupy the targets space.

### Ravage
*2 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Keen average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 13 Keen average`, and the target becomes Afflicted [`1d8 Keen`] for 2 rounds.

### Unsettle
*1 act*

The Sleepless Hordeling eerily distorts their form toward an enemy they can sense within 30 feet of them. The target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=dis dc=15`* or lose `1d4` focus.

### Divide
*1 fre*

The Sleepless Hordeling can divide the swarm that comprises them, decreasing their health by 5 per hordeling sent out. Each hordeling is a Small character with 5 health who otherwise uses the statistics of a Sleepless Hordeling. These hordelings immediately gain a reaction, but they can't use actions until the next round. Anything a hordeling learns is known by the rest of their horde.

### Swarm Weapon
*1 rea*

After the Sleepless Hordeling is hit by a melee attack, the hordelings that comprise them can momentarily swarm the attacker's weapon, giving the attacker a disadvantage on their next test against the Sleepless Hordeling.
