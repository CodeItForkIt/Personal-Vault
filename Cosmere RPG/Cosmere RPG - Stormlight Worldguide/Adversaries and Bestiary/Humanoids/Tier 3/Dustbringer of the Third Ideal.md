# Dustbringer of the Third Ideal

**Tier:** 3  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 4, Intellect 4, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 47/47, Focus 5/5, Investiture 6/6  
**Deflect:** 2  
**Skills:** AGI 4, LWP 4, STL 4, DIS 3, INM 3, LEA 4, PRC 3, DVS 4, ABR 3

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with the Dustbringer:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to prevent the Dustbringer from using their **Burst of Flame** and **Regenerate** actions until the end of the Dustbringer's next turn.

**Complication.** The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to remove the Investiture cost from the Dustbringer's **Decaying Touch** action and **Skate** free action until the end of the Dustbringer's next turn.

##### Tactics

A Dustbringer of the Third Ideal carves a destructive path in and out of melee with their foes. They **Skate** to close the distance, attack with their **Shardblade**, then disengage. **Burst of Flame** is their primary tactic for groups of enemies, and **Decaying Touch** saps the strength of their bulkier foes. Ultimately, the Dustbringer of the Third Ideal devastates an area and is hard to pin down—and their **Eroding Escape** ensures that.

### Invested Speed
While they have 1 Investiture or more, the Dustbringer of the Third Ideal's movement is increased by 10 ft.

### Slippery
*1*

While they have 1 Investiture or more, the Dustbringer of the Third Ideal can't be grazed and Reactive Strikes against them gain a disadvantage.

### Strike: Shardblade
*1 act*

**Attack** +8; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 8 Spirit average`, and the Dustbringer of the Third Ideal can spend 1 focus to use the Disengage action as 0.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

The Dustbringer of the Third Ideal recovers `1d6 + 3 Healing average`. They can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Inescapable Decay
*1 act*

**Attack** +8 vs. *Spiritual*; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `3d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `3d10 + 8 Spirit average`, and the target must succeed on an *Test: `skill=agi dc=14`* or their armor rusts and decays, permanently decreasing its deflect value by 1. Armor damaged in this way can be repaired with proper materials as a downtime activity.

### Skate
*1 fre*

The Dustbringer of the Third Ideal moves up to their movement rate.

### Eroding Escape
*1 act*

The Dustbringer of the Third Ideal targets themself or an ally in reach, ending one condition on them that is either applying the Immobilized condition, the Restrained condition, or a disadvantage on a physical test.

### Burst of Flame
*2 act*

The Dustbringer of the Third Ideal emits a burst of fire that deals `1d12 Energy average` to each character within 10 feet of them and sets unattended objects in that area ablaze. Additionally, the Dustbringer of the Third Ideal can spend 1 focus or more to shower sparks onto that many enemies within 30 feet of them; each target must succeed on an *Test: `skill=agi dc=14`* or lose one 1 on their next turn.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +8; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 8 Keen average`
