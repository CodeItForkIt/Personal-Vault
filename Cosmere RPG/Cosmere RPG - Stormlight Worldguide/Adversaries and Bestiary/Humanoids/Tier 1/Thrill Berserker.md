# Thrill Berserker

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 2, Intellect 1, Willpower 3, Awareness 2, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 22/22, Focus 5/5, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 2  
**Skills:** ATH 2, HWP 2, LWP 1, INM 2, LEA 1, PRC 2

##### Tactics

A **Thrill berserker** rushes into battle, undeterred by formidable enemies and careful fortifications. They fight with alarming ferocity, using Berserker Rage and Shield Bash to cleave their path, regardless of whether friend or foe stands in the way.

### Infectious Thrill
*1*

When an enemy rolls a c on an attack test against the Thrill Berserker, the enemy gains a +3 bonus to that d20 roll. Regardless of whether the attack hits or misses, if any other characters are within 5 feet of the Thrill Berserker, the enemy must choose one. The enemy's attack also grazes the chosen character (without spending focus).

### Shield Bash
*1 act*

The Thrill Berserker makes an Athletics test against the Physical defense of a Large or smaller character within 5 feet of them, knocking them Prone on a success.

### Berserker Rage
*1 fre*

The Thrill surges in the Thrill Berserker. Their next Strike targets each character within 5 feet of them and deals an extra `1d6 Keen average`damage on a hit.

### Lash Out
*1 rea*

After the Thrill Berserker is attacked by an enemy within 5 feet of them, the Thrill Berserker deals `1d8 Keen average` to the attacker.

### Strike: Longsword
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 4 Keen average`, and if the target is Prone, they take an extra `1d8 Keen average`.

### Strike: Dagger
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Range** 20/60 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 3 Keen average`
