# Swordmaster Ardent

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 22/22, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 2, HWP 2, LWP 2, DIS 2, LOR 2, MED 2, INS 2, LEA 2, PRC 1

##### Tactics

A Swordmaster Ardent skillfully analyzes their opponents and adjusts tactics accordingly. Against a reckless or aggressive opponent, a Swordmaster Ardent strikes decisively, taking a slow turn to Gain Advantage and use their **Staggering Blow**, then following with a fast turn to deliver a meaningful message through **Disarm Opponent**. When facing a cautious or unknown opposition, a Swordmaster Ardent gauges their opponent's strength through attacks with their blade, uses **Parry** to limit injury, and waits for an opportune time to unleash their full force.

### Swordmaster
The Swordmaster Ardent gains an advantage on Insight tests against any humanoid they've instructed before.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Keen average`.

### Staggering Blow
*2 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Impact average`, and the target gains a disadvantage on the next Discipline test they make before the end of the Swordmaster Ardent's next turn.

### Disarm Opponent
*2 act*

The Swordmaster Ardent moves up to 10 feet then attempts to disarm an enemy within reach who is wielding a melee weapon. The target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=dis dc=15`* or drop their weapon and have it flung up to 15 feet away in any direction.

### Parry
Before the Swordmaster Ardent is hit by a melee attack, they increase their Physical defense by 3 against that attack, potentially causing it to miss. To do so, the Swordmaster Ardent must sense the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
