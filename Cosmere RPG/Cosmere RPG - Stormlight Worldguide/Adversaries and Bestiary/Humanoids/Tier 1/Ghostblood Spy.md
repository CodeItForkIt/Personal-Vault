# Ghostblood Spy

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 0, Speed 4, Intellect 2, Willpower 2, Awareness 1, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 21/21, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 1  
**Skills:** AGI 1, LWP 2, STL 2, DED 2, LOR 1, DEC 2, INS 2, PRC 2, SUR 1

##### Tactics

A **Ghostblood spy** cunningly begins combat by using **Gain Advantage** and **Coat Weapon** to enfeeble their opponent. They then attack, using **Disquiet** and **Uncanny Evasion** to keep themself alive. In social situations, they use their **Infiltrator** feature to keep their cover intact.

### Disquiet
*1 act*

The Ghostblood Spy manipulates their knowledge of an enemy they can influence to unnerve them. If the Ghostblood Spy knows one or more of the target's goals, their purpose, or their obstacle, the target loses `1d4` focus; otherwise, the target loses 1 focus.

### Coat Weapon
*1 fre*

The Ghostblood Spy coats either their Dagger or Shortbow in poison. The next enemy hit by an attack with the coated weapon must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`* or take `2d6 Vital average` and gain a disadvantage on all tests for 1 minute.

### Uncanny Evasion
*1 rea*

Before the Ghostblood Spy is hit by a weapon attack made by an enemy they can sense within 60 feet of them, they take evasive action. The attacker must spend 2 focus or the hit becomes an miss.

### Inured to Poison
*1*

The Ghostblood Spy gains an advantage on tests to resist the effects of poison. Additionally, before the Ghostblood Spy takes vital damage, they reduce it by 2.

### Strike: Dagger
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft. or **Range** 20/60 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 6 Keen average`

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Keen average`

### Skulking Approach
At the start of each scene, if the Ghostblood Spy is in cover or an area where their enemy’s primary sense is obscured, the Ghostblood Spy can make a *Test: `skill=stl`* (no action required) with an advantage against the Spiritual defense of each enemy who can sense them. Each enemy they succeed against loses track of them and can no longer sense them until the Ghostblood Spy attacks or takes another action that would expose them.

### Infiltrator
*1*

The Ghostblood Spy can spend 1 focus (no action required) to gain an advantage on a Deception, Insight, or Persuasion test.
