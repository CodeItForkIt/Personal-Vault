# Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal

**Tier:** 4  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 4, Intellect 5, Willpower 4, Awareness 5, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 65/65, Focus 6/6, Investiture 7/7  
**Skills:** AGI 5, LWP 4, STL 4, DIS 3, INM 4, LEA 4, PRC 3, DVS 4, ABR 4

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with the Dustbringer:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to prevent the Dustbringer from using their **Burst of Flame** and **Regenerate** actions until the end of the Dustbringer's next turn.

**Complication.** The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to remove the Investiture cost from the Dustbringer's **Decaying Touch** action and **Skate** free action until the end of the Dustbringer's next turn.

##### Tactics

A Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal carves a destructive path in and out of melee with their foes. They **Skate** to close the distance, attack with their **Shardblade**, then disengage. **Burst of Flame** is their primary tactic for groups of enemies, and **Decaying Touch** saps the strength of their bulkier foes. Ultimately, the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal devastates an area and is hard to pin down—and their **Eroding Escape** ensures that.

Becoming a full Knight Radiant by swearing the Fourth Ideal, a Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal is even more powerful and proficient with surgebinding, and has a swarm of gravitationspren they can summon as **Shardplate**.

### Invested Speed
While they have 1 Investiture or more, the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal's movement is increased by 10 ft.

### Slippery
*1*

While they have 1 Investiture or more, the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal can't be grazed and Reactive Strikes against them gain a disadvantage.

### Strike: Shardblade
*1 act*

**Attack** +; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 10 Spirit average`, and the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal can spend 1 focus to use the Disengage action as 0.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

The Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal recovers `1d6 + 4 Healing average`. They can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Radiant Shardplate
*1 spe*

The Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal is equipped with Shardplate. Before they take damage, they can expend a charge (no action required) to increase their deflect value against that damage by 10. While the armor has charges, all Invested tests against the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal gain a disadvantage, and the armor's deflect value applies to all damage types. After the armor runs out of charges, some of the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal's attributes and skills are reduced by 2, along with their related modifiers.

### Breakable Armor
*1*

After the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal is hit by an attack, the attacker can spend O to cause the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal's Shardplate to lose 1 charge.

### Inescapable Decay
*1 act*

**Attack** +8 vs. *Spiritual*; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `3d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `3d10 + 8 Spirit average`, and the target must succeed on an *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or their armor rusts and decays, permanently decreasing its deflect value by 1. Armor damaged in this way can be repaired with proper materials as a downtime activity.

### Skate
*1 fre*

The Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal moves up to their movement rate.

### Eroding Escape
*1 act*

The Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal targets themself or an ally in reach, ending one condition on them that is either applying the Immobilized condition, the Restrained condition, or a disadvantage on a physical test.

### Burst of Flame
*2 act*

The Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal emits a burst of fire that deals `1d12 Energy average` to each character within 20 feet of them and sets unattended objects in that area ablaze. Additionally, the Dustbringer of the Fourth Ideal can spend 1 focus or more to shower sparks onto that many enemies within 30 feet of them; each target must succeed on an *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or lose one 1 on their next turn.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 10 Keen average`
