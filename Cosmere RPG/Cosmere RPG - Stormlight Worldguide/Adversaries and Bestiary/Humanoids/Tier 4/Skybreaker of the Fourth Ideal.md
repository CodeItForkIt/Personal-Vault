# Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal

**Tier:** 4  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 3, Intellect 5, Willpower 4, Awareness 5, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 65/65, Focus 6/6, Investiture 7/7  
**Skills:** AGI 5, HWP 4, LWP 5, DED 4, DIS 4, INM 5, LOR 3, INS 4, LEA 5, PRC 3, GRV 4, DVS 3

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to prevent the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal from using their Offensive Gravitation, Distant Decay, and Regenerate actions until the end of the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal's next turn.

**Complication.** The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to have the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal use their Distant Decay action as r.

##### Tactics

A **Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal** is an aerial combatant who wields Gravitation to devastating effect. They begin combat with **Gravitational Support** to gain a tactical advantage, then use **Offensive Gravitation** and **Lashing Shot** against foes both near and far. They freely **Menace** combatants and they also add the Division surge to their repertoire, using **Inescapable Decay** to weaken their heavily armored foes.

Skybreakers dedicate themselves to pursuing justice. As such, they’re likely to prioritize foes whom they deem criminals, initially ignoring others whom they don’t feel the need to apprehend and punish.\

### Truthseeker
*1*

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal gains an advantage on Deduction and Insight tests made to ascertain truth.

### Lashing Shot (Impact)
*1 act*

**Attack** +9; **Range** 30 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d12 Impact average`;

**Hit** `2d12 + 9 Impact average`

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal touches a huge or smaller unattended object and launches it at the target.

### Lashing Shot (Keen)
*1 act*

**Attack** +9; **Range** 30 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d12 Keen average`;

**Hit** `2d12 + 9 Keen average`

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal touches a huge or smaller unattended object and launches it at the target.

### Offensive Gravitation
*1 act*

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal makes a Gravitation test against the Physical defense of a target within reach. On a success, the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal moves the target up to 40 feet in any direction, and the target becomes Restrained until the start of the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal's next turn. If the target hits an object or surface, they take `2d12 Impact average`. if the target passes within reach of an object that they aren't going to hit, they can make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`*, grabbing the object and stopping this forced movement on a success.

### Gravitational Support
*1 act*

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal gains a flying rate of 40 feet until the start of their next turn. At the start of each of their turns, they can spend 1 Investiture to maintain this effect for an additional round.

Alternatively, they can infuse an ally with a flying rate of 40 feet until the end of the ally's next turn. When the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal does so, they can spend additional Investiture to extend this effect for a number of rounds equal to the Investiture spent.

### Menace
*1 rea*

After the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal is hit with an attack, they make an Intimidation test against the attacker's Cognitive defense. On a success, the attacker loses 1 focus.

### Radiant Shardplate
*1*

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal is equipped with Shardplate. Before they take damage, they can expend a charge (no action required) to increase their deflect value against that damage by 10. While the armor has charges, all Invested tests against the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal gain a disadvantage, and the armor's deflect value applies to all damage types. After the armor runs out of charges, some of the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal's attributes and skills are reduced by 2, along with their related modifiers.

### Breakable Armor
*1*

After the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal is hit by an attack, the attacker can spend O to cause the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal's Shardplate to lose 1 charge.

### Inescapable Decay
*2 act*

**Attack** +8; **Range** 30 ft; **Targets** one who is touching the same surface as the Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal;

**Graze** `4d8 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `4d8 + 8 Spirit average`, and the target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or their armor rusts and decays, permanently decreasing its deflect value by 1. Armor damaged in this way can be repaired with proper materials as a downtime activity.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

The Skybreaker of the Fourth Ideal recovers `1d6 + 4 Healing average`. They can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Strike: Shardblade
*1 act*

**Attack** +9; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 9 Spirit average`
