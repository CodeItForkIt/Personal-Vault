# Brightlord

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 4, Intellect 1, Willpower 3, Awareness 2, Presence 4  
**Resources:** Health 38/38, Focus 5/5, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 3, LWP 3, DED 3, DIS 3, INM 4, INS 3, LEA 3, PRS 2

##### Tactics

In battle, the Brightlord is typically found flanked by [[Adversaries and Bestiary/Humanoids/Tier 1/Spear Infantry|Spear Infantry]] and [[Adversaries and Bestiary/Humanoids/Tier 1/Archer|Archers]]—and if they're important enough, occasionally a [[Adversaries and Bestiary/Humanoids/Tier 2/Duelist Shardbearer|Duelist Shardbearer]] or [[Adversaries and Bestiary/Humanoids/Tier 3/Elite Shardbearer|Elite Shardbearer]]. Brightlords use **Influential Instruction** and **Inspiring Leadership** to strengthen and protect their troops, as well as their **Diversion** to cut down significant threats. When they must engage, they swiftly disarm with their **Sidesword**.

### Inspiring Leadership
When the Brightlord successfully uses the Gain Advantage action, they can choose one ally they can influence. That ally also gains an advantage on the next test they make against the Brightlord's target.

### Influential Instructions
*1 act*

The Brightlord spends 1 focus or more to influence that many allies. Each target can choose one of their defenses to increase by 2 until the end of the Brightlord's next turn.

### Diversion
*2 act*

The Brightlord distracts one enemy they can influence. The target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=dis dc=14`* or lose `1d4` focus and remove the Determined and Focused conditions, if they have them.

### Bulwark of Discipline
When an enemy targets the Brightlord or the Brightlord's ally with an effect that causes the target to lose focus, the target can reduce the amount of focus lost by `1d4`.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 7 Keen average`, and the Brightlord can spend 2 focus to attempt to disarm the target. The target must succeed on an *Test: `skill=ath dc=15`* or drop their weapon and have it flung up to 10 feet away in any direction.
