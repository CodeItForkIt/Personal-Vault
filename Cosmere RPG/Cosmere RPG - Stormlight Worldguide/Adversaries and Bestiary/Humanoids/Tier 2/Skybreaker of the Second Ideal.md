# Skybreaker of the Second Ideal

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 2, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 40/40, Focus 5/5, Investiture 6/6  
**Skills:** AGI 3, HWP 3, LWP 3, DED 3, DIS 3, INM 3, LOR 2, INS 3, PRC 2, GRV 2

##### Tactics

A **Skybreaker of the Second Ideal** is an aerial combatant who wields Gravitation to devastating effect. They begin combat with **Gravitational Support** to gain a tactical advantage, then use **Offensive Gravitation** and **Lashing Shot** against foes both near and far. They freely **Menace** combatants.

Skybreakers dedicate themselves to pursuing justice. As such, they’re likely to prioritize foes whom they deem criminals, initially ignoring others whom they don’t feel the need to apprehend and punish.

### Truthseeker
*1*

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal gains an advantage on Deduction and Insight tests made to ascertain truth.

### Lashing Shot (Impact)
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Range** 30 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `2d8 + 6 Impact average`

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal touches a medium or smaller unattended object and launches it at the target.

### Lashing Shot (Keen)
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Range** 30 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `2d8 + 6 Keen average`

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal touches a medium or smaller unattended object and launches it at the target.

### Offensive Gravitation
*1 act*

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal makes a Gravitation test against the Physical defense of a target within reach. On a success, the Skybreaker of the Second Ideal moves the target up to 25 feet in any direction, and the target becomes Restrained until the start of the Skybreaker of the Second Ideal's next turn. If the target hits an object or surface, they take `2d8 Impact average`. if the target passes within reach of an object that they aren't going to hit, they can make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`*, grabbing the object and stopping this forced movement on a success.

### Gravitational Support
*1 act*

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal gains a flying rate of 25 feet until the start of their next turn. At the start of each of their turns, they can spend 1 Investiture to maintain this effect for an additional round.

Alternatively, they can infuse an ally with a flying rate of 25 feet until the end of the ally's next turn. When the Skybreaker of the Second Ideal does so, they can spend additional Investiture to extend this effect for a number of rounds equal to the Investiture spent.

### Menace
*1 rea*

After the Skybreaker of the Second Ideal is hit with an attack, they make an Intimidation test against the attacker's Cognitive defense. On a success, the attacker loses 1 focus.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

The Skybreaker of the Second Ideal recovers `1d6 + 2 Healing average`. They can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.
