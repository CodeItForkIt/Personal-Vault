# Nightform Regal

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 4, Willpower 2, Awareness 5, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 32/32, Focus 4/4, Investiture 7/7  
**Skills:** LWP 3, STL 3, DED 4, LOR 4, INS 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

A **nightform Regal** is a wily and often-unpredictable enemy who can react to events before they happen. They prefer remaining far from their opponents, using their **Predict** action and their **Shortbow** to harry their enemies while aiding their allies in doing the same. Meanwhile, they use **Intervening Premonition** to disrupt enemy maneuvers and tell their allies where and how to attack. When an enemy bears down on them, the nightform uses **Glimpses of the Future** to deal devastating blows.

### Conflict of Fortune
*1*

Characters can't supernaturally view past or future events for which the Nightform Regal is present. Additionally, the Nightform Regal and each ally within 20 feet of them are immune to any unwanted effect that would force them to reroll or replace the result of their die rolls.

### Glimpses of the Future
*1 spe*

Once per round, after the Nightform Regal rolls the dice for a test, the Nightform Regal can spend 1 Investiture or more to reroll that number of dice.

### Predict
*1 act*

The Nightform Regal makes a Deduction test against the Cognitive defense of a target they can sense, gaining an advantage if the target has already acted this round. On a success, the Nightform Regal and each ally within 20 feet of them gain an advantage on Reactive Strikes against the target until the end of the target's next turn.

### Intervening Premonition
*1 rea*

When a character the Nightform Regal can sense makes a test, but before the test is resolved, the Nightform Regal can roll a `1d20`, then if they wish, choose one d20 the character rolled for that test and replace it with the number the Nightform Regal rolled.

### Strike: Shortbow
