# Ghostblood Enforcer

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 3, Intellect 2, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 4  
**Resources:** Health 42/42, Focus 5/5, Investiture 6/6  
**Skills:** ATH 2, HWP 3, LWP 3, INM 3, DEC 1, PRC 3

##### Tactics

A **Ghostblood enforcer** begins combat by striking with their **Dueling Cane**; if they succeed in knocking their target Prone, their **Ruthless** feature lets them make an **Obsidian Dagger** strike for additional damage. They use **Intimidate** on foes they perceive as threatening, and if they foresee a tough scrap ahead of them, they might **Burn Pewter** at the start of combat.

### Ruthless
*1*

After the Ghostblood Enforcer knocks an enemy Prone, they can make an additional Strike against that target as 0 on that turn (even if they've already used that Strike action).

### Burn Pewter
*2 act*

The Ghostblood Enforcer uses the Invested arts of another world to gain Enhanced [Strength +2], Enhanced [Speed +2], and a deflect value of 2 until the start of their next turn. At the start of each of their turns, the Ghostblood Enforcer can spend 1 Investiture to maintain this effect for an additional round.

### Intimidate
*2 act*

The Ghostblood Enforcer makes an opposed *Test: `skill=inm`* against the Discipline of a target they can influence. If the Ghostblood Enforcer succeeds, the target loses 1 focus and is no longer Focused.

### Inured to Poison
*1*

The Ghostblood Enforcer gains an advantage on tests to resist the effects of poison. Additionally, before the Ghostblood Enforcer takes vital damage, they reduce it by 2.

### Strike: Obsidian Dagger
*1 act*

**Attack** +8; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 8 Keen average`, and if the enforcer had at least one advantage on the attack, it deals an extra `2d4 Keen average`. On a Complication, this weapon breaks.

### Strike: Dueling Cane
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 8 Impact average`, and the Ghostblood Enforcer can spend 1 focus to knock the target Prone.
