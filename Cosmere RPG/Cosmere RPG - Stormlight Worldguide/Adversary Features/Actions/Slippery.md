After an enemy ends a Move action within 5 feet of the Actor Name, the Actor Name can use the Disengage action as r. If the Actor Name ends this movement in cover or in an area where an enemy's primary sense is obscured, enemies affected by that cover or obscured senses gain a disadvantage on attacks against the Actor Name until the end of the Actor Name's next turn.
