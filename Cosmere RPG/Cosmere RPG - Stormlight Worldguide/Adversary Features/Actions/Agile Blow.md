Before an enemy the Actor Name can sense within 30 feet of them spends focus, the Actor Name moves within 5 feet of that enemy and uses their Strike: Claws action against that target. On a hit, the attack resolves as usual. On a graze, instead of the attack's usual effects, increase the focus cost of the target's action by 1. The target must pay the increased cost or choose a different action.
