The Actor Name attempts to rifle through the belongings of a humanoid within 5 feet of them, making an opposed *Test: `skill=thv`* against the target's Perception. If the target is Disoriented, the target gains a disadvantage on their test. If the Actor Name succeeds, they can choose to steal up to 50 marks or one item the target isn't wielding or wearing.
