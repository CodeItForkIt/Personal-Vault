**The** Actor Name throws a boulder at an enemy they can sense within 120 feet of them. Thetarget and each other character within 15 feet of them must make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=19`*. On a failure, that character takes `4d10 Impact average`. On a success, they take `2d10 Impact average`.
