The Actor Name flips an enemy who is Restrained in their claw and slams them against the ground. The target must make a *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`*. On a success, the target takes `1d10 Impact average`. On a failure, the target takes `3d10 Impact average` and is knocked Prone.
