The Actor Name unleashes a trumpeting roar. Each character within 30 feet of them must make a *Test: `skill=dis dc=15`*. On a success, a character is Disoriented until the end of the Actor Name's next turn. On a failure, a character is Stunned and Disoriented until the end of the Actor Name's next turn. Actor Names and characters who can't hear are unaffected by this action.
