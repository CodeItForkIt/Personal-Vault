The Actor Name uses their palm to grind an enemy against the ground. The target must make a *Test: `skill=ath dc=19`*. On a failure, the target takes `4d12 Impact average` and is Stunned until theend of their next turn. On a success, the target takes `2d12 Impact average` and is Disoriented until the end of their next turn.
