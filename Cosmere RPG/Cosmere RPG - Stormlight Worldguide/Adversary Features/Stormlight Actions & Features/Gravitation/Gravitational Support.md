The Actor Name gains a flying rate of 25 feet until the start of their next turn. At the start of each of their turns, they can spend 1 Investiture to maintain this effect for an additional round.

Alternatively, they can infuse an ally with a flying rate of 25 feet until the end of the ally's next turn. When the Actor Name does so, they can spend additional Investiture to extend this effect for a number of rounds equal to the Investiture spent.
