The Actor Name makes a Gravitation test against the Physical defense of a target within reach. On a success, the Actor Name moves the target up to 25 feet in any direction, and the target becomes Restrained until the start of the Actor Name's nextturn. If the target hits an object or surface, they take `2d8 Impact average`. if the target passes within reach of an object that they aren't going to hit, they can make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`*, grabbing the object and stopping this forced movement on a success.
