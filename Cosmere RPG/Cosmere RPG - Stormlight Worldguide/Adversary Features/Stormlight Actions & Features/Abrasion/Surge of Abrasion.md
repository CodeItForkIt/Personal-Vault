The Actor Name chooses a Large (10-foot) area or object within 30 feet of them to alter the friction of.

Depending on the nature of the alteration, this can cause an effect in that area such as making the ground into difficult terrain, making the ground slick to knock enemies Prone, or making a foe's non-Invested weapon nearly impossible to hold. If the effect would apply a condition or cause a character to drop something, they can avoid it with a successful *Test: `skill=agi dc=15`* or *Test: `skill=ath dc=15`*.
