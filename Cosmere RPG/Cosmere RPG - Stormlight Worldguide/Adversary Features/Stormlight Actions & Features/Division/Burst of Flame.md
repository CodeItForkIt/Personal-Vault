The Actor Name emits a burst of fire that deals `1d8 Energy average` to each character within 10 feet of them and sets unattended objects in that area ablaze. Additionally, the Actor Name can spend 1 focus or more to shower sparks onto that many enemies within 30 feet of them; each target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=agi dc=14`* or lose one 1 on their next turn.
