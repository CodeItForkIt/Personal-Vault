The Actor Name can move through solid surfaces as if moving along the ground, ignoring difficult terrain. While fully submerged, the Actor Name must spend 1 focus each time they use an action other than the Move action or their Regenerate free action. The Actor Name can't use this feature on surfaces made of wood.
