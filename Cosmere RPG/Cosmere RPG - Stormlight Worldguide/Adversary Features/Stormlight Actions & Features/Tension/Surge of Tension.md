The Actor Name chooses a Gargantuan (20-foot) or smaller object made of soft materials within their reach, causing it to become rigid and tough.

Depending on the nature of the object being stiffened, this can cause an effect such as giving an ally a +4 bonus to Physical defense, causing an enemy to be Restrained by their own clothing, or creating an improvised weapon that can be wielded as a Tense Spear without spending focus. If the effect would apply a condition to a character, they can avoid it with a successful *Test: `skill=agi dc=19`* or *Test: `skill=ath dc=19`*.
