**Attack** +14; **Reach** 20 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d12 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d12 + 18 Keen average`, and if the target is Huge or smaller, the Actor Name can spend 1 focus to pull the target any number of feet closer to them.

Alternatively, if the target is within 10 feet of the Actor Name when the attack hits, the Actor Name can spend 1 Investiture to make the target Restrained until the end of the target's next turn. While a character is Restrained in this way, the Actor Name can't attack with their Whip or Tense Spear.
