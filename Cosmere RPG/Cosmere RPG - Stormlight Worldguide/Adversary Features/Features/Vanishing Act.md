At the end of the Actor Name's turn, if they're in cover or an area where their enemy’s primary sense is obscured, the Actor Name can make a Stealth test (no action required) with an advantage against the Spiritual defense of each enemy who can sense them. Each enemy they succeed against loses track of them and can no longer sense them until the Actor Name attacks or takes another action that would expose them.
