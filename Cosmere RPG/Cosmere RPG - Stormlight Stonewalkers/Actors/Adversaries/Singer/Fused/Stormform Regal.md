# Stormform Regal

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 4, Intellect 2, Willpower 4, Awareness 1, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 40/40, Focus 6/6, Investiture 5/5  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, LWP 2, DIS 2, INM 3, PRC 3

##### Tactics

A **stormform Regal** is a cunning foe, one specialized in closing distances and defending themself. The Regal uses Storm Leap to jump to a weak-looking foe, hoping to goad them into taking damage from Electrical Charge. Meanwhile, the Regal alternates between using their Shortspear and Bolt of Lightning to strike injured enemies.

### Water Weakness
*1*

While the Stormform Regal is drenched in water, they can't use their Bolt of Lightning or Electrical Charge.

### Barbed Carapace
*1*

While the Stormform Regal is Restrained by the Grapple action or a similar effect, the character restraining the Stormform Regal is Afflicted [`1d8 Keen`]

### Bolt of Lightning
*2 act*

Attack +7; **Range** 60 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d8 Energy average`;

**Hit** `2d8 + 7 Energy average`;

This attack gains a disadvantage unless the Stormform Regal spends 1 focus to ignore it.

### Storm Leap
*1 act*

The Stormform Regal jumps up to 60 feet. If they land within 5 feet of an enemy, the Stormform Regal can use the Gain Advantage action as 0.

### Electrical Charge
*1 fre*

After the Stormform Regal uses the Move action, they become electrically charged until the start of their next turn. While charged, they're immune to energy damage, and whenever the charged Stormform Regal is hit by an attack by an enemy within 5 feet of them, the attacker takes `1d8 Energy average`.

### Strike: Shortspear
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 6 Keen average`
