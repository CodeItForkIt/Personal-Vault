# Nimbleform Singer

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 3, Willpower 2, Awareness 3, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 20/20, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 2, LWP 2, STL 1, CRA 1, DED 2, LOR 1, PRC 2, SUR 2

##### Tactics

While **nimbleform singers** rarely fight, they’re experts at tracking creatures and surveying an area, and they can easily exploit an enemy’s weakness using their Chosen Target action.

### Deadly Speed
*1*

At the start of each scene, if the Nimbleform Singer isn't Surprised, they can take a fast turn before any other characters (this doesn't count as their turn this round).

### Keen Senses
*1*

The Nimbleform Singer can spend 1 focus to gain an advantage on a Perception test (no action required).

### Chosen Target
*2 act*

The Nimbleform Singer chooses an enemy they can sense within 120 feet of them. For 1 hour, when the Nimbleform Singer hits their chosen enemy with a weapon attack, they deal an extra `1d6` damage. In addition, the Nimbleform Singer gains an advantage on Survival tests to track that enemy.

`1d6 Impact average` extra for Mace strikes

`1d6 Keen average` extra for Shortbow strikes

### Strike: Mace
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Impact average`

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Keen average`
