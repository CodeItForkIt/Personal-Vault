# Shadowform Zealot

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 4, Intellect 1, Willpower 3, Awareness 2, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 22/22, Focus 5/5, Investiture 4/4  
**Skills:** AGI 1, LWP 2, STL 3, INM 2, PRC 2, SUR 2

A **Shadowform Zealot** is a slippery foe who takes advantage of tactical positioning. They move opponents into areas of shadow using Drag Through Shadows, then gather around to strike using their Shadow Claws. They wear down significant threats with Grieving Dirge, and if they need to position themselves or escape, Shadowform Zealots rely on Unbodied and Drag Through Shadows.

### Shadow Sense
*1*

The Shadowform Zealot can sense out to 40 ft. in darkness.

### Unbodied
*1*

All attacks against the Shadowform Zealot's Physical defense gain a disadvantage. Additionally, the Shadowform Zealot ignores difficult terrain, and they can move through any gaps with a diameter of 6 inches or greater.

### Drag Through Shadows
*1 act*

The Shadowform Zealot transports themself to a darkened area they can sense within 30 feet of them. This movement doesn't trigger Reactive Strikes.

When the Shadowform Zealot uses this action, they can also transport one character within 5 feet of them. If the target is unwilling, the Shadowform Zealot must first succeed on an *Test: `skill=agi defence=phy`*. A transported target appears in anunoccupied space of the Shadowform Zealot's choice within 5 feet of where the Shadowform Zealot transported to.

### Grieving Dirge
*1 act*

The Shadowform Zealot chooses a target they can influence within 30 feet of them, and makes an *Test: `skill=inm defence=spi`*. On a success, the target becomes Exhausted [-1].

### Manifest
*1 fre*

The Shadowform Zealot briefly coalesces into a more solid form. Until the beginning of their next turn, they can't benefit from their Unbodied feature, but they can use the Strike action even in brightly lit areas.

### Strike: Shadow Claws
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

The Shadowform Zealot can only use this action if they're in a darkened area or if they have used their Manifest free action this turn.

**Graze** `1d8 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 6 Spirit average`
