# Archer

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 1, Intellect 2, Willpower 1, Awareness 2, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 12/12, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 1  
**Skills:** AGI 2, HWP 2, LWP 2, DIS 2, PRC 2, SUR 1

##### Tactics

Soldiers are trained to be effective on a battlefield in their specialized roles. An **archer** fights at a distance. They use Immobilizing Shot to immediately take the upper hand, then follow up with Longbow.

### Take Aim
*1 fre*

On the Archer's first turn of each scene, if they aren't Surprised, they can use the Gain Advantage action as 0.

### Immobilizing Shot
*1 rea*

When an enemy the Archer can sense moves while the Archer is within 150 feet of them, the Archer makes a Longbow attack against them. On a hit, the target is also Immobilized until the end of the Archer's next turn.

### Strike: Knife
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 +3 Keen`

### Strike: Longbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Range** 150/600 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 4 Keen average`

### Minion
The Archer's attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.
