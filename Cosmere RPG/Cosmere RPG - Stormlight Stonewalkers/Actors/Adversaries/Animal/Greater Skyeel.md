# Greater Skyeel

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 3, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 12/12, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 2, STL 1, PRC 2, SUR 1

##### Tactics

A **skyeel** remains in flight and hidden until they choose a target, then the skyeel uses their Dive Bomb to bite and restrain their prey.

### Slippery
The Greater Skyeel doesn't trigger Reactive Strikes while Swimming.

### Dive Bomb
*2 act*

The Greater Skyeel flies up to 60 feet toward a target they can sense on the ground, then uses their **Bite** against the target. This attack gains an advantage. After the Greater Skyeel uses this action, they can’t fly again until the end of the scene.

### Constrict
*3 act*

The greater skyeel squeezes an enemy they have Restrained, dealing `3d6 + 5 Impact average`.

### Bite
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 4 Keen average`, and if the target is Medium or smaller, the Greater Skyeel can spend 1 focus to wrap their body around the target. If they do, the target becomes Restrained, the Greater Skyeel attaches to the target, and the skyeel can't use their Bite against a different target until they release the current one. As 1 , the Restrained target or a character within 5 feet of them can make a DC 12 Athletics test, forcing the Greater Skyeel to release the target on a success.

### Enhanced Senses
The Greater Skyeel gains an advantage on non-attack tests that rely on sight.
