# Juvenile Whitespine

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 0, Willpower 2, Awareness 3, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 42/42, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Senses:** 20 ft.  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, STL 3, INM 2, PRC 2, SUR 2

##### Tactics

Angry about being kept in captivity, a **Juvenile Whitespine** lashes out at anyone they can. They prefer to charge at distant, weak opponents to deal extra damage with their **Goring Tusks**, then follow with their **Claws** to leave prey bleeding out. A whitespine is quick to leave an enemy’s reach to charge yet another with their **Goring Tusks** for more extra damage. When a fight goes poorly, a Juvenile Whitespine uses **Pheromone Release**, stupefying their opponents so the Juvenile Whitespine can retreat or overwhelm them.

### Goring Tusks
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 5 Impact average`.

If the Juvenile Whitespine hits with this attack immediately after moving at least 15 feet, the target takes an extra `1d8 Impact average` and must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=12`* or be knocked Prone.

### Pheromone Release
*2 act*

The Juvenile Whitespine releases foul pheromones. Each non-whitespine character within 10 feet of them must succeed on a *Test: `skill=dis dc=13`* or become Stunned until the end of the Juvenile Whitespine's next turn.

### Spined Carapace
*1*

After an enemy within 5 feet of the Juvenile Whitespine grazes them with a weapon attack, that enemy takes `1d6 Keen average`.

### Strike: Claws
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Keen average`, and the Juvenile Whitespine can spend 1 focus to make the target Afflicted [`1d4 Vital`] until the target regains at least 1 health.

### Enhanced Senses
The Juvenile Whitespine gains an advantage on non-attack tests that rely on smell.
