# Shellmite

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** small  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 1, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 2, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 9/9, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** STL 2, PRC 2

A **shellmite** uses their Rugged Camouflage to hide among common rocks. When unlucky travelers walk into a nest of shellmites, these crustaceans strike quickly with a Pincer, gaining an advantage on their first attack. Shellmites then use their Feeding Frenzy to overwhelm intruders, tearing and ripping at clothing and armor with their strong claws, while sticking together to take advantage of their numbers.

Shellmites are reliant on surprise, so try placing multiple hidden shellmites throughout an area to keep PCs on their toes.\

### Rugged Camouflage
*1*

An undetected Shellmite gains an advantage on attack tests. While the Shellmite is motionless, they're almost indistinguishable from a normal rock, requiring a successful *Test: `skill=sur dc=14`* to identify them. This test gains a disadvantage when the Shellmite is in cover or in an area where the other character's primary sense is obscured.

### Feeding Frenzy
*2 act*

The Shellmite whips up a frenzy against an enemy within 5 feet of them. Each Shellmite within 5 feet of the target can use r to join the frenzy. The target takes `1d4 Keen average` per frenzied Shellmite.

### Strike: Pincer
*1 act*

**Attack** +2; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 2 Keen average`

### Minion
The Shellmite's attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.
