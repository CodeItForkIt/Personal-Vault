# Khornak

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 2, Intellect 0, Willpower 2, Awareness 2, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 26/26, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 2, STL 2, PRC 1, SUR 2

##### Tactics

A dangerous and deadly foe, a khornak first ensnares and tears into their prey with their Crushing Jaw, strengthened by their Terrifying Ambush. While the khornak restrains this enemy in their jaws, they repel other opponents with their Tail Sweep, and they Drag their prey away to feast. If their meal manages to escape, the khornak hunts foes down with Ruthless Predator.

### Terrifying Ambush
*1*

At the start of each scene, each enemy who can sense the Khornak must make a *Test: `skill=dis dc=14`* (no action required). On a failure, that enemy can't take a fast turn this round unless they spend 2 focus.

### Ruthless Predator
*1*

When the Khornak attacks and hits an enemy who hasn't taken a turn yet this round, the attack deals an extra `1d8` damage.

`1d8 Impact average` extra for tail or jaw strikes

### Drag
*1 act*

The Khornak moves up to 15 feet in any direction while dragging an enemy they have Restrained behind them.

### Strike: Crushing Jaw
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 5 Impact average`, and the target becomes Restrained by the Khornak's jaw while the Khornak remains within 5 feet of them. The Khornak can spend 1 focus to also make the target Afflicted [`1d4 Vital`] until the target regains at least 1 health. As 2, the target or a character who can reach them can make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=16`* or *Test: `skill=ath dc=16`*, ending the Restrained condition on a success.

While the Khornak is restraining this target, the Khornak can't make another Crushing Jaw attack.

### Tail Sweep
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 5 Impact average`, and the target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=15`* or be knocked Prone.
