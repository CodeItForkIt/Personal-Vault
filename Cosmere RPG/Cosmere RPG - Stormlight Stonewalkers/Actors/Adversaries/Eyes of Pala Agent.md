# Eyes of Pala Agent

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 2, Intellect 1, Willpower 2, Awareness 3, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 25/25, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 2, LWP 2, STL 2, DIS 2, INS 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

An **Eyes of Pala agent** uses their honed intuition to navigate the battlefield. They identify the largest threat and use Insightful Defense on that target to better avoid deadly attacks. By working together, agents who favor the shortbow and those who favor the sidesword can knock down opponents and deplete their focus. If needed, agents coordinate with their allies, using Quick Escape to retreat when a peer gives them an opening.

### Insightful Defense
*1 fre*

The Eyes of Pala Agent makes an **Insight** test against the Spiritual defense of a character they can sense within 60 feet of them. On a success, the target can’t gain advantage on attacks against the agent for 1 minute or until the Eyes of Pala Agent uses this action on another target.

### Exploit Opening
*1 rea*

After an enemy within 5 feet of the agent grazes them with an attack, the Eyes of Pala Agent makes a Reactive Strike (no action required) against that enemy as if they had voluntarily left the Eyes of Pala Agent’s reach.

### Quick Escape
*1 rea*

After an ally within 5 feet of the Eyes of Pala Agent hits a target with a melee attack, the Eyes of Pala Agent moves up to half their movement rate without triggering Reactive Strikes.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 4 Keen average`, and if the target is Prone, they lose 1 focus.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 4 Keen average`, and the Eyes of Pala Agent can spend 1 focus to knock the target Prone.
