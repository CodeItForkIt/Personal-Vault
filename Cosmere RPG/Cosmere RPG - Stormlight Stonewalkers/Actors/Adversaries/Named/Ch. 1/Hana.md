# Hana

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 0, Speed 1, Intellect 1, Willpower 0, Awareness 1, Presence 0  
**Resources:** Health 10/10, Focus 2/2, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 1, CRA 1, INS 1, PRC 1

##### Tactics

In combat, citizens generally flee and seek safety unless their loved ones are in immediate danger.

When forced to fight, a Hana resorts to an Improvised Weapon, or they might Distract to aid a more capable ally. In conversations, they usually concede to those they consider more competent.

### Capable
*1*

The Hana has a utility expertise in one profession, and they gain an advantage on skill tests related to that profession.

### Distract
*1 rea*

When an ally makes a test against an enemy who is within reach of the Hana, the Hana grants the ally an advantage on that test.

### Minion
The Hana's attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.

### Strike: Improvised Weapon
*1 act*

**Attack** +1; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 1 Impact average`

On a Complication, this improvised weapon breaks.
