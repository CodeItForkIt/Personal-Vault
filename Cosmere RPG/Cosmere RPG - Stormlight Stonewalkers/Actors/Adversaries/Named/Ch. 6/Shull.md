# Shull

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 1, Intellect 1, Willpower 2, Awareness 2, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 24/24, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 2, HWP 1, LWP 2, DIS 3, INM 2, LEA 2, PRC 2

A **warform singer** is a brutally efficient combatant, especially when fighting alongside their warpair—another warform singer. Warpairs descend on a target together with their Warpair Coordination and, using their Martial Experience, they make multiple Axe attacks against a single foe to fell them. In a tight spot, the warform singer relies on their External Carapace for relief from an onslaught while their warpair continues fighting.

### External Carapace
*1*

The Shull can use their carapace like a shield, allowing them to use the Brace action.

### Martial Experience
*1*

The Shull can use their Strike action twice on their turn.

### Warpair Coordination
*1 rea*

After another Shull within 5 feet of this Shull takes the Move action, this Shull moves up to their movement rate, ending their movement within 5 feet of the other warform.

### Strike: Axe
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 4 Keen average`

The Shull can jump up to 10 feet before or after making this attack.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 3 Keen average`
