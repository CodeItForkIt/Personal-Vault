# Ryel

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 2, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 42/42, Focus 5/5, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 2, HWP 3, LWP 2, STL 3, DIS 3, INM 2, INS 3, PRC 3

##### Tactics

An Eyes of Pala watcher uses Insightful Defense either at the start of combat or amidst battle to gain the upper hand over an opponent. Exploitative Escape allows them to retreat after an enemy’s misstep, then they move to an opportune position to hit multiple foes with Fan of Blades.

### Insightful Defense
*1 fre*

The Ryel makes an **Insight** test against the Spiritual defense of a character they can sense within 60 feet of them. On a success, the target can’t gain advantage on attacks against the agent for 1 minute or until the Ryel uses this action on another target.

### Exploitative Escape
*1 rea*

After an enemy within 5 feet of the Ryel grazes them with an attack, the Ryel Strikes the attacker (no action required) then moves up to half their movement rate without triggering Reactive Strikes.

### Fan of Blades
*2 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one or more targets of the Ryel's choice;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 5 Keen average`, and the target becomes Afflicted `1d8 Vital` until they regain at least 1 health.

### Strike: Longsword
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 5 Keen average`, and the target becomes Slowed until the end of the Ryel's next turn.
