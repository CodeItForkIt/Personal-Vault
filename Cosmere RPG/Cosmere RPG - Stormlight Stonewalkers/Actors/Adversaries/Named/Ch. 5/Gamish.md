# Gamish

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 2, Intellect 1, Willpower 1, Awareness 2, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 14/14, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 2  
**Skills:** ATH 2, HWP 2, LWP 1, DIS 1, INM 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

A **spear infantry** topples enemies with their Shield Bash and then strikes with their Shortspear. Their Military Tactics can be used to pin down fleeing targets or bolster their fellow soldiers, depending on how the battle is going.

### Martial Drill
*1*

At the start of each scene, if the Gamish has their shield and isn't Surprised, they gain the benefits of the Brace action until the start of their first turn.

### Military Tactics
*1 spe*

Once per round, the Gamish can spend 1 additional focus to use the Aid or Reactive Strike reaction without using their r.

### Shield Bash
*1 act*

The Gamish makes an Athletics test against the target's Physical defense, knocking them Prone on a success.

### Strike: Shortspear
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 3 Keen average`, and if the target is Prone, they take an extra `1d8 Keen average`.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 3 Keen average`

### Minion
The Gamish's attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.
