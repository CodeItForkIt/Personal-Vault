# Vanzi

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 3, Willpower 2, Awareness 3, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 40/40, Focus 4/4, Investiture 5/5  
**Deflect:** 2  
**Skills:** AGI 3, LWP 3, STL 3, DIS 2, INM 2, LEA 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

A **Dustbringer of the Second Ideal** carves a destructive path in and out of melee with their foes. They Skate to close the distance, attack with their Sidesword, then disengage. Burst of Flame is their primary tactic for groups of enemies, and Decaying Touch saps the strength of their bulkier foes. Ultimately, the Dustbringer devastates an area and is hard to pin down—and their Eroding Escape ensures that.

### Decaying Touch
*1 act*

**Attack** +6 vs. *Spiritual*; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `3d8 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `3d8 + 6 Spirit average`, and the target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=agi dc=14`* or their armor rusts and decays, permanently decreasing its deflect value by 1. Armor damaged in this way can be repaired with proper materials as a downtime activity.

### Burst of Flame
*3 act*

The Vanzi emits a burst of fire that deals `1d8 Energy average` to each character within 10 feet of them and sets unattended objects in that area ablaze. Additionally, the Vanzi can spend 1 focus or more to shower sparks onto that many enemies within 30 feet of them; each target must succeed on a *Test: `skill=agi dc=14`* or lose one 1 on their next turn.

### Eroding Escape
*1 act*

The Vanzi targets themself or an ally in reach, ending one condition on them that is either applying the Immobilized condition, the Restrained condition, or a disadvantage on a physical test.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

The Vanzi recovers `1d6 + 2 Healing average`. They can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Skate
*1 fre*

The Vanzi moves up to their movement rate in a straight line.

### Invested Speed
While they have 1 Investiture or more, the Vanzi's movement is increased by 10 ft.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Keen average`, and the Vanzi can spend 1 focus to use the Disengage action as 0.

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Range** 80/320 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Keen average`
