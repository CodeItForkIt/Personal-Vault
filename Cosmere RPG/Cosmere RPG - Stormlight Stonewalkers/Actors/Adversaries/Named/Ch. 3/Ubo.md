# Ubo

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 1, Intellect 1, Willpower 0, Awareness 2, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 11/11, Focus 2/2, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 1  
**Skills:** AGI 2, ATH 2, LWP 1, DIS 1, INM 3, PRC 1, SUR 1

##### Tactics

Ubo is a simple but efficient attacker. They attack with their Shortbow from a distance, chipping away at an unsuspecting caravanner's health as they move in. Once within range, Ubo charges their target to gain Momentum, then ruthlessly debilitates them with their Mace}. After overcoming the civilians (and usually ending their lives), Ubo makes off with their ill-gotten gains.

### Minion
The Ubo's attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.

### Momentum
If the Ubo moves at least 10 feet in a straight line toward a target then makes a Mace attack against them on that turn, the Ubo gains an advantage on that attack.

### Trip
*1 act*

The Ubo makes an *Test: `skill=ath`* against a target within 5 feet of them, opposed by the target's choice of Athletics or Agility. If the Ubo succeeds on this test, the target is knocked Prone.

### Strike: Mace
*1 act*

**Attack** +2; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 2 Impact average`

### Strike: Shortbow
*1 act*

**Attack** +2; **Range** 80/120 ft; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 2 Keen average`
