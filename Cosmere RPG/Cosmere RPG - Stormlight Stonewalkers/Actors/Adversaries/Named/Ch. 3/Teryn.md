# Teryn

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 2, Intellect 1, Willpower 3, Awareness 2, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 38/38, Focus 5/5, Investiture 0  
**Deflect:** 1  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 1, HWP 3, LWP 2, DIS 1, INM 1, PRC 3, SUR 1

##### Tactics

A **spearmaster** is a skilled and careful combatant, taking slow turns so they can make multiple attacks. They spend focus to knock targets Prone with their Longspear, then prioritize attacking Prone targets to take advantage of Merciless. If an enemy gets too close, the spearmaster tries to Kick them away and line up a Run Through attack. When they have an extra action, they spend it on Gain Advantage or Brace, depending on whether they find themself on the offensive or the defensive.

### Merciless
*1 act*

The Teryn gains an additional advantage on attacks against Prone targets.

### Defensive Flourish
*1*

The Teryn can wield their longspear defensively, allowing them to use the Brace action as if they had a shield.

### Kick
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 4 Keen average`, and if the target is Medium or smaller, the Teryn can spend 1 focus to push them 5 feet.

### Run Through
*2 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** up to two targets within 5 ft. of eachother;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 6 Keen average`.

### Strike: Longspear
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d8 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d8 + 6 Keen average`, and the Teryn can spend 1 focus to knock the target Prone.
