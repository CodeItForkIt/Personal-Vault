# Axies the Collector

**Tier:** 3  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 4, Intellect 6, Willpower 4, Awareness 6, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 60/60, Focus 6/6, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** AGI 2, STL 4, THV 4, DED 2, DIS 1, LOR 4, DEC 2, INS 4, PRC 4

##### Tactics

Despite his immortality, Axies the Collector is a scholar who prefers to avoid any kind of physical conflict. If he finds himself in the middle of a fight, he generally proves unhelpful. He's always happy to use his **Fun Fact** to share some arcane lore about spren, but his **Curse of Kind** trait means his words are likely to distract his allies.

### Immortal
*1*

Axies the Collector can't die due to old age, natural causes, or phyiscal injuries. If Axies the Collector is reduced to 0 health, he suffers no injuries and regains at the start of his next turn.

### Curse of Kind
*1*

Axies the Collector must raise the stakes when making a risky or difficult skill test, and he gains a disadvantage on all plot die rolls.

### Fun Fact
*1 act*

Axies the Collector chooses an ally he can influence within 20 feet of him, then makes a *Test: `skill=lor dc=15`* and raises the stakes. On a success, the chosen ally gains 2 focus. Whether the test succeeds or fails, on O, that ally's next test gains an advantage, and on c, that ally's next test gains a disadvantage.

### Shift Form
*2 act*

Axies the Collector shifts his features to conceal his identity. While disguised in this way, any tests made to see through Axies the Collector' shifted form gain a disadvantage. The disguise ends when Axies the Collector chooses or when he is reduced to 0 focus.

### Strike: Unarmed
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 3 Impact average`
