# Ylt the Truthwatcher

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 3, Willpower 3, Awareness 3, Presence 4  
**Resources:** Health 80/80, Focus 5/5, Investiture 6/6  
**Skills:** LWP 2, DED 3, DIS 3, INS 2, LEA 2, PRC 2, ILL 2, CHS 3, TSN 3, PRG 2

##### Third Ideal Actions

When Ylt the Truthwatcher swears the Third Ideal in [[Journal/07 - 07 What Was and What Could Be|Chapter 7]], he gains the Empowered condition, gaining an advantage on all tests and refilling his Investiture to maximum at the start of his fast turns. He additionally gains the following action:

**Offhand Strike**

##### Opportunities and Complications

The following options are available when an enemy gains an Opportunity or Complication during a scene with Ylt the Truthwatcher:

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to cause Ylt the Truthwatcher to burn too quickly through his Stormlight. Until the end of Ylt the Truthwatcher's next turn, double the Investiture cost of each Tension and Cohesion ability he uses.

**Complication.** When an attacker rolls a Complication while making a melee attack against Ylt the Truthwatcher with a non-special weapon, the GM can spend c to destroy that weapon with Ylt the Truthwatcher's Honorblade.

##### Tactics

Before the final battle in [[Journal/07 - 07 What Was and What Could Be|Chapter 7]], Ylt the Truthwatcher uses his **Illusory Flourish**, **Vaulting Stone**, and **Tension Parry** to stay out of harm's way. He can use **Vaulting Stone** to move close to weaker targets, or he can strike from afar with **Stone Spear**.

In melee, he attacks with the **Honorblade**, uses **Illusory Flourish** to disorient opponents, then retreats to a safe distance. If the tide of battle turns, he heals with **Regrowth**—but doesn't extend the same aid to his allies.

### Flowing Earth
*1 act*

After Ylt the Truthwatcher uses the Cohesion surge, he can immediately use Vaulting Stone on himself (no action or Investiture required), but it doesn't grant an advantage on his next attack.

### Taln's Honorblade
*1*

Ylt the Truthwatcher is bonded to the Honorblade of the Herald Taln. If he loses the weapon, he can summon it as **1**, and it reappears in his hand at the start of his next fast turn. While not wielding it, he can't use the Cohesion and Tension surges (including **Vaulting Stone**, **Stone Spear**, and **Tension Parry**).

### Stone Spear
*2 act*

**Cohesion Attack** +6; **Range** 60 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d8 Impact average`;

**Hit** `2d8 + 6 Impact average`, and Ylt the Truthwatcher can spend an Opportunity to knock the target Prone.

### Illusory Flourish
*1 act*

Ylt the Truthwatcher targets an enemy he has hit with a melee attack since the end of his last turn, making an *Test: `skill=ill defence=spi`*. On a success, the target is Disoriented until the end of their next turn.

### Odious Vision
*1 act*

Ylt the Truthwatcher surrounds himself with a vision in which he glows with golden Illumination. Reminded of his limitless potential, he regains `1d4` focus.

### Vaulting Stone
*1 act*

Ylt the Truthwatcher uses Cohesion to create a pillar of stone that propels him or a willing character within 15 feet of him into the air. That character lands in a space up to 15 feet away without triggering Reactive Strikes, and if their next action on this turn is a melee attack, that attack gains an advantage.

### Regrowth
*2 act*

Ylt the Truthwatcher restores to himself or another character he can touch.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

Ylt the Truthwatcher recovers . He can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Tension Parry
*1 rea*

Before Ylt the Truthwatcher is hit or grazed by an attack against Physical defense, he can infuse Tension into his clothing to increase his Physical defense by 2 until the start of his next turn, including against the triggering attack. If the attack hit, this increase can change it into a miss, and if the attack grazed, he ignores its effects.

### Offhand Strike
*1 act*

*Note: Ylt the Truthwatcher gains this action after swearing the Third Ideal in [[Journal/07 - 07 What Was and What Could Be|Chapter 7]].*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 5 Spirit average`

### Strike: Honorblade
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 5 Spirit average`

### Boss
Ylt the Truthwatcher can take both a fast turn and a slow turn each round. After an enemy finishes a turn, Ylt the Truthwatcher can spend 1 focus to immediately use an extra 1 or 0. Additionally, he can spend 1 focus on their turn to remove a condition from himself.
