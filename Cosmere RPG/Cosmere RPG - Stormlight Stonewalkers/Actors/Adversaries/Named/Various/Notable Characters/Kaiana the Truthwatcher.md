# Kaiana the Truthwatcher

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 1, Willpower 2, Awareness 3, Presence 3  
**Resources:** Health 34/34, Focus 4/4, Investiture 5/5  
**Skills:** AGI 2, LWP 2, DIS 2, MED 2, INS 2, LEA 1, PRC 2, ILL 1, PRG 2

##### Tactics

Kaiana the Truthwatcher is particularly skilled in Progression and uses it to support her allies: Her **Reflexive Growth** deters attackers, and when needed, she heals allies with **Regrowth**. Meanwhile, her **Distracting Illusion** can protect weaker or injured allies. When she isn't needed to react and protect (or when she runs out of Investiture), she uses her **Passionate Ally** to give allies an opening, and she uses her **Knife** to impede enemies.

### Passionate Ally
*1 spe*

When Kaiana the Truthwatcher successfully uses the Gain Advantage action, she can choose one ally she can influence within 60 feet of her. That ally also gains an advantage on the next test they make against Kaiana the Truthwatcher's target.

### Quick Escape
*1*

Once per scene, Kaiana the Truthwatcher can use her **Disorienting Flash** as **0**. After she does, she immediately gains **2** that she can only use on the Interact, Disengage, and Move actions.

### Distracting Illusion
*1 act*

Kaiana the Truthwatcher creates an illusory duplicate of herself or an ally she can sense within 30 feet of her. The illusion appears in that character's space and moves with them. Attacks against that character gain a disadvantage and can't graze. The illusion ends after an attack misses that character or at the end of the scene.

### Disorienting Flash
*2 act*

Kaiana the Truthwatcher projects light, making an *Test: `skill=ill`* against the Cognitive defense of each character within 5 feet of her. Each character she succeeds against becomes Disoriented until the end of their next turn.

### Regrowth
*2 act*

Kaiana the Truthwatcher restores to herself or another character she can touch.

### Regenerate
*1 fre*

Kaiana the Truthwatcher recovers . She can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Reflexive Growth
*1 rea*

Before Kaiana the Truthwatcher or an ally she can sense within 20 feet of her is hit by a melee attack, she tosses a seed from her pouch and makes a *Test: `skill=prg`* against the attacker's Physical defense. On a success, a plant rapidly grows around the target; they are Restrained (adding a disadvantage to their test, potentially changing the hit into a miss), and they can't make Reactive Strikes until the end of their next turn.

### Strike: Knife
*1 act*

**Attack** +5; **Reach** 5 ft. or **Range** 20/60 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 5 Keen average`, and Kaiana the Truthwatcher can spend 1 focus to make the target Slowed until the end of the target's next turn.

### Strike: Raysium Knife
*1 act*

*Note: Kaiana the Truthwatcher only gains this action if she is wielding the raysium knife in [[Journal/04 - 04 Into the Valley|Chapter 4]].*

Kaiana the Truthwatcher makes a **Knife** attack (no action required). On a hit, the target also loses 1 Investiture.
