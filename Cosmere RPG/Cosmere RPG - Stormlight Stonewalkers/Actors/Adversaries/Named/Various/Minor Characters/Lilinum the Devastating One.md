# Lilinum, the Devastating One

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 4, Intellect 6, Willpower 3, Awareness 4, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 41/41, Focus 5/5, Investiture 6/6  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, LWP 3, DIS 4, INM 4, PRC 2, SUR 2, DVS 3

## Opportunities and Complications

**Opportunity.** An enemy can spend O to disable Lilinum's Shroud of Dust feature and prevent her from using her Surge of Division and Regenerate actions until the end of Lilinum's next turn.

Complication. The GM can spend c from an enemy's test to have Lilinum use either her Claws or Shardblade action as r. When she does, she gains an advantage on that attack test.

##### Tactics

**Lilinum** fights with a playful recklessness, engaging foes for maximum destruction and chaos with little heed for her safety or that of her allies. She favors her Shardblade in combat, then follows it with her Devastating Touch or Claws. If a group of characters is within a structure, she uses her Surge of Division to collapse it on them.

### Shroud of Dust
*1*

When an enemy makes a ranged attack or other test against Lilinum, the Devastating One from at least 25 feet away, if that test relies on sight or smell, it gains a disadvantage.

### Instinctive Aggression
*1*

It costs Lilinum, the Devastating One no focus to use the Reactive Strike reaction.

### Surge of Division
*2 act*

The Lilinum, the Devastating One chooses a Large (10-foot) object within 30 feet of them that isn't being worn or held, or a portion of such an object, destroying it in a manner of their choosing.

Depending on the nature of the destruction, this can cause an effect such as obscuring the area with dust, opening a passage in a wall, triggering a rockslide that deals damage or applies the Restrained condition, or electrifying the area with a blast of light and heat that applies the Disoriented condition. Each character potentially affected by that destruction must make a *Test: `skill=agi dc=15`* or *Test: `skill=ath dc=15`*. On a failure, a character takes `3d8` damage of an appropriate type, or they otherwise suffer an effect that fits the destruction. On a success, a character takes no damage and suffers no other effect.

### Devastating Touch
*2 act*

**Attack** +9; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `3d6 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `3d6 + 9 Spirit average`

### Regenerate
Lilinum, the Devastating One recovers health. She can use this free action even while Unconscious or otherwise prevented from using actions.

### Strike: Shardblade
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `2d10 Spirit average`;

**Hit** `2d10 + 7 Spirit average`, and the Lilinum, the Devastating One can spend 1 focus to use the Disengage action as 0.

### Claws Followup
*1*

*Followup damage `1d6 Spirit` for **Strike: Claws**.*

### Demolish Weapon
*1 rea*

After Lilinum, the Devastating One is hit or grazed by a non-Invested weapon, she can turn that weapon to dust.

### Strike: Claws
*1 act*

**Attack** +7; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average` and `1d6 Spirit average`.

**Hit** `1d4 + 7 Keen average` and `1d6 Spirit average`.
