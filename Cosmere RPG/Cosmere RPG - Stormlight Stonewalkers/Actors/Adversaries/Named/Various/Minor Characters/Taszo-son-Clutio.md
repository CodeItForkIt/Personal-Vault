# Taszo-son-Clutio

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 0, Speed 3, Intellect 1, Willpower 2, Awareness 2, Presence 1  
**Resources:** Health 17/17, Focus 4/4, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** DED 1, DIS 2, LOR 1, INS 2, PRC 2, PRS 2

##### Tactics

Taszo-son-Clutio seeks to be helpful, though he is unaccustomed to fighting with his broken arm. He assists whoever is most threatened, spending his focus on **A Friend in Need** to aid allies in tough situations.

### A Friend in Need
*1 rea*

When an ally within 5 feet of Taszo-son-Clutio fails a test, Taszo-son-Clutio adds an Opportunity to the test result.

### Resolved
*1*

After Taszo-son-Clutio spends focus to resist a character's influence, that character loses 1 focus.

### Broken Arm
*1*

Taszo-son-Clutio can only use one hand.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 3 Keen average`
