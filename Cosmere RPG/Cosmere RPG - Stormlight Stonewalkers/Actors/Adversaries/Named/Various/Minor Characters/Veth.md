# Veth

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** medium  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 2, Intellect 1, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 2  
**Resources:** Health 40/40, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0  
**Skills:** ATH 1, LWP 1, STL 2, DIS 2, INS 2, PRC 2

##### Tactics

Veth uses **Insightful Defense** against the most obvious martial threat, then engages them in close combat. He spends focus quickly to gain extra turns, and he switches to secondary targets using **Exploit Advantage**. To benefit from his **Targeted Strikes**, he favors targets that he has an advantage against, such as Prone targets. If harried with ranged attacks, he uses **Intuitive Redirect** to make any missed attacks hit his enemies.

### Targeted Strikes
*1*

While Veth has an advantage on an attack, he ignores deflect when dealing damage.

### Exploit Advantage
*1 act*

Veth moves up to his movement rate. If he moves at least 10 feet and ends this movement within 5 feet of an enemy he hasn't attacked this round, this movement doesn't trigger Reactive Strikes, and he makes a *Test: `skill=prc defence=spi`*. On a success, Veth gains an advantage on his next attack against the target.

### Insightful Defense
*1 fre*

Veth chooses a target he can sense within 60 feet of him and makes an *Test: `skill=ins defence=spi`*. On a success, the target can't gain an advantage on attacks against Veth for 1 minute or until Veth uses this free action on another target.

### Intuitive Redirect
*1 rea*

Before an attack misses Veth, he can cause the attack to instead hit a target within 5 feet of him. This target can't be the character who attacked him.

### Strike: Sidesword
*1 act*

**Attack** +3; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 3 Keen average`

### Boss
Veth can take both a fast turn and a slow turn each round. After an enemy finishes a turn, Veth can spend 1 focus to immediately use an extra 1 or 0. Additionally, he can spend 1 focus on their turn to remove a condition from himself.
