# Acknowledgments

*We offer our sincere gratitude to all the people who dedicated hours of playtesting time to the* *Stonewalkers* *adventure. As the first major adventure for the* *Cosmere* *RPG, it required special care, attention, and iteration. With the help of these players and GMs, we were able to balance our desire to tell a canonical story with our need to deliver a rich gameplay experience with divergent choices. Thanks to all of these playtesters for being the original Stonewalkers!*
