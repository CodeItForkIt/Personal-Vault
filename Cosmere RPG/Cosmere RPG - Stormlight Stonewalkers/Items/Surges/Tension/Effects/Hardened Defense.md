When you infuse a soft material worn by you or a willing character, you can increase the wearer’s Physical defense by 2. Alternatively, before you infuse a material, you can wrap it around a character as part of that action, granting the same effect. A character’s Physical defense can’t benefit from more than one Tension surge at a time.
