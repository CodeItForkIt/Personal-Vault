**Damage** 1d4 keen; **Range** Melee; **Traits** Discreet, Unique; **Expert Traits** Offhand, Thrown [20/60];

This knife is forged of golden-white metal—raysium, the God Metal of Odium. Set in the knife’s pommel is a perfect emerald.

**Unique Traits.** The raysium knife has the following unique traits:

**Drain Investiture.** On a hit, the attack’s target loses 1 Investiture.

**Extract Soul.** This weapon can permanently kill a Cognitive Shadow in a physical body (such as a Herald or Fused). If such a character is reduced to 0 health by this weapon and suffers an injury, they immediately die and the raysium conducts their soul into the emerald. Their soul is captured in the gemstone, and for as long as their soul remains captured, they can’t be reborn in another body or escape to the Cognitive Realm. This process is traumatic enough to sever a Herald from the Oathpact. Any Cognitive Shadow captured with this knife will eventually fade, permanently destroying them, though it’s up to the GM how fast that captured soul fades. A captured soul is also destroyed if exposed to the corresponding anti-Light.
