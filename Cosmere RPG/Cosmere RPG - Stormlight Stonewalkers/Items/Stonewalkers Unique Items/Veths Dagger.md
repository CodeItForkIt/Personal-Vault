**Damage** 1d4 keen; **Range** Melee; **Traits** Discreet; **Expert Traits** Offhand, Thrown [20/60];

**Dagger.** The dagger, made from a spearhead, is etched with the same glyph that was on the bandit’s wrist and on the faces of Kaiana, Ylt, and Veth.

**Glyph.** Characters with the Religion expertise or who succeed on a DC 14 Lore test identify the glyph as a variant of “Palah,” one of the ten fundamental glyphs of Vorin theology. Palah is associated with emeralds, plant life, knowledge, and generosity.. Nobody in the caravans recognizes it.
