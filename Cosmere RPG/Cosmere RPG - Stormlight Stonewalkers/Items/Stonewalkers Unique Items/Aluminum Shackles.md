*Equipment, 3 lb.*

These shackles are forged from Soulcast aluminum, a silvery metal known to singers as ralkalest. Aluminum is magically inert and can block Investiture. As a result, the shackles can’t be cut by a Shardblade, and they can’t be affected by Surgebinding or other Invested abilities.

The shackles come with a key, and they function as a lighter, slightly less sturdy pair of manacles: they bind the wrists or ankles of a Medium or smaller character.

These manacles can be escaped with a successful *Test: `skill=agi dc=25`*, broken by succeeding on an *Test: `skill=ath dc=20`* or by dealing 10 damage to them, or lockpicked by succeeding on a *Test: `skill=thv dc=20`*.
