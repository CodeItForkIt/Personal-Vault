**Purchase Price** 200 mk; **Carrying Capacity** 1,500 lb.; **Travel Speed** 2 mph

Chulls are large, docile land crustaceans that resemble enormous hermit crabs with stone-like shells. A full-grown chull's shell is over twice the height of most adults. Depite their slow speed, chulls have been domesticated since before memory. Their endurance, strength, and ability to withstand highstorms make them the most popular mount for extended joyuneys.
