**Weapons:** One sidesword, greatsword, longsword, or longbow

**Armor:** None

**Equipment:** Alcohol (bottle of violet wine), fine clothing

**Spheres:** A pouch containing `4d20` marks

**Connection:** You’re supported by a patron of your noble house (see the [[Stormlight Handbook/09 - Ch 8 Goals and Rewards#Patrons|Patrons]] section of chapter 8). This affords you accommodations and a certain standard of living.
