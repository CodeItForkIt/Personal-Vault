**Price** 200 mk; **Weight** 0.2 lb.; **Charges** 3;

Emotion bracelets blink or change color in the presence of different emotions. The bracelet’s wearer can spend 1 charge as 0 to gain an advantage on an **Insight** test against a character within 10 feet of them.
