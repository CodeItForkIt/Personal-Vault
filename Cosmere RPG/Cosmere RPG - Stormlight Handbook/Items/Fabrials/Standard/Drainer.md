**Price** 1,000 mk; **Weight** 0.5 lb.; **Charges** 2;

Drainer fabrials can partially drain Stormlight from infused gems to alter the strength of their glow; this is primarily done for aesthetic purposes. You can use an Interact action to touch this fabrial to an Invested or infused object or character. If the fabrial isn't fully charged, the target loses 1 Investiture or charge, and this fabrial gains 1 charge. If the target is an unwilling character (or an object being worn or held by one), you mush succeed on an **Agility** test against that character's Physical defense; on a failure, you don't drain the target and can't try again for 1 minute. Once this fabrial is fully charged, it can be emptied by using a turning fork, by a Radiant drawing in its Investiture (and regaining an equal amount of Investiture), or by allowing the Investiture to fade over the course of 5 days.
