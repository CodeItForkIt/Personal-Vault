**Price** 750 mk; **Weight** 1 lb.; **Charges** 3;

The amplifying panrial increases a target’s sense of pain. It is a melee weapon that uses the **Light Weaponry** skill and deals 1d6 vital damage. On a hit, you can spend 1 charge to add your skill modifier to the damage one additional time.
