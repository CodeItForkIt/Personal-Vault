**Price** 1,500 mk; **Weight** 8 lb.; **Charges** 5;

Each repeller is attuned to a particular Essence or material, such as water, smoke, or another Essence. While activated, the fabrial weakly pushes away the attuned material within a specific radius. Both the material it attracts and the radius are determined when the repeller is created. If the repeller is heavier, it pushes the attuned material away from it at a rate of 1 foot per round, until the material is outside the repeller's radius. If the material is heavier, the repeller instead pushes itself away form the material. This fabrial can't affect anything being worn or carried by a character. A repller expends 1 charge for each hour it is active.
