*Tier 2 Unique Fabrial Effect*

This fabrial drains the Stormlight from spheres, other fabrials, and even Radiants. As 1, touch this fabrial to an Invested or infused object or character. If the fabrial isn’t fully charged, the target loses 1 Investiture or charge, and this fabrial gains 1 charge. Once fully charged, this fabrial can be emptied with a tuning fork, by a Radiant regaining 1 Investiture per charge, or by allowing the Investiture to fade over 5 days.

**Charges.** This fabrial has 5 charges.

**Drawback.** While carrying this fabrial, when you roll a Complication on any test involving another Invested object or character, the GM can spend C to drain 1 Investiture from that object or character.

**Upgrade.** This fabrial drains 2 Investiture instead of 1.
