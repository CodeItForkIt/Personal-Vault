*Tier 1 Unique Fabrial Effect*

This fabrial can cause flexible objects it touches to become rigid. As 1, make an Agility test against the Physical defense of a target within your reach. On a success, you can spend 1 or more charges to cause the target to become [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Slowed|Slowed]] and to gain a disadvantage on all physical tests for that many rounds.

**Charges.** This fabrial has 3 charges.

**Drawback.** While Slowed in this way, the target’s Physical defense increases by 2.

**Upgrade.** The target becomes [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Immobilized|Immobilized]] instead of [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Slowed|Slowed]].
