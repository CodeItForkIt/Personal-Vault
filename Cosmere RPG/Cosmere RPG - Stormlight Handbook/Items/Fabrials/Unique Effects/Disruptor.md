*Tier 3 Unique Fabrial Effect*

This fabrial disintegrates objects with a touch. As 1, spend 1 charge to touch this fabrial to a Small (2.5-foot) object. If that object is unattended, you destroy it. If it’s being held or carried by a character, you must make an Agility test against that character’s Physical defense, destroying the object on a success. You can’t use this fabrial on Invested or infused objects.

**Charges.** This fabrial has 4 charges.

**Drawback.** You can only target unattended objects.

**Upgrade.** You can also use this fabrial against characters. As 1, spend 2 charges to make a Discipline attack against the Spiritual defense of a target in reach. Roll 2d6 spirit damage.
