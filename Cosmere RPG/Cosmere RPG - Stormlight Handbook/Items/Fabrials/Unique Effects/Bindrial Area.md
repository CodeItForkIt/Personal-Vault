*Tier 1 Unique Fabrial Effect*

This fabrial causes everything nearby to stick to the ground. As 1, spend 1 charge to activate it, making all objects and characters (other than you) within 5 feet of it become [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Immobilized|Immobilized]]. Until deactivated, the fabrial expends 1 charge at the start of each of your turns.

**Charges.** This fabrial has 3 charges.

**Drawback.** You’re [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Immobilized|Immobilized]] by the fabrial as well.

**Upgrade.** The fabrial affects everything within 10 feet.
