*Tier 2 Unique Fabrial Effect*

This fabrial reduces your body’s friction. As 0, spend 1 charge to move up to your movement rate in a straight line.

**Charges.** This fabrial has 3 charges.

**Drawback.** You must make a DC 15 Agility test each time you use this fabrial. On a failure, fall [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Prone|Prone]] at the end of your movement.

**Upgrade.** You don’t have to move in a straight line when using this fabrial.
