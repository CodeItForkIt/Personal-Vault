**Price** 50 mk; **Weight** 0.5 lb;

A tuning fork can transfer Stormlight from one sphere or gemstone to another. While using a tuning fork, you can use spheres to recharge fabrials, [[Items/Armor/Shardplate|Shardplate]], and [[Items/Weapons/Half-Shard|half-shards]] as if the spheres were unencased gemstones.
