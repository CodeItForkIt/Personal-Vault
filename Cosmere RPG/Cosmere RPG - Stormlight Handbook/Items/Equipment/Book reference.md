**Price** 10–500 mk; **Weight** 1–5 lb.;

Each reference book contains information on a particular topic, such as a historical era, engineering, biology, architecture, or crafting a particular type of fabrial. When you have time to reference a book while making a related test using your **Intellect** attribute, you’re considered to have an expertise in the topic (see “Expertise Benefits” in chapter 3).
