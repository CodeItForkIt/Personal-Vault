**Price** 2 mk; **Weight** 0.01 lb;

Unlike spheres with gemstones encased in glass, unencased gems leak Stormlight quickly, going dun within several days depending on their cut. This permeability allows unencased gems to recharge fabrials and [[Items/Armor/Shardplate|Shardplate]]. Radiants can also breathe in Stormlight from unencased gems as if they were spheres, gaining the same benefit.
