At the start of your turn, you can use **Investiture** to heal your wounds; you can use this free action even if you’re **Unconscious** or otherwise prevented from taking actions. Spend 1 **Investiture** to recover health equal to 1d6 plus your current tier (1 for a tier one character, 2 for tier two, etc.).
