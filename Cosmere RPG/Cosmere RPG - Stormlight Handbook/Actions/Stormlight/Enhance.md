Spend 1 **Investiture** to become **Enhanced (Strength +1)** and **Enhanced (Speed +1)** until the end of your next turn. At the end of that turn and each of your turns thereafter, you can spend 1 **Investiture** (0) to maintain these conditions until the end of your next turn.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

(**Req:** Speak the Second Ideal) While your current **Investiture** is 1 or greater, you can use Enhance as 0, and you don’t need to spend Investiture to use this action or maintain its effect.
