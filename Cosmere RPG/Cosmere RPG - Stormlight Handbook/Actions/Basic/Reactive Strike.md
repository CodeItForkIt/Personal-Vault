As an enemy retreats, you use the opening to attack. When an enemy voluntarily leaves your reach, you can use this reaction and spend 1 focus to make a melee weapon attack or unarmed attack against the enemy’s Physical defense. You can’t use this reaction to attack a character who transports themself with [[Stormlight Handbook/07 - Ch 6 Surges|Transportation]] or who otherwise moves instantaneously.
