Through strength and skill, you forcibly maneuver your opponent. Make an Athletics test against the Physical defense of a character within your reach. On a success, you push or pull the target 5 feet horizontally. If you successfully **Shove** a character who has grappled you, it ends the[[Actions/Basic/Grapple| Grapple ]]effect.
