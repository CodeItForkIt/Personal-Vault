Before an enemy targets you with an attack, you can use this reaction and spend 1 focus to add a disadvantage to their attack test. You can’t use this reaction on area attacks or on those that target multiple characters, though you might be able to [[Actions/Basic/Avoid Danger|Avoid Danger]] and move out of the way.
