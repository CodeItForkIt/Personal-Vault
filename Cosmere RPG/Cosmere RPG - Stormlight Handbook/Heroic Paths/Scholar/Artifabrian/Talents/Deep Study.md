**Activation:** 8

*Driven by academic inquiry, you love nothing more than getting lost in an archive of books, scrolls, and dusty secrets.*

When you acquire this talent, your [[Heroic Paths/Scholar/Erudition|Erudition]] talent grants you one additional cultural or utility expertise, and it grants you two additional cognitive skills that aren’t surge skills. You can reassign these in the same way as the others you gained from that talent.
