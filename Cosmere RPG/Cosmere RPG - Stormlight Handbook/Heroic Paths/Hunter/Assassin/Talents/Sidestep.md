**Activation:** \*

*Sliding like a shadow, you flit past your foes’ strikes and evade blows that seem certain to connect.*

Gain an additional r at the start of combat and at the start of each of your turns, which you can use only to [[Actions/Basic/Dodge|Dodge]]. You can’t benefit from this talent while wearing armor with a deflect value of 2 or higher.
