**Activation:** 0

*Speaking quickly gets you out of trouble as often as it gets you into it, and you never miss a chance to get a word in edgewise.*

Spend 2 focus to gain 2, which you can only use to make spiritual skill tests via the following actions: [[Actions/Basic/Use A Skill|Use a Skill]], [[Actions/Basic/Gain Advantage|Gain Advantage]], or an action from any [[Heroic Paths/Agent/Agent|Agent]] talent.

During a conversation or endeavor, at the GM’s discretion, you can instead use both 2 to make an additional contribution for the round.
