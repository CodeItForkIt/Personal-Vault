**Activation:** 2

*You swiftly strike at your target’s throat, knees, or another vulnerable area. Before the target can cry out, they are silenced.*

Make an unarmed attack using Insight against the Cognitive defense of a character who is **Surprised**, does not sense you, or doesn't view you as a threat (such as if you are disguised). The target must be your size or smaller. Raise the stakes on this attack test. On a hit, the target also can’t communicate until the end of their next turn. Also on a hit, you can spend an Opportunity to [[Actions/Basic/Grapple|Grapple]] them as 0, automatically succeeding on your test; while **Restrained** by this grapple, they are also **Prone**.
