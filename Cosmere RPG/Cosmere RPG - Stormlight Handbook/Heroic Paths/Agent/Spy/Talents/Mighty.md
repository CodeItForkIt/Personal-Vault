**Activation:** 8

*You deliver your swings, punches, and kicks with practiced efficiency.*

When you hit with a weapon or unarmed attack, for each 1 you used on that attack’s action, increase the damage you deal by 1 + your tier. For example, if you’re level 6 and you make an attack that costs 2 , each 1 deals an extra 1 + 2 damage, for a total of an extra 6 damage.
