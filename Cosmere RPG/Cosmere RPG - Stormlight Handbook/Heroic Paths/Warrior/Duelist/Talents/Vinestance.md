**Activation:** 1

*You assume a flexible stance, allowing you to quickly switch between inexorable advances and rapid reactions to wear down your foe.*

Your Physical and Cognitive defenses increase by 1. Additionally, after you’re hit or grazed by a melee attack, you can use r to make an **Athletics** test against your attacker’s Cognitive defense. On a success, your target loses `1d4` focus, and you can push them horizontally up to 5 feet × your ranks in **Athletics**.
