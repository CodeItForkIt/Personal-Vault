**Activation:** 1

*You enter an aggressive, squared stance that favors swift flurries of decisive blows.*

While in this stance, you gain an advantage on **Intimidation** tests. Additionally, while there is exactly one enemy within your reach and none of your allies are within the reach of that enemy or you, you can use 0 to gain 1, which you can spend only to [[Actions/Basic/Gain Advantage|Gain Advantage]] or use an action that includes an attack test targeting that enemy.
