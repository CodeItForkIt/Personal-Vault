**Activation:** 1

*You adopt a mobile stance that maximizes your area of attack, delivering wide sweeps that slash across groups of foes.*

While in this stance, you gain an advantage on **Agility** tests. Additionally, while there are two or more enemies within your reach, you can use 0 to gain 1, which you can spend only to [[Actions/Basic/Disengage|Disengage]] or use an action that includes an attack test targeting one or more of those enemies.
