**Activation:** 2

*You vault aggressively above your foes and slam into them with your full weight—a leap made all the more terrifying by unyielding Shardplate.*

Spend 2 focus to leap up to a quarter of your movement rate, then make an unarmed attack against the Physical defense of each character of your choice within your reach. While wearing [[Items/Armor/Shardplate|Shardplate]], you gain an advantage on this test. Roll double the usual damage dice for this attack. On a hit, each target whose **Strength** score is lower than yours is also knocked **Prone**.
