**Activation:** 1

*You shift into a solid stance, delivering powerful blows without giving up ground to the enemy.*

While in this stance, increase your deflect value by 1. Additionally, before an enemy within your melee weapon’s reach spends one or more 1 to attack one of your allies not in Stonestance, that enemy must spend one additional 1.
