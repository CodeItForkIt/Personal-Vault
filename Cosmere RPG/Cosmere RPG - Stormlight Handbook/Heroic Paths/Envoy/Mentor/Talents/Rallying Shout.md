**Activation:** 8

*Your words cut through pain, fatigue, and even unconsciousness, helping your allies regain their feet at critical moments.*

When you use your [[Heroic Paths/Envoy/Rousing Presence|Rousing Presence]], you can target an **Unconscious** ally you can sense. When you do, the target is no longer **Unconscious**, and if they have 0 health, they roll their recovery die and recover health equal to the result + your ranks in **Leadership**.
