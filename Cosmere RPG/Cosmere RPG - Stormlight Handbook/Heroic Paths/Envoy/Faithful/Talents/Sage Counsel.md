**Activation:** \*

*You offer reassuring guidance alongside physical assistance, making your allies confident of your approval.*

After you use the [[Actions/Basic/Aid|Aid]] reaction on an ally, spend 1 focus to grant that ally the benefits of your [[Heroic Paths/Envoy/Rousing Presence|Rousing Presence]] (no action required).
