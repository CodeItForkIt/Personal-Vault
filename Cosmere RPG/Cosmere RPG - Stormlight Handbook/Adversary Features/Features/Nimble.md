The Actor Name can move through enemy spaces and can end their turn in the same space as a willing character. While occupying the same space as another character, the Actor Name can’t be [[Stormlight Handbook/11 - Ch 10 Combat#Step 3: Resolve Damage|Grazed]] and attacks against the Actor Name gain a [[Stormlight Handbook/01 - Introduction#Adding Additional Dice|Disadvantage]].
