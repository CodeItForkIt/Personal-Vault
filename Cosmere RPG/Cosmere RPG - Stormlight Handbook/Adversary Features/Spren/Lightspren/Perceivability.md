Unlike many Radiant spren, lightspren are visible and audible to other characters, so any spren interaction has the chance of revealing their (and likely your) position. However, lightspren can move exceptionally fast through the air, and they can read their Radiant’s thoughts within spren bond range, significantly reducing the risk of detection.
