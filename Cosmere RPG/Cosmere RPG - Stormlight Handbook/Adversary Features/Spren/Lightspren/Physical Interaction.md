Lightspren are mostly insubstantial in the Physical Realm, but they have more substance than most spren. Lightspren can’t pass through objects— and they can even interact with lightweight objects using their [[Adversary Features/Spren/Lightspren/Push Object|Push Object]] ability, despite not having hands or the ability to change their form.
