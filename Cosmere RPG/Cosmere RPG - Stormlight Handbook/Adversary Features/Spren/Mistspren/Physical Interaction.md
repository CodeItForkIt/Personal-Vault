Mistspren are nearly incorporeal in the Physical Realm. They can’t pass through objects, but they can easily flow through even the smallest gaps. Additionally, Enlightened mistspren can hide within the body of non-sapient characters and remain within them. If they do so with a cremling, the mistspren gains some control of it; however, at the GM’s discretion, you may need to spend some focus for the cremling to perform complex tasks or act against its instincts.
