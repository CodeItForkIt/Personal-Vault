A mistspren can make themself visible and audible to whomever they wish; otherwise, mistspren in the Physical Realm are generally invisible and inaudible to physical entities who don’t have a specific ability to perceive spren. If your mistspren is Enlightened, you can hold silent telepathic conversations with them while they’re in your spren bond range. If your mistspren isn’t Enlightened, you must speak aloud to them (until you reach the Third Ideal and manifest them as a Radiant Shardblade).
