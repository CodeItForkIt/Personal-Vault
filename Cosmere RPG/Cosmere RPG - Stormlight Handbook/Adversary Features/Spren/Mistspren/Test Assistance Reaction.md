Your spren uses their knowledge and experiences to grant you an advantage on a specific test. Typically, mistspren can help with approaching and engaging in new experiences, understanding others’ perspectives, **Persuasion** tests, and **Lore** tests related to topics they’re familiar with, though your spren may be able to help in additional ways.
