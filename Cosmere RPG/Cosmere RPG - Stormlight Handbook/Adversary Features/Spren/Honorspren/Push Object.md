Your honorspren slowly pushes an unattended, unsecured object that weighs no more than 0.1 pounds (but they aren’t strong enough to carry it). This movement must begin and end in your spren bond range. If your spren is trying to do so undetected, the GM might ask you to make a **Stealth** test against a DC they set. Once you speak the Third Ideal, your honorspren can push objects that weigh up to 1 pound.
