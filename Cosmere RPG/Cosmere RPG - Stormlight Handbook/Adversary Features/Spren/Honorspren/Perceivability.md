An honorspren can make themself visible and audible to whomever they wish; otherwise, honorspren in the Physical Realm are generally invisible and inaudible to physical entities who don’t have a specific ability to perceive spren. However, you can always see and hear your bonded spren, even in pitch darkness or gale-force winds. Your spren glows faintly, but they can’t illuminate their surroundings.
