Honorspren are mostly insubstantial in the Physical Realm, and at first, they have very little ability to affect their surroundings. They can’t pass through objects, but their malleable form enables them to pass through tight spaces like keyholes and closed storm shutters. Honorspren can interact with lightweight objects using their [[Adversary Features/Spren/Honorspren/Push Object|Push Object]] ability.
