Peakspren are nearly incorporeal in the Physical Realm. They can’t pass through most materials, but they can move through and occupy stone and other earthen materials as if in thin air. While within stone, their sight, hearing, and ability to speak audibly aren’t limited. When a peakspren leaves their rocky hiding place, they appear to be breaking out of the rock. The stone quickly melds back together and becomes whole, leaving no trace of the peakspren’s passage.
