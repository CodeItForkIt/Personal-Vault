Unlike many Radiant spren, peakspren are visible and audible to other characters, so any spren interaction has the chance of revealing their (and likely your) position. However, peakspren blend in well with rocky environments, and they can hide within stone (see “[[Adversary Features/Spren/Peakspren/Physical Interaction|Physical Interaction]]” below), making them extremely difficult to detect.
