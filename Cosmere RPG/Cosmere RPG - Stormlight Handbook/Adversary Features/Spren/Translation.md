For the next minute, your spren helps you communicate by translating spoken and signed Rosharan languages (including Rosharan Shadesmar). During this time, characters who know at least one Rosharan language can understand you, and you can understand any Rosharan language you don’t normally know. Alternatively, you can use this ability to have your spren read aloud one page of text that’s written in a Rosharan language you can’t read.
