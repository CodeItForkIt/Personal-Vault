A Cryptic’s language-learning abilities far surpass those of other spren. After your Cryptic spends 10 minutes listening to a spoken language or 1 hour deciphering a written one, they become permanently able to use the spren [[Adversary Features/Spren/Translation|Translation]] ability with that language, even if it isn’t Rosharan.
