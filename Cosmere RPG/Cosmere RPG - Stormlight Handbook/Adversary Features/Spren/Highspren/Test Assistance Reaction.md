Your spren uses their knowledge and experiences to grant you an advantage on a specific test. Typically, highspren can help with investigating crimes and determining guilt or innocent, **Intimidation** tests, and **Lore** tests related to topics they’re familiar with, though your spren may be able to help in additional ways.
