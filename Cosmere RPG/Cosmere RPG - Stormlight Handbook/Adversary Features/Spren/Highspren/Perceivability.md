A highspren can make themself visible and audible to whomever they wish; otherwise, highspren in the Physical Realm are generally invisible and inaudible to anyone (including other spren) without a specific ability to perceive spren. Unlike other spren, highspren can become invisible even to their bonded Skybreaker. They can also read their Radiant’s thoughts while within spren bond range, allowing for completely silent communication between the two.
