Your spren uses their knowledge and experiences to grant you an advantage on a specific test. Typically, inkspren can help you with **Deduction**, **Discipline**, and **Stealth** tests, along with **Lore** tests related to topics they’re familiar with, though your spren may be able to help in additional ways.
