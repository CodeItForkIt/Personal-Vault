**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 4 Keen average`, and if the target is Medium or smaller, the can spend 1 focus to knock the target Prone, then move up to 10 feet while dragging the target behind them.
