The Actor Name retracts their head and legs to defend themself until the start of their next turn. While retracted, the Actor Name's deflect value increases to 6 and they can use the Brace action as if they were in cover. At the start of each of their turns, the Actor Name can spend 1 focus to maintain this effect for an additional round.
