The Actor Name becomes Enhanced [Strength +1] and Enhanced [Speed +1] until the end of their next turn. This temporarily increases each of their Physical skills by 1, including their Bite action's attack and damage rolls. At the end of each of the Actor Name's turns, they can spend 1 Investiture to maintain this effect for an additional round.
