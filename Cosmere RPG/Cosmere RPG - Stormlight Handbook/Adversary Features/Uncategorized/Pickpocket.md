The Actor Name makes a *Test: `skill=thv defence=phy`* against the [[Stormlight Handbook/04 - Ch 3 Character Statistics|Physical defense]] of a character within 5 feet of them. On a success, the Actor Name secretly takes an object from the character that weighs less than 5 pounds and that the character isn’t wearing or holding. On a failure, the Actor Name doesn’t take an object, and the target notices the attempted theft unless the Actor Name spends an Opportunity or 1 focus to avoid notice.
