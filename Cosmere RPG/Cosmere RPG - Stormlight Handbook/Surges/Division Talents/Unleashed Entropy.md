**Activation:** \*

*You’ve grown so powerful that the ability to destroy everything around you is almost alarmingly easy.*

It costs you one fewer 1 to use **Division** (but not its talents). Additionally, your DCs on the [[Stormlight Handbook/17 - Appendix 2 Tables|Division Under Pressure]] table are reduced by 5.
