**Activation:** 1

*You infuse yourself with Cohesion to shift stone from your path as you move.*

Spend 1 Investiture to infuse yourself with **Cohesion**. For the duration, you can move through stone surfaces and objects as if moving through difficult terrain. You leave a 5-foot-radius tunnel in your wake through which others can follow. At the start of each of your turns, you can spend 1 Investiture as 0 to maintain this infusion.

If you create tunnels near the surface, an observant character might detect ripples from the diverted material, potentially sensing your presence.
