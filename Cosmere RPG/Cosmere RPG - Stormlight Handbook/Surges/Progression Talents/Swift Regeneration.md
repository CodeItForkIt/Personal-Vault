**Activation:** \*

*You can heal yourself with greater efficiency and make those infused with your Regrowth more resistant to injury.*

When you use [[Actions/Stormlight/Regenerate|Regenerate]], instead of recovering the normal amount of health, you can recover health equal to `1d6` + your **Progression** modifier. The size of this die increases with your ranks in **Progression**: at 3 ranks, roll `1d8` (instead of 1d6), and so on.

Additionally, while you have 1 Investiture or more, you and any character infused with your **Regrowth** can add your **Progression** modifier to injury rolls.
