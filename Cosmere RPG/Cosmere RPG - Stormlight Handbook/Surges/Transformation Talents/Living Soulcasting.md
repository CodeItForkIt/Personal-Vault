**Activation:** 2

*You’ve mastered the subtleties of affecting living flesh with Soulcasting.*

Spend 1 Investiture and make a melee **Transformation** attack against the Spiritual defense of a character or other living organism within your reach. Roll `3d4` spirit damage. The size of these damage dice increases with your ranks in **Transformation**; at 2 ranks, roll `3d6` (instead of 3d4), and so on.

If this reduces the target to 0 health, they die, and you transform their body into one of the essences of your choice.
