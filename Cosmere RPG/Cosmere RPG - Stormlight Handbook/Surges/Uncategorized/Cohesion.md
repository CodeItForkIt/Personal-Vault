**Radiant Orders:** Stoneward, Willshaper

**Activation:** 1

Infuse 1 Investiture or more into an unattended object or portion of a surface within your reach that is made of stone; to do so, you must have a hand free and touch the target. This infusion uses 1 Investiture each round, and for the duration, the stone becomes moldable and soft like clay. When you activate this surge, you can immediately form the stone into a rough shape as part of that action, but any elaborate molding requires more time (see [[Stormlight Handbook/07 - Ch 6 Surges#Shaping Stone|Shaping Stone]]).

This object or portion of the surface can’t exceed the surge size for your ranks in Cohesion (see the [[Stormlight Handbook/07 - Ch 6 Surges#Surge Scaling|Surge Scaling]] table).

You can’t use this surge on characters, Invested objects (like [[Items/Armor/Shardplate|Shardplate]]), or objects that have been infused with Stormlight (like infused spheres or objects affected by surges).
