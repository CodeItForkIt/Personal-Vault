**Activation:** \*

*You are poetry in motion, able to perform nearly any feat without slowing down.*

When you **Skate** or otherwise [[Actions/Basic/Move|Move]] while infused with **Abrasion**, you can interrupt your movement at one or more points, use other actions as if you’d finished your **Move**, then use your remaining movement as if you were still in the middle of that **Move** action.

Once per round, when you make a melee weapon attack after starting that **Move** but before finishing it, you can roll an extra `1d4` damage for that attack. The size of this die increases with your ranks in **Abrasion**: at 2 ranks, roll an extra `1d6` damage (instead of 1d4), and so on. However, if you add this damage die, the GM can spend c from the attack test to cause you to fall **Prone** and immediately end your **Move** action.
