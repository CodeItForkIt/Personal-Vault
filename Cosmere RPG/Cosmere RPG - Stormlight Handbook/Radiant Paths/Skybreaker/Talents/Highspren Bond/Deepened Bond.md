**Activation:** 8

*The strength of your Nahel bond now allows your spren to manifest more fully in the Physical Realm.*

Your spren bond range increases from 30 feet to 100 feet.

Additionally, when you spend focus to give your spren a task, it costs you 1 fewer focus (to a minimum cost of 1).
