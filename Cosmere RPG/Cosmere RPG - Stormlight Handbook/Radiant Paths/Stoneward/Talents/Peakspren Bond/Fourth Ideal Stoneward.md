**Activation:** \*

*You seek to become a full Knight Radiant by speaking the Fourth Ideal.*

You gain the goal [[Radiant Paths/Stoneward/Goals/Speak the Fourth Ideal|Speak the Fourth Ideal]]. After you complete this goal, you gain the following reward:

- You become **Empowered** until the end of that scene.

- You can call a swarm of bindspren as [[Items/Armor/Shardplate Radiant|Shardplate (Radiant)]] (see chapter 7).
