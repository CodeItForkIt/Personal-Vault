*You seek to advance your Nahel bond even further by speaking the Third Ideal.*

After you complete this goal, you gain the following reward:

- You become **Empowered** until the end of that scene.

- You can summon your spren as a [[Items/Weapons/Shardblade Radiant|Shardblade (Radiant)]].
