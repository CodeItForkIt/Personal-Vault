**Activation:** \*

*You begin the process of bonding a Cryptic, giving you some small ability to breathe in and use Stormlight. You might be aware of this nascent bond, or clueless—using the powers subconsciously—until you get closer to speaking the First Ideal.*

When you choose this talent, you gain access to **Investiture**, beginning with a maximum Investiture of 2 + your **Awareness** or **Presence** (whichever is higher). You can now use the [[Actions/Stormlight/Breathe Stormlight|Breathe Stormlight]], [[Actions/Stormlight/Enhance|Enhance]], and [[Actions/Stormlight/Regenerate|Regenerate]] actions.

Additionally, you gain the goal [[Radiant Paths/Lightweaver/Goals/Speak the First Ideal|Speak the First Ideal]]. After you complete this goal, you gain the following reward:

- You become **Empowered** until the end of that scene.

- You gain the [[Surges/Uncategorized/Illumination|Illumination]] and [[Surges/Uncategorized/Transformation|Transformation]] surges.

- You gain an additional skill on your character sheet for the above surges, starting with 1 rank in each.
