At the start of each session, roll two d20s and record both results. When an enemy or willing ally you can sense makes a test, you can use this reaction to replace the test’s d20 roll with one of your recorded numbers. You can replace that number after you see their die roll and after they apply any advantages, but you must do so before the effects of the test are resolved. You lose a recorded number when you use it to replace a d20 roll or when the session ends, whichever comes first.
