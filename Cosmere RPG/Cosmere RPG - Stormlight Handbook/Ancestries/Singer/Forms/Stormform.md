*Stormform is an elite battle form optimized for physical prowess and honed attacks. You’re covered in finesse-enhancing armored carapace that grows under your skin, poking through in ridges and spikes. You can manipulate and unleash powerful red lighting on your foes.*

You’ve bonded with a stormspren, a type of Voidspren. While in this form, your **Strength** increases by 1, your **Speed** increases by 1, and your deflect value increases by 1. This deflect does not stack with armor. If you wear armor over your carapace, you can choose which deflect value to use.

Additionally, you can use [[Ancestries/Singer/Forms/Actions/Unleash Lightning|Unleash Lightning]].
