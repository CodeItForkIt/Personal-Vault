# Chull

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** large  
**Attributes:** Strength 4, Speed 0, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 0  
**Defenses:** Physical 0, Cognitive 0, Spiritual 0  
**Resources:** Health 30/30, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0/0  
**Deflect:** 0  
**Speed:** 10 ft.  
**Senses:** 20 ft.  
**Skills:** ATH 2, PRC 2

### Plodding Pace
*1*

The Chull can only use the Move action once per turn.

### Defense Curl
*2 act*

The Chull retracts their head and legs to defend themself until the start of their next turn. While retracted, the Chull's deflect value increases to 6 and they can use the Brace action as if they were in cover. At the start of each of their turns, the Chull can spend 1 focus to maintain this effect for an additional round.

### Strike: Pincer
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 10 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Keen average`

### Beast of Burden
*1*

The Chull's carrying capacity is 1,500 lbs.
