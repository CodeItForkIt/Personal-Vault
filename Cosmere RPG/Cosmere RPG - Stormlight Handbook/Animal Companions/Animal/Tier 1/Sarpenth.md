# Sarpenth

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** small  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 1, Willpower 0, Awareness 3, Presence 1  
**Defenses:** Physical 0, Cognitive 0, Spiritual 0  
**Resources:** Health 10/10, Focus 2/2, Investiture 0/0  
**Deflect:** 0  
**Speed:** 0 ft.  
**Senses:** 40 ft.  
**Skills:** STL 2, THV 2, PRC 2, SUR 1

### Sleek
The Sarpenth counts as one size smaller when squeezing through smaller spaces.

### Skilled (Intimidation)
*1*

The Sarpenth gains an additional skill rank in one skill of your choice. This increases to two additional ranks when you reach tier 2, and to three additional ranks when you reach tier 3.

### Aquatic
Swimming doesn't cause the Sarpenth to become [[Stormlight Handbook/10 - Ch 9 Adventuring#Slowed|Slowed]].

### Nimble
The Sarpenth can move through enemy spaces and can end their turn in the same space as a willing character. While occupying the same space as another character, the Sarpenth can’t be [[Stormlight Handbook/11 - Ch 10 Combat#Step 3: Resolve Damage|Grazed]] and attacks against the Sarpenth gain a [[Stormlight Handbook/01 - Introduction#Adding Additional Dice|Disadvantage]].

### Pickpocket
*1 act*

The Sarpenth makes a [[Stormlight Handbook/04 - Ch 3 Character Statistics#Thievery (Speed)|Thievery]] test against the [[Stormlight Handbook/04 - Ch 3 Character Statistics|Physical defense]] of a character within 5 feet of them. On a success, the Sarpenth secretly takes an object from the character that weighs less than 5 pounds and that the character isn’t wearing or holding. On a failure, the Sarpenth doesn’t take an object, and the target notices the attempted theft unless the Sarpenth spends an Opportunity or 1 focus to avoid notice.

### Strike: Bite
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 4 Keen average`
