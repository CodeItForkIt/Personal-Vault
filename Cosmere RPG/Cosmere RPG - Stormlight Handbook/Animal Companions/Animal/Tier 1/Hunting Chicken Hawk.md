# Hunting Chicken (Hawk)

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** small  
**Attributes:** Strength 2, Speed 3, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 0  
**Defenses:** Physical 0, Cognitive 0, Spiritual 0  
**Resources:** Health 12/12, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0/0  
**Deflect:** 0  
**Speed:** 10 ft.  
**Senses:** 5 ft.  
**Skills:** AGI 1, STL 1, PRC 2, SUR 1

### Fleet
*1*

The Hunting Chicken (Hawk)'s fly speed increases by 10 feet.

### Skyborn
*1*

The Hunting Chicken (Hawk) doesn't trigger Reactive Strikes while flying.

### Dive Bomb
*2 act*

The Hunting Chicken (Hawk) flies up to 60 feet toward a target they can sense on the ground, then uses their **Bite** against the target. This attack gains an advantage. After the Hunting Chicken (Hawk) uses this action, they can’t fly again until the end of the scene, unless they spend 1 focus to do so.

### Minion
The Hunting Chicken (Hawk)'s attacks can't critically hit, and they're immediately defeated when they suffer an injury.

### Enhanced Senses (sight)
The Hunting Chicken (Hawk) gains an advantage on non-attack tests that rely on sight.

### Strike: Bite
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 4 Keen average`
