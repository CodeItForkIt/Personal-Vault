# Poison Cremling

**Tier:** 1  
**Size:** small  
**Attributes:** Strength 1, Speed 3, Intellect 1, Willpower 0, Awareness 3, Presence 1  
**Defenses:** Physical 0, Cognitive 0, Spiritual 0  
**Resources:** Health 10/10, Focus 2/2, Investiture 0/0  
**Deflect:** 0  
**Speed:** 0 ft.  
**Senses:** 40 ft.  
**Skills:** AGI 1, STL 2, THV 2, PRC 2, SUR 1

### Carapace
Increase the Poison Cremling’s deflect value by +2.

### Pickpocket
*1 act*

The Poison Cremling makes a *Test: `skill=thv defence=phy`* against the [[Stormlight Handbook/04 - Ch 3 Character Statistics|Physical defense]] of a character within 5 feet of them. On a success, the Poison Cremling secretly takes an object from the character that weighs less than 5 pounds and that the character isn’t wearing or holding. On a failure, the Poison Cremling doesn’t take an object, and the target notices the attempted theft unless the Poison Cremling spends an Opportunity or 1 focus to avoid notice.

### Nimble
The Poison Cremling can move through enemy spaces and can end their turn in the same space as a willing character. While occupying the same space as another character, the Poison Cremling can’t be [[Stormlight Handbook/11 - Ch 10 Combat#Step 3: Resolve Damage|Grazed]] and attacks against the Poison Cremling gain a [[Stormlight Handbook/01 - Introduction#Adding Additional Dice|Disadvantage]].

### Strike: Venomous Bite
*1 act*

**Attack** +4; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d4 Keen average`;

**Hit** `1d4 + 4 Keen average`

On a hit, the Poison Cremling can spend 1 focus to cause the target to become Afflicted `1d4 Vital average` until the target regains at least 1 health.
