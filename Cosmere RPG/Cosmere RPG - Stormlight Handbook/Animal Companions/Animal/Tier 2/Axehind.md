# Axehind

**Tier:** 2  
**Size:** large  
**Attributes:** Strength 3, Speed 4, Intellect 0, Willpower 1, Awareness 3, Presence 1  
**Defenses:** Physical 0, Cognitive 0, Spiritual 0  
**Resources:** Health 25/25, Focus 3/3, Investiture 0/0  
**Deflect:** 0  
**Speed:** 40 ft.  
**Senses:** 5 ft.  
**Skills:** AGI 3, ATH 3, PRC 2

### Jumper
The Axehind can jump twice as far as usual without making a test.

### Strike: Hooves
*1 act*

**Attack** +6; **Reach** 5 ft.; **Targets** one;

**Graze** `1d6 Impact average`;

**Hit** `1d6 + 6 Impact average`

### Beast of Burden
*1*

The Axehind's carrying capacity is 500 lbs.

### Charge
*2 act*

The Axehind moves up to their movement rate in a straight line without triggering Reactive Strikes. During this movement, the Axehind can pass through the spaces of Medium and smaller enemies, trampling them underfoot. Each Medium or smaller enemy whose space the horse enters must succeed on a *Test: `skill=ath dc=15`* or be knocked Prone and take `2d6 type=Impact average` impact damage.

### Carapace
The Axehind has a natural deflect value of 2.
