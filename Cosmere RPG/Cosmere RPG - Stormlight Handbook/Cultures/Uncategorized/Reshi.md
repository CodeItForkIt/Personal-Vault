The Reshi Isles are a series of misty islands and inhabitable Tai-na greatshell carapaces, each ruled by a monarch. The Tai-na islands roam the Reshi Sea, occasionally colliding in brief conflicts. Reshi people worship these greatshell islands as their gods. Tourism is common on the stationary islands.

Most Reshi have brown skin, black hair, and round faces. Unfamiliar with the horrors of devastating wars, they tend to fight fiercely with words but resolve disagreements quickly. The intonation of their language is sharp and spoken in quick bursts, making it difficult for foreigners to learn.

**Reshi Names**

Ashi, Avand, Calsi, Fashan, Hailor, Ilesh, Isna, Jersa, Lish, Naila, Neshor, Rifta, Sha, Tana, Trin, Vara, Wesh.

**Reshi Expertise**

As a character with the Reshi expertise, you know about the existence of Tai-na greatshells, spren deities, and tales of islands going to war. You’re familiar with the basic ecology of the Reshi Sea and the locations of major stationary islands. Choose how proficient you are in speaking and writing Reshi. You know which resources are unavailable in the Reshi Isles and which nations they are typically sourced from. Regardless of whether you choose to engage with the tradition yourself, you’re familiar with the Reshi inclination toward fierce but quickly resolved social conflicts.
