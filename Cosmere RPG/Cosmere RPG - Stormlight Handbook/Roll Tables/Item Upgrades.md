# Item Upgrades

*Roll: 1d6*

| Range | Result |
|---|---|
| 1 | You salvage enough materials from the scraps to make something else. The item you just crafted has no material cost. |
| 2 | If made or sold publicly, the item's superior craftsmanship earns you acclaim among discerning crafters and buyers. |
| 3 | Your item is far lighter than normal. Halve its weight. |
| 4 | The item is sturdy. It ignores Complication effects that might cause it to be damaged or break. |
| 5 | The item is incredibly beautiful and impresses everyone who sees it. |
| 6 | Your item has gained you recognition among scholars or ardents; you attract the attention of a potential patron (see "Rewards" in chapter 9). |
