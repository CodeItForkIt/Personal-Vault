They say to be your own ship 
Don’t let others change you 
To stay resolute 
But what if the ship you are is bad 
What if it has defective parts 
What if the mast is breaking 
What if the hull is cracking 
What if the wheel isn’t greased 
How can you be your own ship when your ship doesn’t work 
To be a better ship would be to not be your ship 
How far can you go before you aren’t you 
There’s a difference between stasis and growth 
Between adaption and assimilation 
You can be your own ship while changing 
You can be a better ship 
A ship of Theseus 
For Theseus’ ship had all its parts replaced as they rotted and decayed 
But it was still the same ship 
So go out 
Grow 
Adapt 
And be yourself 
While still being better 
For a better ship may not be the same ship 
But it is still you
