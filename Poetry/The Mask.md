A mask 
A mask made by others 
A mask made by me 
A mask I wear in front of others 
The face that they all see 
I ask; Why do I wear this mask? 
Is the mask a role I act in some grand play? 
Or is the mask me? 
Is the mask even real? 
This mask I wear, does it fool even me? 
The mask can be a shield 
The mask can be a wall 
The mask can be a crutch 
The mask can be a burden 
The mask is who I want to be 
The mask is who I can’t be 
For the mask isn’t me 
I let you see through my mask 
And you saw me 
Why must I wear this mask? 
Why can't I show others me? 
Thank you for seeing through my mask 
Thank you for seeing me 
One day I will tear down this mask So I can be free
