Fear
A cage
A prison
A ball and chain
Each link in the chain reminding you, before every action you take, what dire consequences it will bring
How sitting next to someone will spell your doom
How telling someone how you feel will end any chance you had with them
How using this phrasing will make no one ever like you
How its better to keep your feelings bottled up inside you
Because then
People will like you
And so you do
You listen to them
Because your life is happy(or so you think)
And your friends are great
But the chain keeps you from doing what you want to
Asking for a dance
Telling someone how you feel
Being there for someone
Saying the things you want to
And slowly, you chip away at the chain
You tell someone how you feel(over email, but it’s progress)
You sit at their table
You make friends with them and their friends
You hold hands with them
And it feels amazing, making progress on that chain, being able to do what you want to, one bit at a time
Trying to be who you want to be
But then they leave you
And all seems lost
And the loss forges the chain stronger
The ball grows heavier
For if they could leave you, anyone can
And so you fear more
And so you’re back at square one
You tell yourself you’re ok
That you’re over it now
But that wound never healed
You just put a bandaid over it
Hiding it
Because if you didn’t, who knows what would happen
All your friends would leave you too
Because, after a breakup, everyone knows you’re allowed a time of mourning, then you have to get up and get along with your life and get over it
But you can’t
And you don’t tell anyone this
Until you do
You find someone who trusted you with history of theirs
And so you trust them with yours
And they help you through it
And you tell them more, about how hard it is to push through the fear
And they help push you through it
They get you to say things you wouldn’t have before, but wanted to
They help you become more you
And they are always there for you
And so you’re working at the chain again
Their lended strength helping you
And so the chain grows weaker
And eventually it’ll break
And you’ll be free
Free to be yourself
Free of irrational fear
Free to exist
