A vein of gold in a mountain 
Rocks that I thought were gold 
Making me miss the real gold 
I dismissed the gold, thinking it to be a rock 
I thought of the rocks, thinking them to be gold 
And now the gold is gone 
And all I have are rocks 
Not everyone needs gold though 
Some people only need rocks 
I will wait for the gold
If I do not like the gold, I will wait some more 
But while I wait, I have the rocks 
And after the gold leaves, I will have the rocks 
And while the gold is there, I will have the rocks 
Rocks help me find the gold 
Rocks give me the strength to keep waiting 
I do not thank the rocks enough 
But they deserve all the thanks 
For they are the ones who will stand by me, through thick and thin 
Through rain and snow 
Through life and death 
Thank you rocks 
Thank you
